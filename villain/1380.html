On the other side of the vortex lay a scene as calm as a lake.

Light and shadow intertwined upon the chessboard, while vortexes of time and space rose and fell. Gu Changge was actually quite surprised.

Just as he was preparing to intervene in the current chaotic situation and, in the process, had begun to unravel the karmic ties of the figure lurking behind the scenes, he stumbled upon a new discovery.

The origins of the Scavenger’s Book are deeply intertwined with the former ancestral court of Yao Guang.

The fact that its origins were linked to the Yao Guang Ancestral Court came as something of a surprise to Gu Changge.

To be precise, it is likely connected to the faction that emerged after the collapse of the Yao Guang Ancestral Court.

However, the whereabouts of that first-generation treasure of Civilization are now unknown; it has vanished without a trace, its current location a mystery.

Following the collapse of the Yao Guang Ancestral Court, that new doctrinal faction was established upon its ruins.

Yet, that doctrinal faction was obscured by the dust of history and vanished into the torrent of time, leaving it virtually unknown today.

Had Gu Changge not glimpsed it in the river of time while tracing the history of the Yao Guang Ancestral Court, it would have been nearly impossible to discover that once-existing faction.

All causal threads leading to that faction had been severed, deliberately causing it to be obliterated by the passage of time.

Thick fog shrouded everything, yet when he looked, he still discerned numerous clues and causal connections, as well as some rather intriguing scenes.

Had he not accidentally glimpsed those karmic ties while divining the future of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, Gu Changge would likely have forgotten her entirely.
