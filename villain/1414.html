If she hadn’t seen it with her own eyes, she wouldn’t have dared to believe the words she was saying.

Yet that was the reality: the Black Lotus Cult was scouring the land for children with certain unique traits, training them to become “holy children,” and then using these holy children to aggressively spread their doctrine, expand their faith, and recruit followers.

Through this method, the Black Lotus Cult has been quietly spreading across all territories in recent times, spreading as rapidly as a plague and proving impossible to contain.

With the arrival of chaotic times, all beings live in fear, especially as dark spirits draw near, ready to strike at any moment.

This prevailing environment has become a breeding ground for the Black Lotus Sect’s expansion and growth.

Yao Yao had personally witnessed a ritual where the Black Lotus Cult used the Holy Children to spread their teachings and attract followers. Afterward, she secretly followed the cultists to a dungeon where numerous young boys and girls were being held captive.

In that gloomy dungeon, the young boys and girls captured by the Black Lotus Cult were subjected to a selection process by the cultists using a strange, eerie stone slab to choose candidates for the "Holy Child."

Afterward, the Black Lotus Cult members would take the chosen "Holy Child" to perform a peculiar and eerie ritual.

It was during that strange and eerie ritual that Yao Yao witnessed a mysterious phantom descend, bestowing blessings and protection upon the chosen Holy Child.

At first, she thought it was just a run-of-the-mill cult ritual, but she never expected that the mysterious apparition that descended would bear such a striking resemblance to Gu Changge.

This left her frozen in place, her emotions in turmoil, until she was discovered by Black Lotus Cult members and forced to flee.

During her escape, in an effort to uncover the truth behind it all, Yao Yao took a risk and abducted a holy child—the very boy now imprisoned in the cage before her.

This set the stage for the series of events that followed.

Because this matter was deeply entangled and might even implicate Gu Changge, Yao Yao proceeded with great caution, not wanting to alert the surrounding orthodox sects and make the situation public knowledge.

For this reason, she planned to secretly bring the Holy Child back to the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

But she never imagined that the Black Lotus Sect’s influence might far exceed her expectations—to the point that even a Dao Realm cultivator had been dispatched to pursue them.

“Temporarily transform into a dark being? Are you saying that members of the Black Lotus Sect can shift between being ordinary beings and dark beings?”

“Is such a thing truly possible?” Jiang Weiyang’s confused voice interrupted Yao Yao’s train of thought.

As far as she knew, once a cultivator was corrupted by dark matter and fell into darkness, they would be completely consumed by the will of darkness, gradually degenerating into a dark being—with no possibility of recovery.

Even in ancient times, the sages who fought against the Black Calamity had no way to counter it.

In fact, connecting the recent events in the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, Jiang Weiyang even dared to speculate that perhaps even her father had met with misfortune and been tainted by the darkness.

Of course, this was merely a bold conjecture; she dared not dwell on it, nor could she bring herself to accept it.

"Actually, dark matter isn’t entirely incurable. My master once helped someone expel dark matter, restoring their clarity of mind and bringing them back to their senses..."

Yao Yao knew Jiang Wei Yang might find this hard to believe, but after a moment’s thought, she still mentioned what she had heard from some members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance regarding Gu Changge’s rescue of the people of the Floating Continent.

Among them was an elderly man who had been infected by dark matter, his mind clouded and on the verge of falling into darkness.

But with Gu Changge’s intervention, the old man eventually regained his senses, and the dark matter within him was completely purged.

This incident caused quite a stir within the Heaven Slaying Alliance at the time and greatly inspired many of its members, who no longer feared the dark substance.

Yao Yao had even heard that Ling Yuling, the Deputy Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, had been corrupted by the dark substance and trapped in the Dark Realm. In the end, it was her master, Gu Changge, who led numerous elders from the Yuxian Palace to rescue her, bringing her out and purging the dark substance from her body.

"Is that really true?" Upon hearing this, Jiang Weiyang’s red lips parted slightly

, looking somewhat surprised and shaken. Then, her beautiful eyes sparkled with a radiant glow, making her exceptionally captivating.

If this were true, then even if her father were to be infected and corrupted by the dark substance, wouldn’t there still be a way to resolve it?

Even though she was as gentle as a rabbit, preferring peace and tranquility, her heart couldn’t help but race at that moment, filled with unmasked joy and excitement.

"Just go ask around at the Heaven Slaying Alliance and you’ll find out—it’s no secret." Yao Yao shook his head, puzzled as to why Jiang Wei Yang had become so agitated after hearing this.

Shouldn’t her focus be on something else?

If ordinary beings can be artificially controlled and transformed into dark beings, doesn’t that imply that dark beings can likewise be transformed into ordinary beings, and then silently infiltrate the various Daoist lineages, civilizations, and realms?

Thinking it through like this was truly chilling.

The emergence of the Black Lotus Sect was simply another tactic employed by the dark beings to counter the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the various races and civilizations of the Vast Expanse.

"I must return to the Heaven Slaying Alliance as soon as possible, inform my Master of this matter, and let him prepare in advance." Yao Yao looked at the dazed youth in the corner, feeling a mix of pity and regret, but above all, helplessness.

She had no way to relieve him of this suffering.

"This matter is indeed terrifying; we cannot afford to waste any time." Jiang Weiyang snapped back to reality, nodded, and agreed.

She finally understood what Yao Yao had been worried about.

In the current state of Cangmang, it appears that the Heaven Slaying Alliance, the Righteous Unity Alliance, and the Dark Spirits are locked in a three-way standoff, but it is highly likely that the Righteous Unity Alliance has already been infiltrated and taken over by the Dark Spirits.

If even the Heaven Slaying Alliance were to fall, then the entire Vast Expanse would truly face a Dark Age, and a genuine apocalyptic catastrophe would descend.

"Especially since that source of darkness is drawing near, plummeting toward the realms of Cangmang—time is running out..." Jiang Weiyang sighed inwardly.

Yet at this moment, she also harbored a different kind of hope. Since ancient times, times of chaos have given rise to heroes. Might the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance single-handedly save the crumbling edifice?

In the Ancient Yin River Realm, within a palace shrouded in perpetual gloom and shrouded in endless mist.

In a pool several zhang in length and width, built of blue jade, the black water churned and bubbled ceaselessly. A massive black lotus platform floated and sank within it, its petals crystalline and radiating an immortal glow like dark jade—a truly wondrous sight.

Upon this lotus platform sat a shadowy figure clad in black robes, cross-legged. The figure appeared to be in deep meditation, yet also seemed to be wandering through time and space.

Around the pond, flickering candlelight illuminated one figure after another, each clad in black robes and wearing a similar black lotus mask, kneeling there with reverent fervor.

"The Great Lord of the Black Lotus will protect each of his followers, guiding them back from the darkness of their lost paths..."

Suddenly, the figure seated upon the black lotus platform opened his eyes. A flash of wisdom seemed to gleam within his cloudy gaze, as if he were a sage who had penetrated the true essence of all worlds and their boundless mysteries.

As his aged voice rang out, the figures kneeling and prostrating themselves around him grew even more fervent.

The already bubbling pool now spewed forth one black bubble after another; black mist swirled and surged up and down, as if some living creature were about to burst forth.

The next moment, with a splash, a brand-new skeleton emerged from the pool.

It seemed to possess no consciousness, moving only by instinct as it followed the steps within the pool toward the exit.

As it moved, the black mist in the pool, as if alive, kept clinging to it, causing flesh and blood to begin growing and taking shape on its body.

This process didn’t take long; before it had even stepped out of the pool, it had already developed a complete set of facial features, grown arms and legs, and its eyes had become lively and expressive.

Yet this miraculous scene—of the dead being brought to life and bones covered in flesh—seemed to be nothing out of the ordinary to the frenzied figures surrounding it.

They merely knelt and prostrated themselves with ever-increasing fervor, their mouths constantly chanting phrases such as, “The Great Lord of the Black Lotus, who will forever protect his followers.”

The old man seated cross-legged on the lotus platform paid them no mind. He simply looked at the figure emerging from the pool and kneeling before him, asking calmly, “Why did you die?”

The figure seemed not yet fully awake, his thoughts still muddled. After a long pause, he replied respectfully, “I was killed by a strange, blind woman wearing a gauze hood, who summoned a sword embryo.”

“You possess the strength of the Dao Realm, yet you died so silently and without a trace. It seems the opponent’s power truly far surpassed your own. But a strange woman wearing a gauze hood to conceal her face, yet blind?” The old man on the black lotus platform seemed to fall into deep thought as well, connecting the various possibilities in his mind.

"Could it be the princess who eloped from the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty? If so, she must also know about the Holy Child."

Upon hearing the words “Sacred Child,” the figure kneeling on the ground visibly trembled, seemingly filled with fear, worried of facing punishment.

The old man paid him no mind, merely muttering to himself, “But it doesn’t matter if she knows. Even her father can’t save himself right now—how could he possibly meddle in such matters?”

“Wuxu Zi must be about to make his move as well.”

As the Source of Darkness reappeared across the realms, the situation throughout the vast lands grew increasingly chaotic and turbulent. In just a few short months, numerous large-scale wars had already erupted between the various clans and sects.

Along the borders of the Dark Beings’ domain, one could witness countless powerful figures leading vast armies to the frontlines every day, intent on vanquishing the Dark Beings.

As the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance engaged in fierce combat, an increasing number of reclusive clans were forced to enter the fray and join the conflict.

"Those six beams of light are the remnants of the six ancient treasures of Civilization. Alas, the power they hold has been nearly exhausted..."

On a majestic mountain within the Righteous Unity Alliance, a figure clad in a white robe, as ethereal as smoke, stood quietly with hands behind his back, gazing upward at the vast, mysterious realm of darkness slowly approaching beyond the heavens.

In the dim expanse of the heavens and earth, six faint beams of light were emerging and flickering; even across the vast expanse of time and space, they could be faintly discerned.

Enveloped in a chaotic aura, those six hazy beams of light faintly revealed the ethereal forms of six distinct weapons: an ancient mirror, a portal, a pool, a coffin, a wheel, and a Daoist text.

"Master, why are you aiding the master of the Yinxu Cave? Rumors abound that the Righteous Unity Alliance has already been infiltrated and controlled by dark entities. If the source of darkness were to fall into the Void, the Righteous Unity Alliance might turn against us in the heat of battle, bringing disaster upon the Void."

Beside the figure in white robes stood two young attendants with rosy lips and pearly teeth—a boy and a girl. Both had striking features and bright, lively eyes, bearing a striking resemblance to one another.

At that moment, the young girl also looked up at the scene in the sky and asked sweetly,

“The master of the Yinxu Cave?”

The figure in white merely smiled upon hearing this and said, “What you’ve heard in rumors isn’t necessarily true, and the Righteous Unity Alliance won’t necessarily bring disaster to Cangmang. New life is born from destruction; it is only in adversity that hope can be found.”

The girl nodded, seeming to understand yet not quite grasping the meaning.

The figure in white said no more, instead turning his gaze toward the distance, where the cries of battle pierced the heavens. The entire cosmos was drowned in the sounds of slaughter, and dazzling lights shot skyward, shaking the heavens and the earth.

The sun, moon, and stars rotated in their orbits; countless great stars trembled, and some were shattered by the aftershocks of the battle, turning into fragments that scattered across the sky in all directions.

Terrifying figures stood tall between heaven and earth. As their massive palms swept through the void, they swept up countless stars. It resembled an ancient battle between gods and demons; the aura emanating from them was powerful enough to obliterate an entire star domain.

The entire universe had become a battlefield, where the armies of the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance clashed, the fighting reaching unimaginable ferocity.

However, faced with the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s relentless and overwhelming offensive, the Righteous Unity Alliance’s forces were clearly on the verge of defeat, retreating step by step. In many areas, their ranks had already collapsed, and soldiers were scattering in all directions.

"Master, will you intervene?" the young boy on the mountain peak asked, his eyes shining brightly, as radiant as the sun.

“I’ve intervened repeatedly, and the powerful figures of the Heaven Slaying Alliance have already set their sights on me. This time, I’m afraid I’ll be facing a formidable opponent,” the figure in white robes said, shaking his head slightly.

The moment his words fell, a cold, piercing gaze swept over from a distant corner of the cosmos.

It was a woman of divine beauty, her face as fair and flawless as jade, yet her eyes were as cold and sharp as ice. As her black hair danced in the wind, she strode forward, shifting through space and time as the stars shifted in their orbits. In an instant, she traversed an entire universe, raising her palm to strike the white-robed figure atop the mountain.

“A former sage?”

The white-robed figure seemed slightly taken aback, then grabbed the two young attendants by his side and vanished from the spot in an instant, while the mountain beneath his feet crumbled into dust without a sound.

A woman in a white gown, her sleeves fluttering in the breeze, stood aloof and ethereal in the void, gazing at him impassively. Her eyes, too, held a trace of surprise—it was Ling Yuling.

“I suppose you must be that mysterious figure in white who appeared at the Righteous Unity Alliance recently?” she asked calmly.
