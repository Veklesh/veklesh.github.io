"What exactly is the Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance trying to do? Is he aiming to boost the Alliance’s power to a level capable of resolving the Black Calamity in the shortest possible time?"

"And how exactly has he managed to achieve all this? Simply by spreading his faith and attracting followers, he has caused so many powerful figures to emerge from the Heaven Slaying Alliance in such a short time, their fortune surging like a dragon, ceaselessly rising..."

"Such methods have been employed before, but the ancestral gods of those ancient divine civilizations—who grew powerful by absorbing the power of faith and offerings—never achieved this level of accomplishment. This is a single individual single-handedly transforming the entire strength of the Heaven Slaying Alliance."

"Could it be that, as the Ancestor Xu said, he truly holds the third path in his hands..."

Within the Ancient Treasure Civilization, a figure situated across multiple dimensional planes had been observing the changes in the Heaven Slaying Alliance over the years.

He wore a Taoist robe and sported a Taoist topknot, yet his expression was grave. On his otherwise clear and vigorous face, shock and disbelief were impossible to conceal.

This was none other than the Master of Destiny, who had arrived at the Ancient Zang Civilization alongside the Ancestor Xu.

After the collapse of the Righteous Unity Alliance, Gu Changge had not come after him. The Master of Destiny had been in hiding for many years and had gradually let his guard down, believing that Gu Changge might not be concerned with him.

Consequently, during this time, he had been closely monitoring the movements of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Dark Clans signing a pact of non-interference: the Dark Clans continued their grand rituals, while the Heaven Slaying Alliance spread its core tenets and teachings, increasing its followers. Across many realms of the cosmos, the sounds of chanting and proclamations rang out day and night.

The power of faith shone silver and vast as an ocean, overwhelming all under the heavens; even across vast stretches of time and space, one could sense its blazing, boundless intensity.

However, what truly shook the Master of Destiny was not the power of faith itself, but the changes brought about by the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

He could not fathom how Gu Changge had managed to achieve all this—constantly creating extraordinary encounters and transformations, altering the grand trends of heaven and earth, and casting the entire future into a state of utter confusion and obscurity.

Those Chosen of Destiny, the protagonists of heaven and earth, began appearing in droves within the Heaven Slaying Alliance. Radiating outward from the Alliance like a spider’s web, they rapidly spread across the entire Vast Expanse.

This completely disrupted the waters of the Vast Expanse. The glimpses of the future they had once been able to catch were now utterly blurred, leaving them unable to perceive anything.

Consequently, the Fate of the Heaven Slaying Alliance soared relentlessly, triggering a cascade of changes in the Fate of the Vast Expanse. The most evident of these was… the Epoch Cataclysm would arrive prematurely.

The Master of Destiny no longer had much opportunity to lie low and prepare. Likewise, he was eager to know how Gu Changge had managed to accomplish all this—creating so many serendipitous opportunities and divine blessings through the power of a single individual.

Is it by bestowing his own power upon others? Or does he extract that power from elsewhere, then bestow it as serendipitous opportunities?

This clearly defies the laws of the universe; such power cannot come from nothing. Could it be that he drew it from the faith of his followers…?" The Master of Destiny felt he had grasped the most crucial and vital point.

This is likely the root of the third path mentioned by the Ancestor Xu!

Unfortunately, with Gu Changge stationed deep within the Heaven Slaying Alliance, he dared not probe further. For now, he could only observe the Alliance’s developments from across the vast expanse of time and space.

"The power of faith and the power of destiny are indeed wondrous and profound. They contain things we have long been unable to fathom or fully comprehend through our calculations and investigations..."

Suddenly, a ripple appeared beside the Master of Destiny, and the figure of the Ancestor Xu stepped out.

The place where the two of them currently stood was a barren, lifeless planet, inhabited only by a few ancient lifeforms.

"The birth of dark matter was the same back then—it was created out of thin air by him. He possesses a method I have never been able to comprehend, one that allows him to create something from nothing."

"But despite my relentless research, I know nothing."

"That is why I am certain: he truly possesses secrets unknown to all living beings in the Vast Expanse..."

Before the Primordial Void appeared, he had already heard these words from the Master of Destiny. Upon hearing them, a meaningful smile flickered across his vague, formless face.

"From nothing to something?"

The Master of Destiny furrowed his brow. At their level, they could naturally create worlds, but they needed to rely on the Chaos.

Likewise, they could bestow extraordinary opportunities for transformation, yet they could not create out of nothing; they needed to draw upon the power of the White Body or strip away another’s power to bestow it upon others.

To truly create something from nothing—to nurture life, create the myriad heavens, and then forge a world as vast as the Vast Expanse—would that not be a trivial matter?

This would no longer align with the natural laws of the Vast Expanse, contradicting the Great Dao they have cultivated and embraced, and running counter to it.

"It cannot be said to be creating something from nothing, but what exactly is this 'nothing'?"

“If we can clarify this, perhaps we too can reach the level of the Ruler of Heaven.” The Ancestor Xu said.

At that moment, Yunzhu finally began to understand why the Ancestor Xu was so obsessed with studying the Ruler of Heaven. After all, the more he delved into it, the more he discovered the oddities and secrets within it.

Regardless of which interpretation was correct, for those living amidst the Vast Expanse, it was a massive shock—for it was something that completely defied the laws of nature and the universe.

The Master of Destiny felt a chill run down his spine as he pondered this deeply, and a wave of dread washed over him.

“Is this the reason for the emergence of Dark Matter and the Dark Races? Even before the Primordial Era, I had never heard of these races. And their reproduction does not rely on bloodlines…” he sighed softly.

Why did the Age of Black Calamity leave such a profound mark on the Vast Expanse of history, and why did the Dark Tribes, unlike other tribes that had appeared before, not vanish during the Epoch Cataclysm?

The most crucial reason is Dark Matter—a substance that even the Epoch Cataclysm cannot erase.

Regarding the origin of Dark Matter, theories abound and speculation runs rampant. Some believe it was created by the Lord of Heaven, others that it originated from the Realm of Truth, and still others claim it emerged from the unknown Age of Nothingness, unearthed from the depths of Chaos…

But as things stand now, it appears that it was indeed created by the Lord of Heaven.

"When we draw on a sheet of paper, we are the creators of that world. We can create whatever we wish, needing nothing more than ink and brush. Yet to the figures within that painting, they cannot comprehend what these things—arising out of thin air—truly are. They can only witness a river, a city, or even an entire world suddenly and inexplicably appearing beside them..."

"But they cannot fathom how these things came to be."

"We are already, in a sense, the most powerful beings in that painted world, yet we still cannot reach the world beyond the painting..."

The Ancestor Xu said wistfully.

This was the sense of their own limitations and fear of the unknown that they had sensed upon reaching this realm.

Hearing this, Yun Zhu’s spiritual resolve was indeed shaken to its core. Before witnessing the changes in the Heaven Slaying Alliance with his own eyes, he would never have believed the Ancestor Xu’s words so readily.

But now, he was beginning to waver.

"Tell me," the Ancestor Xu asked, suddenly flashing a somewhat eerie smile as he looked at him, "why is it that during every cosmic cataclysm, so-called 'authority' emerges? And might those who claim to have seized and abolished that authority simply have happened to touch the ink that fell at that moment, thereby gaining the ability to paint upon this blank canvas..."

Yun Zhu’s expression darkened, and he snapped coldly, “Do not disturb my spiritual resolve.”

The Ancestor Xu chuckled and said, “Why so tense, fellow Daoist? I was merely sharing my understanding. At our level, everyone has a different perspective on this world—why should you take what I say to heart?”

“I came to see you because there is something I wish to discuss. The Disaster King has matters to consult with both of us. The Ruler of Heaven intends to bring the Epoch Cataclysm forward, and this has already caused great dissatisfaction among certain parties…”

Upon hearing this, the Master of Destiny’s expression softened slightly.

The Disaster King mentioned by the Ancestral Void was the ruler of the Disaster Clan, one of the five great royal clans of the Ancient Zang Civilization—an entity of equally immense antiquity.

Although the Ancient Zang Civilization is ostensibly ruled by the Ancient Zang Imperial Family, there is actually a Sacred Temple that stands apart from it. The High Priest within holds an exceptionally exalted and unique status, one that transcends even imperial authority.

Apart from the Ancient Zang Imperial House, the Ancient Zang Civilization also comprises five great royal clans, each possessing a foundation and strength comparable to that of a supreme True Realm.

The Zai Clan is one of these clans.

The Ancestor Xu and the ruler behind the Disaster Clan share a deep connection. This time, the two of them, accompanied by their followers, have come to the Ancient Zang Civilization, effectively aligning themselves with the Disaster Clan.

"The one behind the Zai Clan—are you referring to the Zai Lord?" Yun Zhu’s expression remained impassive. As the ruler of the Zai Clan, he had heard of the Zai Lord, but the latter’s power was at most on par with their own; it was insufficient to warrant such mention from the Ancestor Xu.

“It is not the Lord of Calamity, but rather a certain being who wields the authority of creation…” said the Ancestor Xu.

“Are you referring to the one on Creation Mountain?” Yun Zhu narrowed his eyes, deep in thought.

Time flowed on, and thousands of years passed in the blink of an eye. The Fortune of the Heaven Slaying Alliance surged and grew, reaching its zenith, becoming ever more intense and overwhelming.

Vast waves of faith surged from every corner of the cosmos, overwhelming the heavens as they converged upon the depths of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

During this period, masters emerged in droves, springing up like bamboo shoots after rain. The number of Immortal Realm cultivators who appeared over the course of a single millennium far exceeded those of several previous eras combined.

Numerous protagonists blessed with profound fortune and "Children of Destiny" also began to challenge higher realms, propelled by the accumulation of opportunity and divine providence.

Meanwhile, under the watchful eyes of the myriad races and civilizations in the Vast Expanse, the Dark Clans transported the source of darkness away.

The moment it touched the Vast Expanse, endless dark matter completely enveloped the lair of the dark beings. An unimaginably terrifying spectacle emerged there, casting its shadow across all realms, causing everyone to tremble with fear.

The phantom of the Coffin of the World’s Demise floated before the Hall of the Celestial Mandate. A fissure appeared within it, from which a hazy light burst forth. Even denser dark matter poured down in endless streams, as if a corner of the heavens and earth had been torn apart, engulfing the entire Nightmare Demon Realm.

No one could make out what was happening there, but they could vaguely sense a terrifying aura—one so oppressive it weighed down the Vast Expanse—as if it were about to awaken.

At the same time, the Dark Clans launched their campaign to erode the Nine Heavens.

The Nine Heavens, which had been hidden in an unknown realm of time and space with no known path, suddenly revealed its coordinates to the Dark Clans for reasons unknown.

A terrifying pitch-black fissure tore through the spacetime beyond the Nine Heavens, while a faint, ethereal light pierced through it, connecting the two realms.

The Dark Horde, as vast and overwhelming as a tidal wave, swept in and overran the Nine Heavens, a realm that had remained untouched for countless ages. After a long period of silence and tranquility, it faced its most ferocious battle yet.

The entire Nine Heavens began to tremble. Dark matter, like a mist, swept in, obscuring the sky and the sun, completely shrouding the realm of time and space there.

The Grave Keeper stationed before the Nine Heavens was forced to intervene, facing off against the four High Priests of the Dark Horde. The sight of all four High Priests acting in unison was an exceedingly rare occurrence.

That battle shook the entire Vast Expanse; the river of time evaporated, and fate was thrown into chaos. It raged on for a long time, and to this day, no victor has emerged.

With the Grave Keeper no longer guarding the entrance, the Dark Clan’s vast army surged forward like a torrential flood, forcing the Nine Heavens—accustomed to peace and detachment—to respond with grave resolve and choose to resist.

The ancient figures in seclusion within each of the Nine Heavens were compelled to emerge and mount a defense. The battle was fierce, in no way inferior to the original clash between the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance.

As a result, the Nine Heavens were exposed to the eyes of the world.

During this time, the Heaven Slaying Alliance chose to stand by and watch coldly, paying no heed. Due to the alliance they had forged with the Dark Clans, they had no intention of intervening.

The same held true for the many members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance. When the Dark Clans had once ravaged the various realms, the Nine Heavens had never intervened. Now that their hidden realm had been exposed and faced such an invasion, the other True Realm civilizations naturally had no intention of coming to their aid.

In just a few thousand years, the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s strength grew rapidly. This gave the various races and True Realms—which had previously lacked the confidence to resist the Black Calamity—considerable new resolve.

Even many figures at the Dao Realm have, during this period, abandoned their former cultivation methods to devote themselves entirely to the power of faith, constantly expanding the ranks of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s followers.

It was precisely because of this benefit that the bottlenecks in their cultivation realms—which had remained unchanged for so long—began to shift.

Gu Changge, having refined the World Sphere, had long since perfected its various laws within it. Those who brought him more followers would, while being condensed into Immortal Substance and Creative Substance, receive corresponding nourishment in the form of cultivation benefits.

This created the most direct positive feedback loop. Combined with the *Sutra of Summoning* he bestowed upon them, it led many members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance to develop a false impression: that they, too, could gain cultivation through the power of faith.

Of course, this applies only to ordinary Dao Realm cultivators.

As for those who have long since ascended to the Ancestral Dao Realm, they have already seen through the true nature of Gu Changge’s actions and thus do not actively seek to engage with the power of faith.

However, since the Righteous Unity Alliance has disintegrated, it has become incredibly difficult to accumulate battle merits as before and exchange them for those two substances at the Hall of Battle Merits.

To advance their cultivation, they can only proceed step by step as they did before; yet the shifting tides of the Vast Expanse have made it easier for them to grasp their own path and the Great Dao.

Compared to ordinary cultivators, those who have entered the Dao Realm possess a far more resolute will. They firmly believe in their own strength and refuse to rely on external aids, viewing the cultivation gains derived from the Power of Faith as nothing more than a castle in the air or a mirage—something that could crumble at any moment.

Gu Changge did not interfere with them, nor did he force everyone to accept the tenets and doctrines of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Today, the number of True Realms within the Heaven Slaying Alliance—both large and small—is beyond count, and the sentient beings in each of these worlds number in the billions.

With such a vast foundation, it was more than enough to provide him with ample faith energy.

The vast expanse of cosmic fortune was also undergoing earth-shattering changes, unfolding exactly as he had desired.

During this time, he used two substances to transform the Prosperous Dao True Realm. While he detected no abnormalities, all sentient beings within the realm benefited as a result.

Gu Changge also noted that the ancestors of the Gu family had left the Prosperous Dao True Realm many years ago; now, the Gu family remaining in the realm consisted only of the original clan members.

Furthermore, beings at the Dao Realm were beginning to emerge.

Jiang Chuchu, Wang Zijing, and even Yin Mei had successively broken through to the Dao Realm, experiencing earth-shattering changes in both lifespan and power. Gu Changge had not forced their growth prematurely.

At present, whether or not they had reached the Dao Realm held no significance for him.

Over the past thousand years, Gu Changge has also been monitoring the shifts in Yue Mingkong’s aura. Though she remains in slumber, her aura continues to evolve and grow.

During this period, Zi Wanhe, the ruler of Zixiao Mountain, had come to see him. Due to the matter of the Prosperous Dao True Realm, he voluntarily brought up the deal that had once been struck between the Menggui Clan and himself.

Zi Wanhe was at a loss; he could not go back on his promise to the Menggui Clan, nor did he know why the Menggui Clan was searching for the words “Prosperous Dao.”

However, the existence of the Prosperous Dao True Realm was no longer a secret within the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

However, after he informed the Dream Ghost Clan of the Prosperous Dao True Realm’s existence, the clan seemed to vanish without a trace.

When Gu Changge learned of this from Zi Wanhe, he wasn’t actually surprised; he had long sensed traces of the Primordial Era within Yue Mingkong…

The true reason the Dream Ghost Clan was searching for "Prosperous Dao" was actually to find Yue Mingkong.

Since Yue Mingkong had always been by his side, the Dream Ghost Clan naturally dared not reveal themselves lightly.
