Gu Changge’s words were not loud, yet at that very moment they resonated like a divine decree across the Vast Expanse of all worlds, through endless time and space, and throughout countless universes.

Almost every universe echoed with this sound; the heavens and earth rumbled as if billions of thunderclaps were exploding in the deepest recesses of the void, deafening and resounding like the great bells of Huangzhong and Dalu.

A terrifying and astonishing spectacle emerged between heaven and earth; in that instant, the cosmos seemed to sense it, as if the very heavens and earth were enraged by it.

That rolling thunder and boundless sea of blood churned in the deepest recesses of time and space, within which countless corpses seemed to rise and fall—the souls of innumerable beings throughout history who had defied the will of Heaven and sought to engage in Heaven Slaying.

Ordinary beings did not yet sense this, but the more profound one’s cultivation, the greater the trembling and fear. An intangible, vast pressure weighed upon their hearts, and in the depths of their being, it seemed as though some will was about to awaken.

Heaven cannot be resisted; Heaven cannot be defied. Yet at this very moment, a voice resounded within the hearts of all beings: Heaven can be overthrown; Heaven can be defied!

Originally, the Will of Heaven had severed the notion of “Heaven Slaying” from the hearts of all beings, erasing the concept of “Heaven Slaying” from the minds of sentient beings across countless realms and Civilizations.

This caused countless beings to dare not entertain even the slightest thought of resisting the will of Heaven.

In the beginning, precisely because the concept of "Heaven Slaying" was absent, the emergence of the Heaven Slaying Alliance faced far fewer obstacles and difficulties.

But now, the Heaven Slaying tide is unstoppable; it has formed a surging momentum capable of overturning Heaven’s will. Heaven and Earth sense this and are enraged, preparing to punish all living beings.

The entire Vast Expanse of the celestial landscape has shifted; in the infinite depths of time and space, it seems as though something terrifying is about to be born.

Within the Ancient Hidden Civilization, the Ancient Zang Emperor and his companions all fell silent, filled with dread. They had already sensed that a cataclysmic upheaval—unprecedented in all of history—was about to unfold across the Vast Expanse. Perhaps, as the Master of Destiny had spoken in that final moment before his demise, this was not merely a matter of the Epoch’s Great Calamity.

That overwhelming sense of wrath from the void had just pressed down upon them, causing their minds and spirits to tremble and giving rise to an unprecedented sense of unease.

End this era of the False Heaven?

It sounds good in theory, but who could possibly achieve it?

That real realm, suspended beyond the Vast Expanse like a distant shore, has never been reached by any living being.

They were well aware of the secrets of this universe, yet what fate had befallen those who had defied Heaven’s will since time immemorial? What became of those who challenged Heaven, those Heaven Slaying sages of old?

Even now, only the newly born True Realm, unaware of the secrets of the Vast Expanse, still dares to engage in Heaven Slaying.

Every being standing atop the Vast Expanse seeks to avoid it: defying the will of Heaven has always been a dead end—a path with no hope in sight.

Although Gu Changge’s power was unfathomable, surpassing their wildest imaginings, to engage in Heaven Slaying? In their eyes, this remained a distant, unattainable dream—utterly hopeless.

However, at this moment, the Ancient Treasure Emperor and the others said little, tactfully refraining from opposing Gu Changge.

Qingyi stared at Gu Changge in astonishment, completely unprepared for the depth of his plans. Heaven Slaying? Could that truly be done?

As a princess of the Ji Chan clan, she was well aware of the secrets of this world. The so-called Vast Expanse was nothing more than a prison confining all living beings.

The difficulty of the Heaven Slaying far surpassed that of breaking free from this prison.

No one in all of history had ever managed to break free, let alone defeat Heaven Slaying. Wouldn’t that mean destroying this prison?

"My master is so amazing; my master can definitely do it..." It was the carefree Little Wangyue who, tugging at the green-robed figure’s delicate hand, gazed up with eyes brimming with joy and reverence.

"Heaven Slaying……"

Ji Qiyi, however, trembled slightly as she kept murmuring those two words. Her snow-white skin, set against her black skirt, glowed as translucent as jade, yet now it flushed with a blush as vivid as rouge from her excitement and anticipation.

She had been waiting for this day ever since she left the Ji Chan clan’s territory and journeyed far to the Heaven Slaying Alliance—she had been waiting to hear those words.

And now… not only had she heard it, but she might even witness it with her own eyes.

"If I can witness this day with my own eyes, what does it matter if I am shattered to pieces and reduced to ashes..." she murmured, her eyes fixed intently on Gu Changge’s retreating figure, her cheeks flushed.

Qingyi glanced at her third elder sister, then gently averted her gaze and shook her head.

Ever since they were children, her second sister, Ji Qiyi, had always spoken words of rebellion, but she never imagined that her third sister harbored such immense ambition.

"Still, if he truly intends to do this, no matter the outcome, I will stand silently by his side."

“It’s a pity that with my current strength, I can no longer help him.” Qingyi sighed softly to herself as countless memories flashed through her mind, bringing back scenes from their time in the Upper Realm of the Mountains And Seas True Realm. Back then, he was both the Young Master Changge and the Demon Lord, while she was his Guardian.

Time had passed swiftly. Now, upon their reunion, he had become the ruler of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, having unified the Vast Expanse.

If not for seeing it with her own eyes, who would dare to believe any of this?

But… upon closer reflection, hadn’t his identity been a mystery from the very beginning?

"The matter of the Heaven Slaying Alliance is simply too incredible. Alliance Master, might you grant the Ancient Hidden Civilization half a month to deliberate? After half a month, we will certainly provide you with a satisfactory answer."

At that moment, the Emperor of Gucang seemed to have thought of something and spoke up of his own accord.

The rulers of the five great royal clans behind him all fell silent, clearly aligning themselves with the Emperor of Gucang.

"Half a month?"

Gu Changge’s expression remained casual and unconcerned. Upon hearing this, he seemed to smile slightly and said, “Why should I give your Ancient Hidden Civilization half a month? Whether you deliberate or not, whether you agree or not, it matters little to me. In other words, even if you disagree, you must agree.”

The Ancient Treasure Emperor’s expression shifted slightly.

In the distant cosmos, the many ancient, supreme beings who had been observing this scene also shifted their expressions, furrowing their brows as they felt Gu Changge was being far too arrogant and domineering.

Even if he were to invoke the Heaven Slaying cause, wasn’t it overly tyrannical and unreasonable to expect everyone to submit to him?

Not everyone in this world is cowardly and afraid of death. Beings who have reached this level would rather die than be humiliated.

Pressuring them in this manner would only backfire.

As Gu Changge finished speaking, he fixed the Ancient Treasure Emperor with a look of keen interest, as if waiting to see his reaction—or perhaps he had said those words deliberately.

"The Alliance Leader speaks the truth. The Ancient Hidden Civilization indeed has no grounds to refuse. Your cultivation, magical power, and realm far surpass mine—I am no match for you, and I have no other recourse. However, the Ancient Hidden Civilization does not heed my words alone, so I humbly request that the Alliance Leader grant the Ancient Hidden Civilization half a month’s time."

At that moment, the Emperor of Gu Cang remained eerily calm, his voice deep and muffled.

As he spoke, under the incredulous gaze of countless beings of the Ancient Hidden Civilization, he knelt on one knee in the void.

In that instant, a boundless, muffled thunder seemed to reverberate throughout the Ancient Hidden Civilization, as if the heavens themselves had grown furious—as if some sacred authority had been offended, and certain cosmic forces were boiling and shifting with frenzied intensity.

"Emperor..."

The rulers of the Five Great Royal Clans of Zai Guo, Shou Jin, and Kong were shaken to the core, gazing at the Emperor of Gu Cang in disbelief—they never expected him to make such a move.

These scenes not only stirred boundless turmoil and upheaval within the Ancient Hidden Civilization but also shook the Vast Expanse of time, space, and planes.

The Sovereign of the Ancient Treasure Imperial Clan, the supreme being who ruled over the Ancient Hidden Civilization—a figure who had already stood among the shattered supremes—had actually knelt on one knee before the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, appearing utterly willing to submit.

Qingyi, Ji Qiyi, and the others were equally stunned, unable to believe their eyes.

"You are far wiser than the Lord of Destiny. Since that is the case, I shall grant the Ancient Hidden Civilization half a month. No secret of the Ancient Hidden Civilization can be hidden from me. If you wish to employ some petty tricks during this half-month, you are free to try."

“Or perhaps have the Lord of Reincarnation and the World Lord join forces to see if they can stop me?”

Gu Changge, however, was not the least bit surprised. He gave a faint smile. Above the Sea of Reincarnation, space-time split open, and a golden path of light spread out. The golden light enveloped Qingyi and the others, and in an instant, they vanished.

His own figure, too, vanished as if he had never been there, dissolving into a wisp of light without a sound.

The Sea of Reincarnation returned to its former calm and stillness, but the Mother Tree of the Eon was gone. It had vanished into the boundless spacetime passage, trembling as it receded into the distance, seemingly disappearing into the endless expanse of spacetime. No one dared to stop it.

Only after Gu Changge had departed did the Ancient Treasure Emperor slowly rise from the void. His features were blurred, his expression indistinguishable, yet everyone could sense his gloom and fury; even the Lord of Calamity, the Lord of Longevity, and the others dared not utter a word.

"Summon and recall all beings from every spacetime plane..."

"To discuss matters of great importance within the Temple of Sacrifice." He issued the order in a detached tone; compared to moments before, he seemed like a completely different person.

The moment his figure vanished from this place, the Emperor of Ancient Treasures cast a cold glance toward the depths of the Sea of Reincarnation.

"It seems the Emperor of the Ancient Treasury is already dissatisfied."

Deep within the Sea of Reincarnation, in that profound hall, the Ruler of the World smiled with a hint of malice and said, “To abandon one’s dignity before the entire Vast Expanse, buying half a month’s time for the Ancient Hidden Civilization—but what good is that? What is destined to come will come sooner or later.”

The Lord of Reincarnation mused, “With the High Priest fallen into the hands of the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, even if the Great Calamity descends, the Emperor of Ancient Treasure cannot escape punishment. In the eyes of the Reckoning Envoy, even the mightiest beings in the Vast Expanse are merely slaves to be subjugated. Without the High Priest to preside over the rituals, the Emperor of Ancient Treasure cannot bear this guilt—he too is seeking a way out…”

“Hah. Those mere Reckoning Envoys merely rely on the authority of that side. Without that aura, they could be crushed with a single flick of the wrist.”

The World Lord sneered, but as he recalled the scene from moments ago, his brow furrowed slightly. “Yet what exactly did the Fate Lord mean by those words before he vanished? Why did they stir an inexplicable sensation within me—one that sends a chill down my spine?”

"I’ve been pondering it as well, but I haven’t found any clues yet. As long as it doesn’t affect our grand plan, it’s fine." The Lord of Reincarnation’s gaze was deep as he shook his head slightly.

The events unfolding within the Ancient Hidden Civilization had, without a doubt, instantly swept across the entire Vast Expanse.

Countless civilizations and realms trembled; even in the most deeply hidden alternate dimensions and planes of existence, massive upheavals occurred, as Gu Changge’s words sent ripples of boundless turmoil throughout the cosmos.

At that moment, in a region that seemed to lie at the deepest level of boundless spacetime.

The surrounding void matter intertwined, forming an amber-like barrier. Within it stood another towering divine tree. Unlike the Epoch Mother Tree, however, this towering divine tree was imbued with a mysterious life force—like green mist—nourishing this expanse of spacetime. At the various nodes where the flow of spacetime was interrupted, one could see vast worlds existing one after another.

Each strand of green mist was like a band of life, enveloping and encompassing countless universes—too numerous to count, far exceeding billions.

Beneath the divine tree, a figure resembling an elf sat cross-legged, her hair coiled into a green spiral. Endless life energy surged around her, as if even the dead could be revitalized and gradually revived by the nourishment of her life force.

"Master of Life..."

At that moment, this expanse of spacetime warped, and a portal appeared. A blurred figure emerged, as if stepping out of the endless realms, twisting and turning through countless dimensions, overlapping and shifting, traversing billions of layers of spacetime in an instant.

The being known as the Lord of Life opened its eyes—clear as a lake, resembling the purest substance in the world.

"Lord of the Void?" She studied the figure emerging before her, as if deep in thought.

“Our last meeting was before the Era of the Broken Ages. A single slumber—I never imagined it would lead me to this present age, where I might witness so many wondrous sights.”

The being known as the Void Lord spoke, her voice seeming to emanate from the depths of endless time and space, making it difficult to discern her true location.

"Are you referring to the Mother Tree of the Era being moved and taken away by human hands? Or the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance and his intent to fell the heavens?"

The Fate Mistress seemed to grasp the events unfolding in the Vast Expanse of the present in an instant. She paused briefly, her eyes—like a jade-green lake—radiating an inscrutable calm.

"Have you not sensed it? The heavens and earth are undergoing a great upheaval. Things that were once impossible have now come to pass. In the depths of the unknown, the Heavenly Dao has awakened me from my slumber. You and I are beings who wield authority; in the Vast Expanse, we are no longer subject to decay—we have reached the very pinnacle of existence."

“Yet now, I sense a faint trace of the path ahead. This suggests that the Heavenly Dao may be faltering—and this will be our opportunity,” said the Lord of the Void.

"A hint of the path ahead?"

The Lord of Fate seemed deep in thought, his gaze piercing through layer upon layer of the Vast Expanse as he looked toward a certain point. He said, “For countless ages, innumerable sages and beings have calculated and tested this with their own lives: it is impossible for a being more powerful than the True Path to emerge from the Vast Expanse. This is a fallacy. The leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance is indeed an exception, but his origins are a mystery—like an anomaly born outside the Heavenly Dao, different from both you and me."

"You and I are ancient beings born of the Chaos, masters of the oldest and deepest secrets of this world. We have even witnessed the figures of those three supreme beings with our own eyes. Their existence proves that a path forward certainly exists; we too can reach that realm, but they have simply blocked the way back."

"And have you forgotten the former leader of the Fate-Defying Palace? How do you explain her power? She was even considered the only being capable of transcending this realm."

The Lord of the Void spoke, a calculating gleam flashing in his eyes.

The Lord of Life shook his head. “Fallacies and anomalies are fundamentally different. Moreover, what you speak of is merely conjecture. Even the ancient beings born first in the Chaos could not reach the level you and I have attained today; at best, they reached the end of the path."Those three—they may not even be sentient beings at all, nor do they possess any concept of life. They are a form of matter beyond our comprehension, much like painting on a blank sheet of paper and expecting the creatures within the painting to understand what a blank sheet of paper is. The reason you and I have reached this stage is not because we are stronger than the other ancient beings, but because we have become parasites upon the Heavenly Dao."

The Void Lord suddenly laughed and said, “Indeed, you and I, who wield authority, are—in the eyes of the Heavenly Dao—parasites that have gone to every length to latch onto it. But compared to reaching the end of the road, we are truly a thousand or a hundred times more powerful. Isn’t that the truth? As long as we can grow stronger, it means there is still a path ahead.”

"At present, the authority of the Heavenly Dao shares the same origin as you and I. I have sensed it: the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance has defied the great tide of Heaven’s will, presumptuously attempting to overthrow Heaven.This act has already enraged the Heavenly Dao. He is like a worm attempting to devour the Heavenly Dao. As long as you and I restore order, realign the course with nature, and return everything to normal, we will gain even more authority from the Heavenly Dao and advance further along this path..."
