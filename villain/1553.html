"Who?"

"What's going on?"

All living beings and powerful figures were stunned. Even those engaged in battle with the Ancient Treasure Emperor and his companions were taken aback by this sudden turn of events, filled with utter horror.

The Five Avatars of Heaven represent the Heavenly Will. Each is unimaginably powerful, indestructible, and possesses an immortal body. Who could be so bold as to dare lay a hand on them?

"This fluctuating aura... I’ve sensed it before... It’s those five entities that share the same origin as the Heavenly Will."

"It is indeed that entity—it has made its move..."

Many figures at the End of the Path level finally realized what was happening.

They had witnessed the changes in the Realm of Sacred Truth and knew that an unimaginable entity had emerged there—one said to share the same origin and lineage as the Heavenly Will.

Now that entity has actually struck, brazenly attacking the Five Celestial Avatars.

"Heh, see if I wasn’t right? Yitian absolutely couldn’t hold back. To it, the Celestial Avatars are nothing but nourishment—this is the bait left behind by the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance." The Three-Headed Master wore an expression as if he had foreseen this all along.

The Black-White Master, however, was deeply shaken and astonished. The Three-Headed Master had foreseen all of this long ago—every word was spot on.

"Next, all of this should come to an end. Once the bait is taken, the angler will reel in the hook. The dishes on the menu should be served now."

The Three-Headed Master’s gaze was profound as he looked toward that region beyond the reach of sight. The fierce battle between the malevolent divine wills of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness and the Lord of Heaven—a “700” slaughter—had absolutely no impact on the situation here.

From the perspective of the Vast Expanse, this was clearly good news.

“Why did this pop up at such a critical moment? This is not good news…” However, the expression on the Ancient Treasure Emperor’s face darkened.

“How could this be?”

The expressions of the retinue of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness were equally grim; they had never anticipated that such a terrifying entity would emerge from the side of the Vast Expanse.

This was clearly an entity sharing the same origin as the Heavenly Will—essentially a fragment of the Heavenly Will weakened countless times over.

The appearance of such a thing was undoubtedly aimed at the Celestial Avatar.

A buzzing sound…

At the shattered edge of the High Heavens, the fissure continued to widen, spewing forth an endless torrent of pestilential energy. Behind the protruding green hand, a vast world saturated with pestilence slowly came into view.

However, the chaotic temporal currents where the great world’s barrier once stood all took on a blood-red hue, rising like vast, boundless mounds of blood vessels against the sky.

Countless streams of fresh blood dripped down, accompanied by tentacle-like appendages that danced wildly, tearing apart any living creature that dared to approach. This scene sent shivers down the spines of countless beings, filling them with dread; they felt that if they looked too long, they themselves would descend into madness.

Both sides of that azure arm were covered with countless azure eyes, and from each eye extended a pale, icy arm, hanging down as if countless cold, lifeless corpses had been forcibly crammed into that space.

At that moment, this blue arm—which sent shivers down the spines of all living beings—suddenly stretched across the heavens and earth, reaching out to seize the Five Avatars of Heaven with overwhelming force, as if any obstacle in its path would be crushed.

Many living beings could not bear such a sight; even a single glance made their scalps feel as if they were about to burst, blood gushing from their eyes, causing them to collapse to the ground.

The Five Avatars of Heaven were arguably the most powerful beings in the True Path, yet even they appeared somewhat insignificant in the face of that massive blue hand.

However, as embodiments of the Heavenly Will, they erupted with an incredibly intense radiance in the face of this oppressive aura—an aura that transcended the limits of the End of the Path.

Huangtian, Haotian, Mintian, Shangtian, and Cangtian seemed to merge into a single, even more terrifying and majestic being—five heads and ten arms.

Each head was utterly terrifying, bearing no resemblance to a living being—some shrouded in ominous gray mist, others cracking and exploding, covered in blood, or covered in thick, long black hair…

"Fallen Heaven..."

The five great incarnations of Heaven merged, their mouths emitting a cold, majestic voice. The five heads turned in unison, fixing their gaze upon that massive green hand. All ten arms swung simultaneously, causing the entire cosmos to tremble as they directly blocked it.

To the Heavenly Will, beings like Plague Heaven were all fragments of its own corruption.

"I am Epidemic Heaven..."

"By the Heavenly Dao of the Plague, those afflicted by the plague shall enjoy eternal life, never to die or perish, existing alongside the Heavenly Dao."

Dark-blue plague energy, like mist, shrouded the very edges of heaven and earth. Through the haze, one could vaguely make out a terrifying blue eye gazing down upon all living beings. That eye was cold, bearing the indifference of one who surveys billions of souls, devoid of even a trace of emotion. From within it came a deep, ancient, and majestic voice.

All living beings could not help but tremble, compelled to prostrate themselves in worship, bowing their heads ceaselessly.

That supreme majesty felt as though the true Heavenly Dao had descended, governing all order and laws across the multiverse.

Yitian manifested a portion of his true form; his colossal eye seemed capable of crushing all matter into dust.

It was like the very source of a plague; any living being or substance that came near would be corroded and infected.

Even spiritual energy would turn into pestilential energy; any being that absorbed it would undergo bizarre transformations within—flesh rotting, internal organs mutating, or marrow blooming with flowers…

Around the Plague Heaven, all tangible chains of order were corroded and severed; even the Great Dao had turned a dull bluish-gray, constantly emanating a terrifying plague aura.

All the powerful figures of the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Palace of Defiance were keeping their distance from that area.

Facing such an existence—one akin to the Heavenly Dao itself—for the first time sent shivers down many spines; even those at the End of the Path tier turned pale and felt waves of dread.

This eerie entity, capable of altering the very essence of life, struck fear into their Dao Fruits, triggering an instinct to flee.

"It seems the Plague Heaven intends to devour the Avatar of Heaven. For us, this is actually an opportunity."

"Without the Avatar of Heaven, the Ancient Hidden Civilization will have no means to fight back."

The Lord of Power and Si Zhu exchanged thoughts; their confidence soared, as they felt they had been walking onto a chessboard that had been laid out from the very beginning.

Whether it was the emergence of the Lords of Heaven representing Heaven’s malevolent will, the descent of the Avatar of Heaven, or the current move by the Plague Heaven, everything was unfolding along a predetermined trajectory.

On this chessboard, every piece has already been placed; the downfall of the Ancient Hidden Civilization is, without a doubt, inevitable.

And as for who was orchestrating this game from behind the scenes—that, of course, went without saying.

“How dare you claim the Avatar of Heaven for yourself alone?”

Suddenly, a cold snort rang out. A handsome young man, bearing a sword embryo on his back, appeared without a sound, materializing abruptly in the center of the arena.

As he drew the sword embryo and gave it a gentle twist, billions of strands of sword light burst forth, resembling fading radiance. All the surrounding laws and Dao trembled and warped until they withered and vanished.

Yi Tian’s outstretched green hand was severed cleanly in two, plummeting from the far reaches of the sky and transforming into a sky-filling plague mist that sent a chill down one’s spine.

The fair-featured youth stood there, like a very source of decay, constantly exuding a breath of weakness that caused the river of time itself to recede; this power could corrode the very fabric of time.

And the sword embryo in his hand was even more eerie—shaped like a black hole, utterly pitch-black and profound. Wisp after wisp of decay radiated from within, accompanied by faint fluctuations of the Heavenly Dao’s essence.

This sudden scene left every living being on the battlefield stunned once more.

"Decaying Heaven?"

But soon, Yitian’s cold, icy voice snapped everyone back to reality—this must be one of the five inconceivable entities that had been born.

Decaying Heaven?

Could it be that it exists in the same way as Epidemic Heaven?

The Lord of Power and the others couldn’t help but feel a chill run down their spines. If that were the case, would the other three also make their appearance?

Sure enough, a terrifying tremor once again shook this shattered expanse of the cosmos, as if the heavens were collapsing and the earth splitting apart. Order disintegrated, and the Heavenly Dao retreated.

The entire cosmos seemed to be collapsing, as if it could not bear the weight of such terrifying energy.

Three indescribable figures appeared here, striding in from different directions. They were so incredibly powerful that even True Path-level beings paled in comparison.

The aura of each of them was in no way weaker than that of Yi Tian.

The battlefield fell into complete silence. Even the followers of the Supreme Yin Heavenly Lord, who were in the midst of a fierce battle with Yue Mingkong, had their eyes filled with deep, trembling fear.

What on earth had the Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance done? To have conjured up five such terrifying beings.

"It seems you intend to compete with me." Yi Tian’s tone was icy.

From the farthest reaches of the sky, Su En, clad in a golden robe, emerged from that immense, azure eye.

Between his eyebrows, a vertical green eye flickered ceaselessly, radiating faint yet heart-stopping ripples.

From the moment Yitian was born, it knew of the existence of the other four Heavens—Decay, Chaos, Decay, and Lust—which the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance had created by exploiting the weaknesses in the hearts of all sentient beings.

It could be said that they share a common origin with the Heavenly Will, yet they also draw from the will within the hearts of all sentient beings; this is why they have developed self-awareness.

"The Five Great Avatars of Heaven—we shall each claim one."

“As for the nine lesser incarnations, let each vie for them by their own means; there’s no need to fight to the death over them.”

The fair-featured youth bearing the Sword Fetus spoke without the slightest pretense of concealment, nor did he care if the surrounding beings learned his name: Qin Yi.

Hailing from a realm known as the Great Sword Origin World, he had become the host chosen by Decaying Heaven and had inherited the supreme sword arts that Decaying Heaven had brought with him when he infiltrated and altered the future trajectory of this ancient sword-cultivating world.

The Sword Embryo in his hand was a manifestation of a portion of Shuai Tian’s true form. The true Shuai Tian resided within his body in the form of a nine-story, delicate Sword Tower.

Before being chosen by Shuai Tian, he was merely an obscure, ordinary person in the Great Sword Origin World.

The Great World of Sword Origin is a realm where sword cultivation reigns supreme, and the art of the sword has reached its pinnacle.

His father was the Prince of the North in the Northern Moon Empire of the Great Sword Origin World. He fought for the imperial family across the land, achieving glorious military feats, but his achievements eventually overshadowed the emperor, arousing the imperial family’s suspicion.

To sever the Qin family’s bloodline, the imperial family sent agents to secretly poison him while he was still in swaddling clothes, leaving him with a chronic illness from childhood that prevented him from cultivating. Later, they hypocritically arranged a marriage between him and the Sixth Princess. During a gathering, they had the Sixth Princess feign drunkenness, tear her own skirt, and frame him for the act, ruining his reputation and bringing him to ruin.

Under the weight of these blows, he sought justice from the Sixth Princess in a fit of rage, only to be severely wounded by a blow from one of her maidservants, nearly costing him his life.

Fortunately, while he lay unconscious, the Decaying Heaven suddenly descended and chose him as its host.

From that day forward, with the aid of Decaying Heaven, he drew upon the energy of decay to strengthen himself, embarking on a path to invincibility as he exacted revenge with relish.

First, he executed the Sixth Princess’s vile handmaiden and aided his father in overthrowing the Northern Moon Empire; later, he unified the Sword Origin Great World.

Qin Yi understood Decaying Heaven’s origins and power, so he developed steadily, using the Jianyuan Great World as his base to gradually encroach upon and corrupt the surrounding worlds, slowly reaching the point he is at today.

The descent of the Five Avatars of Heaven is the finest nourishment for Shuai Tian to further strengthen himself.

Even for someone as cautious as he, there was no choice but to heed Shuai Tian’s words and come to this battlefield to compete.

Unless absolutely necessary, Qin Yi did not wish to engage in a major battle with the hosts of the other five Heavens, nor did he want to engage in senseless slaughter.

He is well aware of the difficulties of cultivation in the Vast Expanse today; one could say the path forward is cut off.

But as the hosts of the Five Heavens, they had no such concerns; as long as Declining Heaven was sufficiently powerful, by relying on him, they too could possess terrifying strength.
