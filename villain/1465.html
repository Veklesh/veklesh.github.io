"The Heaven Slaying Alliance has now solidified its dominance; there is no longer any possibility of resistance. However, I have calculated the most crucial factor—I am not entirely without a chance."

Amidst the Vast Expanse, in a realm known as the Xiao Xi Sacred Domain, there stood a rusted bronze palace situated within the turbulent currents of multiple timelines.

The palace was vast beyond measure; its interior seemed to contain a constellation of countless stars, each star a vast continent in its own right.

And within each of these continents dwelled countless living beings, among whom were no shortage of Dao Realm beings.

On the surface, the Xiao Xi Sacred Domain was ruled by Buddhist cultivators, but in secret, it had always been under the control of the Heaven’s Decline Clan.

At this moment, the Ancestor Xu sat in deep contemplation upon the throne of this rusted bronze palace. Below him sat the high-ranking members of the Heaven’s Decline faction in order of seniority.

However, Wuxuzi and the others who had accompanied him to the Nightmare Demon Realm had all perished, and even he was unable to resurrect them.

“What is the point of all our scheming and plotting? In the end, we have suffered heavy losses.” Upon hearing these words, the Master of Destiny responded with utter indifference.

He led all his subordinates, including his two Five-Essence Bodies.

A figure clad in white robes, bearing the bloodline of the Central Lord, had once shone brilliantly in the battle against the Heaven Slaying Alliance. Using the Fourth Killing Formation of Ancient Times, he had sealed Ling Yuling and exiled her to the Chaos Realm.

The two young attendants who had previously followed him always addressed him as “Second Master.”

His real name was Ji Wudao, and he was considered his offspring.

This was because the Master of Destiny, in his Five-Element form, had once been entangled in a relationship with the Central Lord’s youngest daughter, Ji Hanshuang.

Ji Hanshuang bore him a son, and that son was Ji Wudao.

Through Ji Wudao, he manipulated the final remnants of the Yao Guang Ancestral Court’s destiny, enabling him to absorb all of its fortune, severing any future prospects, and successfully obtaining the Disk of Transcending Calamity, which had once been in the Central Lord’s possession.

And another figure, a black silhouette from the Hall of No-Birth, bound by chains, is also present here.

He possessed five bodies of the Five Essences, one of which now served as his true form.

The remaining two are still in hiding, serving as a contingency plan for unforeseen circumstances.

Within the grand hall were also the remaining high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance, varying in strength. The strongest among them were at the Ancestral Dao Realm level, while the slightly weaker ones were at the Real Dao Realm level.

These were all subordinates and pawns cultivated by the Master of Destiny over the years. Now that the game has been shattered, he refuses to accept this outcome. He can only lead all his followers to seek another opportunity, intending to make a comeback.

His ties with the Ancestor Xu run deep, so discovering his whereabouts was not difficult.

After the Righteous Unity Alliance collapsed and disintegrated, he came to seek out Shizu Xu, but Shizu Xu avoided him during that time.

The Master of Destiny had to resort to certain measures to force a meeting and discuss the possibility of cooperation.

“If we act blindly without first testing the waters, not only will I have no chance of survival, but neither will you. Though you possess many hidden cards and contingency plans, surely you don’t truly believe it would be difficult for the Ruler of Heaven to kill you?” The Ancestor Xu’s expression was calm and detached; his entire form resembled a faint, ethereal shadow, indistinct to the eye.

Upon hearing this, the Master of Destiny did not grow angry. Having survived the battle in the Nightmare Demon Realm, he had gained a clear understanding of the Ruler of Heaven’s power.

At the very least, neither he nor the Primordial Void could stand against the Ruler of Heaven in his current state.

Even if the Primordial Void possessed numerous tactics—and even claimed to have fully deciphered the Ruler of Heaven’s origins—

In the face of such terrifying, utterly overwhelming power, none of it would serve any purpose.

"Unless one holds authority, there is little chance of victory against the Ruler of Heaven. And as far as I know, he has neither seized nor abolished any authority..."

“Why is he so powerful? Even his dormant power is this unimaginable,” the Master of Destiny asked.

The Ancestor Xu replied calmly, “This is the other path I mentioned to you earlier. To walk that path, one must penetrate the true essence of its flesh and blood, understand everything about it, and only then can we forge a new path for ourselves.”

"You do not understand my resolve and ambition, nor do you realize that I am forging a new path for the entire Vast Expanse. I am different from you."

The Master of Destiny did not refute him. At their level, their principles and beliefs were entirely different; it was impossible to persuade one another.

Just as with the path he himself adhered to, he had already set foot upon it; there was no possibility of turning back, nor could he turn back. It was a matter of either burning the boats and achieving success, or perishing along with his path, his form and spirit both annihilated.

“During the chaos among the Heavenly Hosts, what did you gain? Why are you so certain that a third path will emerge?” the Master of Destiny asked.

“What did I gain?”

The Ancestor Xu smiled faintly. On his face, which had been a blank void, a sudden expression of icy coldness and laughter appeared. He said, “I gained his blood and flesh. I have studied and contemplated them for many years and have come to understand his origins. That is why I promised I could help more people take the next step…”

“Why do the remaining factions of the Eight Clans all heed my words? Why do I still have my hidden hands and pawns within the Heavenly Host? It is because I control the power they so desperately crave.”

"The emergence of the Black Lotus Sect this time was merely an experiment of mine, and I am very satisfied with the results."

The Master of Destiny had no interest in listening to this nonsense; right now, he only wanted to know how he should face the Ruler of Heaven’s impending reckoning. Did he really think that hiding here would keep him from being found?

"Let me tell you this: in truth, the Lord of Heaven is not a being born of the Vast Expanse at all. It is precisely because of this that his existence shatters all our perceptions—yet it is also because of his existence that I have been granted the opportunity to touch upon what is known as ‘truth’."

Hearing this, the Master of Destiny furrowed his brow.

Not a being from the Vast Expanse—could it be that he comes from the Realm of Truth?

As for the third path that the Ancestor Xu had always spoken of, he knew full well in his heart that it was nothing more than a conjecture, for no one had ever walked that path to completion.

Since ancient times, a rumor has circulated throughout the Vast Expanse: that after surviving nine Celestial Waning Tribulations, one could achieve transcendence in a single stroke.

But they all knew it was nothing but a lie.

Even if one were to survive nine Celestial Waning Tribulations, there would still be no path to transcendence before them; instead, the mist would grow thicker, and the way forward would be cut off.

And this realm is known to them as the "End of the Path" tier.

Over the course of countless ages, the sages of old, through trial and error and one after another, finally discovered two clues within that mist that might lead forward.

As for why they are merely clues rather than paths, it is because no one has ever truly reached the end.

One of these methods involves taking a single step further into the Realm of the End of the Path, shattering one’s own path, renouncing all that one possesses, and with supreme resolve, shattering all past memories and experiences—thus entering the Supreme Shattered Realm.

The Supreme Shattered Realm is the most extreme realm that has ever emerged in the Vast Expanse of time.

The slightest misstep leads to true death and the extinction of one’s path.

There is also a saying that when one takes a step with supreme resolve at the end of the path, a gateway will appear before one’s eyes. This gateway is believed to be the Gateway of Transcendence, which can be entered after shattering.

But only those who have truly established themselves in the Supreme Shattered Realm know that it is, in fact, merely the gate of one’s own Nirvana Path—the very gate pushed open upon entering the Dao Realm.

Supreme Shattering is merely the shattering of the gateway to the Dao Realm, establishing oneself on a plane that transcends the Dao Realm.

Another method is to wait until the era-defining cataclysm descends and the entire Vast Expanse undergoes a reckoning. At that time, the celestial opportunities will enter a period of obscurity and blurriness.

At that time, the authority of Heaven and Earth will manifest, regarded as a kind of Dao fruit.

Whoever can abolish or seize it will be able to dwell within this realm, coexisting with the Great Dao of Heaven and Earth, becoming immortal and indestructible—transforming into an inconceivable being that truly transcends the Dao Realm, unaffected by anything.

This realm is also referred to by many ancient beings as the Realm of the True Path.

Only beings who stand within this realm possess the true ability to evade the Heavenly Mechanism and the Reckoning. Even when the Cataclysm of the Eon descends, they can effortlessly conceal the Heavenly Mechanism to preserve themselves and the beings they wish to protect.

As for the Supreme Shattered Beings, though they are already powerful enough to far surpass the Dao Realm, they lack the True Path Realm’s ability to conceal the will of Heaven and blend into the Great Dao of Heaven and Earth.

Therefore, they must be extremely cautious in every move; the slightest slip-up could attract attention and scrutiny, inviting retribution.

In truth, there is no inherent superiority in strength or means between the Supreme Shattered Existence and the True Path Realm; these are simply two distinct choices made at the end of the Path Realm.

Naturally, having established a foothold in the True Path Realm, no one would willingly choose the path of the Supreme Shattered Realm. Setting aside the question of success, even if one were to succeed, they would still have to tread as if on thin ice, proceeding with utmost caution, for fear of revealing the aura of karma and facing retribution—a fate that would render all their efforts throughout the ages utterly in vain.

The Ruler’s long-standing scheme has been to seize the Dao Fruit—that is, the so-called authority over heaven and earth—before the great calamity of this cosmic cycle, thereby establishing himself within the True Path Realm.

Alas, the emergence of the Heaven Slaying Alliance and Gu Changge has rendered all his calculations and schemes utterly futile.

Apart from these two paths, there has never been a third path throughout history. Yet, during the Cataclysm of the Black Calamity, the emergence of the Ruler of Heaven has given many who dwell in the Realm of the End of the Path new hope.

The Ruler of Heaven did not appear to be a Supreme Shattered Being, nor did he stand within the Realm of the True Path; yet his power seemed to transcend this realm...

Thus, many speculated that he might have forged a third path. The Ancestor Xu had once voluntarily followed the Ruler of Heaven and repeatedly sought his guidance, all in an effort to uncover information about this third path.

"I have no interest in the third path you speak of. You and I are now merely grasshoppers on the same string."

"Faced with this situation, what solution do you propose?" The Master of Destiny snapped back to the present and turned to ask the Ancestral Void.

"There is no solution."

Shizu Xu spoke bluntly, “In the Vast Expanse, those capable of standing against the Lord of Heaven—or even those who might pose a threat to him—will not reveal themselves lightly. I have followed him for a long time, and now, having personally risked my life to test him, I understand that he is still fishing. Those who hold power, or those who have already achieved the Supreme Shattered state, will not so easily take the bait and meet their end.”

"He’s been casting his net for a long time, but those fish aren’t stupid—they wouldn’t just walk into the net. I suspect he’s running out of patience and is ready to overturn the table at any moment."

The Master of Fate paused, taken aback—he hadn’t considered these possibilities.

He fell silent for a moment. Though he felt a sense of reluctance, he had no choice but to accept this reality.

Aizu Xu said calmly, “You and I have only just set foot in the Realm of the End of the Path. In his eyes, what difference are we from mere shrimp and fish?”

The Master of Destiny fell silent.

In truth, another thought crossed his mind: back then, those sages of old were able to seal the Lord of Heaven using the six treasures of the First Civilization.

Why couldn’t they do the same now?

Gu Changge’s power certainly hasn’t returned to its former peak; they should still have a chance.

The Ancestor Xu looked down at him and said, “However, it’s not that there’s no chance. We must bide our time and wait for the opportunity. The Epoch Cataclysm is fast approaching, and the Ancient Treasure Civilization is about to make its move…”

In his eyes, the Master of Destiny was nothing more than a pawn. Now that the Master had come to him for help, he could certainly make use of him once more.

As for whether the Master of Destiny had his own schemes, he didn’t care.

Compared to everything he was plotting, the Master of Destiny was indeed nothing more than a mere minnow in his eyes.

"I truly have no other choice but to trust you." The Master of Destiny looked at the many powerful figures following behind him.

He had not kept his conversation with the Ancestor Xu a secret from them.

As for Gu Changge’s true identity, he had considered whether to expose it to the public and then harness the combined strength of all races and civilizations in the Vast Expanse to confront him.

But he rejected that idea.

Setting aside whether Gu Changge had anticipated such a scenario, even if all the visible forces of the Vast Expanse were combined, they would still be insufficient to stand against him.

The momentum of the Heaven Slaying Alliance was unstoppable; no one could stand in its way. Once he revealed his identity, Gu Changge would simply stop playing along—just as the Ancestor Xu had said moments ago—and flip the table.

What then would become of them? Without the power to counter Gu Changge’s supreme might, all their schemes and plans would be in vain.

The Dark Clans would obey Gu Changge’s commands, and the entire Vast Expanse would once again plunge into the Dark Age of old, living under his rule and shadow.

Therefore, while Gu Changge intends to continue casting his net and maintain the current status quo, they can only lie low and plot their next moves from the shadows.

Although he wished to cooperate with the Ancestor Xu, he remained wary deep down. Those six treasures of the First Civilization were his greatest hope and opportunity to turn the tide.

The Primordial Void was unaware that he actually possessed the Disk of Transcending Calamity.

"I’ve been plotting for so long, aiming for the Dao Fruit of this very life, yet now it seems I’ve become something of a savior destined to rescue the Vast Expanse..."

The Master of Destiny spoke with a touch of self-mockery.

"Over in the Ancient Treasure Civilization, I have a friend I’ve known for a long time. We can head there next."

"It seems the great reckoning of this era is about to arrive sooner than expected..." said the Ancestral Void.

Another hundred years passed slowly.

The situation in the Vast Expanse gradually stabilized. The Dark Clans abandoned the Heaven Slaying Alliance and shifted their offensive; while searching for clues to the Nine Heavens, they systematically swept through and eradicated the cosmic civilizations scattered across various latitudes and timelines.

Some deeply hidden civilizations were also exposed amidst the Vast Expanse as the Dark Clans advanced.

Among them were arcane civilizations rivaling the Divine Path, as well as heretical civilizations born from imitations of faith.

These civilizations fell one after another, suffering the onslaught of the Dark Clans and forced to mount a resistance.

Universe after universe was invaded by dark matter; black rain fell from the sky, black mist spread, and corrosion continued unabated. Even the multiverse, composed of numerous great realms, could not escape this fate. In the process, the True Realms fell one after another, completely transforming into the territory of the Dark Clans.

But the Vast Expanse is indeed vast beyond measure. The Dark Clans no longer possessed the full might of their former golden age; even when they held grand rituals, they could not reach every corner.

A hundred years was simply not enough to complete the Great Ritual. Thus, the four High Priests of the Heavenly Host descended once more. Beyond the hazy heavens, beyond the barriers of all worlds, the ancient sounds of ritual resounded again, and countless living beings appeared.

Surrounding the vast, boundless, blood-red ancient altar at the center, four more altars rose once more.

The scale of the Grand Ritual grew even greater.

Amidst this process, within those shattered and fragmented realms of the True Realm, it seemed that many lingering threads of fate began to intertwine.

In the vast void, it seemed as though some great cosmic destiny was being born in response to the times; a certain force in the heavens and earth was converging according to an indescribable law.

"The Dark Clan destroyed my home, ravaged my homeland, and killed my parents. I am sworn enemies with the Dark Clan, and in this lifetime, I will surely put an end to this dark calamity."

On a landscape charred and shattered, a young man roared in fury, his fists clenched.

At this moment, a certain destiny seemed to be coalescing within him. Streams of indescribable energy surged forth, transforming into a mighty power born to confront this calamity.

At the same time, a sound seemed to emerge from the heavens and earth, as if the heavens themselves could no longer bear witness to the Dark Clan’s slaughter and plundering of all living beings, responding to the youth’s cry.

Strange forces were brewing within him, giving birth to a peculiar vortex in his dantian—a vortex that resembled a starry river yet was as vast as an ocean.

From those shattered and fragmented cosmic dimensions, streams of residual energy seemed drawn to the vortex, surging ceaselessly into his body.

In the blink of an eye, the youth had attained a terrifyingly formidable power.

Meanwhile, in the Heaven Slaying Alliance, situated in a distant dimension of time and space, a pair of eyes watched him with calm detachment.
