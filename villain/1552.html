The Heavenly Will was once confined by the Three True Ancestors, which altered its very nature. As a result, its power has been significantly diminished compared to when it governed the spacetime of the old cosmic order.

Of course, to the infinite masses, it remains unimaginably powerful; after all, the Heavenly Dao governs all order, embodying all divine might, all creation, and the source of all things.

In this lawless realm, the massive hand that Gu Changge brought crashing down unleashed a multitude of unimaginable, unfathomable powers at that very moment.

It was an aura that transcended the so-called Heavenly Seal Realm; had this occurred within the spacetime of the old cosmic order, it would have been a power truly comparable to the Heavenly Dao itself.

Only power of the Heavenly Dao could shake the Heavenly Will; this was an immutable law. Although the Heavenly Will was no longer at its peak and lacked the full might of the Heavenly Dao, it was still far beyond what the so-called Dao Realm or Heavenly Seal Realm could hope to challenge.

The Thirteen-Tiered Heavenly Dao Realm of the Old Order era existed only in theoretical speculation; no living being had ever reached that level.

Today, the Heavenly Seal Realm—which corresponds to the Heavenly Dao Realm in the Land Of Truth—is essentially a derivative of the Heavenly Dao Realm. It is a brand-new realm born from the fusion of the old and new cosmic orders.

Even if the thirteen layers of the Heavenly Seal Realm possess the magical power of thirteen Chaos Heaven and Earth Eons, they are still far from comparable to the Heavenly Will.

Just how long it will take for sentient beings to accumulate the necessary power to reach that level remains an unknown.

Gu Changge’s current strength lies in the fact that he truly transcends the Heavenly Seal Realm.

No matter how many layers the Heavenly Seal Realm may have, his power remains consistently above it, far surpassing all others—after all, the establishment of the Heavenly Seal Realm was, in a sense, his grim decree.

It is precisely for this reason that the Heavenly Will is retreating, gathering the collective power of the Heavenly Will from all the Vast Expanse realms to counter him.

To outsiders, it certainly appears as though Gu Changge and the Heavenly Will are locked in a struggle, but in reality, the Heavenly Will is attempting to break free from his control and suppression.

Hum…

This golden cocoon, formed from the Heavenly Will, erupted with a terrifying brilliance unlike anything seen before, emitting a sizzling sound like oil splashing into hot water. The ever-shifting faces within it twisted ceaselessly until they finally collapsed, dissolving into billions of strands of pure, primordial energy.

All manner of emotions dissipated—joy, anger, sorrow, and delight; greed, hatred, delusion, and resentment… It seemed as though every living being in the multiverse had converged within it. This was the manifestation of sentient beings and their collective consciousness residing deep within the Heavenly Dao.

A battle of this magnitude—even the slightest ripple escaping it would be enough to shatter the heavens and destroy the earth. Countless universes and realms would be reduced to ashes, returning to the Void.

Fortunately, Gu Changge intervened to seal off this expanse of spacetime, preventing the fluctuations from escaping.

"Suppress."

Gu Changge’s expression remained calm as he clasped his fingers together and reached forward.

Ahead of him, the wills of countless billions of beings converged ceaselessly—chaotic and tangled, resembling a strange substance twisted into a billion faces.

All manner of emotions surged forth: violence, greed, resentment, fear, bitterness, and obsession…

"Die."

From within that strange head, a voice rang out—one capable of striking terror into the hearts of countless beings and reducing them to nothingness.

It was not a sound a living being could produce; it was more like the screeching and roaring of all life in the multiverse, capable of shattering any physical form under its impact.

Gu Changge’s massive hand, reaching out to seize it, showed no sign of stopping, surging with an unimaginable power that even the Heavenly Will feared.

The moment the two touched, it was as if snow met the blazing sun, emitting a constant hissing sound. Causality, fate, spacetime—all tangible and intangible orders—vanished and evaporated.

"Ah…"

"I’ll devour you…"

"I’ll kill you..."

The twisted collective will of all living beings let out a agonized roar. Billions of different faces shattered simultaneously, only to rapidly reassemble, opening a terrifying maw to devour Gu Changge.

The collective will of all beings was born from the depths of the Heavenly Will, representing a thread of its spiritual essence.

However, this thread of spirituality had long been twisted and thoroughly corrupted by the Three True Ancestors.

Naturally, the collective will had also become twisted; not a shred of its original spirituality remained, and it now functioned solely according to the rules and laws set by the three True Ancestors.

Now, a faint remnant of instinct had driven it to savage greed; it believed that by devouring the being before it, it might escape that seemingly endless state of mindless ferocity.

"How ridiculous..."

Gu Changge shook his head lightly.

He withdrew his grasping hand and instead struck out with a flat palm.

This palm strike contained all the power beyond the Heavenly Seal Realm—or, one might say, encompassed every conceivable force within the Heavenly Dao Realm of the old cosmic order’s spacetime.

After all, no living being had ever set foot in the Heavenly Dao Realm of the old cosmic order’s spacetime; everything existed only in theoretical calculations. The power of this palm strike represented the absolute limit achievable within the calculations of that realm.

Perhaps in the future, this limit would be shattered, and all established laws and principles would be broken, but at least for now, this palm encompassed everything, contained everything, represented the source of the Great Dao and the beginning and end of the Dao, and spanned the chaotic spacetime and countless realms.

"Ah..."

The collective will of all beings let out a agonized scream as that strange head exploded with a thunderous boom.

A dazzling radiance erupted from the golden cocoon. The Heavenly Will was also severely wounded; its endless tentacles rapidly retracted, attempting to retreat deeper into this space.

Gu Changge watched this scene unfold and chose not to intervene.

For he had no intention of refining the Heavenly Will. To him, even if he were to claim this power as his own, it would bring him little tangible benefit.

He repelled the Heavenly Will merely to alert the Five Days of Plague, Decay, Corruption, and Lust, prompting them to act and subsequently devour and consume the power of the Heavenly Will.

After all, if they failed to perceive the decline of the Heavenly Will, the Five Heavens would not dare to act lightly.

Ultimately, the Five Heavens were merely fragments of the Heavenly Will. If the Heavenly Will were to manifest fully across the myriad realms, the Five Heavens would undoubtedly be swallowed back into it.

Gu Changge allowed the Five Heavens to grow not only to make the Vast Expanse believe he was weakening the power of the Heavenly Will, but more importantly, because he intended to nurture a "poisonous insect" and reshape the future landscape of the cosmos.

To forge a new path means establishing a new realm. The Heavenly Will—which now stretches across the Vast Expanse of all worlds, governing all karmic order—will cease to exist in the future.

Only then will more vigorous "chives" sprout, giving rise to greater "crocodiles," "giant whales," and even "true dragons."

Please support with flowers…

The moment Gu Changge repelled the Heavenly Will, the Vast Expanse, infinite spacetime, and countless universes seemed to undergo a massive explosion. Layer upon layer of space trembled like torn paper, and storms swept through every realm.

With the exception of the Avatar of Heaven, every being who wielded the authority of the Heavenly Dao let out a heavy, agonized scream from the depths of their souls.

Not even the Lords of Power, who had already completely severed their own power from the authority of self-reliance, were exempt.

"Alliance Leader, I sense your presence once more..."

"That agonized cry just now came from the Heavenly Will." A look of unhidden joy flashed across the Lord of Power’s face, filled with immense excitement and elation.

The Master of the Realm felt the same, and could not help but whisper, “The Heavenly Will may have suffered another severe blow. We will witness a landscape unlike any we have ever seen.”

"The Heaven Slaying will no longer be willing to slaughter the Fallen."

She paused for a moment, gazing into the Vast Expanse. The immense, boundless Ancient Altar of Reckoning seemed to grow even more blurred, as if it were about to vanish completely, disappearing to the other side of the Land Of Truth.

“You are not mistaken. I felt it too—the Heavenly Will let out an agonized scream just now…”

Far away in another spacetime, the Black-White Lord’s expression was equally shaken, having sensed the ripples transmitted through the authority of Yin and Yang.

The Three-Headed Lord standing beside him let out a series of cold laughs, seemingly completely at ease.

This was nothing short of a reassuring sign for everyone in the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Palace of Defiance.

The Alliance Master is about to return.

"Changge……"

Yue Mingkong’s movements paused for an instant. The bloodstains on her robes and armor did nothing to diminish her aura; her gaze grew even more piercing.

The River of Dreams unfolded once more, radiating across the entire void—boundless and endless—enveloping all of the Absolute Yin Heavenly Lord’s followers within its reach.

"A golden opportunity."

"The Heaven Slaying Alliance Leader has once again wounded the Heavenly Will. These incarnations of Heaven will serve as perfect nourishment for me."

Within the Realm of Divine Truth, Su En—the spokesperson for the Plague Heaven—sat in a golden robe upon a throne shrouded in boundless plague energy.

A crack blossomed between his eyebrows, revealing a vertical, azure eye that gazed greedily upon the great battle erupting on the front lines of the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

“Would striking now be too conspicuous, provoking the wrath of the Land Of Truth?” Su En remained cautious and couldn’t help but ask.

"We can’t wait any longer. I sense the fluctuations of the other five Days—they too covet this power. Whichever of us claims these Avatars of the Days will rise above all the others."

“This is our only chance. Even if it’s likely a clever ploy by the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, it doesn’t matter.” Epidemic Heaven said coldly.

It did not consult Su En; though it appeared to be parasitizing Su En’s body, everything was now under its control.

BOOM!!!

Accompanied by a violent tremor, an indescribably massive fissure appeared in the sky above the front lines of the Ancient Hidden Civilization. Overwhelming waves of blue plague energy surged forth from it.

Amidst the blue plague energy that nearly obscured the sky, a terrifying blue hand reached out, grabbing toward the Five Celestial Avatars who were currently locked in a fierce battle with the Lord of Power and the others, its power and dominance overwhelming.
