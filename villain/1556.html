Amidst the vast, endless path of light, the Lord of the Three Purities, bearing a terrifying pressure as crushing as a collapsing Mount Tai, covered in blood, trudged forward with great difficulty, leaving a crimson footprint with every step.

One by one, the Daoist armor formed from Great Dao runes twisted and disintegrated, constantly being pierced and obliterated.

The Three Purities and Three Talents Great Formation above their heads had already been shattered.

As for the disciples of the Eight Immortals’ Ancestor, they had long since been utterly crushed, their bodies and spirits annihilated, reduced to clouds of blood mist.

The Ancient Array of the Eight Immortals was indeed a primordial marvel from the Great Abyss, yet it could not withstand the boundless pressure emanating from the Land Of Truth.

With the death and dissolution of the Eight Immortals’ disciples, the ancient formation itself completely disintegrated into the void, reduced to a pile of ashes.

Within the Daoist true mansions, the foundations accumulated over billions of years were likewise reduced to dust at this moment, steadily eroding away.

They had no concept of just how many disciples and descendants had perished, or how many celestial realms and blessed lands had been destroyed… For this very day, the Three Purities Dao Lords were willing to sacrifice everything, even if it meant burning away their Dao Fruits.

BOOM!!!

Suddenly, a violent tremor shook the ground; tens of thousands of steps quaked as if the heavens were collapsing and the earth splitting apart.

The Lord of the Three Purities gritted his teeth against the pain of his bones on the verge of shattering, struggling to shift his gaze. He saw a bloodstained, shattered green vessel charging in from beyond the Path of Heavenly Light.

The Gate of the Six Realms of Reincarnation hung high above, floating like a circular disk. A towering figure, his face radiating excitement and elation, stood upon the green vessel.

Though he too was covered in blood, his condition was far better than theirs; he was completely unaffected by the oppressive might of this place.

It was none other than the Lord of Reincarnation.

"What is this..."

"How is this possible? He actually withstood the oppressive might of this place..."

The Lord of the Three Purities could scarcely believe his eyes; they widened in disbelief.

"It’s that bloodstained blue boat that once appeared like a fleeting flower in the Vast Expanse—said to be an artifact of the Earth Ancestor—and it has actually fallen into his hands."

The Jade Purity Daoist Master suddenly grew excited, his breathing becoming rapid, as if he too had glimpsed a glimmer of hope.

"Lord of Reincarnation, we implore you to carry us for a while. Once we reach the other shore, we shall surely reward you generously."

The Lord of Taiqing cried out urgently.

However, the heavens and earth were filled with a hazy glow, surging with terrifying auras of destruction, and the gale-force winds of the Great Dao swept through. Had any other being at the End of the Path been in their place, they would have been reduced to ashes long ago.

Their words were instantly drowned out by the boundless gales of the Great Dao.

Nevertheless, the Lord of Reincarnation had also noticed the Three Purities Dao Master.

His tone was wild and excited as he roared with laughter, “You three, I’ll be waiting for you on the other side.”

He could naturally guess what the Three Purities Dao Masters had said, but whether they could reach the other shore was none of his concern.

The three were indeed somewhat clever, but they had not even set foot in the realm of Supreme Shattering.

Even if they were to reach the other shore together, what good would that do him?

They might even conspire against him along the way—why go to all that trouble?

Amid his roaring laughter, the Lord of Reincarnation withdrew his gaze and steered the bloodstained, shattered green boat, racing through the raging waves as if cutting through a storm.

Though the waves were staggering and the aura terrifying, the journey was surprisingly smooth. He bypassed over a thousand steps, heading straight for the end of the Sky-Reaching Path of Light.

"Ascension is imminent; the Vast Expanse of the multiverse is boundless. Farewell..."

Gazing at the end now just a stone’s throw away, the Lord of Reincarnation could not hide the excitement in his eyes; his expression was filled with elation.

"You..."

The Three Purities Dao Master’s face darkened with anger. He hadn’t expected the Lord of Reincarnation to completely ignore them and set off alone, piloting that shattered green boat. Wasn’t this precisely the moment they should be joining forces? What if some unexpected disaster were to occur?

Wouldn’t an extra pair of hands be helpful?

"How infuriating..."

"I hope something goes wrong."

The Sovereign of Taiqing muttered under his breath, then once again focused all his energy on resisting this overwhelming, sky-shaking pressure.

BOOM!!!

However, the very moment his words fell, a terrifying, earth-shattering tremor struck once more.

The entire Sky-Piercing Path seemed to tremble, nearly warping and shattering.

Before their trembling eyes, a massive, deathly pale hand—enough to obscure the heavens—reached out from the end of the Celestial Path.

The hand was boundless, its bony fingers still clinging to shards of shattered cosmos and the remains of unknown creatures—a sight of utter horror.

As the colossal hand lunged forward, tens of thousands of steps began to tremble violently.

The bloodstained, shattered green vessel swayed from side to side like a withered leaf in raging waves, utterly unable to steady itself.

"What is this?"

The Lord of Reincarnation was equally taken aback; the excitement on his face froze instantly. His eyes widened, his pupils trembled violently, and he felt as though everything before him had suddenly gone dark.

His face turned deathly pale as he mustered every ounce of strength, even going so far as to summon the power of the Reincarnation Authority once more to resist.

Yet, as that colossal hand descended, all his efforts proved futile—like foam shattering against cold, hard rock.

The Lord of Reincarnation, having set foot in the True Path, could not even evade the blow. He was struck directly by that colossal hand, his body suddenly cracking open. The essence of life exposed within exploded with a thunderous roar, scattering into wispy strands of dissipating vital energy.

"No..."

The Lord of Reincarnation still retained a sliver of consciousness.

He was filled with terror, never having imagined that at the end of this celestial path, such a terrifying entity would stand in his way.

He gathered all his primordial essence and attempted to pilot the shattered green vessel away.

Yet that colossal hand, spanning the heavens, merely gave a gentle clap. All of space-time and the laws of the universe froze solid, as if the entire cosmos had been reduced to a single point.

Boom!!!

Deep despair and regret lingered in the Lord of Reincarnation’s eyes as his final shred of consciousness faded completely.

The authority of the Cycle had not dissipated; it transformed into the primordial Heavenly Will of the Heavenly Dao and drifted out from the Path of Heavenly Light.

The bloodstained, shattered green vessel, having lost the power to sustain it, saw all its radiance fade once more, blending into the dust. It finally tumbled from the heights of the steps, falling all the way to the feet of the Three Purities Dao Lord.

Their faces were frozen in shock, their bodies trembling; they dared not pick it up, their eyes still filled with deep terror.

Was the Lord of Reincarnation really dead?

As if swatting a fly—yet this was a True Path-level being, of whom there were but a handful across the Vast Expanse.

"Are we still going to continue the ascent…?" The Jade Purity Dao Lord’s voice trembled, his face pale.

Dead silence.

Whether it was the Lord of Taiqing or the Lord of Shangqing, fear was evident in their eyes.

Even if they were to eventually reach the end of the Path of Heavenly Light, their fate would be the same as the Lord of Reincarnation’s—crushed to death by that sky-obscuring giant hand, their bodies and spirits utterly annihilated.

But now, did they even have a choice?

"There is no turning back for us now. We have sacrificed everything, burned everything to ashes..."

"Seekers of the Dao, what does death matter?"

The Sovereign of the Great Purity muttered.

A moment later, his trembling body gradually straightened.

The Jade Purity Dao Master and the Supreme Purity Dao Master also slowly regained their composure. They looked at the shattered green boat lying before them, without a moment’s hesitation.

Clusters of brilliant Daoist true fire erupted from the crowns of their heads, gradually enveloping them. Every inch of their skin shone like Daoist gold, radiating an aura of immortality and indestructibility.

Before them, a shattered path seemed to materialize—hazy and indistinct—with infinite Heavenly Dao sounds echoing along its banks and blossoms of the Golden Court of Heavenly Dao blooming everywhere.

"Supreme Shattering lies right before us..."

The three murmured in unison, stepping forward without hesitation. Their figures gradually merged into one, and the past, future, and present all vanished together.

At that very moment, one after another, the true mansions of the Daoist sects and the blessed lands reaching to the heavens collapsed, and countless disciples and descendants turned into light and vanished into the distance.

……

A deafening, earth-shattering tremor reverberated throughout the cosmos, as if a resplendent golden lotus of the Dao were blooming. A crimson radiance overshadowed the Nine Underworlds and the Azure Heavens, before slowly fading back into silence and returning to stillness.

All traces of energy gradually faded away.

Only a bloodstained, shattered green vessel fell from that celestial path, crashing down beyond the Abyss of Heaven. Its surface bore even more scars, and the blood staining it had grown even darker.

The colossal hand that had reached out to cover the heavens withdrew, settling in silence at the end of the celestial path.

Outside the Celestial Path, figures such as the Lord of Power and the Lord of Restraint fell into a moment of silence.

Although the Three Purities Dao Master did not follow the same path as them, maintaining a stance of neutral observation—and even acting somewhat underhandedly—his obsession with seeking the Dao ahead was no less intense than theirs.

To be precise, all sentient beings across the Vast Expanse share the same obsession with the path ahead.

To hear of the Way in the morning and die in the evening would be enough.

"Amitabha..."

The Tathagata Buddha Lord, too, was at a loss for words regarding the actions of these three acquaintances; in the end, he could only press his palms together and softly chant the Buddha’s name.

The other supreme beings—Chu Ruyongzu, the Yellow Lord, the Arrow Lord, the Lord of Light and Darkness, the Lord of the Five Elements…—also fell silent at that moment.

All sentient beings gazed at that path of light reaching to the heavens, watching as that stark white, sky-spanning hand slowly withdrew.

Beyond lay the so-called Other Shore, but who could climb and reach it?

"One day, this gatekeeper will pay the price..."

Gu Xihan, the First Ancestor of the Gu Clan, withdrew her gaze, her jade-like hands clenched tightly, her eyes burning with hatred.

She would never forget that day—when the Gu family’s distant ancestor had been gravely wounded by that same colossal hand, ultimately sacrificing himself to become the barrier.

"Hahahaha..."

"Lord of Reincarnation, you thought you were so clever, yet you too met a tragic end."

In the deepest recesses of the Ancient Hidden Civilization, before a majestic and imposing palace, the Ancient Hidden Emperor lay sprawled on the ground, coughing up blood incessantly.

Sensing the Lord of Reincarnation’s vanished aura, he let out a bitter, maniacal laugh.

His hair was disheveled, his imperial robes in tatters, and his body covered in profound wounds. Around him, waves of overwhelming energy descended as the Immortal-Forgotten Elder, the Lord of the Bureau, Gu Xihan, and others landed, surrounding him with icy gazes.

"Even if you kill me, what will that change?"

"Nothing will change. A momentary victory is but fleeting; soon, beings will descend upon the Land Of Truth."

“Then, everything will repeat itself. You have no idea what the Land Of Truth is truly like…”

The Ancient Treasure Emperor growled. He was fully aware of his fate, so he offered no pleas for mercy, nor did he harbor any regrets.

For a being like him, his resolve was unshakable; from the very beginning, he had understood exactly what he must do and what the consequences would be.

"Heaven Slaying may be incredibly difficult, but someone must eventually take that step. If we make no changes, what meaning does our existence hold? Even a torch’s flame requires a predecessor to ignite it."

"I have always believed that a single spark can set the prairie ablaze," the Elder of Forgotten Immortals said calmly.

“Hah, a single spark…”

The Ancient Treasure Emperor continued to sneer coldly.

His own immense power surged ceaselessly. Having embarked on the path of the Supreme Shattering, he relied on nothing external whatsoever.

The vital energy surging forth from his desperate, all-out effort was like raging waves, sweeping across past, present, and future—so overwhelmingly powerful it defied belief.

"There’s no point in wasting words with him," Gu Xi said coldly.

She struck. A mighty sword light pierced through heaven and earth, illuminating the entire area. It seemed as though countless versions of her were emerging from the past, present, and future, transforming into terrifying streaks of light that reached from the highest heavens to the deepest underworlds, casting their glow across countless worlds.

The Palace Master said nothing either.

The Elder of Forgotten Immortals let out a soft sigh and struck once more.

This earth-shattering battle erupted in the deepest recesses of the Ancient Hidden Civilization. All the palaces and structures crumbled; all the stars and continents were reduced to nothingness, as if seeking to obliterate the entire cosmos. In the end, the outcome was, of course, inevitable.

The Ancient Treasure Emperor, who had traversed the Vast Expanse of countless eras and participated in innumerable purges, met his end amidst the heavens and earth, dissolving into an endless rain of blood that fell from the sky, his form and spirit utterly annihilated.

The other great royal clans were subjected to the reckoning and were successively annihilated.

From that day forward, the Ancient Hidden Civilization was erased from the annals of all worlds.

Beyond this battlefield, the great battle between Yue Mingkong and the followers of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness also drew to a close.

Relying on the Eight Desolate Demon Halberd and the Six Heavens Abyss, Yue Mingkong combined offense and defense into one, ultimately unleashing his most powerful strike—a terrifying force capable of shaking the very foundations of the Vast Expanse. The followers of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness were utterly annihilated and consigned to eternal oblivion.

Yue Mingkong herself had sustained significant injuries; her primordial essence was severely depleted, and her face was deathly pale.

However, she paid it no mind, her gaze shifting instead to the distant, boundless horizon of the heavens.

The battle between the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness and the Lord of Heaven—Evil Thought—was still raging, but neither of them could intervene.

"What are the casualties among the Heaven Slaying Alliance?"

Without wasting a moment, Yue Mingkong descended from the farthest reaches of the heavens, arriving deep within the already-destroyed Tongzang Civilization.

Numerous high-ranking officials and powerful figures from the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Palace of Defiance were busy clearing the battlefield. The Ancient Hidden Civilization possessed profound foundations, accumulated over countless ages, making it impossible to fully assess the damage in such a short time.

Factions from other True Realms and the Super Multiverse were also clearing the battlefield.

"Reporting to the Mistress..."

Although Zhuo Fengxie had sustained severe injuries, they were not life-threatening. Having recovered considerably, he now joined many other high-ranking officials in approaching Yue Mingkong to report on the situation.

They all held great admiration for Yue Mingkong, who had earned a formidable reputation in this battle.

Even figures like the Lord of Power and the Lord of Restraint were no exception; it could be said that Yue Mingkong was now the strongest combatant within the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Had she not annihilated the retinue of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness, this battle would not have concluded so easily.

"The Demon Ancestor Dihuang is dead, Lao Hun is dead, Chu Heng is nothing but a wisp of a soul, and the White Bone Ancestral King’s presence can’t even be detected anymore. And so many others we knew…"

"The Alliance Master’s recruits—Mu Fuguang and the others—have also vanished without a trace; they likely perished on the battlefield, along with Emperor Wei Yang and the others..."

Zhe Fengxie was deeply grieved; these were all faces he once knew well.

During this battle, they had fought side by side; even in the face of the Ancient Hidden Civilization’s temptations, they had never ultimately abandoned the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

As for those who had left the Heaven Slaying Alliance and met a tragic end, he made no mention of them.

"Our ancestor’s aura has also vanished."

Yan Ji’s eyes reddened as she approached Yue Mingkong, her voice low and heavy.

The Fire God Zhu Rong was the first to arrive at the front lines to lend his aid. He was subjected to a joint assault by the Lord of Calamity and others. As he was still in the Origin Traversing stage of the End of the Path, he was no match for the Lord of Calamity and his allies.

Later, the Lord of Power and others held Li Zhu back, leaving him no time to come to their aid.

Yue Mingkong’s expression grew grave. He sighed deeply and said, “Their deaths will not be in vain. I trust Changge; he would never let anyone die in vain.”

Yan Ji nodded. As one of the earliest to follow Gu Changge, she knew his capabilities well, so she still harbored some hope.

“Casualties are inevitable in any great battle; there’s no need for you all to be so saddened.”

“I’ve noticed that the Ancient Altar of Reckoning, though it absorbs the primordial essence of the souls who perish on the battlefield, has not been fully assimilated into it. It seems to have been blocked by an unknown force…”

“With the Alliance Leader’s abilities, as long as even a single thread of their essence remains, they should be able to return.”

"Don’t forget, the Alliance Leader is capable of creating something from nothing."

The Lord of Power walked over, gave a slight bow to Yue Mingkong, and then explained.

"Changge must have other plans."

Yue Mingkong nodded slightly, agreeing with Lord of Power’s conjecture.
