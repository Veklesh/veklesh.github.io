Ten days passed in the blink of an eye.

The day of the Immortal Dao Alliance’s founding arrived, as expected, without much surprise.

On Piao Miao Peak, divine light intertwined and misty clouds surged. The ancient beings of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm had long since established a domain here, creating a self-contained universe within it, much like a miniature world.

Across the heavens and earth, the sounds of the Dao converged, causing the void to rumble and tremble.

One after another, flying vessels and jade ships sliced through space, descending from all directions. The pavilions and palaces were filled with dignitaries from every corner of the realm who had come to witness the ceremony.

It could be said that every major sect and faction capable of making the journey had sent representatives to the Vast Expanse.

Within the domain of Piao Miao Peak stood a majestic, ancient hall. On the vast and expansive plaza, immortal mist swirled like clouds, ethereal and hazy. Surrounding it stood divine mountains and ancient peaks, with rivers, lakes, and breathtaking scenery.

Now, the peaks of this Dao Realm are teeming with figures. Representative figures from civilizations across the True Realms have been dispatched, and there are even a significant number of beings at the Ancestral Dao Realm—though their true forms remain indistinct, suspended in the void, as if they did not truly exist within this realm of time and space.

The four great families of the Ethereal Holy City were responsible for receiving and guiding guests and dignitaries to their respective destinations.

"The Deputy Leader of the Righteous Unity Alliance has arrived; he is currently seated at the head of the table, accompanied by what appear to be two figures at the Ancestral Dao Realm."

"But at this hour, no one from the Heaven Slaying Alliance has arrived yet. Could it be, as speculated, that they truly intend to prevent the formation of the Immortal Dao Alliance?"

"Given the prevailing trend, the Heaven Slaying Alliance shouldn’t go that far."

"Earlier, some factions noticed signs of activity from the Heaven Slaying Alliance. They must have dispatched a high-ranking official as well, who should be on the way. The only question is which one it will be. As for that Deputy Alliance Leader Ling Yuling—though she was exiled to the Boundless Void—she has apparently been seen moving about within the Heaven Slaying Alliance recently. It seems she was rescued by the Alliance Leader..."

Many places and many people were watching closely; factions and lineages from all sides were eager to know the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s final stance.

Over the past few days, the Black Lotus Sect incident has thrown the entire Ethereal Sanctuary into turmoil. Major clans have dispatched numerous powerful figures to purge their strongholds and shrines, yet these efforts have yielded little result.

The Black Lotus Sect’s followers, like a spreading plague, rapidly swept across all directions, proving virtually impossible to contain.

In the end, the Piao Miao Holy Sect could only issue a temporary order to strictly control access to the major cities within the Piao Miao Holy Realm. They planned to address the situation gradually after the Immortal Dao Alliance’s ceremonial assembly concluded.

However, this approach only allowed the Black Lotus Sect to spread even more terrifyingly, like ignited willow catkins and dry grass, spreading to nearly every corner of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm.

In just a few short months, not a single spot in the Piao Miao Sacred Realm remained untouched.

The eerie and terrifying nature of the Black Lotus Sect has sent shivers down the spines of countless cultivators and living beings.

While they were still discussing how to counter the Dark Tribes and resolve the calamity of the Black Plague, the Black Lotus Sect suddenly emerged—a development that carried a distinct air of retaliation from the Dark Tribes.

Even many of the clans and factions that had rushed to attend the Immortal Dao Alliance’s ceremonial assembly were tormented beyond endurance by this suddenly emerging Black Lotus Sect.

For cultivators at the Dao Realm level, with their single-minded focus on the Dao Fruit and unshakable mental fortitude, the Black Lotus Sect’s doctrines hold no sway over them.

But for the younger generation and those in their prime who have not yet reached this level, it is a terrifying calamity.

Cult factions from all quarters are devising ways to contain this sudden Black Lotus calamity, and cultivators and sentient beings across the land are investigating the origins of the Black Lotus Sect.

There are only three days left until the start of the Immortal Dao Alliance Assembly.

In a void of spacetime, accompanied by a rumbling roar, a black portal emerged. It gradually expanded, forming a tunnel piercing through space and time.

One ancient warship after another crushed the ripples of spacetime as they descended from within. The ancient, austere banners of the Heaven Slaying Alliance fluttered in the wind, exuding an air of timeless grandeur.

Upon the warships, pavilions, towers, divine islands, and celestial mountains floated, while cultivators and sentient beings moved about, creating a scene as vibrant and lifelike as a pure land.

"Master, have you heard? Lately, the Black Lotus Sect has grown increasingly rampant. They’ve sprung up all over the Vast Expanse like bamboo shoots after rain—they’re truly becoming too brazen..."

On an ancient warship, within a secluded courtyard.

Gu Changge, dressed in simple white robes with his jet-black hair flowing freely, sat sipping tea in the pavilion, looking utterly at ease.

Before him stood Yao Yao, dressed in a long red gown and radiating exquisite beauty, speaking with a look of indignation on her face. Her jade-like features were filled with seething anger.

If one were to ask what has drawn the most attention in the Vast Expanse of Cangmang these past few days, it is undoubtedly the Black Lotus Sect, which has suddenly emerged.

Its momentum was so fierce, it was like a wildfire spreading across the land, madly spreading and devouring everything in its path.

Yao Yao had stumbled upon the Black Lotus Sect’s sacrificial ritual several months ago.

In fact, she had even taken a Holy Child from the Black Lotus Sect with her, prompting a relentless pursuit by the cult.

To this day, that sacred child remains within the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

But at the time, Gu Changge hadn’t paid it much mind, merely saying he would send people to investigate. As for what the investigation uncovered, Yao Yao knew nothing.

She fretted for a while, worried that the Black Lotus Sect was deliberately targeting the Heaven Slaying Alliance. To that end, she even went so far as to probe the sacred child’s memories and experiences, but unfortunately, she found nothing.

In the end, these matters could only be set aside for the time being.

But in the past few days, all sorts of news about the Black Lotus Sect has begun spreading like wildfire across the various territories.

Even many figures within the Heaven Slaying Alliance were discussing the matter, eager to discover the true origins of this suddenly emerged Black Lotus Sect.

The Black Lotus Sect undoubtedly has the most direct connection to the Dark Races, as it preaches the doctrine of saving all beings and assimilating the darkness.

Followers of the Black Lotus Sect are immune to the onslaught of dark matter.

Moreover, due to their unwavering faith, some followers are bestowed with so-called “holy blood,” granting them powers as formidable as those of dark beings.

Yao Yao has personally witnessed the Black Lotus Sect’s rituals and knows full well that this “holy blood” comes from the sect’s so-called “holy children.”

The blood of these "holy children" contains sacred power; once consumed, it allows ordinary beings to withstand the corrosive effects of dark matter, and enables some practitioners to temporarily transform into dark beings, possessing power comparable to theirs.

As for how these "Sacred Children" actually came to be, she wasn’t entirely sure, but she speculated that perhaps, due to their unique physical constitution, they served as vessels for some kind of substance?

"The Black Lotus Cult has indeed been running rampant these past few days."

Gu Changge sipped her tea leisurely, appearing unconcerned. “But they won’t be able to run wild for much longer.”

“Master, aren’t you worried that the Black Lotus Sect might become entangled with the Heaven Slaying Alliance, and that this might lead the world to suspect you—to believe you have ties to the Dark Clans?” Yao Yao lifted her face, her expression filled with concern.

Even someone as close to Gu Changge as she was—someone who had known and understood him for so long—had been stunned for a moment upon witnessing the Black Lotus Sect’s sacrificial ritual, nearly mistaking the projection of the Black Lotus Lord for Gu Changge himself.

If the other high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance were to witness this scene, who knows what they would think?

Would Master then be accused of colluding with the Dark Clans, making enemies of the entire world?

"If they suspect me, they’ll have to produce evidence, won’t they? Even you’re only saying that the Lord of the Black Lotus bears a strong resemblance to me. Since it’s merely a resemblance, what is there to worry about?"

“If I really were the Lord of the Black Lotus, why wouldn’t I change my appearance instead of revealing my true face?” Gu Changge said with a faint smile.

Yao Yao faltered, suddenly at a loss for words.

“Master, am I just too slow…” she sighed softly.

“It’s only natural to lose one’s composure when worried,” Gu Changge said with a smile.

Standing behind him, Yao Yao couldn’t help but ask, “Master, since you already know the Black Lotus Sect has sinister intentions and wants to frame you, why haven’t you dealt with them directly?”

At a time like this, the Black Lotus Sect’s emergence was bound to make some people suspicious.

Even she had heard that many regions were growing increasingly chaotic because of the Black Lotus Sect; numerous tribal factions had begun dispatching forces to conduct thorough investigations and demolish many of the sect’s shrines and temples.

Yet the Black Lotus Sect’s infiltration remains relentless, so much so that many clans and factions have begun to suspect this is yet another tactic of the Dark Clans.

But why, of all things, did the blurry silhouette of the Black Lotus Lord at that time bear such a striking resemblance to Gu Changge? Wasn’t this deliberately shifting the blame onto him?

Of course, very few people had actually seen Gu Changge’s true appearance—only a handful of high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Yao Yao, however, had been by Gu Changge’s side back when they were still in the Lower Realm, so she was intimately familiar with him.

That was why, when she stumbled upon that sacrificial ritual and caught a fleeting glimpse of the Master of the Black Lotus descending, she mistook him for Gu Changge.

"Master, you know this very well yourself, yet you don’t seem to care at all. What if someone uses this as an excuse to come after you?" Yao Yao felt like she was worrying more than the emperor himself.

"Now is not the time to take action against the Black Lotus Sect."

Gu Changge shook his head slightly; that was the only way to put it. He understood Yao Yao’s concerns all too well.

But this was not something he could explain to her.

After all, the emergence of the Black Lotus Sect was, in itself, a test set by certain parties to gauge him.

Now that the Black Lotus Sect has suddenly appeared across the Vast Expanse of Cangmang and brazenly linked itself to the Dark Clans, it was exactly what he had anticipated.

When Yao Yao had rushed back some time ago to inform him of this, Gu Changge had chosen not to act—he had already anticipated what would happen next.

In reality, the Black Lotus Sect has absolutely no connection to the Dark Clans.

Now that Wuyue rules over the Heavenly Host, without her permission, the Dark Clans could never have concocted something like the Black Lotus Sect.

And the very source of the Black Lotus Sect’s power is, in fact, a portion of Gu Changge’s own strength. So it comes as no surprise that the so-called “Lord of the Black Lotus,” who materialized as a projection, bears such a strong resemblance to him.

For the time being, Gu Changge has no intention of getting involved in the Black Lotus Sect’s affairs.

After all, no matter how large the Black Lotus Sect grows, it ultimately stems from his own power. He can reclaim it at any time and control it at will—so why bother?

The Black Lotus Sect itself is merely a bait cast out by an entity like the progenitor Xu Na to test him.

Only the mastermind behind the Black Lotus Sect might still delude themselves into thinking they can use this period of chaos and turmoil to recruit a large number of followers and accumulate the power needed for their own transformation and advancement.

Little do they know that this will only serve to play into Gu Changge’s hands, reducing them to mere pawns in someone else’s game.

Yao Yao could only half-understand what Gu Changge was saying.

"It’s about time; we should be arriving at the Piao Miao Sacred Realm soon."

Gu Changge raised his gaze and looked into the distance.

Based on the timeline he had set for Jing Tianyuan’s journey to the Nightmare Demon Realm, Jing Tianyuan should have arrived there by now.

Based on his understanding of the Ancestor Xu, since the Black Lotus Sect—that bait—had failed to lure him out, the Ancestor Xu would choose another method to test him; he would never actually intervene personally.

That guy is extremely cautious.

Now that he had sent Jing Tianyuan to the Nightmare Demon Realm, he had given Wuyue a clear enough warning.

If the Master of Destiny believes the Ancestor Xu’s deal and actually takes action against the Nightmare Realm, then in this game of strategy, he likely won’t even have the chance to face the consequences—he’ll crumble and collapse in an instant.

Gu Changge has always been well aware of Shizu Xu’s intentions.

Otherwise, he wouldn’t have bluntly revealed his true identity to him after encountering that old Taoist from the Heaven’s Decline Sect…

From that moment on, he had been setting a trap for Shizu Xu.

And Shizu Xu had also guessed that he was doing this to set a trap, but given his nature, he would also suspect whether this was actually Gu Changge’s deliberate ploy to sow doubt.

Since Gu Changge had not yet fully recovered to his peak, he acted so “recklessly” as to reveal his presence to the Heaven’s Decline Clan early on, thereby causing them to misunderstand and fear him, believing he was acting with impunity and brimming with confidence.

But in reality, since Gu Changge might not have fully recovered, he had no choice but to set this deceptive trap, feigning complete indifference.

The intricate calculations at play here are actually layered upon one another—one layer isn’t enough, so they add another… and another…

None of those ancient figures who have been in hiding for so long are truly foolish; they are all highly cunning.

The Primordial Ancestor Xu and the Master of Destiny had long been in contact and collaborating—a fact Gu Changge had noticed when he delved into the Master of Destiny’s past.

This founding assembly of the Immortal Dao Alliance seemed to draw the attention of the Vast Expanse and the various clans and lineages.

But only those who truly understand the reality and perceive the grand trends of heaven and earth know that this is merely a superficial pretext…

Their true focus lies on the Master of Destiny, who has emerged prematurely; the Ancestor Xu, who controls the Heaven’s Decline Clan and has long accumulated the essence and fate of countless eras while lying dormant; and Gu Changge, the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

The three most powerful factions on the Vast Expanse of the present age are none other than the Heaven Slaying Alliance, the Righteous Unity Alliance, and the Dark Clans that have reemerged into the world.

The Dark Clans are ruled by the Heavenly Host, but within the Heavenly Host, the Primordial Void has long since infiltrated their ranks.

Gu Changge had already realized this when he sensed the faint traces of his power scattered across the Vast Expanse of Cangmang.

After all, those were fragments of his flesh and blood from his time as the Ruler of Heaven, imbued with the power he once possessed.

His physical body, however, had lain dormant within the coffin of the World-Burying Coffin since the end of the Age of Black Calamity, yet now a small portion of his power had been dispersed across the Vast Expanse of the world.

This was sufficient proof that a turbulent struggle to seize his power had once erupted within the ranks of the Heavenly Host.

"Alliance Leader, we are approaching the territory of the Ethereal Sanctuary..."

"Shall we proceed directly there?"

Outside the courtyard, the Skeleton Ancestral King and his companions approached to report.

Beside the Skeletal Ancestral King stood his disciple, Mo Tong. With gray hair and eyes, and a figure as fair and delicate as jade, he seemed carved from ice and snow, exuding an icy aura that warned all to keep their distance.

Within the Heaven Slaying Alliance, although figures like Zhuo Fengwei and Hun Yuanjun were far weaker than the Ancestral Dao Realm beings who had later sworn allegiance,

However, because they had been among the first to follow Gu Changge, they held significant seniority, so even the Ancestral Dao Realm beings treated them with great courtesy.

For this journey to the Piao Miao Sacred Realm, Gu Changge had the White Bone Ancestral King accompany him, entrusting him with the management of various affairs.

At first, the White Bone Ancestral King was merely a “madman” wandering the Vast Expanse, belonging to no civilizational lineage and drifting aimlessly with the currents.

However, after he decided to follow and pledge allegiance to Gu Changge to establish the Heaven Slaying Alliance, it was as if he had been reborn; he took a keen interest in the affairs of the Alliance.

In fact, there are quite a few beings like the Bone Ancestor King in the Heaven Slaying Alliance today.

Their former homelands were shattered and scattered during the Great Cataclysm, since then sinking into the silence of the river of time. Only they remained, clinging to life, drifting and slumbering in the Vast Expanse, accompanied only by solitude.

The emergence of the Heaven Slaying Alliance gave them a purpose—whether it be revenge or simply a reason to exist.

"Just go straight there; don’t worry about anything else," Gu Changge’s calm voice rang out.

Ling Yuling, Chu Heng, and the others, following his earlier orders, gathered their forces and set off toward the borderlands of the Righteous Unity Alliance.

By making a personal appearance at the Immortal Dao Alliance’s ceremonial gathering, he had certainly shown them the utmost “respect.”

And now, a grand spectacle was about to unfold.

If those big fish still chose to hide and evade, Gu Changge wouldn’t have the patience to play along with them any longer.

However, that didn’t really matter anymore. Since Gu Changge had decided to “step into the fray” personally, it meant that if no one else made a move in this chess game, he would either choose to end the game—or… simply drain the water from the pond.

If it weren’t for the fierce fighting raging on the side of the Divine Path Civilization, Gu Changge might even have considered meddling once more to present the Immortal Dao Alliance’s ceremonial gathering with a truly unforgettable “gift.”
