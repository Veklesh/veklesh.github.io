The Land Of Truth is also known in the Vast Expanse as the Source World, the Place of Origin.

Its vastness is beyond measure, boundless and immeasurable; its boundaries are simply unimaginable.

Since the Land Of Truth came into being, it has undergone a total of thirteen Chaos Cosmos Eons.

Each era of the chaotic cosmos spans four hundred and eighty million Great Calamities—a duration of over three hundred million trillion years.

During this endless span of time, it can be said that virtually no living beings from the Vast Expanse have ever set foot here.

Furthermore, since the Land Of Truth knows no concept of reincarnation and lacks corresponding cosmic laws, the number of sentient beings remains constant. Compared to the Vast Expanse, their numbers are so scarce they can only be described as "as rare as a speck of dust."

Consequently, although countless eons have passed since its creation, many regions of the Land Of Truth still retain the primordial wonders of the very first creation.

These regions are ethereal and mysterious, unpredictable and eerie.

Even the members of the Nine True Clans rarely venture into these regions, doing their utmost to avoid them.

In the Eastern Sea lies the Void Abyss.

A solitary, boundless abyss, as if completely severing this terrifying sea, shaped like a colossal gaping maw of heaven and earth, devouring all three realms.

On either side, the surging, turbulent waters churned up colossal waves that plunged invisibly downward, falling straight into the abyss without a single echo—as if it possessed infinite depth.

Here, any living creature would be swept in, with no bottom in sight.

This stretch of sea is perpetually shrouded in a mysterious fog; even the followers of the Heavenly Seal Realm dare not approach it.

Rumor has it that this mist is the murky fog extracted from the boundless pure qi by the Three True Ancestors when they first established the Land Of Truth.

Any living being that comes into contact with this mist will be eroded, losing the protection of the Land Of Truth, and will thus be assimilated by the heavens and earth, becoming nourishment for the Land Of Truth.

The Land Of Truth has always been expanding—a fact well-known to all living beings within it.

Upon death, living beings transform into pure energy, nourishing the heavens and the earth.

In response, the Earth Mother, moved by this, scatters a thread of the source of life throughout the heavens and earth, much like the dispersal of dandelion seeds.

Therefore, many tribes have built ancestral tombs and mounds. Whenever a being within the tribe passes away, the descendants born of marriage and procreation will come before the ancestral mound to welcome that thread of the source of life, awaiting the arrival of new life—with a high probability of guiding the true spirit of the deceased ancestor to them.

Thus, the cycle repeats endlessly, sustaining the eternal balance of the cosmos.

On ordinary days, no living being would willingly approach the Void Abyss, for it is said to be a gateway connecting the Land Of Truth to the Vast Expanse.

Only during the grand rituals do creatures from various tribes gather nearby to observe and pay their respects.

However, this particular Eternal Grand Sacrifice has not gone smoothly, having already drawn the attention of the major True Clans.

Near the Abyss of Void-Union, the ocean’s waves roared and surged, ceaselessly churning up colossal tidal waves tens of millions of feet high.

The majestic and grand Ancient Altar of Reckoning, a spectral image, stood towering high above the Abyss of Unity, projected from distant realms, weighing down upon the entire world.

The ancient peaks surrounding it, though towering to the heavens, appeared insignificant in the presence of the ancient altar of reckoning, as if mere specks of dust.

Countless beings appeared here, radiating solemnity and reverence, prostrating themselves in prayer. It seemed as though nothing in the world could distract them from this moment.

“How could the Heavenly Lord Absolute Yin be covered in wounds, with even his arm severed, and his face so pale…”

“Even his retinue has vanished. Could it be that something went wrong during this grand ritual, causing Lord Jueyin, the Heavenly Lord, to suffer a severe setback?”

"The Heavenly Lord of the Absolute Darkness was summoned by a Heavenly Seal bestowed upon him; he is boundless and majestic. How could he have ended up in such a state?"

"In past Eternity Grand Sacrifices, not even a single Heavenly Being needed to be present to preside over everything."

"How could this have happened..."

Whispers of murmuring rose from the surrounding majestic, towering peaks.

These peaks were shrouded in a chaotic, primordial hue, standing tall amidst the sea. They withstood the pounding of the waves, capable of enduring forces capable of shattering the heavens and obliterating the earth, yet remained steadfast. A faint, supreme aura permeated their slopes.

These beings resembled humans, bore the traits of ancient races, or had lost their physical forms entirely—some appearing as mere shards of hazy light, others taking the shape of weapons or artifacts, floating in the air.

Though the Land Of Truth boasts a history that could be called endless, it is fundamentally no different from the Vast Expanse beyond—its hierarchical structure is strikingly distinct.

The Nine True Clans rule over all, supreme and unmatched.

Beyond them, those who wield the Heavenly Seal Talismans and have attained the Heavenly Seal Realm are also peerless titans, ruling over vast territories and countless subjects.

However, due to the unbreakable barriers of the Land Of Truth’s laws, even those in the Heavenly Seal Realm find it difficult to arbitrarily distort and alter the laws of heaven and earth as they might in the Vast Expanse, acting with utter lawlessness.

At this moment, the majority of the beings murmuring in the surrounding area possessed a Dao Realm-level aura, and even within that tier, their ranks were not particularly high.

Of course, those capable of reaching the Hexu Abyss are, in their own right, formidable figures within the Land Of Truth, each commanding vast territories.

In terms of the difficulty of cultivating to the Dao Realm, the Land Of Truth is actually not much different from the Vast Expanse.

However, due to the absence of the Heavenly Dao, beings in the Land Of Truth do not need to undergo the trials of the Heavenly Tribulation.

Even Dao Realm beings need not worry about the Celestial Waning Tribulation; as long as their cultivation and magical power are sufficiently accumulated, they can naturally and smoothly break through to the Dao Realm.

Overall, even though the population of the Land Of Truth is sparse, the number of beings in the Dao Realm on this side far exceeds that of the Vast Expanse.

The Heavenly Seal Realm, however, remains a significant hurdle.

After all, the entire Land Of Truth possesses only thirteen Heavenly Seal Symbols. One must either vie for a Heavenly Seal Symbol or become a follower of a being who possesses one; otherwise, one is doomed to remain stuck at the ninth layer of the Dao Realm, eventually exhausting one’s lifespan and dying of old age.

By the banks of the Void-Merging Abyss.

On the grand platform presiding over the grand ritual, the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin sat cross-legged with his eyes closed. Above his head, the Heavenly Seal Summoning had transformed into a swirling mass of light, from which threads of patterns surpassing the True Path tier drifted down, enveloping his entire form.

He appeared severely injured: his robes were tattered, his hair disheveled, his face pale, and even the massive trunk of his Ma’eryi had been severed.

A mysterious, unfathomable domain radiated outward from him, spreading like a giant cocoon and completely isolating him from the surrounding world.

It also blocked the intense, burning gazes of many beings who sought to peer within.

The struggles in the Land Of Truth were no less intense than those in the Vast Expanse.

Moreover, precisely because only thirteen Heavenly Seal Charms had been summoned, the conflict was, in some respects, even more intense.

The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness had no shortage of enemies here, so even as he presided over the Eternal Grand Sacrifice, he dared not let his guard down.

It felt like a mere instant, yet also as if millions of years had passed.

The Heavenly Seal Summoning Symbol transformed into a ball of light and sank into his body.

Only then did the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness slowly open his eyes. His gaze was somber; he said nothing, merely remaining seated in the lotus position.

“I sacrificed the magical power of an entire Chaos Heaven and Earth Era, and I nearly didn’t make it out alive…”

“I thought it would be just an ordinary Eternal Grand Sacrifice, yet the losses were so severe—all the followers I brought with me perished on that side.”

He seethed inwardly.

Having lost the magical power of an entire Chaos Heaven and Earth Era, he could no longer even hold onto his position as a Heavenly Lord; he was now little more than a Heavenly Being.

"Fortunately, I still have my followers. By absorbing their magical power and cultivation, I might be able to recover significantly and reclaim my position as a Heavenly Lord."

"The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin took a deep breath.

His followers were, after all, his servants; their lives and deaths were in his hands.

To serve as nourishment for his recovery of magical power and cultivation was, in a sense, an honor for them.

"Master..."

"Ancestor..."

Beyond the Void Abyss, numerous terrifying figures, powerful and surging, swiftly swept through the air, descending toward the platform.

Ripples spread out in all directions, revealing within them the wondrous scenes of realms being born and destroyed, and the unfolding of myriad spirits. This stretch of sea was clearly no ordinary place; even the seawater itself contained profound and mysterious truths.

The myriad creatures on the surrounding peaks hurriedly retreated and dodged, fearing they might obstruct their path, leaving no trace behind.

The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness looked on, his expression darkening. He had already guessed what had happened.

Not only had this ancient grand ritual failed to be successfully performed, but it had even led to the destruction of the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

Even the ancient sacrificial altar had been awakened. Given the immense cost, he could not escape impeachment and punishment by the Council of Truth.

The Heavenly Seal of Sealing the Heavens, summoned by the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness, had been bestowed upon him by “Xuan,” one of the Three True Ancestors.

It could be said that he was the first ruler; aside from the Council of Truth, no one dared to challenge the authority of this Sealing Heaven Talisman.

To vie for it would be to defy and rebel against "Xuan’s" will—an unthinkable act throughout the entire Land Of Truth.

To defy a True Ancestor is tantamount to violating the heavenly laws and precepts—a crime for which there is no atonement, even at the cost of ten thousand deaths.

However, once the Truth Council has reached a unanimous decision through deliberation and ratified it via the Holy Book of Truth, the Nine True Clans have the authority to strip him of the Heavenly Seal Summoning Talisman and bestow it upon other beings.
