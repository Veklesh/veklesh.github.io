Ling Yuling took the lead and stepped inside. Immediately, a powerful force surged forth, sweeping Zhu Xuan and the others along with her, and they quickly vanished into the void.

Zhu Xuan wasn’t sure if it was just his imagination, but he couldn’t shake the feeling that, compared to before, Ali and her snow-white Zhenkong mount seemed unusually tense at that moment.

Soon, the spatial passage before them blurred. A flash of light dazzled their eyes, and when they reappeared, they found themselves on a sunny, flower-filled plain where birds sang and the air was fragrant.

Towering mountains rose all around, ancient trees stood majestic, orchids and lilies flourished in lush greenery, and the air shimmered with rosy light.

In the distance, emerald-green lakes dotted the landscape. A few majestic deer lowered their heads to drink from the water’s edge, occasionally lifting their heads to glance at them without a trace of fear. The grass was lush and green, and a gentle breeze blew, creating a scene of natural harmony.

From afar came the faint scent of peach blossoms, and a splash of brilliant red caught everyone’s eye—it was a peach orchard stretching for miles, its blossoms in full, each one dazzling and enchanting, with fallen petals drifting through the air.

"The Alliance Leader lives in seclusion right here."

Ling Yuling’s voice snapped everyone back to reality. If they hadn’t seen it with their own eyes, who would have dared to believe that the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance actually lived in seclusion in such a serene, idyllic paradise?

Passing through that peach blossom paradise, a cluster of quiet, rustic courtyards came into view. Tall bamboo, majestic pines, thousands of ancient cypresses, and countless stalks of slender bamboo created a serene and delightful scene.

In the courtyard, a young man sat cross-legged, leaning back in a bamboo chair. He appeared to be brewing tea, his chin resting on his hand. Dressed in white robes and white socks, his jet-black hair cascading loosely, he looked rather carefree and lazy.

“You’re here?”

Gu Changge seemed to have heard the sound; he lifted his gaze to glance outside the courtyard, a gentle smile curving his lips.

“I’ve brought them all.”

Ling Yuling smiled faintly, then stepped forward first.

Zhu Xuan and the others, however, stood rooted to the spot in astonishment and bewilderment, seemingly unable to reconcile the scene before them—which looked like a painting—with the image they had imagined of the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

In their eyes, the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance should have been imposing and towering, commanding respect without raising a voice, rarely smiling, with every word and action exuding the vast, overwhelming authority of Mountains And Seas.

Yet the man in white before them seemed far too young and strikingly handsome—more like an immortal who had stepped out of a painting, with a grace as pure as jade and a gentleness as soothing as water.

Had they encountered him elsewhere, they might well have taken him for a noble young gentleman.

“You’ve arrived just in time; the tea has just come to a boil.” Gu Changge smiled faintly, his gaze sweeping over Zhu Xuan and the others.

Zhu Xuan and the others stood outside the courtyard, shifting uneasily. Although Gu Changge’s gaze seemed to merely brush past them, they felt as though his eyes were fixed on them at all times.

This made them exceptionally nervous.

Although the appearance and demeanor of this leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance had taken them by surprise, he was, after all, a figure who truly held that position—one who had shattered the dark spirit’s channel of connection with a single palm strike, his strength unfathomable.

How could they possibly fathom such power?

“Come in. There’s no need to stand outside the courtyard. Yu Ling has already briefed me in detail on matters concerning the Floating Continent.”

Gu Changge rose from the bamboo chair, sat up straight, and then waved his hand, beckoning Zhu Xuan and the others to approach.

His face bore a smile as refreshing as a spring breeze, causing the lingering tension in Zhu Xuan and the others’ hearts to vanish almost instantly.

At that moment, Ling Yuling also came forward at just the right time, bringing several teacups and pouring tea for Zhu Xuan and the others.

Representatives from the six major families of the Floating Continent had accompanied Zhu Xuan and his party. They now stood outside the courtyard, waiting with evident reserve, not daring to step inside.

“Greetings, Alliance Leader.”

Zhu Xuan stepped forward first, took a deep breath to steady himself and avoid making a fool of himself, then approached Gu Changge and bowed respectfully.

Behind him, Ali and the others followed in, bowing in turn before standing to one side with their heads bowed and hands clasped, waiting.

Their primary reason for coming today was to plead with Gu Changge to save Zhu Xuan’s grandfather; for this, they were willing to pay any price.

The people of the Floating Continent all harbored hope. Old Zhu had guarded the Hall of the Holy Ancestor all his life, and even in his final moments, he continued to fend off the onslaught of dark spirits.

They did not want to watch him be eroded by the dark substance and transformed into a dark entity.

“I’ve already heard about your situation from Yu Ling. I can indeed help your grandfather rid himself of the dark matter corroding him…”

Gu Changge took the tea from Ling Yuling’s hands, took a leisurely sip, and then turned to look at Zhu Xuan.

Upon hearing this, Zhu Xuan’s face lit up with joy, and the people on the floating continent around them also revealed expressions of excitement and delight.

Beneath his conical hat, A Li’s gaze was fixed intently on Gu Changge’s face, as if he were trying to see right through him.

Yet, his perception—which had always been infallible—now felt as though it had been blocked and vanished.

Whether she kept her eyes wide open or closed them, it felt as though the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance before her simply did not exist.

All that remained was a formless void, a boundless abyss of darkness, an endless, bottomless ocean.

This sensation caused a cold sweat to break out on her back, and her limbs grew gradually colder.

She even sensed that the Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s gaze had been fixed on her all along, scrutinizing her constantly, laying bare every truth and falsehood about her.

"As long as you, Lord Alliance Leader, are willing to save my grandfather, I am willing to pay any price."

Zhu Xuan’s voice was filled with urgency as he spoke, interrupting the chaotic whirl of thoughts swirling in Ali’s mind.

She raised her eyes to look at Zhu Xuan. Just as he was about to kneel, an invisible force suddenly appeared, holding him up and preventing him from falling to his knees.

“Saving your grandfather is no trouble, and I don’t need you to pay any price. After all, Yu Ling has already come to me with her plea—how could I possibly refuse?” Gu Changge smiled faintly and waved his hand, signaling Zhu Xuan to stand up.

Upon hearing this, Zhu Xuan and the others were momentarily taken aback, then looked at Ling Yuling with immense gratitude.

They could all see that Senior Ling Yuling and the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance shared an enigmatic relationship; otherwise, how could she come and go from this place so freely and meet the Heaven Slaying Alliance Leader at any time?

A faint blush rose to Ling Yuling’s cheeks. She glanced imperceptibly at Gu Changge and said, “The Alliance Leader is kind-hearted and compassionate. Even if I hadn’t asked him, he would have helped you all anyway.” She had never actually asked Gu Changge to intervene on behalf of Zhu Xuan’s grandfather.

But why did he say that now? Was he trying to make Zhu Xuan and the others grateful to her, or did he have some other motive?

Gu Changge smiled, simply sipping his tea without saying anything further.

Seeing this, everyone from the Floating Continent breathed a collective sigh of relief, their faces lighting up with joy and excitement as they expressed their deepest gratitude to both Gu Changge and Ling Yuling.

Zhu Xuan’s heart was also filled with gratitude, joy, and excitement. Before coming here, he had already resolved to pay any price necessary, but he never expected things to go so smoothly—it was completely beyond his expectations.

Whoosh!

Immediately, a faint glow shot out as Zhu Xuan released his grandfather, Old Zhu—who lay unconscious and shrouded in black mist—from the Sumeru Space.

Ever since he had been corrupted by the dark substance that day, his grandfather had fallen into this semi-comatose state; now, only the Sea of Consciousness within his brow remained untouched by the corruption.

"The method used to stabilize his Sea of Consciousness was quite effective; it bought him at least half a year’s time." Gu Changge glanced at Old Zhu’s current condition and nodded approvingly.

Hearing this, sorrow and grief involuntarily flashed across Zhu Xuan and the others’ faces, and their thoughts turned to Qing Xianzi, who had selflessly sacrificed her life to save them.

The moment Gu Changge finished speaking, he extended a finger and tapped Old Zhu’s brow. Instantly, a tiny black hole formed there.

A terrifying suction force emanated, as if space and time were about to collapse. Wispy strands of black mist swirled and twisted, spreading like a thick fog from every pore of Old Master Zhu’s body, then rapidly converging toward that tiny black hole, where they were continuously drawn away.

"What?"

Everyone was stunned and horrified by this unfathomable scene, their eyes wide with shock.

The dark matter that all beings feared and fled from offered no resistance whatsoever against the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance; it was drawn away as effortlessly as ordinary mist.

"Back on the Floating Continent, Qing Xianzi once mentioned that the Holy Ancestor had tried many methods to expel the dark matter for those departed souls, but none succeeded. The origin of the dark matter likely involves the legendary Realm of Truth; ordinary methods are ineffective. In fact, the more one tries to expel it, the faster it may spread.magic, consciousness, mental power, faith—even a single thought—could all serve as a medium for its spread…” “But now…”

Shangguan Qing, the head of the Shangguan family, widened her eyes and murmured in disbelief.

Such a method was simply beyond belief; once it became public, who knew what kind of uproar and sensation it would cause.

As she watched Old Zhu’s once ashen and sallow face gradually regain its healthy, rosy hue as the dark substance was drawn away,

Zhu Xuan couldn’t help but tremble with emotion, clutching his grandfather’s hands tightly as tears welled up in his eyes.

"Lord Alliance Leader, your kindness is beyond measure. I, Zhu Xuan, will never be able to repay you in this lifetime..."

With a thud, he knelt directly before Gu Changge, his voice trembling and choked with emotion.

The rest of the Floating Continent’s people knelt in unison, offering endless thanks and gratitude.

A Li had been staring blankly and in disbelief at the methods Gu Changge used to save Old Zhu, but now he had no choice but to kneel alongside the others from the Floating Continent, lest he appear too different.

"How is this possible..."

The shock and awe she felt inside were simply beyond words.

Could dark matter really be dispelled so easily?

Who exactly was this leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance? Why did he possess such unfathomable abilities?

"I’ve already expelled the dark matter from him. With proper rest for a while, he’ll make a full recovery."

Gu Changge shook his head slightly and flicked his finger. The black hole that had absorbed the dark matter instantly vanished, dissolving into nothingness as if it had never existed.

"That’s wonderful…"

"Thank you, Alliance Leader."

Zhu Xuan looked at his grandfather, whose complexion was now a hundred times better than before, and his gratitude knew no bounds.

To him, this act of kindness was nothing short of a life-saving favor.

Now, even if the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance asked him to be a beast of burden, he wouldn’t hesitate for a moment.

"There’s no need to thank me. I’m helping you because I have my own agenda. Who knows? Perhaps in the not-too-distant future, you’ll be able to help me as well."

Gu Changge waved his hand, gave a faint smile, then gestured for Zhu Xuan and the others to rise, indicating they need not remain kneeling.

Zhu Xuan froze upon hearing this. In the not-too-distant future, he might actually be able to help the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance?

A wry smile tugged at the corners of his mouth; he sensed this was merely Gu Changge’s way of offering comfort.

He knew his own limitations. Although he was capable of handling beings at the Quasi Immortal Emperor level, his strength, in the face of the Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, could only be described as insignificant.

How could he possibly be of any help to Gu Changge?

The people of the Floating Continent were also taken aback by these words, their faces filled with confusion.

"The Black Calamity is descending upon the world once more. The entire Vast Expanse will face this trial and disaster, and you, as a descendant of the Pan Clan, hold the key to the Pool of Creation."

"With your current strength, you may not yet be able to influence the course of this upheaval, but who can say what the near future holds?"

Gu Changge smiled faintly, then swept his gaze over the people of the Floating Continent. “You should be able to guess the purpose of this sudden attack by the dark beings. As descendants of the ancient sages, you surely know all too well about the six treasures of the First Civilization.”

“And now, one of the Treasures of the Primordial Civilization has already emerged…”

These words startled even Ling Yuling, causing her to glance at Zhu Xuan. Could he truly be a descendant of the Pan Clan?

“The Central Lord, Southern Venerable, Eastern Emperor, Western Sage, and Northern Emperor—could Zhu Xuan truly be a bloodline descendant of the Eastern Emperor?” She naturally knew of the being once revered as the Eastern Emperor.

The ruler of the Pan Clan was revered by the world as the Eastern Emperor and served as the clan’s leader. In that era, the Pan Clan was known as the Heaven-Opening Divine Clan, possessing overwhelming power that surpassed all others.

Moreover, could the clue to the Pool of Creation actually lie within Zhu Xuan? Could it be that the Pan Clan had already been annihilated and vanished?

Ling Yuling’s heart was truly shaken to its core. Moreover, Gu Changge had apparently known all this all along and was well aware of Zhu Xuan’s origins.

No wonder he agreed to help Zhu Xuan’s grandfather.

Zhu Xuan and the others were equally shaken by Gu Changge’s words, and soon fell into an involuntary silence.

Without Gu Changge needing to say more, they all understood just how turbulent and chaotic the current situation was.

Everyone was on edge, panic reigned across the land, and even some nations of civilization had already plunged into a state of utter despair and devastation.

Of course, what astonished them most was how Gu Changge had discerned Zhu Xuan’s background and knew he held the key clue to the Pool of Creation.

After all, this was something they had never discussed with anyone—not even Ling Yuling knew of it.

Gu Changge seemed to have noticed the confusion and bewilderment on Zhu Xuan and the others’ faces. He smiled casually and said, “What I need to know, I will naturally know. There’s no need for you to be puzzled.The Gate of Immortality, one of the six treasures of the First Civilization, is now in my possession—that is no secret.” “As for the Mirror of Reincarnation, it is in the hands of the Holy Maiden of the Yuan Hope Civilization, while the Coffin of the World’s Demise has fallen into the hands of the Dark Beings.” “Next, the Dark Beings will certainly try to seize the Mirror of Reincarnation and search for the Pool of Creation”

, the Disk of Transcendence, and clues regarding the other Treasure of the Primordial Civilization."

"If given the chance, the Dark Beings will also seek to destroy all these treasures of the First Civilization, ensuring they do not endure in this world."

"If the vast realms and civilizations do not unite and stand together, the time left for all living beings is running out. Compared to the Great Reckoning that lies ahead, the return of the Black Calamity is but a mere trifle."

At this point, Gu Changge sighed deeply, his eyes growing deep and vast, as if he could see all the horrors of the future at a single glance.

"The Great Reckoning to come?"

“Has the situation truly become this dire?” Zhu Xuan and the others were deeply shaken, sensing a suffocating, terrifying pressure in Gu Changge’s words.

It was as if they had already witnessed the near future: the vast, desolate world shrouded in darkness, blood and fire erupting from the void, endless dark creatures descending to slaughter, the heavens and earth turned upside down, the universe collapsing, and order crumbling.

Ling Yuling also felt a chill run through her heart. She turned her beautiful eyes toward Gu Changge, furrowing her brow slightly, sensing that he was not joking, but had truly seen something.

“However, you need not worry. Before that day arrives, I can still secure a period of peace and tranquility for all sentient beings.” Gu Changge offered a faint smile, breaking the atmosphere that had suddenly grown heavy and solemn.

Zhu Xuan and the others snapped back to reality, yet a sense of heaviness still weighed on their hearts.

In this turbulent and chaotic age, no one can truly remain detached from the world or look out only for themselves.

Nevertheless, Gu Changge’s words filled them with even greater admiration and respect; this was the true magnanimity that all living beings looked up to.

"The most urgent task now is to reclaim the Pool of Creation before the dark beings do, and we must not let them destroy it."

“Back then, the Holy Ancestor and the other sages of old relied on the six Treasures of Civilization, paying a tremendous price, to finally manage to seal that entity.”

"If the Treasures of Civilization are destroyed, the vast expanse of the cosmos will no longer possess the power to threaten the Dark Beings. I fear that this calamity of darkness has descended as a grand ritual prepared by the Dark Beings to revive that entity,"

Shang Gong Guanqing said in a grave tone.

Gu Changge nodded slightly and turned to Zhu Xuan,

“Zhu Xuan, you hold the key to the Pool of Creation. The crucial task of retrieving the Pool of Creation before the dark beings do falls to you. I am currently unable to leave the Yuan Hope Civilization.”
