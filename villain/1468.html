In the concept of "God," the realm of reality is incomplete; therefore, no matter how far they journey, they cannot transcend the universe that contains them.

Therefore, from the very source, He discerned and brought forth what are known as the Substance of Immortality and the Substance of Creation.

The existence of these two substances created chaos; from this chaos, the world evolved; the world gave birth to all living beings; and these beings, in turn, generated forces such as vital energy and faith, from which immortal matter and creative matter could once again be refined.

This forms a perfect cycle; the Vast Expanse is like the natural soil that nurtures all life.

The two substances He desires can both be distilled from it.

In a sense, the emergence of the Great Cataclysm of the Eon was actually intended to harvest this vital energy.

Unlike Gu Changge’s previous method of harvesting the Children of Destiny—slowly wearing them down and squeezing every last drop of value from them before reaping their rewards—

The method of the Great Cataclysm of the Eon is more akin to a clean sweep. After all, following the passage of countless ages, the Vast Expanse of the Primordial Void will once again give birth to new beings, in an endless cycle of renewal.

The Realm of Truth differs from the Vast Expanse; all the matter within it was brought from the time of the creation of heaven and earth. To perfect the Realm of Truth and make it more complete, more immortal matter and primordial matter are needed.

The three True Ancestors were all exploring their respective ideals, and Gu Changge was no exception...

Of course, he also embraced the concept of “God,” establishing comprehensive rules for the Great Calamity, thereby bringing upon the sentient beings of the Vast Expanse a long and unchanging era of reckoning.

Minor reckonings occurring once every few eras, major reckonings occurring once every few hundred eras, and the Era Reckoning that plunges the Vast Expanse into an era-long winter.

His thoughts returned.

At the heart of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, after Gu Changge had completed the refinement of the World Orb, he turned his attention to the Prosperous Dao True Realm.

"Next, Wuyue’s forces will move against the Nine Heavens. With the Heavenly Host serving as the vanguard, they can cause no small amount of trouble for the Nine Heavens."

Through this process, he used immortal substances and primordial substances to reshape everything within the Prosperous Dao True Realm. Substances drifted across the sky, falling like starlight into the realm and merging with the void.

It wasn’t that he suddenly had the leisure to do this; he merely wished to use this opportunity to verify his previous conjectures.

In this world, no one understands the True Ancestors better than the True Ancestors themselves. The three were conceived from the void and born in a place beyond words and imagination; if anyone truly understands them, it is still themselves.

Thus, Gu Changge had been pondering one matter during this time: whether it be the "Divine" or the "Mysterious," each held its own philosophy, one they had adhered to for ages, walking this path for a long time. It was only after he had confirmed this beyond a doubt that he began his own plans.

Given the current situation, it cannot be ruled out that the two Ancestors have also outmaneuvered him, concealing plans unknown to him. Yet there is also the possibility that a situation has arisen that he did not foresee.

"But no matter what the situation, it is impossible for anyone to spy on my actions within the world I have created."

"The existence of the Prosperous Dao True Realm is no secret, but compared to the Vast Expanse, it is like a small puddle. Any movement within it, no matter how slight, I can detect instantly. This allows me to verify whether there are any eyes—unseen by me—spying on all this."

The Vast Expanse is as boundless as the sea; the ripples caused by a single pebble falling into it are completely negligible.

Gu Changge could not possibly keep a constant watch over the Vast Expanse, let alone notice such a tiny ripple. But in the Prosperous Dao True Realm, it was different; the moment a pebble fell, he would notice it instantly.

It was precisely for this reason that he had reshaped the Prosperous Dao True Realm using immortal and primordial substances.

The Transcendence Compendium, the legacy of the Jutian Sect, and the previous self-inflicted blow of Heaven’s Will—all of which caused many beings to forget the Heaven Slaying matter—along with the existence of Ye Suyi, all told him that, in the depths of the Vast Expanse, a force was manipulating the Vast Expanse, and it had no intention of hiding this from him.

Now, by openly gathering and condensing Immortal Substance and Creative Substance to reshape the Prosperous Dao True Realm, he aims to draw the attention of that force. If that force wishes to peer into his plans, it cannot avoid observing his current actions.

"On the path I have chosen, has someone already glimpsed the future and gained a vague understanding of my plans and intentions?"

This was the conjecture Gu Changge had arrived at recently; the figure behind the scenes might not necessarily be “Shen” or “Xuan.”

He needed to make further preparations for this.

While Gu Changge was reshaping the Prosperous Dao True Realm, the Heaven Slaying Alliance saw its faith energy surge as its doctrines spread. As he refined immortal and transformative substances, Gu Changge also intentionally scattered these materials, creating various opportunities and transformations across the Alliance’s territories.

He was intentionally forcing growth; individuals of great destiny—figures akin to the Chosen Ones—who would normally take millions of years or several eras to emerge, began to appear during this period like a gushing spring, surfacing in different realms and universes.

Sometimes, within a single universe, multiple individuals possessing the grand fortune of a protagonist would be born, and as they grew, they began to vie for the title of the universe’s protagonist.

Within a single True Realm, which contained even more worlds and universes, such individuals of immense destiny began to emerge at a terrifying pace.

With the emergence of these individuals of immense destiny, the overall fortune of the Heaven Slaying Alliance has been surging and soaring.

The sheer scale and speed of this phenomenon left the civilizations across the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s True Realms in a state of disbelief and awe.

For many have begun to notice that the more devout, fervent, and steadfast one’s faith, the more opportunities and blessings one attracts—this has already become an undeniable trend.

Over the years, every figure who has emerged with great destiny and grand presence has, without exception, been among the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s most devout followers—those who hold an unwavering faith in the Alliance Leader and are willing to strive for the Alliance’s grand cause.

Consequently, many cultivators have abandoned their original paths of cultivation to devote themselves entirely to the study of the *Summons Sutra*, continuously contributing to the growth and expansion of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s destiny.

Gu Changge has also been monitoring this situation, constantly working to set things right.

This is essentially a case of taking from the flock and using it for the flock’s benefit. The power of faith originates from the hearts of sentient beings across all races and lineages. After he gathers it, he refines and extracts it; even a mere trace of immortal or primordial substance leaking from between his fingertips is enough to send these people into a frenzy of delight, as if they had found a priceless treasure.

Even those at the Dao Realm were gradually losing their minds, becoming obsessed with studying the power of faith. The entire Heaven Slaying Alliance was engulfed in an indescribable, feverish frenzy.

Yet the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s overall strength and fortune were also growing rapidly. What once required tens of thousands of years of cultivation to reach—the Immortal Realm—could now be attained far more swiftly under the blessing of the power of faith, with the opportunities and divine blessings bestowed upon them increasing exponentially.

Perhaps it would take only a few thousand or even a few hundred years. More terrifying still, some would obtain a "golden finger" blessing akin to a system, allowing them to ascend to the Immortal Realm in a single leap—reaching that realm within a matter of months.

Of course, there is a downside to this: if one’s intentions become impure, faith wavers, or doubts and instability arise—or worse, if one harbors thoughts of rebelling against the Heaven Slaying Alliance Leader and seeking to dominate alone—then all the opportunities and blessings obtained will vanish overnight, flowing away like water.

"Hahaha, so such a system actually exists in this world. Given enough time, I will surely surpass the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance and overthrow him..."

In a remote small town, a young man who had long been looked down upon and shunned by his family went to the Heaven Slaying Shrine to pray and chant after his engagement was broken off today. He had previously been an unwavering believer in the Heaven Slaying Alliance, going there punctually every day, rain or shine, to pray and offer sacrifices—a practice he had maintained for many years.

He craved power. He had always heard that by believing in the Heaven Slaying Alliance and becoming one of its followers, one could gain numerous benefits—freedom from illness and disaster, and rise above others.

He had also always felt a deep fervor and reverence for the Heaven Slaying Alliance Leader, that magnificent being who ruled over the Vast Expanse, and every day he fantasized about how he might become such a figure himself.

But today, after his former fiancée broke off their engagement, he unexpectedly discovered a system in his mind—one he had never heard of before.

As long as he followed the system’s commands and carried out certain tasks, he could reap corresponding rewards—this was nothing short of a divine blessing, a chance to rise to the top without lifting a finger.

The young man was thrilled beyond measure. After completing today’s task as the system instructed, he gained thirty years of cultivation experience, instantly becoming a Third Realm cultivator in this world. He then tracked down those who had once bullied him, taught them all a lesson, and broke their arms and legs.

The clan members who had looked down on him also suffered his retribution; no one dared to underestimate or offend him anymore.

Seeing such good fortune bestowed upon him, a surge of pride filled the young man’s heart. All the past setbacks and hardships vanished in an instant. He felt that, given enough time, he might even overthrow the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance and rule over the Vast Expanse.

As this thought arose, the young man felt an uncontrollable surge of excitement and exhilaration welling up within him.

"System, what is my next mission? My current cultivation level is still too low..." he hurriedly asked his system.

But soon, the young man was left speechless.

As the awe and reverence he had felt toward the Heaven Slaying Alliance faded from his heart, the System, which he had been able to hear just moments ago, vanished in the blink of an eye.

There was no response whatsoever; all his calls fell on deaf ears.

If anyone had been watching at that moment, they would have seen a faint glow shoot out from between the boy’s eyebrows, then drift away to other places.

Before the boy could process what was happening, panic spread across his face as he frantically questioned his system. Yet no matter how he pleaded, there was no response.

Then, his face turned deathly pale as he finally realized that his once-in-a-lifetime opportunity had vanished right before his eyes. The cultivation energy that had just filled his body deflated like a burst balloon, disappearing completely.

Everything that had just happened felt like a terrible dream.

"Why did this happen..." The youth was utterly disoriented, unable to fathom the reason.

Scenes like this had been unfolding across the various territories of the Heaven Slaying Alliance for some time now.

More and more people were taking notice of this miraculous spectacle, finding it simply too incredible to believe. Wasn’t this nothing short of ascending to the heavens in a single step?

As long as one became a believer of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, held an unwavering and pure faith, and gained its recognition, one could attain supreme blessings, possess vast magical power, and instantly rise to become a person of distinction.

Compared to those cultivators who toiled in arduous practice, advancing step by step, this method seemed too incredible, too unimaginable—and too difficult to accept or comprehend.

Yet, simply because of their faith in the Heaven Slaying Alliance, they were blessed with extraordinary fortune. One day, they stumbled upon a street stall, casually bought a ring, only to discover within it an unclaimed treasure cave. Relying on the opportunities and blessings within, they reached in just a few short months what it took you half a lifetime to achieve.

The recent emergence of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s core tenets is tantamount to announcing to the world that anyone who becomes a devout follower of the Alliance has the chance to become the protagonist of a historical chronicle or novel.

Gu Changge continues to artificially fashion beings akin to protagonists, stirring these waters ever more turbid.
