Countless eyes from civilizations across the Realm of Truth were fixed on the scene, watching intently.

The two superpowers have deployed their forces and formed their battle lines; the decisive showdown is on the verge of erupting, with the air thick with the scent of gunpowder.

"The Heaven Slaying Alliance has gone too far; this battle is inevitable."

A high-ranking official of the Righteous Unity Alliance appeared in the ancient city, standing in the void with a cold gaze.

Behind this high-ranking member of the Righteous Unity Alliance, several figures of overwhelming power emerged one after another, facing off against the vast army of the Heaven Slaying Alliance across the distant, boundless starry sea.

Over the years, for various reasons, the Righteous Unity Alliance had been retreating, doing its utmost to avoid direct confrontation with the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

This had long caused discontent among many high-ranking members, who harbored deep resentment and suppressed their rage. Now, having finally received the order to fight with all their might, this battle was inevitable—there was no room for retreat. What was there to fear?

Rumble!

One after another, ancient bronze war chariots rolled across the heavens, advancing in a mighty tide. Their wheels raced through the starry realms, causing the entire cosmos to tremble.

Upon these ancient chariots stood row after row of soldiers, each clad in cold-weather armor and wielding celestial blades and war spears. Their cold, eerie glow sliced through the starry sky, sending shivers down the spine.

Within the Righteous Unity Alliance, there are multiple factions: the hawkish faction that favors war, the dovish faction that seeks peaceful coexistence, and the turtle faction that chooses to avoid conflict.

But this time, as the Alliance Leader and several Deputy Leaders simultaneously declared their intent to fight the Heaven Slaying Alliance to the death, even the doves and tortoises were forced to dispatch their strongest warriors to the front lines.

A vast army was arrayed here, their iron armor gleaming menacingly, casting a cold, eerie light that flickered across the cosmos.

"Is the Heaven Slaying Alliance truly intent on launching a decisive battle? The Righteous Unity Alliance has never shied from a fight, but you must consider carefully what price you will pay."

A powerful figure from the Righteous Unity Alliance roared in fury, his voice booming like a great bell, reverberating throughout the cosmos and causing the surrounding stars to tremble.

Across a vast expanse of star fields, the many powerful figures of the Heaven Slaying Alliance watched all this coldly; none responded to his words.

The Lord of the Demon Court, Demon Emperor Di Kun, had descended in person. Behind him, the Demon Clan’s vast army radiated an equally fierce killing intent

, densely packed like a sea of demons.

Over the years, he had led the Demon Clan’s vast army in campaigns across the eight directions, forging a formidable reputation long ago—one far surpassing that of his early days upon ascending to the throne of Demon Emperor.

Even if the Demon Ancestor, Emperor Huang, were to appear now, his might might not surpass his own.

"By the Alliance Leader’s command, we march to subdue the Righteous Unity Alliance."

"The Righteous Unity Alliance has colluded with dark beings, subverted the cosmos, and brought calamity upon the people. Today, we of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, acting in accordance with Heaven’s will, shall restore order and purge the realm of evil."

"All who stand in our way shall be slain!"

Emperor Kun’s expression was cold and impassive as he rose from his palanquin and stepped out. He wore a loose-fitting purple robe embroidered with qilin and dragon patterns, a crown upon his head, and his gaze was profound.

As he shook his wide robes, a scroll of imperial edict, suffused with the aura of the Supreme, materialized. The scroll shot skyward, radiating countless strands of rosy light as innumerable Dao runes flashed across its surface. Every character was written in bold, unyielding strokes, each triggering a true seal.

A terrifying aura surged and raged, causing countless cultivators—and the entire cosmos—to tremble.

On the surface of this scroll, a faint, slender figure seemed to be visible.

He appeared to sit majestically at the edge of the cosmos, surrounded by the vast, boundless phantom of the world and swirling clusters of divine fire, gazing down upon all living beings across endless dimensions.

All the powerful figures and vast armies of the Heaven Slaying Alliance prostrated themselves before that ethereal figure with fervent devotion.

"That is the phantom of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s leader…"

"It seems he is truly determined to take action against our Righteous Unity Alliance."

At that moment, the expressions of all the high-ranking officials and powerful figures of the Righteous Unity Alliance changed as they felt an indescribable, terrifying pressure.

Cold sweat beaded on many foreheads; their faces turned pale as their hearts pounded and trembled. They could barely resist the urge to prostrate themselves and bow in worship, their legs growing weak and their spines feeling as if they might collapse under the weight.

Countless troops of the Righteous Unity Alliance were now overcome by a sense of fear and dread, as if they were lowly ants facing a true dragon.

That overwhelming sense of terror bearing down on them was suffocating; not a shred of fighting spirit could be mustered.

Their faces were pale with fear; they had no idea if there was any possibility of resistance if they were truly to face an entity like the Heaven Slaying Alliance Master.

With a single thought, that being could effortlessly obliterate the cosmos and wipe out an entire era.

Within the ancient city, a blue bird soared through the sky, its wingspan stretching thousands of zhang. Chu Xiuyan stood upon it, her expression shifting ever so slightly.

To the various races and civilizations of the Vast Expanse today, the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance was a figure all too familiar. Yet this familiarity stemmed from the fact that the Heaven Slaying Alliance he had founded was renowned throughout the Vast Expanse, its ferocious might striking fear into all worlds.

Yet those who have actually laid eyes on the Heaven Slaying Alliance Leader are few and far between.

His air of mystery rivaled that of the benefactor who had aided her father, as she had glimpsed in the Book of Primordial Chaos.

Yet, at this moment, Chu Xiuyan still felt a vague sense of familiarity. She couldn’t put her finger on it, but it was as if she had seen that figure somewhere before.

Though the image was hazy and indistinct, as if obscured by a mist of chaos, she was certain she had truly seen that figure before.

"Is it you?" Chu Xiuyan suddenly thought of a certain figure. Her expression froze in shock as she stared blankly into space, her red lips moving slightly as she murmured the question.

"Kill!"

Just as Chu Xiuyan stood there in a daze, across the vast starry expanse, the sound of endless battle suddenly erupted. The radiance of Dao laws shot toward the heavens, and waves of powerful energy swept across the four seas and the eight corners of the world.

As the Demon Emperor Di Kun issued his imperial decree, countless troops of the Heaven Slaying Alliance surged forward in an instant.

It was a truly overwhelming, endless army, surging forward like wave after wave, engulfing the entire cosmos.

Heaven and earth were torn asunder, and the very fabric of the cosmos trembled and cracked. Thus, the great battle erupted suddenly.

The Heaven Slaying Alliance was clearly well-prepared; they had long known that a great battle would erupt today, so they had set up various grand formations in advance. War chariots and ancient warships rumbled past, constantly shifting positions across the starry expanse, using human formations to channel the Dao.

Endless cosmic energy was drawn forth, ceaseless and surging. Banner after banner, inscribed with ancient formation runes, were unfurled by the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s many powerful warriors, arrayed in all directions.

The heavens and earth were shrouded in a haze as a misty fog rose. An endless army, like a host of ghosts and gods, charged forth from the mist, sweeping all before them with unstoppable force.

In the other realms of the cosmos, massive battles erupted simultaneously. Many powerful figures from the Heaven Slaying Alliance stepped forward, severing all communications between the various territories of the Righteous Unity Alliance. They truly intended to encircle and wipe out the Righteous Unity Alliance’s high-ranking officials in one fell swoop.

"Kill them! Don’t give them a chance!"

"They have colluded with the dark spirits; let the entire world unite to destroy them."

From ancient warships, divine light shot skyward as figures radiating overwhelming vitality stepped forth, crossing the star fields ahead to engage in fierce combat with the many powerful warriors of the Righteous Unity Alliance.

"There is no retreat in this battle. The Heaven Slaying Alliance harbors wolfish ambitions, seeking to devour the vast cosmos and rule over all worlds. If the Righteous Unity Alliance falls today, there will be no force left to restrain or check the Heaven Slaying Alliance from this day forward."

In the depths of the cosmos, a mighty figure roared in fury as blood spattered across the heavens, surrounded and attacked by numerous powerhouses of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Boom!

Within that ancient city, engraved formation patterns were activated, unleashing boundless light that shot toward the heavens, instantly illuminating the pitch-black, profound cosmos.

In the void, one portal after another burst open. Radiant light surged forth, and banners fluttered in the wind. The Righteous Unity Alliance’s reinforcements swept in like a torrent of steel, clashing fiercely with the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s forces.

In the other realms and universes, the battles were equally fierce. Beings whose cultivation surpassed the Dao Realm clashed on battlefields across the cosmos; blood seeped and flowed down from the void, a sight of unimaginable horror.

"If I can just kill three more Ancestral Dao Realm beings, I’ll have accumulated enough battle merits to exchange for a vial of Creation Essence. Then I’ll have a chance to survive the Nine Celestial Waning Tribulations." The battlefields beyond the mortal realm were shrouded in darkness; even the most powerful cultivators could not hope to see through the chaos within.

Here, the laws were incomplete and the Great Dao shattered; any Daoist arts would be suppressed.

And at this moment, the terrifying silhouette of a Golden Crow spanned the entire void of time and space. The river of time manifested at its feet, shimmering with ripples as fragments intertwined, and vast waves of energy swept across the past and present.

Its form was immense beyond measure, as if all worlds could not contain it.

His eyes alone shone as brightly as the rising sun, radiating a blinding golden light, within which flickered a mix of obsession and madness.

It was none other than the Ancestor of the Demon Court, the father of the current Demon Emperor Di Kun—the Demon Ancestor Di Huang.

Opposite him stood a figure with crimson hair, standing atop a surging sea of blood where countless terrifying demonic shadows swam.

Blood-red mist rose from the sea of blood, and figures capable of striking fear into the hearts of all, past and present, emerged from it—ancient sages, emperors of the primordial ages, and great emperors of antiquity, all radiating peerless divine might

—all enemies who had once fallen at his hands.

The two clashed fiercely on this battlefield beyond the mortal realm. In an instant, countless techniques unfolded and interchanged; chaos shattered, only to be reborn anew, as if the heavens and earth were being created anew, intent on reshaping all that had come before.

The aura of the Ancestral Dao Realm was overwhelmingly powerful, sweeping across the entire expanse of time and space. Any ordinary Dao Realm being who dared to step into this realm would be instantly crushed into dust by this aura.

"Demon Ancestor, I never expected even you to have submitted to the Heaven Slaying Alliance. I heard back then that the Demon Court was outmaneuvered by the Alliance’s leader and reduced to a tool for dealing with the vast lands of Xianchu."

Bloodlight filled the heavens. Within that boundless, vast sea of blood, a figure with crimson hair coalesced into human form—a middle-aged man clad in a blood-red robe, his face devoid of a single white hair.

His expression was grim, his eyes crimson, and the Dao of Blood surged around him.

“Hah. Blood Ancestor, aren’t you also working for the Righteous Unity Alliance now? I just wonder what benefits the Righteous Unity Alliance has offered you that make it worth fighting me so fiercely?”

The Demon Ancestor, Di Huang, sneered coldly. His colossal, boundless form seemed to be the only presence in this space.

A blazing sun emerged behind him, illuminating the entire sea of blood, as if it could evaporate everything before it at any moment.

Existences like him—who had ostensibly survived eight Celestial Waning Tribulations in the Ancestral Dao Realm—were few and far between in the vast cosmos, so few that one could count them on a single hand.

And the adversary now standing before him was an ancient figure renowned across countless eras in the vast cosmos, known as the Blood Ancestor.

Rumor has it that the Blood Ancestor had received the legacy of a being from the Primordial Era, having been nurtured within the physical form of that being’s descendant. He had already survived eight Celestial Waning Tribulations many eras ago, and his power was unfathomable.

The Demon Ancestor had not anticipated that the Blood Ancestor would ultimately submit to the Righteous Unity Alliance, pledging his service to them, and come here to engage in a fierce battle with him.

One must understand that beings of their caliber already stand at the very pinnacle of the vast cosmos; no one can force them to do anything.

The Demon Ancestor’s willingness to serve Gu Changge stemmed naturally from his covetousness for that small vial of mysterious substance that the Demon Emperor, Di Kun, had been ordered to bring him at the time.

His cultivation had stagnated for countless eons, showing no signs of progress. But after refining a wisp of that mysterious substance from the vial, it began to show signs of breaking through.

The path ahead, once shrouded in thick fog, seemed suddenly illuminated by a ray of dawn, showing him the way forward.

Having lived through countless eras, this was the first time the Demon Ancestor had encountered such a mysterious substance, and he was naturally deeply shaken and astonished.

In that instant, it was as if a mere mortal had suddenly come to understand what spiritual energy was, what it meant to live as long as the heavens and earth, and what immortality truly entailed.

That sensation of waking from a dream left the Demon Ancestor both terrified and trembling.

He did not know where Gu Changge had obtained that mysterious substance, but it was indeed what a being at the Dao Realm required.

The sensation was akin to having been parched for many years, only to suddenly drink a large bowl of sweet spring water.

Unable to contain his curiosity, the Demon Ancestor later secretly sought out Gu Changge, hoping to learn the nature of this substance.

Gu Changge did not hide anything at the time; he gave him a faint smile and said it was a substance of creation, containing the secret to transcending the path.

It was only from that moment onward that the Demon Ancestor fully understood why so many figures of legend were willing to follow Gu Changge and serve him.

Gu Changge’s background was likely beyond imagination. He had simply produced this incredibly mysterious substance of creation and openly placed it in the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s merit exchange, available for anyone to redeem.

The Demon Ancestor dared not dwell on how this substance had come to be; all he wanted now was to accumulate enough battle merits as soon as possible so he could exchange them for a vial of the substance of creation.

He had a premonition that this would be the key to his future survival through the Nine Celestial Waning Tribulations.

Over the years, he had frequently traveled across various battlefields, hunting down peers from the Righteous Unity Alliance. However, figures at the Ancestral Dao Realm were all cunning; upon sensing or anticipating danger, they would choose to flee immediately rather than engage in a head-on clash with him.

As a result, the Demon Ancestor had only managed to kill one Ancestral Dao Realm being so far, falling far short of the battle merits required to exchange for a vial of Creation Essence.

However, the Blood Ancestor before him was far more powerful than the average Ancestral Dao Realm being—he was an entity who, like himself, had survived eight Celestial Waning Tribulations.

If he could kill the Blood Ancestor, the military merit would certainly be enough for him to exchange for a vial of Creation Essence.

"`We all serve our respective masters; we’re merely doing our duty. Why must you push yourself so hard, Demon Ancestor?

"The Blood Ancestor’s eyes glowed crimson as he stared at the Demon Ancestor, his voice deep and gravelly.

He could sense that the Demon Ancestor truly intended to fight him to the death—that he had genuinely resolved to kill.

What kind of temptation could possibly make the Demon Ancestor serve the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance

to the point of risking his life?

Or was it, as the rumors claimed, that the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance possessed mysterious powers, capable of controlling minds and converting those around him to his cause?

“Hah, so it seems you aren’t truly willing to die for the Righteous Unity Alliance—you’re merely being coerced.”

The Demon Ancestor sneered coldly, as if he had instantly seen through the Blood Ancestor’s thoughts. “Blood Ancestor, if you know what’s good for you, you’d better switch sides and join the light sooner rather than later. The Righteous Unity Alliance has no chance of standing up to the Alliance Master. Take advantage of the fact that he doesn’t want to take matters into his own hands just yet.”

"If you think that by joining forces with the Dark Beings, the Righteous Unity Alliance stands a chance against the Alliance Master, then you’re still far too naive."

Hearing these words, the Blood Ancestor’s expression shifted between light and shadow.

He couldn’t fathom why the once domineering and arrogant Demon Ancestor would utter such words.

Just who exactly is this Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance?

"It seems even you, the Demon Ancestor, have been brainwashed."

"I’ve heard you once clashed with the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, but after a single move, you fled billions of miles away without a word. This doesn’t sound like the Demon Ancestor I once heard of—the one who looked down upon all worlds and struck fear into every race."

The Blood Ancestor glared at him coldly, saying no more. The Blood Sea surged, and countless formidable figures burst forth from its depths, all charging toward the Demon Ancestor.

In the next instant, the heavens split and the earth shattered; ghosts wailed and gods howled, as if a great cataclysm had descended. Terrifying Dao laws and orders swirled, forming a massive tempest that swept everything away.

Between the two, the Great Dao unfolded. These were no longer mere laws of the cosmos; they were the Dao itself, transformed into order. As they collided, they rippled out like waves of the Great Dao, sweeping across the world, striking fear into all ages, and disrupting the flow of time.

Such a storm, were it to appear in the myriad heavens and realms, could easily destroy every universe and Civilization—far more terrifying than any natural disaster.

Since time immemorial, who has ever witnessed a clash between beings of the Ancestral Dao Realm who have survived eight Celestial Waning Tribulations?

In other secluded realms, earth-shaking battles have also erupted—all clashes between peers of equal standing. Those whose power falls short of the Ancestral Dao Realm have no right to intervene.

The great war between the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance has shaken the entire vast cosmos.

Countless civilizations and races were filled with horror and disbelief. In all the ages since time began, when has such a world-shaking, terrifying war ever erupted?

Countless civilizations and True Realms may vanish in this conflict.

And there is no telling how this war will ultimately end.

With the Heaven Slaying Alliance advancing so aggressively, do they truly intend to annihilate the Righteous Unity Alliance in one fell swoop?

Moreover, at a time like this, what will the Dark Beings do? Will they join forces with the Righteous Unity Alliance to jointly resist the Heaven Slaying Alliance? Or will they sit back and watch the two sides fight it out, refusing to aid either?
