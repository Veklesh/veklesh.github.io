The emergence of the Authority of Cause and Effect captured the attention of everyone on the battlefield.

In the past, this would inevitably have sparked a fierce struggle among countless powerful beings, with battles fought to the death—even True Path-level beings would have joined the fray.

But now, with the precedent set by the Lord of the Void and others, all sentient beings shunned this Authority of Causality as if it were a venomous snake, their eyes filled with terror, not daring to approach it in the slightest.

Clearly, the sudden descent of the Authority of Causality was undoubtedly intended to give birth to the final Celestial Avatar.

Who would want to become a puppet, stripped of their own will?

"Thank goodness it’s not me."

The Lord of Reincarnation felt a sense of relief and quietly exhaled.

Originally, he and the Lord of the World had been plotting to take advantage of the chaos to journey deep into the Sea of Reincarnation, using that celestial light path to sneak across to the Land Of Truth.

Now that the Lord of the World had become the Great Avatar of Heaven, the matter of crossing over could only be left to him alone.

Fortunately, that shattered, mysterious green vessel was still in his possession; he hadn’t handed it over to the Lord of the World at the time.

"Who would have thought I’d have the joy of reappearing in the world of Lechu once more."

“Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, you never expected this, did you? I have returned—and I have been resurrected by the Heavenly Will itself.”

"Though I have become the Great Avatar of Heaven, what of it if I can destroy the Heaven Slaying Alliance you established? It’s a pity you can’t witness any of this now."

"Hehe..."

Just as everyone watched that mass of karmic authority with utmost vigilance, a vast surge of energy suddenly emanated from within it, accompanied by faint, wistful murmurs.

At the same time, overwhelming malevolence and hatred filled the air.

Space and time twisted there, and an aura surged like an endless torrent of the Dao, as if the karma of all worlds were converging, finally coalescing into a dazzling, indistinct figure… It was none other than the Fate Master, who had been crushed to death by Gu Changge back in the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

The many powerful figures of the Heaven Slaying Alliance all paled.

"How is this possible..."

Ji Hanjing’s gaze was icy, radiating nothing but coldness.

"The Heavenly Will has actually resurrected this man."

"However, I fear only the Heavenly Will could have achieved this. By reshaping and projecting him through the remnants of his causal ties, it cannot be considered a true resurrection." The Lord of Power stared intently at the Master of Destiny emerging from that place and spoke in a deep voice.

Since the Heaven Slaying Alliance was founded, its leader, Gu Changge, had rarely taken a life—let alone laid a hand on a being of the End of the Path tier.

The only exceptions were the Lord of Power, and the High Priest of the Ancient Hidden Civilization, who had once tortured and humiliated the Ninth Princess of the Ji Chan clan.

“Master of Destiny…”

The Lord of Reincarnation and the Emperor of Ancient Zang were also deeply shaken.

After all, they had witnessed with their own eyes as the Fate Master was crushed to death by Gu Changge, his body refined into a single elixir containing the Dao Fruit of the End of the Path. Any being who consumed it would instantly attain sainthood and reach the pinnacle of the End of the Path.

It could be said that the Lord of Destiny was as dead as could be.

"Hehe, I have returned."

"You never would have guessed, would you?"

The Lord of Destiny’s gaze swept over the Lord of Reincarnation and the Ancient Treasure Emperor; having been brought back to life, he still retained many of his former self’s memories.

The next moment, amidst the stunned gazes of countless beings on this vast battlefield, he reached out and let that cluster of karmic threads slip from his grasp.

In life, he had taken that final step to seize the authority of the Heavenly Dao, plotting and scheming across countless eons, investing immeasurable effort.

The Yao Guang Ancestral Court was destroyed because of him; the Central Lord died because of him. He established the Yin Xu Cave, orchestrated the Chosen Ones of each era, and supported the vast lands of Xian Chu… Yet, tragically, due to Gu Changge’s interference, everything had vanished like water flowing downstream.

Even in the moment of his death, he remained filled with rage, resentment, and bitterness...

Fortunately, the supreme Heavenly Will of the Heavenly Dao, hidden in the depths of the cosmos, seemed to sense his obsession and the hatred he harbored toward Gu Changge.

"Today, I shall be the Lord of Causality."

A deep, resonant voice suddenly echoed throughout the Vast Expanse, brimming with overwhelming obsession and hatred.

All the memories from his lifetime flowed through the Master of Destiny’s mind like a river, only to be ruthlessly discarded and severed by him without hesitation.

He chose to actively merge with the authority of cause and effect.

Of course, this also meant he had abandoned all his past memories. From this moment forward, he was the Lord of Causality—the final great incarnation of Heaven.

BOOM!!!

An endless storm of the Great Dao suddenly erupted, surging out in all directions from the Master of Destiny at its center.

Within them, countless Dao runes intertwined and coalesced, forging for the Master of Destiny a True Path-level physical form and a flawless foundation of the Dao.

Every living being on this battlefield was stunned and deeply moved, witnessing with their own eyes the birth of a True Path-level being.

Amidst it all, it seemed as though the primordial scenes of the dawn of time were unfolding: the pervasive creation of life, the essence of the universe’s opening and the evolution of chaos, the origins of heaven and earth, the source of existence, myriad causes and effects, and the supreme creation…

Amidst the surging currents of time, space, and the cosmos, a supreme voice resounded, as if proclaiming to all sentient beings the descent of a True Path-level figure.

At this moment, the five great celestial avatars descended in full. They represented the Imperial Heaven, the Vast Heaven, the Azure Heaven, the Supreme Heaven, and the Boundless Heaven.

Five distinct auras of the Supreme Dao collided, causing one great world after another to perish and shatter, enacting the process from creation to destruction.

They seemed to span all of time, their radiance so intense it illuminated the entire river of time. No realm of space and time could escape their reach; all were swept up in boundless Dao storms.

The instant they acted in unison, they set the Vast Expanse trembling; all things were swept into cycles of birth and death, rise and fall, their immense might beyond comprehension.

Existences such as the Lord of Power, the Lord of Restraint, the Master of Governance, and the Elder of Forgetful Immortals were all sent flying, coughing up blood, having suffered unprecedented, devastating wounds—they were no match for this.

"Kill."

Behind the Five Great Avatars in the heavens, the Nine Minor Avatars had also struck, targeting the powerful figures of the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Palace of Defiance.

Without a doubt, with the aid of this terrifying power, the tide of battle turned once more.

Figures of the "End of the Path" tier—such as the Yellow Lord, the Eternal Ancestor, the Grave Keeper, the Arrow Lord, and the Lord of Light and Darkness—were no match for the nine lesser incarnations, even when joining forces. Despite exhausting every means at their disposal, they suffered a crushing defeat.

They embodied the Heavenly Will; as the heavens and earth crumbled and the cosmos plunged into sudden darkness, this marked the ultimate limit of the collapse of the twenty thousand realms since the creation of the multiverse.

In the end, the nine figures seemed to merge, becoming a single, unimaginably majestic Dharma Body. Its blazing light, like the eternal day of the Great Ro, illuminated the cosmos and the destinies of all beings.

With a single gesture, realms were born and destroyed; all things were affected by it. Every fleeting instant was an infinite repetition of the creation of heaven and earth, and the control of the rise and fall of all phenomena.

Under such fluctuations, even True Path-level beings would suffer severe injuries.

It could be said that the fusion of the Nine Minor Avatars of Heaven had brought their power infinitely close to the True Path tier—and perhaps even made them more powerful and unfathomable.

Across the Vast Expanse of all worlds, due to this great battle, even the traces of the Path of Aversion seemed to have been utterly obliterated.

Many realms and spacetime have turned pale and void, with all causal order vanishing.

The Heavenly Avatars are indestructible; even if the mightiest beings from all races and super-multiverses on the Vast Expanse side joined forces to repel them, they could not achieve a complete victory.

The Lord of Power and his companions faced the Celestial Avatar time and again, frequently finding themselves in peril and suffering heavy casualties.

Although Yue Mingkong was eager to assist, the retinue of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness would not let her do so, keeping her occupied the entire time.

Unless some other variable intervenes, the outcome of this great battle will likely be reversed by the appearance of Yanfahuo.

"Why is this happening..."

Across the various realms and timelines, countless beings who had been watching this battle saw their eyes gradually cloud over with despondency and despair.

Is it truly impossible to change anything?

The outcome is, after all, predetermined.

Deep within the Nine Heavens, in a small world nestled within the depths of the Palace of Defiance.

The space was vast; the lake’s surface was calm, shimmering with ripples, and wisps of ethereal mist drifted through the air. One could even see, beneath the water’s surface, countless small worlds—each as crystalline and dazzling as tiny bubbles.

The power to casually create worlds, stabilize the flow of time and space, and establish a realm of its own—such capabilities are, at the very least, the domain of those who have reached the End of the Path.

Deep within, upon the crystal-clear lake, sat a simple courtyard—high walls and green tiles, floating quietly on the surface.

Surrounded by verdant willows and bamboo, the scene brims with vitality. Amid the drifting mist, a slender, graceful figure seems to be hidden within, brewing tea by the hearth in a pavilion high above.

"I told you not to worry."

“This battle may seem dire, but in truth, the outcome has always been within the control of the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.”

"I would never lie to you."

That slender, graceful figure, draped in a mist-like veil, wore a moon-crescent embroidered blouse and a colorful phoenix-tail silk skirt beneath. Leaning against a soft couch, she sat in a pose that was both revealing and coy.

Her slender, delicate arms—as white as snow-covered lotus roots—partially supported her flawless, exquisite face, while one fair wrist held a palm-leaf fan, gently wafting air over the small red-clay stove before her, giving her an air of utter languor.

Even her tone carried a hint of languid indolence.

Steam rose in wisps from the tea, where unidentifiable, precious tea leaves swirled, filling the air with a rich, fragrant aroma. A single whiff seemed enough to transport one to the heavens, every pore in the body relaxing.

Threads of the divine essence of the Dao manifested nearby, resembling scenes of creation and destruction.

The rising mist gathered in the void, forming a mirror-like surface that vividly reflected the great battle currently unfolding on the front lines of the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

On the simple, ancient-looking tea table beside her—carved from a billion-year-old Xuan Yang Dao Wood—lay snacks such as candied fruits, sunflower seeds, and pastries. From time to time, the woman would lazily pop them into her rosy, lustrous lips, chewing them slowly with a carefree air.

"……"

Wang Zijing stared silently at the woman before her, who looked exactly like her, completely at a loss for words.

Uncharacteristically quiet, she remained silent—a rare occurrence for someone so usually free-spirited and carefree.

There are countless secrets in this world that defy explanation, but when one day you see a person who looks exactly like yourself standing right before you, that strange and unsettling feeling is truly indescribable.

The story actually begins with Gu Changge establishing the Divine Realm within the courtyard where he usually rested.

Doing so certainly made it much more convenient for Wang Zijing, Jiang Chuchu, and the other young ladies to come and go, allowing them to visit the Vast Expanse to relieve their boredom and restlessness, and occasionally ease their longing.

Wang Zijing, who had little interest in cultivation to begin with, had originally treated the passageway Gu Changge had opened merely as a gateway to wander over to the Vast Expanse whenever she was bored.

But one day, she suddenly awoke from her meditative state and sensed a faint, mysterious call emanating from the void.

This call actually originated from the gateway beyond the Divine Realm—beyond the passageway Gu Changge had opened—and specifically from this side of the Vast Expanse.

So she passed through that portal to this side of the Vast Expanse to meet Gu Changge. After spending a few days together, she expressed her desire to travel through the Vast Expanse.

Gu Changge gladly agreed and left a protective barrier on her.

Afterward, Wang Zijing set out to travel across the Vast Expanse of Cangmang, as naturally as a fish in the ocean or a bird returning to its forest. Along the way, she used that sense of calling to search for its location.
