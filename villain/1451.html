Many years ago, at the ruins of the Yao Guang Ancestral Court, a Dao Source split open, and a woman of unparalleled beauty emerged, causing a stir throughout the Vast Expanse.

The elder uncles and clan elders of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty personally escorted her back. They later discovered that she was the legitimate descendant of the Yao Guang Ancestral Court, making her, in the truest sense, the founding ancestor of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty.

When the ruler of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, Emperor Weiyang, journeyed to the Righteous Unity Alliance to assume the role of Alliance Leader, the entire Weiyang Immortal Dynasty also acknowledged that woman as their sovereign.

Under her bold and decisive, iron-fisted governance, the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty became orderly and prosperous, presenting a scene of national peace, public well-being, and prosperity in all directions.

However, just recently, rumors suddenly spread throughout the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty that the matriarch intended to purge the Emperor’s closest confidants and the imperial lineage.

A wave of bloodshed and turmoil swept through the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, with many clans and ancient aristocratic families caught up in the purge. This move naturally sparked widespread discontent across the realm, setting off a massive upheaval.

Numerous feudal lords seized the opportunity to raise their armies, rallying under the banner of “restoring imperial order” and “purifying the Weiyang Immortal Court.” They gathered vast forces across the land, intending to storm the imperial capital.

Many had noticed that since assuming the role of Alliance Leader of the Righteous Unity Alliance, the Emperor of Weiyang had scarcely interfered in the affairs of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, appearing to focus his energies entirely on the Righteous Unity Alliance.

This naturally stirred restlessness among the feudal lords of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty. With their deliberate instigation, rumors spread across many territories that the “ancestral matriarch” had usurped the throne. Moreover, her actions to purge the Emperor’s direct lineage had already crossed the line…

Under these circumstances, the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty was naturally no longer at peace. Coupled with the fact that the Weiyang Emperor was stationed at the Righteous Unity Alliance and no longer intervened, chaos began to spread across the land.

Of course, in the imperial capital of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, other rumors have emerged, suggesting that the reason the Ancestor is purging the Emperor’s loyal confidants is actually to protect the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty and root out the parasites currently infesting it.

The Weiyang Immortal Dynasty had long been infiltrated by the Dark Clans; even the Emperor of Weiyang had fallen victim to this, his body corroded by dark matter, no longer the wise and virtuous sage-ruler he once was.

His decision to remain stationed at the Righteous Unity Alliance without offering any explanation was, in fact, a move born of desperation—he did not wish to see the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty completely devoured by the Dark Clans because of him.

After all, that ancient ancestor was the descendant of the Central Lord, the founder of the Yao Guang Ancestral Court, and having personally survived the Dark Cataclysm, her words carried immense authority.

As this news spread throughout the imperial capital of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, it soon caused a sensation across many of the dynasty’s cosmic realms, though its veracity remained unclear.

However, the fact that the Emperor of Weiyang would assume the role of Alliance Leader of the Righteous Unity Alliance and cease to intervene in the affairs of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty certainly raised many questions.

Of course, no one could be certain whether this was simply a frame-up orchestrated by that ancient ancestor.

After all, the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty is a renowned and powerful force in the Vast Expanse of the present age; the upheaval occurring there naturally drew significant attention. However, as the days drew near the establishment of the Immortal Dao Alliance, relatively fewer eyes were focused on it. Instead, the gaze of most civilizations and realms was fixed on the Piao Miao Sacred Domain.

And with Gu Changge stepping in personally, such attention has only intensified, to the point where few are even bothering to inquire about the upheaval in the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty.

"The Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance has personally intervened—this suits me perfectly..."

"The light of the Black Lotus will shine upon more realms of the cosmos. As long as there is sufficient power of faith, I shall truly become the Lord of the Black Lotus."

In a Vast Expanse of spacetime, within a pitch-black, profound, and mysterious underground palace.

A figure clad entirely in black sat cross-legged in silence. He wore ancient armor, its surface mottled with rust in places, exuding an aura of primordial bloodshed.

Before him, a black lotus platform floated in the air, enveloped in a blood-red glow. Vaguely, one could make out an object enshrined within, radiating an intensely thick black mist.

It was none other than… a piece of flesh the size of an infant’s fist. A surging, astonishing vitality radiated from it, like the primal source of life at the dawn of creation. It emitted rhythmic sounds akin to a beating heart, as if it could transform into human form and come back to life at any moment.

The figure stared at that piece of flesh, its gaze brimming with an unmistakable intensity and fervor.

Threads of dark matter flowed out from the flesh, as if it were a wellspring of darkness.

Beneath the Black Lotus lay a pool, now filled to the brim with a layer of black mist.

As countless threads of silver faith energy converged from the void and surged into the pool, dark matter soon began to spread along the path from which the faith energy had come.

Rather than a fusion, it was more accurate to say that the dark matter was eroding those strands of faith.

Every strand of faith represented a devout believer. Here, these strands were countless, endless, as vast as rivers and seas, overwhelming the heavens and earth, nearly filling every inch of the void—a testament to just how many believers existed in the present age.

The figure sitting cross-legged here watched this scene, his breathing growing rapid.

He was getting closer and closer to the goal he so desperately sought.

Once he became the Lord of the Black Lotus, he would not only surpass Xu, the progenitor of the Heaven’s Decline, but even the High Priest of the Heavenly Host—he could take their place. In the Vast Expanse of the present age, no one would possess the power to rival or match him; he would once again forge a new Dark Age of his own.

"Soon... soon... it’s getting closer and closer..."

"Once I become the Lord of the Black Lotus, I will be able to set foot in the Realm of the End of the Path, seize the power of the Primordial Void, and even go one step further."

"To bargain with a tiger for its pelt, one must ultimately be prepared to be devoured."

Thick black mist surged forward, quickly engulfing the underground palace and swallowing the figure within. His murmurs soon faded into silence.

Long ago, a great upheaval occurred among the Heavenly Host, leading to the Forced Opening of the Coffin of the End of the World. The physical body of the Lord of Heaven, sealed within, was then plundered. Even though the High Priest of the Heavenly Host intervened in time to suppress the chaos, several fragments of flesh and some blood were lost.

Now, a fragment of the Ruler of Heaven’s flesh is enshrined in the deepest recesses of the Black Lotus Sect’s underground palace, providing the entire sect with a nearly inexhaustible supply of dark matter that corrodes and corrupts the faith of its followers.

The central region of the Righteous Unity Alliance is likewise home to vast palaces and towers. In certain areas, ancient figures have established their own cultivation sites, vast and boundless, forming their own self-contained worlds.

"The leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance has personally arrived at the Immortal Dao Alliance’s ceremonial assembly. Spies have noted that the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s numerous armies stationed throughout the cosmos have suddenly vanished..."

Within one such practice hall, an ancient being clad in armor rose from his cross-legged position on a meditation cushion.

A fissure split open between his eyebrows, revealing a vertical golden eye that glared into the distance. A terrifying golden light shot forth, piercing through countless realms of time and space in an instant.

"It seems they are about to make a move against the Righteous Unity Alliance again?" The being’s muttered words boomed like thunder rumbling through the heavens. With a single stride, he vanished from the scene.

In another realm, amidst mist-shrouded mountains, clouds swirled and the sky glowed with vibrant hues, exuding an ethereal, otherworldly atmosphere.

A figure clad in white robes sat quietly in lotus position within a pavilion. A clear stream flowed by, and a gentle mountain breeze stirred, causing his robes to flutter in the wind.

Before him lay an ancient zither, though he did not play it. Two young attendants stood by his side, brewing tea; only the rising steam of the tea filled the air, making the scene seem utterly serene.

"Just as he predicted, the Heaven Slaying Alliance will not cease hostilities."

Suddenly, the figure in white seemed to sense something. A look of confusion flashed across his face, mixed with puzzlement, as he muttered to himself, “Do they assume the Righteous Unity Alliance is vulnerable right now, with no powerful figures stationed here? Or are they so confident in their own strength that they have nothing to fear?”

"Master, is the Heaven Slaying Alliance coming to attack again?"

Standing beside the white-robed figure, two young attendants couldn’t help but ask curiously.

The white-robed figure nodded. “If my senses are correct, the Heaven Slaying Alliance is likely about to strike again.”

In the next instant, he swept his robe, summoning the two young attendants, and strode away from the mountain range.

In the central palace of the Righteous Unity Alliance, numerous figures had already gathered.

Upon learning that Gu Changge had appeared at the Immortal Dao Alliance’s ceremonial gathering, the Righteous Unity Alliance immediately formulated a response. Many venerable figures had rushed to the scene, gathering in the grand hall to deliberate.

As the current headquarters of the Righteous Unity Alliance, this location was manned by figures of genuine depth and influence dispatched by various member factions. Only a few members had accompanied Deputy Alliance Leader Zhao Tianqi and others on their trip to the Immortal Dao Alliance.

News of this reached the Righteous Unity Alliance, naturally causing the entire Righteous Unity Alliance to treat the situation as if facing a major enemy, remaining on high alert.

“Alliance Leader, why have you kept this from us until now? Who exactly is the elder supporting the Righteous Unity Alliance from behind the scenes?”

At that moment, an ancient being spoke. Golden radiance swirled around him, making him resemble a resplendent sun—dazzling and awe-inspiring. This was, of course, related to his true form, having evolved from a Holy Spirit; he was no weaker than a typical Ancestral Dao Realm cultivator.

"You need not ask about this matter. When the time comes for you to know, you will naturally know," Jiang Yun said calmly.

The master of Yinxu Cave was extremely cautious and would not easily reveal himself before the members of the Righteous Unity Alliance.

To this day, he has merely been manipulating the situation within the Righteous Unity Alliance through various means.

Even Jiang Yun did not know exactly how many people here were pawns of the Master of Yinxu Cave.

And the master of Yinxu Cave did not seem to be pressuring him any further at the moment, as if unconcerned that he might interfere or betray them.

"The leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance has personally arrived at the Immortal Dao Alliance. I fear he might join forces with the Immortal Dao Alliance, and together they will put pressure on our Righteous Unity Alliance. If that happens, we’ll be attacked from both fronts, and we’ll also have to guard against an assault by the Dark Clans…"

Another ancient being spoke, its aura no less formidable than that of the Holy Spirit who had spoken earlier.

"We have, in fact, heard some rumors concerning you, the Alliance Master—that you have long been corrupted by darkness. We simply do not know whether this is true or false."

At that moment, another being spoke, their tone indifferent yet tinged with a hint of amusement and skepticism.

The other beings who had gathered in the hall all shifted their tone slightly upon hearing these words.

However, the majority of the beings remained silent, adopting an attitude of neither interjecting nor inquiring.

Jiang Yun said indifferently, “What do you all think—is this news true or false?”

The being who had spoken earlier merely gave a faint smile and said nothing more, though a hint of scrutiny had appeared in his eyes.

After all, there’s no smoke without fire. The fact that the Emperor of Weiyang would assume the role of Alliance Leader of the Righteous Unity Alliance and cease to meddle in the affairs of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty was, in itself, fraught with mystery.

“A large army is gathering along the borders of the Righteous Unity Alliance. Be prepared.”

Suddenly, a calm voice echoed through the air. Ripples spread across the void as a figure clad in white robes stepped forward.

"Senior..."

The senior members of the Righteous Unity Alliance present in the grand hall all bowed respectfully. They held him in high esteem, having witnessed him personally deploy the Fourth Ancient Killing Formation to trap Ling Yuling, the Deputy Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, and ultimately banish her.

Moreover, this figure in white robes clearly had deep ties to the forces backing the Righteous Unity Alliance.

Even the Alliance Master, Emperor Wei Yang, was not held in high regard by him.

"Could it be that the Heaven Slaying Alliance is preparing to strike against our Righteous Unity Alliance at this very moment?" Someone realized the implications of the white-robed figure’s words, their heart trembling with disbelief. Hadn’t the truce only just been declared? Was the Heaven Slaying Alliance already plotting to stir up trouble again?

Many others looked grim, feeling that the Heaven Slaying Alliance was truly arrogant and had gone too far.

The figure in white offered no reply, merely casting a dispassionate glance at Jiang Yun before saying, “Emperor Wei Yang’s daughter, Jiang Wei Yang—is she currently with the Heaven Slaying Alliance?”

Jiang Yun’s expression remained unchanged as he replied, “That rebellious daughter defied our betrothal and disobeyed her father’s orders. I have long since ceased to concern myself with her. Wherever she may be now, it will not affect my stance. If you do not believe me, Senior, you may ask my Master yourself.”

This was the first time he had ever addressed the Master of Yinxu Cave—the Ruler of Destiny—as “Master” in front of others.

The expressions of the high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance present all shifted slightly. Only then did they realize that behind Emperor Wei Yang stood a Master. If that were the case, did this mean his mysterious Master was the true patron of the Righteous Unity Alliance?

The figure in white robes smiled faintly. “It’s not that I don’t believe you, but I’m just wondering: with such a massive upheaval occurring in the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty—it seems even the princess of the Yao Guang Ancestral Court has emerged—how is it that the Emperor of Weiyang shows not the slightest concern or interest in this?”

Jiang Yun’s gaze grew sharp as he turned to face him, saying in a deep voice, “If you put it that way, Senior, I have a question of my own. Given your connection to the Yao Guang Ancestral Court, why haven’t you gone to reunite with that princess?”

The figure in white robes narrowed his eyes and said with interest, “It seems you are aware of my identity.”

“After all, the Fourth Killing Array has always been in your possession. It’s not hard to guess your identity,” Jiang Yun replied.

“Interesting. It seems he intends to take you as his disciple, but it’s not merely a matter of having you establish the Moyang Immortal Dynasty.”

The figure in white gave a faint smile, then swept his gaze over the high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance and said, “The Heaven Slaying Alliance has raised an army to invade; the Righteous Unity Alliance naturally cannot sit idly by and wait for destruction. Now that the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance is not stationed at their headquarters, isn’t this an opportunity for the Righteous Unity Alliance to strike back?”

Jiang Yun furrowed his brow but said nothing.

Hearing this, the senior members in the grand hall all began to feel stirred. Rather than waiting for the Heaven Slaying Alliance to launch another attack, why not seize this opportunity and strike first?

“I wonder what the Emperor of Weiyang thinks of this?” The figure in white robes turned to look at Jiang Yun, his gaze tinged with a hint of amusement.

Jiang Yun’s heart sank. He knew his actions had already aroused suspicion. Yet for some reason, the Master of Destiny had not yet revealed himself. Could it be that he intended to remain in hiding, biding his time?

The white-robed figure before him was clearly some manifestation of the Master of Destiny. He even suspected that this might be one of the Master’s alter egos.

"You speak wisely, Senior."

Jiang Yun nodded. “It is time for us to strike back at the Heaven Slaying Alliance.”

None of the high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance raised any objections at this moment. Immediately, a series of decrees shot out like divine light, piercing through the hall and darting across the cosmos, flying toward every corner of the Righteous Unity Alliance’s cosmic realms.

Boom, boom, boom…

Simultaneously, across the cosmos, war drums were beaten and ancient horns sounded. The sounds contained supreme Daoist laws, like the reawakening of a true dragon or the low chant of an ancestral demon, disrupting heaven and earth and startling all worlds.

On Piaomiao Peak, the Immortal Dao Alliance Assembly was held as scheduled. On this day, all eyes were naturally upon it; countless cultivators and sentient beings watched with bated breath, their hearts filled with tension.

Fortunately, the proceedings went smoothly afterward, without any setbacks or surprises. The Dark Clans did not attack, and Gu Changge, the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, personally appeared to preside over the event.

Founding-level figures from civilizations across the True Realms also took the stage one after another, appearing at the Immortal Dao Alliance Assembly to participate in the proceedings.

On Piaomiao Peak, a scene of joyous celebration unfolded—it could be called the grandest gathering of the recent era.

The number of Ancestral Dao Realm beings present was immense—all ancient titans who had remained hidden for countless eras. As for ordinary Dao Realm beings, their numbers were even greater, too vast to count.

Civilizations from all corners of the True Realm signed the Immortal Dao Alliance Pact, vowing to jointly resist the Dark Tribes. The grand gathering was scheduled to last for half a month.

However, on the fourth day of the gathering, an unexpected incident occurred. Across vast swathes of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm, chaos erupted as followers of the Black Lotus Sect emerged en masse. They erected shrine after shrine, each carried by massive black lotus blossoms that stretched across the heavens and earth, crushing everything in their path as they rained down a torrent of black rain.

The powerful figures of the Four Great Families of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm had long been monitoring the Black Lotus Sect’s movements. They immediately dispatched forces to engage them, attempting to surround and annihilate the enemy.

At the same time, an even greater uprising erupted within the Piao Miao Holy City.

Many Black Lotus Sect followers who had been lying low emerged. Among the numerous powerful figures from various civilizations and realms who had come to witness the event, many fell victim to corruption. A chaotic and massive battle erupted within the Piao Miao Holy City.

In the Vast Expanse, unprecedented battles erupted simultaneously along the borderlands between the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance. The ceasefire was broken, and this conflict proved far more intense than any previous clash.
