In terms of cosmic scale, the Supreme True Realm actually falls within the category of super-multiverses, encompassing numerous dimensional universes capable of giving birth to Ancestral Dao Realm figures who have survived seven or eight Celestial Waning Tribulations.

These super-multiverses, suddenly emerging and reviving, represent a further evolution of the Supreme True Realm. Just as these super-multiverses began to emerge, envoys from the Hall of No-Birth and Mount Forgotten Immortals also appeared within the Vast Expanse.

Without a doubt, these were two supreme and unfathomable powers, with figures comparable to those of the Nine Heavens or the Ancient Hidden Civilization presiding over them. No wonder the rulers of the major super-multiverses adopted such a posture.
