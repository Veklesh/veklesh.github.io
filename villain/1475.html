"We humbly welcome our Lord..."

Before the Hall of Divine Mandate, the Coffin of the World’s Demise floated and hung high in the air. At this moment, all the coffins within it opened silently, shrouded in an endless aura of darkness.

Vaguely, a massive, indistinct figure seemed to sit up within it, yet it remained utterly blurred; no light could penetrate the darkness. The cold, pitch-black void exuded a profound depth capable of swallowing all worlds.

That terrifying, wide-open eye filled the entire sky, seeming to survey everything. The realms of time and space it swept over crumbled and disintegrated, as if utterly unable to withstand such a colossal presence.

"We welcome our Lord..."

From the realm where the Dark Clans resided, endless cries of ecstasy echoed out, like a roaring tide, shaking the entire cosmos.

All the priests and guardians prostrated themselves there in unison, their faces radiant with fervor.

A dense, dark mass of beings spread out like a dark ocean, covering the surrounding mountains and land.

"You have finally returned."

High Priestess Wu Yue raised her luminous, jade-like face; her eyes brimmed with reliance and longing, and her entire body trembled slightly.

For this day, she had waited for an immeasurable time. Through endless ages, countless civilizations and realms had crumbled and collapsed in the river of time.

It was she who single-handedly quelled the rebellion of the Eight Clans, for she had always believed that the Lord of Heaven would one day return.

In this lifetime, she had even heard that familiar breath. From Jing Tian Yuan, who came from the outside world, she learned that the Ruler of Heaven had long since been revived, and she received a strand of the source of life bestowed upon her by him.

Now, after thousands of years of grand rituals, the will that had sealed him within the Source of Darkness has finally been awakened.

How could she not be both astonished and overjoyed?

The entire Nightmare Realm was immersed in unimaginable jubilation and joy. All the priests, regardless of their previous intentions, now bowed their heads and knelt, daring not to harbor any disrespectful thoughts.

As for the Vast Expanse, the revival of the Ruler of Heaven—who had personally shaped the Age of Black Calamity—was nothing short of adding insult to injury to the already turbulent and chaotic situation.

Everyone’s expressions shifted dramatically; fear trembled within them as they sensed that all-encompassing, boundless aura that swept across all realms. A chill ran down their spines, and the moment that gaze swept over their souls, they felt as though they had been frozen solid.

With the Lord of Heaven presiding deep within the Dark Clans, those clans would only grow even more brazen.

In the region where the Nine Heavens clashed with the Dark Clans, the battle grew increasingly fierce. The slaughter shook the heavens, and a torrent of blood surged forth, engulfing one universe after another.

And with the Ruler of Heaven’s revival, many of the most powerful figures in the Nine Heavens could no longer sit idly by.

"This is bad news; the situation is becoming even more chaotic."

"If the Ruler of the Heavens intervenes personally, the other realms simply won’t be able to withstand him..."

"Even those at the Ancestral Dao Realm would stand no chance against him. If he were to destroy the Nine Heavens, it might cause the celestial avatars once buried here to emerge and awaken, leading to even more terrifying consequences."

Within a majestic palace on the Sixth Heaven, figures bustled about; every named powerhouse in the Nine Heavens had gathered here.

These powerhouses all exuded the aura of the Dao Realm, hailing from civilizations across the cosmos, the vast majority of them traveling alone.

Their former homelands had been buried in the river of time for various reasons, and they had eventually found their way to the Nine Heavens to settle here.

Of course, there were also clans that had migrated here en masse. Having lived in the Nine Heavens for a long time, untouched by the Great Calamity of the outside world, they prided themselves on their detachment.

At this moment, the chieftains of these tribes have personally gathered here to deliberate, their expressions grave; many even wear looks of panic and tension.

Living and thriving in the Nine Heavens has caused many clans to lose their former competitive spirit; lacking the necessary trials and tribulations, they possess absolutely no power to resist the advancing armies of the Dark Clans. They are suffering defeat after defeat, with heavy casualties.

"I have already reported this to those beings."

The leader of the Ninth Heaven, Eternal Dao Lord, the Patriarch of the Futiang Sect, had also appeared in this majestic hall.

With his hands clasped behind his back, he appeared remarkably composed compared to the other Dao Realm beings present.

Seeing his composure, the other clan leaders who had rushed to the scene felt their panic subside slightly.

From their vantage point, they could see that the entire universe and spacetime of the Nine Heavens were collapsing at varying speeds. The number of dark entities was simply too great—an endless stream, like a boundless wave of darkness surging toward the isolated island of the Nine Heavens.

Amidst that dark tide, one terrifying dark entity after another stood tall, including many who had already reached the Ancestral Dao Realm.

Once these powerhouses moved to attack, ordinary beings stood no chance against them.

Previously, outside the Nine Heavens, the mysterious figure known as the Grave Keeper had stood guard. Now, however, he lay gravely wounded, having fallen into an unknown realm of time and space. The strongest figure openly present in the Nine Heavens was none other than the Eternal Dao Lord, a being of the Ancestral Dao Realm.

Under normal circumstances, such chaos would never occur in the Nine Heavens; the mere presence of the Eternal Dao Lord presiding over the Nine Heavens was sufficient.

But now, it was clear that the Eternal Dao Lord stood no chance against the powerful figures of the Dark Clan; any single High Priest could easily annihilate him.

Amidst that seemingly endless tide of darkness, they could even faintly make out the figures of those High Priests who had previously clashed with the Gravekeeper.

They continued to wait, refraining from intervening personally, as if aware that another supreme being still presided over the Nine Heavens.

"I have already gone to report this to him personally..."

“He should intervene. But why has the Gu family remained so unresponsive? The Dark Clan has already closed in, yet they still show no reaction?”

The Eternal Dao Lord stood with his hands behind his back, yet his eyes flickered with calculation.

Beside him stood Ji Yu, who had once cultivated on Slanting Moon Mountain alongside Gu Xian’er and the others.

However, the aura emanating from him had now broken through to the Dao Realm. During his years in the Nine Heavens, he had evidently received some divine blessing, rising to the Dao Realm in one fell swoop, with the aura of destiny surging through him.

"The Dark Clans and the Heaven Slaying Alliance have reached a certain agreement, so they will not intervene in this battle."

“But why was the Nine Heavens Realm hidden within a multi-dimensional spacetime, concealing all traces of its presence—even its external formations had never been deciphered—yet it was exposed to the Dark Clans and its coordinates were revealed?”

Within the majestic hall, many were filled with confusion, wondering what could have caused the Nine Heavens Realm to be completely exposed to the Dark Clans’ sight.

At that moment, within a realm of spacetime suspended high above the Nine Heavens, ripples spread out. It was a terrifyingly vast training ground, so boundlessly immense that its edges could not be seen; any star within it appeared no larger than a speck of dust.

And now, deep within that domain, an immense figure opened its eyes. Endless energy surged around it; a single breath felt like the light of time sweeping past, enough to cause all matter to decay.

He wore a dragon robe that appeared somewhat weathered and worn, his features indistinct. Upon his head rested a crown adorned with dangling tassels.

Beside him, an inexplicable, mighty power radiated outward, maintaining the stability of this realm of time and space.

If the Infinite Timeline is a tapestry woven from endless, boundless threads of time, then this realm of spacetime is a pristine, undisturbed sanctuary within it.

Within this realm, the past remains undisturbed, the future is unpredictable, and everything unfolds naturally along the present timeline.

Consequently, within this realm of time and space, all living beings and worlds exist in orderly harmony, following the laws of nature.

"Is the Land of the Nine Heavens in danger again?"

At that moment, the immense, boundless figure opened its eyes, and various scenes of vicissitudes flashed through them.

Before him stood several figures as tiny as specks of dust—figures not even as large as a single strand of his hair.

"Please, Eternal Ancestor, intervene..."

The figures, as tiny as specks of dust, all knelt down in unison, prostrating themselves and crying out in unison.

"Are the other members of the Fate-Defying Palace not here?" A colossal voice boomed, like thunderclaps colliding—vast and majestic.

"We do not know," replied the figures prostrate on the ground. Merely as the colossal figure spoke, they were nearly torn apart by the gust of air that swept out from it.

The disparity was as vast as that between an ant standing before a true dragon—a single blast of the dragon’s scorching breath would be enough to reduce them to nothing.

The existence of the Fate-Defying Palace was deeply mysterious; its members were beyond their comprehension.

Outside, they were Dao Realm beings who looked down on the world, but within this realm of time and space, they weren’t even worth mentioning.

"Are they trying to make me test the waters?"

A colossal being named Yongzu sat majestically upon the dragon throne, yet showed no intention of intervening.

In his gaze, scenes from beyond this realm of time and space emerged: endless hordes of darkness charging forward, like two torrential currents colliding in the cosmos, leaving nothing but devastation and chaos in their wake.

"Heh..."

"I warned them back then to be wary of the Ruler of Heaven behind the Dark Clans. If possible, perhaps I could have recruited him into the Fate-Defying Palace..."

He laughed, his voice still booming like thunder, causing the entire realm of time and space to tremble.

"Hm?"

At that moment, as if sensing something, his eyes narrowed suddenly.

At that moment, beyond the Nine Heavens, countless armies charged forward. Beyond the cosmos, within an unseen realm of spacetime, a fissure suddenly shattered. Immediately afterward, a pale, terrifying giant hand abruptly descended from above.

That hand was so immense it instantly enveloped the entire cosmos. Under its shadow, all living beings were as insignificant as dust before a blinding sun; even the faintest breath of energy it emitted was enough to tear everything apart like a raging storm.

Any world, barrier, spacetime, or era... in the instant that hand descended, they were instantly obliterated, reduced to ashes and vanishing without a trace.

The Nine Heavens trembled violently as vast swathes of the cosmos collapsed and disintegrated, as if this world could no longer contain such a presence.

In that instant, the countless dark armies locked in fierce combat vanished without a trace, effortlessly swept away with a single flick of the hand, exploding into ashes, leaving nothing behind.

Even beings as powerful as those at the Ancestral Dao Realm felt a chill run through their very bones. Under such an overwhelming aura, they could not even muster the thought of resistance—they were as insignificant as ants before a colossal star.

"A figure beyond the End of the Path has intervened..."

"The Grand High Priest was indeed correct; the Nine Heavens harbor a being beyond the End of the Path."

Amidst the endless waves of darkness, the expressions of the four High Priestesses of the Dark Clan shifted drastically. Relying on the innate artifacts bestowed by the Grand High Priest, they could, when united, hold their own against a single figure at the End of the Path.

But faced with such boundless, overwhelming power, there was simply no chance of resistance; the life levels of the two were no longer even on the same plane.

To put it bluntly, such an existence could obliterate an entire realm of supreme power with a single breath.

The moment the four High Priestesses’ expressions twisted in horror, that massive hand struck toward them with pinpoint accuracy, as if they were mere ants facing a terrifying mountain crashing down upon them—no matter which way they fled, escape was impossible.

The other dark beings around them all wore expressions of fear and trembling, filled with utter despair.

The surrounding spacetime universe shattered instantly; any means of resistance seemed feeble and powerless in the face of this overwhelming force.

In the Nine Heavens.

Inside the majestic palace within the Sixth Heaven, all the powerful beings stared intently at the scene, their very breath seeming to catch in their throats. They were filled with shock and awe; even they had never imagined that such an unimaginable being could be stationed within the Nine Heavens.

Even the Eternal Dao Lord could not hide the surprise and reverence on his face as he gazed toward the direction from which that massive hand had reached out.

"The Dark Clans dare to invade the Nine Heavens Realm at will? Truly, ignorance is no excuse. Do they really think the Celestial Graveyard buried within the Nine Heavens Realm is merely a decoration?"

As that pale giant hand reached out, the entire Nine Heavens seemed to fall silent; even the Vast Expanse trembled in response. Countless True Realm civilizations and countless powerful beings were utterly horrified, an indescribable sense of dread sweeping over them.

Under such an aura, even a supreme True Realm could be effortlessly obliterated and crushed—its might was utterly peerless.

Within the Heaven Slaying Alliance, Gu Changge remained seated quietly in the courtyard.

"Have you finally lost your patience?" He glanced over, a look that was either mocking or indifferent playing at the corners of his mouth.

Deep within the Nine Heavens, the Fate-Defying Palace had always existed. Every member of the Fate-Defying Palace was a figure who had left an indelible mark in the annals of history, possessing extraordinary and unparalleled strength.

However, just as the name “Fate-Defying Palace” suggests, these members all share a common, grand ambition.

And at this moment, the intervention of a supreme being within the Fate-Defying Palace was not a test of the Dark Clans, but a test of Gu Changge and his Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Amidst the humming.

Before the Hall of the Celestial Mandate, now shrouded in dark matter, the Coffin of the World’s Demise began to tremble. Then, a crisp clang—as if something were opening and closing—echoed throughout the Vast Expanse.

An endless, colossal beam of bloodlight burst forth from that place, piercing through the boundless cosmos and space-time. It transformed into a boundless, blood-red ancient sword, which with a sharp “thud” struck at the pale giant hand descending from beyond the Nine Heavens.
