"This Black Lotus Cult is truly bizarre."

Such a tactic was spine-chilling, sending a cold shiver down one’s spine.

"No living being in all the worlds can truly die and be reborn, especially at our level."

"These Black Lotus Sect guardians are not ordinary beings at all; they are nothing more than war puppets forged through alchemy."

"However, destroying the source of that faith is even more impossible."

If that were truly the case, wouldn’t it mean that the Ruler of Heaven’s power had long been capable of influencing the Vast Expanse?

Could he awaken at any moment and descend upon the world?

"It’s over, it’s over. The emergence of the Black Lotus Sect is undoubtedly an attempt to round up all the powerful beings participating in the Immortal Dao Alliance, perform a dark blood sacrifice, and pave the way for the Lord of Heaven’s Descent..."

"This is undoubtedly a conspiracy by the Dark Clans."

Panic and trembling spread across Piaomiao Peak; everyone felt a sense of suffocation and despair.

Under such overwhelming cosmic forces, even those at the Dao Realm risked becoming cannon fodder at any moment—let alone the younger generation like them.

This era did not belong to them; for them, it was, in fact, the most tragic and despairing of times.

"Has the Black Lotus Sect actually become entangled with the Ruler of Heaven?"

The high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance were equally shocked.

Even the Ethereal Holy Ancestor, in the end, wore an expression of disbelief and despair.

With his power, he simply could not kill all the beings that were resurrected from the Black Lotus.

Whenever he killed one of the so-called Black Lotus Sect guardians, the black lotus would soon unleash an eerie power, reviving them to attack once more.

He had intended to destroy those black lotuses, but they contained some supreme and terrifying power capable of withstanding his attacks.

Whenever the Black Lotus faced the brink of destruction, a vague, ethereal shadow would emerge. Though its presence was but a faint wisp, it was enough to shock the world, sending a chill down even the Spindrift Holy Ancestor’s spine.

Under such circumstances, even he felt an indescribable tremor of dread.

He was also convinced by the words of that ancient being from earlier: there truly was an intricate, inextricable connection between the Black Lotus Sect and the Lord of Heaven’s Mandate.

That faint, blurry phantom was most likely a projection of the Lord of Heaven sent from beyond the mortal realm.

No wonder the Black Lotus Sect acted with such brazen confidence—it had a powerful backer and the means to rely on, completely unconcerned by the presence of so many powerful beings here.

Even those at the Ancestral Dao Realm were powerless to act; those at lower Dao Realm levels were even less capable, forced to watch helplessly, unable to intervene.

In the other realms of the Ethereal Sanctuary, the scene was even more dire.

Instead, they would truly transform into beings akin to semi-dark creatures and actively turn on those closest to them.

These scenes were enough to turn anyone’s face pale with horror.

After their attempts to intervene proved fruitless, many ancient figures sighed in resignation. They had no choice but to withdraw, retreating back to Piaomiao Peak to do their utmost to protect their own clan members and younger generations.

"Lord Alliance Leader, do you have any means to deal with the Black Lotus Sect?"

Someone turned their gaze toward Gu Changge and, finally unable to contain themselves, asked.

Moreover, as the current Alliance Master of the Vast Expanse Heaven Slaying Alliance, Gu Changge’s strength went without saying; even the Piao Miao Holy Ancestor treated him with deference, daring not to show the slightest disrespect.

The fact that he had yet to make a move had also sparked a sense of anticipation among many, who believed he might have a way to handle the situation.

Many members of the Righteous Unity Alliance, too, turned their gaze toward him, filled with the utmost apprehension toward this “great enemy.”

“This Black Lotus Sect is rather tricky. I’m not entirely confident we can handle them; we’ll need to wait and see.”

The ancient beings present all sighed inwardly.

The Heaven Slaying Alliance had also dispatched numerous powerful figures and high-ranking members to this location. As the Alliance Leader, Gu Changge would certainly not allow his subordinates to perish here.

If even he said so, it was clear that the Black Lotus Sect was indeed a formidable foe—one that even he might struggle to handle.

“Heh…”

"Now that all of us fellow cultivators have gathered here, may I ask, Alliance Master, why are you plotting against us?"

“How dare you!”

"I wonder how much longer the Alliance Leader intends to keep up this charade?"
