They were all aware of each other’s existence, though pinpointing their exact locations had always been difficult.

He had intended to tear through the Realm of Sacred Reason with a single strike, capturing and carrying away the will within—a will whose level rivaled that of the Heavenly Will, yet whose power was countless times weaker—but he never expected anyone to interfere and vie with him for it.

Even amidst the clash between True Path-level and Supreme Shattering-level beings, the Realm of Divine Reason still maintained its repulsion.

A layer of pale blue mist shrouded it, as if it might vanish into deeper layers of spacetime at any moment. Arms flailed wildly, sprouting from beyond the Realm of Divine Reason, appearing utterly grotesque. The aura fluctuations from moments ago could not penetrate it; they were completely blocked out.

This scene further shocked the other figures at the End of the Path observing the battle; this vast world contained unimaginable secrets.

In the next instant, above the Realm of Sacred Reason, the shattered layers of spacetime twisted and blurred continuously, as if a terrifying, indistinct figure were flickering in and out of view from within, poised to descend at any moment. Yet all the world could see was a swirling blend of black and white divine light.

"Three-Headed Master, what do you mean by this?"

The figure of the Black-White Lord materialized here, though this was not his true form. He remained extremely cautious; after that strike, he had completely obliterated all causality and traces, preventing others from pinpointing his spatiotemporal coordinates, and continued to hide within the deepest recesses of spacetime.

"This is a fragment of the Heavenly Will that has broken away..."

"I have come in response to its call. The Heavenly Will promised me that if I recover this fragment for it, it will restore the path for me beyond the End of the Path."

The spacetime above the Realm of Divine Truth churned ceaselessly, as if a passage were about to shatter. A similarly indistinct three-headed figure stood there, boundless in size, like an immutable demonic shadow from time immemorial.

Each head radiated a terrifying aura of power beyond human imagination. He, too, had not descended in his true form, but was merely a projection from a distant dimension within the deepest realms.

Even as the Three-Headed Sovereign, Yi did not wish to reveal his location lightly.

He stood in confrontation with the Lord of Black and White. Fluctuations of the True Path and Supreme Shattering levels continuously surged and swept through, shaking everything to its core. Within the spacetime where the two stood, endless storms of the Great Dao churned, colliding ceaselessly, while simultaneously collapsing and disintegrating.

Even Chaos itself was being eroded; at every moment, miniature worlds were being recreated, while others were disintegrating.

The cultivation of these two was now sufficient to overlook the Vast Expanse of all worlds. Any universe or spacetime would shatter at a single glance from them. Throughout history, eras where True Path-level figures coexisted were rare, let alone the even rarer Supreme Shattering-level figures.

Even during the Great Annihilation and the Great Reckoning of the past, True Path-level figures rarely made an appearance, let alone took action as they did today.

The boundless fluctuations of energy from moments ago had sent shockwaves throughout the Vast Expanse. Countless beings across universes and civilizations trembled in fear, their blood running cold, nearly convinced that the end of the world had arrived.

"A fragment of the Heavenly Will that has broken away?"

A divine light surged in the eyes of the Black-White Lord as he stared intently at the Realm of Sacred Truth. Though he wished to intervene and save his two emissaries, it was already too late. Those pale-blue tentacles had pierced through the chests of the Black and White Emissaries, enveloping their life-flames and completely devouring their lives, using their bodies as fertile soil to nurture even more pale-blue mist.

Within every wisp of mist, countless spores were coalescing; once released, they would taint the entire cosmos.

In any era, such a bizarre entity would be sufficient to corrode and taint the multiverse and endless spacetime, giving birth to a deviant Heavenly Dao.

"The power of the Heavenly Will has been suppressed and weakened by the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance; otherwise, the Heavenly Will would not have descended so urgently to summon."

"It senses unease and now seeks to reclaim its original power..."

The Three-Headed Master spoke calmly. The face of the figure at the very center was a blur, but the shrouding mist gradually dissipated, revealing the ordinary face of a middle-aged man.

The Black-and-White Lord had also surmised this possibility. Not long ago, nearly all True Path-level figures had sensed it: five incredibly bizarre entities had emerged from the void, entities so strange that they even threatened their own authority over the Heavenly Dao.

"It can be said that these entities were created by the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance to counter the Heavenly Will. He first weakened the power of the Heavenly Will, then divided and separated these forces. As a result, in order for the Heavenly Will to maintain the vast order and operation of all worlds, it must now divert even more of its power."

"Now, the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance has repeatedly engaged in decisive battles with the Will of Heaven."

The Three-Headed Master spoke in a measured tone.

Although he had never appeared in the Vast Expanse before and no one knew of his existence, that did not mean he was unaware of the current state of the Vast Expanse.

Moreover, he was unique; long ago, he had uncovered the secrets of the new cosmic order’s spacetime and proposed the concepts of “End of the Path” and the “Supreme Shattering Technique.”

It could be said that he was one of the pioneers of this path. Many speculated that he had once struck some sort of deal with the Heavenly Will, which is why he was privy to so many secrets.

The Black-White Lord’s expression shifted slightly, then he looked down once more at the Realm of Sacred Reason. No wonder he had always felt that all of this was too much of a coincidence; if explained this way, it all made sense.

Now, the Heavenly Will urgently needs to reclaim its original power.

But the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance would certainly not let the Heavenly Will succeed.

The numerous figures at the End of the Path closely watching this side, as well as the True Path-level beings hiding even deeper, were all deeply shaken by the Three-Headed Master’s words. If that were the case, then aside from this Realm of Sacred Reason, there were currently four other sources of the Heavenly Will, all sharing the same origin and lineage, scattered throughout the Vast Expanse of the multiverse.

This revelation instantly set their minds racing.

"In the face of these forces sharing the same origin as the Heavenly Will, the so-called 'Call of Heaven' becomes insignificant. In many super-multiverses, the hearts of those at the End of the Path level burned with fervor; no one could remain unmoved.

It could be said that the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s actions, to a certain extent, mitigated the allure of the Heavenly Dao’s call upon the Vast Expanse.

Of course, there were also those who hoped to use this opportunity to earn the rewards of the Heavenly Will.

However, many True Path-level figures, including the Lord of Black and White, firmly rejected the temptation at this moment.

"Since this is the power the Heavenly Will requires, it must not be allowed to reclaim it. Moreover, it now appears that even the Heavenly Will has its hands full with its own concerns."

The Lord of Black and White spoke, his gaze fixed on the Three-Headed Master.

The Three-Headed Master’s sudden appearance to prevent him from seizing the Realm of Divine Reason—though cloaked in the banner of responding to the Heavenly Dao’s call—likely concealed a true intention to claim this Realm of Divine Reason for himself.

"I merely sensed that various variables would arise in the coming days and did not wish for you, Lord of Black and White, to stray from the path. The entity within this Realm of Sacred Reason should be called the Plague Heaven, correct? Since you deliberately revealed your presence to attract us, you must have ulterior motives. Why not get straight to the point and state your intentions clearly?"

As the Three-Headed Lord spoke, an invisible fog suddenly expanded outward, instantly enveloping the entire spacetime containing the Realm of Sacred Reason.

This fog arrived abruptly and was far too mysterious; it pulsed with scattered hints of calamity, blocking the scrutiny of other figures at the End of the Path, including those of the True Path level.

His subsequent words were clearly directed at the current will within the Realm of Sacred Reason.

The Black-White Lord was taken aback for a moment, then realized that the Three-Headed Lord’s sudden appearance was likely not to vie for the will within the Realm of Sacred Reason, but rather for some other purpose.

He did not intervene, merely watching quietly. However, just to be on the safe side, he reinforced the restrictions he had previously set, shielding and concealing this expanse of spacetime to prevent any other True Path-level figures from prying.

Wave after wave of divine senses and thoughts surged through the spacetime outside the Realm of Sacred Truth, but without exception, they were all blocked, unable to gain any insight into the situation.

This included even the leaders within the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

The Ruler of the Realm of Life also withdrew his gaze and fell into deep contemplation.

The sudden appearance of the Three-Headed Master, who had relayed this information, clearly indicated that he had uncovered some secret.

Moreover, despite the commotion just now, the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance had not intervened. Could it be that he had already entered seclusion and no longer concerned himself with the affairs of the Vast Expanse?

At that moment, above the Realm of Sacred Reason, layers of barriers shimmered brilliantly as pestilential energy surged forth. Then, a passageway materialized. A youth with a head of golden hair, a slit forming a single eye on his forehead, strode out with utter confidence. Following behind him was a figure with a blank expression; Hei Baisheng immediately recognized this as his own Hua Yuan from the Realm of Sacred Reason.

Not only that, but his Black and White Servants had now reformed, becoming servants of this strange will within the Realm of Divine Reason.

The Master of Black and White looked somewhat displeased, but he did not act, choosing instead to wait and see, wanting to understand what this strange will within the Realm of Divine Reason was truly after.

"Are you trying to make a deal with me?"

The blond-haired youth who emerged from the Realm of Divine Reason was none other than Su En.

However, he had long since become the leader of the Plague Heaven. Facing a True Path-level figure and a Supreme Shattering-level being, he showed not the slightest fear, appearing remarkably composed.

"The Heavenly Will is entangled with the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance. In the Vast Expanse of ages to come, the myriad realms will lose the governance and control of the Heavenly Will, and all order will descend into chaos."

"If we wish to forge a new path, we must know how to proceed beyond the True Path, yet you have not yet grown to a level comparable to the complete Heavenly Will."

"I believe we can cooperate. You reveal the secrets of the Heavenly Dao’s authority to us, paving a new path for us, and we will help you locate the remaining fragments of the Heavenly Will, allowing you to devour them and replace the previous Heavenly Will."

The figure of the Three-Headed Master was vague and shadowy within the fabric of spacetime, not entirely distinct, yet his words carried great weight, imbued with a seductive allure as he sought to strike a deal with the Plague Heaven before him.

The Black-White Lord’s eyes flickered. Possessing extraordinary cunning, he had already surmised a certain outcome and the nature of the power at play.

The current situation was most likely orchestrated by the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, and the appearance of this Plague Heaven was no exception.

Otherwise, this new path would never have come into being.

The leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance was playing a grand game; during this time, everyone involved—including the Heavenly Will—was merely a pawn in his scheme.

The birth of the New Path inevitably requires the Heavenly Will to release the constraints on the Heavenly Dao from its authority, only then can they experience what is known as the Dao Realm.

Otherwise, the Heavenly Dao Realm would forever remain a mirage, a castle in the air, beyond the reach of the Vast Expanse.

This so-called "Plague Heaven" before us is clearly a part of the Heavenly Will, so he must surely know the secret of the Dao Realm.

In the eyes of the Black-White Lord and the Three-Headed Lord, this is precisely how the Plague Heaven before them appeared.

If the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance intends to raise a "venomous insect," he will not allow the Plague Heaven to grow too powerful, lest it leave them—these True Path-level and Supreme Shattered-realm beings—feeling helpless.

Only when the disparity in strength between the two sides is not too great, reaching a state of mutual balance, can the so-called "rearing of the plague" achieve its ultimate goal.

Apart from this Epidemic Heaven, there are four other sources of the Heavenly Will, which are certainly also within the scope of this “rearing of the bug,” serving as part of the E-Buy-Ying-Yue.

The Three-Headed Master replied calmly, showing no sign of anger at Su En’s tone.

On the contrary, he knew that these successive “Five Heavens” had undoubtedly been tainted by the Heavenly Will—that lofty, all-seeing nature - that surveys all living beings—and could never place them on the same level or plane.

The Black-and-White Lord also stared at Su En before him. He could clearly sense that vast, supreme aura emanating from him. The authority of the Heavenly Dao he wielded began to hum and tremble slightly, conveying a sense of fear—as if, should the true Heavenly Will so desire, it could at any moment reclaim this portion of power, causing him to plummet back to the End of the Path.

"Only by comprehending the authority of the Heavenly Dao and grasping its true essence from within can one stand firm in the Dao Realm, no longer relying on external forces."

A sink of dread fell over the Black-White Lord’s heart.

In the spacetime of the Old Cosmic Order, those who had comprehended the authority of the Nine Supreme Celestial Daos were known as Dao Ancestors. It can be said that only the path of Supreme Shattering can, in this era, provide the remedy to break free from the authority of the Dao.
