That rampaging energy contained creation, vitality, destruction, and reincarnation… Even a single wisp of it, as it dissipated into the surrounding chaos, gave birth to one universe after another.

The next moment, an unimaginable crimson light flared up, engulfing the heavens and earth.

At the same time, no one could clearly discern or comprehend such a technique.

The entire Vast Expanse seemed to have plunged into a state of utter shock and deathly silence at that moment.

Within the Nine Heavens, all the Dao Realm beings and supreme sentient beings were filled with shock and disbelief.

“Could this be the Ruler of Heaven intervening?”

“This is one of the few remaining leaders of the Fate-Defying Palace…”

At this moment, no words could describe the horror and disbelief in his heart.

He had thought that stirring up a leader of the Fate-Defying Palace would be enough to annihilate the invading Dark Tribes.

But the Ruler of Heaven had awakened at this critical moment and, across vast spans of time and space, had actually clashed with that leader.

Hadn’t the Lord of Heaven only just awakened? How could he possess such power?

Or had they vastly underestimated the Ruler of Heaven’s might?

"Is this merely a projection of his power? Not his true form?"

"It seems this is indeed a test, but for the Ruler of Confinement to personally intervene—and to block it so effortlessly—shows that the Ruler of Heaven at the height of his power was truly far superior to the three leaders."

"But for the Ruler of Confinement to intervene personally—could it be that he wishes to determine whether the Ruler of Heaven has truly revived, or if it is merely a facade to deceive others?"

Within that vast expanse of spacetime, the boundless eyes of the Eternal Ancestor narrowed, his expression growing somber.

Since its founding, the Fate-Defying Palace has had three leaders. The one who has now intervened is the “Restrainer” among them. No one knows his origins or identity; not even those at the Ancestral Dao Realm can make contact with that being.

Members of the Fate-Defying Palace, such as himself, could only refer to him as the Master of Restraint.

Just now, witnessing his intervention, even he was utterly shocked—he almost thought the Master of Restraint had gone mad. After all, such brazenness meant that the more traces his actions left behind, the harder it would be to hide and the more difficult it would be to conceal.

Moreover, once revealed and seen by the world, the commotion and karmic consequences would be immense, making it easy to be held accountable during the Great Calamity.

This was also why he had hesitated to intervene when the Dark Clan attacked earlier—he did not wish to become entangled in such karmic consequences.

Especially since, in his perception, the Great Calamity of this era was about to descend. The more he stirred things up before it arrived, the greater the likelihood he would face retribution during the ordeal.

"We humbly welcome our Lord..."

Amidst the endless black tide, countless dark beings prostrated themselves there, filled with fervor.

The four High Priests, still trembling with fear and their eyes wide with terror, knelt down in turn, their devotion boundless.

At this moment, the heavens and earth seemed to have been completely torn apart. Amidst the turbulent and chaotic Vast Expanse, a pair of indifferent and serene eyes gazed upon this place from a distant dimension of time and space.

Endless dark matter swirled around, sending shivers down the spine and making the heart tremble; it felt as though a single glance from those eyes would cause one’s soul to wither and shatter.

Yet around these eyes, everything warped, and the scene blurred to the extreme; even the ethereal and elusive substances of time, causality, and destiny would give way as they flowed past.

It was a state beyond words, as if “He” were all matter, all energy, the origin of all things… the very embodiment of the inconceivable.

Before the Nine Heavens, the pale hand that had retreated seemed to fall silent for a moment, then a voice, ancient and timeless, resounded.

"Return."

As this single word echoed, the once chaotic and shattered cosmos began to restore itself. A terrifying and unimaginable power descended, causing the remaining layers of the Nine Heavens—which had long since collapsed—to be restored.

Mountains and rivers remained as before; lakes, marshes, mountain ranges, cities, towers, palaces, and divine islands… as if time had reversed, they returned to their original forms. Even the living beings who had fallen in the carnage of this great war reappeared.

Some beings, their bodies corroded by darkness, were shrouded in rising mist as they struggled; before long, they regained consciousness.

This scene was so overwhelming and incredible that all the beings and cultivators of the Nine Heavens were left speechless, their eyes wide with awe at such a divine miracle.

Yet, in a distant dimension of time and space, those indifferent and serene eyes remained suspended high above, showing no sign of movement and offering no response.

All the dark races and beings knelt there, like a boundless ocean of darkness, silent and still.

"Even at the height of the Celestial Dynasty’s power, they would not have dared to invade the Nine Heavens so brazenly. You have only just revived—do you truly intend to wage war against the Nine Heavens?"

“With the Great Calamity imminent, why waste your energy in this manner?”

An ancient, resonant voice echoed through the Vast Expanse of the multiverse, calm and devoid of much fluctuation. Yet the moment the voice fell, boundless power surged through the realms of the true worlds, piercing past and present, and shaking every inch of space-time and the void.

Only at that moment did countless beings and cultivators realize that within the Nine Heavens, there existed such an unimaginable presence—one powerful enough to stand against the Dark Clans.

Within the Nine Heavens, countless cheers erupted.

For many, this was the first time they had learned of such profound power stationed here. If that were the case, what was there left to fear or dread?

The panic and fear of moments past seemed to vanish in an instant, replaced by joy and elation on every face.

And the instant that voice faded, a figure clad in a yellow robe appeared silently in the void beyond the Nine Heavens.

The figure’s features were indistinct, but its eyes were deeply weathered, as if they held all the secrets of the world.

He stood there; the myriad paths beneath his feet seemed to have fallen still, and all sound ceased. He appeared as if he were the very end of the Dao; by his side, all matter was suppressed.

Fate, time, and space held no meaning before this figure.

"—The Supreme Being at the End of the Path..."

The expressions of the four High Priests of the Dark Clan all shifted dramatically. They had never imagined that the true depth of the Nine Heavens would be so terrifying.

"Has the Yellow Lord also appeared?"

Witnessing this scene, the colossal figure within that realm of space and time shifted its gaze ever so slightly. As it rose and took a step, its form rapidly shrank to the size of an ordinary person, then appeared silently beside the figure clad in yellow robes.

The moment he appeared, the very fabric of this realm seemed unable to contain the auras of both figures; it began to crack, and even the Great Dao itself developed clearly visible fissures.

"Eternal Ancestor?"

The figure in the yellow robe looked at him and gave a slight nod, as if in greeting.

“Another being who has reached the end of the path…”

Countless civilizations across the True Realms were now trembling with utter shock; it was simply beyond belief.

Throughout history, such "End-of-the-Path" beings have been few and far between; even at the Ancestral Dao Realm, such figures are rarely seen. Yet now, they have appeared one after another beyond the Nine Heavens.

Is this the true depth of the Nine Heavens?

Not to mention the one who had just spoken—was his true strength and cultivation level absolutely beyond the Path’s End? Could he be a Supreme Shattered Being?

At that moment, the entire Vast Expanse fell into a deathly silence, devoid of any sound or movement. Even the boundless expanse, which had been crashing like waves, seemed to have completely frozen in place.

No one could have imagined that the revival of the Lord of Heaven would summon so many terrifying and unimaginable beings. Even when the Lord of Heaven once ruled the Vast Expanse, such a shocking spectacle had never been witnessed.

"It truly can no longer be contained..."

In a void of time and space, a being whose presence was deeply concealed opened its eyes, witnessed this scene, and was deeply shaken.

Within the vast, decaying palace, suffused with an aura of slaughter, the Lord of the Hall of the Unborn gazed on with an impassive expression, showing no sign of disturbance.

For a moment, gazes from every corner of the cosmos turned toward this scene, filled with a trembling so profound it defied description.

In comparison, the fierce battle raging outside the Ancient Hidden Civilization—no matter how chaotic—seemed like children playing house, utterly incomparable to this.

Deep within the Sea of Reincarnation, within a majestic hall.

Existences such as the World Ruler, the Lord of Reincarnation, the Primordial Void, and the Lord of Destiny all turned their gaze in that direction, their expressions varying widely.

The Lord of Reincarnation wore a look of deep contemplation.

Meanwhile, outside the Nine Heavens, the vast, endless horde of dark beings remained utterly still and silent.

Vast, formless darkness cascaded down like a starry river, as if a grand, ancient blood-red altar hung high above.

Upon that blood-red altar, a vague, towering figure emerged just as silently. It seemed to have walked out of time immemorial, yet also as if it had always been there. The spacetime around it remained perpetually in a state of collapse, decay, and destruction.

With the reawakening of the Lord of Heaven and the emergence of the many supreme beings from the Nine Heavens, the Vast Expanse seemed to have fallen into a state of absolute, deathly silence.

"What does this mean?"

"Do you truly intend to wage war against us?"

The voice echoing from beyond the Nine Heavens resounded once more. Compared to before, it carried a note of greater decisiveness and directness. Moreover, the mention of “we” sent shivers down the spines of the civilizations of the True Realm—could it be that within the depths of the Nine Heavens, there existed another being of equal standing?

BOOM!!!
