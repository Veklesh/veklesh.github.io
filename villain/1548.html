"……"

"I refuse to accept this..."

The Lord of the Void struggled ceaselessly, his entire body enveloped in dazzling, brilliant runes. The authority of the Heavenly Dao seemed poised to fuse completely with every inch of his flesh and blood, taking root deep within.

Once, he had looked down upon the succession of eras and the rise and fall of Civilizations, standing high above all, ruling over countless realms of time and space. He had never imagined that one day he would end up in such a pitiful state, with even his own nature being worn away, reduced to a puppet of the Heavenly Will.

He was, after all, a being who had set foot on the True Path—how could his end be so pitiful?

"Ah... "

The Lord of the Void refused to accept this fate. With a low growl, he mustered his last ounce of strength, attempting to shatter his own Dao Fruit in a desperate act of self-destruction.

A being of his caliber would rather perish and see his Dao vanish than become a puppet, a mere walking corpse.

Suddenly, blinding divine light erupted violently from within the Lord of the Void, as if a super-multiverse were collapsing.

From every fissure in his flesh, it seemed as though countless particles of chaotic light were spewing forth.

In the realm of the Dao of Space-Time, the Lord of the Void had actually advanced very far; his true form was, in fact, a vast and boundless expanse of space-time itself, where time, space, and all matter within were created by him alone. It could be said that even a single drop of his blood could form its own space-time.

At this moment, this vast and boundless spacetime universe collapsed and exploded with a thunderous roar. Countless blazing True Fires of the Dao—numbering "697"—thoroughly ignited the endless particles of chaotic light.

However, though the Lord of the Void had exhausted every means to destroy his own Dao Fruit, the aura of the authority of the Heavenly Dao had long since permeated his body.

Bright patterns blossomed, enveloping and wrapping his shattered, collapsing physical form.

Within them, an indescribably majestic and supreme power surged, causing all worlds to tremble. The raging True Fire of the Dao was extinguished, and his torn flesh reassembled and healed.

All of the Lord of the Void’s struggles were in vain.

He was like an insect caught in a spider’s web; the more violently he struggled, the tighter the web contracted, until it bound him so firmly he could no longer move an inch.

Soon, the surging, terrifying power in this place subsided; all the Dao Fires were extinguished, and the light dimmed.

The Lord of the Void’s figure reappeared before the world, though compared to before, he now stood taller and more aloof. His pale silver eyes held an abyss of depth, as if capable of reflecting the entire cosmos. Merely standing there, he exuded an aura that made all Dao tremble.

Even ordinary True Path-level beings were nowhere near this powerful.

The Nine Minor Avatars of Heaven all bowed their heads before him in unison, paying their respects.

On the front lines, a deathly silence fell. Even the Lord of the World, the Lord of Reincarnation, and the others felt their hearts tremble with fear, their eyes betraying a hint of dread.

Though they were all beings who wielded the authority of the Heavenly Dao, none of them knew whether they themselves would follow in the footsteps of the Lord of the Void in the very next moment.

At present, only the first of the Five Celestial Avatars had appeared. Who were the remaining four?

At this moment, throughout the Vast Expanse of multiverses, all beings who held the authority of the Heavenly Dao felt their minds waver, their spines tingling with cold dread.

Even the Lord of the World and the Lord of Reincarnation, who stood on the side of the Ancient Hidden Civilization, did not wish to become a Great Avatar of Heaven.

As evidenced by the Lord of the Void before them, the authority of the Heavenly Dao had long since erased his own will; he was no longer his original self, but merely a walking corpse, a puppet.

Unlike ordinary puppets, because he was shaped by the authority of the Heavenly Dao, he was called the Great Avatar of Heaven.

But in essence, there is no difference between the two.

They devoted themselves wholeheartedly to serving the Ancient Hidden Civilization. When the Lord of the Void turned against them, he had hoped to evade retribution, yet he was the very first to fall victim to the calamity.

The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness truly has a heart of stone.

The two trembled slightly.

"Who is next?"

Over on the side of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, the Lord of Power had long been aware of the flaws in the Heavenly Dao, so he had long since broken free from its shackles and remained unaffected. He too sensed the faint fluctuations emanating from the Authority of Strength at that moment, but paid them no heed.

His gaze swept across the vast battlefield, then locked onto the Master of Command standing beside him.

The Lord of Restraint’s expression suddenly changed as he said, “It could still be…”

Gu Xihan, the First Ancestor of the Gu Clan, also noticed that something was amiss with the Master of the Office.

The Master of the System stood in the vast void, her bare feet as white as snow. Her eyes were tightly closed, her long black hair flowing in the wind, and her snow-white robes fluttered without a breeze. One by one, crystalline and dazzling Heavenly Dao runes appeared on her arms and face, radiating waves of the Heavenly Dao’s aura.

This boundless, supreme aura was slowly awakening, keeping the First Ancestor of the Gu Clan and the others at a safe distance, unable to approach.

"How is this possible..."

"Is the Lord of the Bureau about to become a great incarnation of Heaven as well?"

"How could this be?"

This scene caused the expressions of many powerful figures from the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Palace of Defiance to change abruptly. Many found it hard to accept, their bodies trembling with grief, anger, and helplessness.

Existences such as the Yellow Lord, the Eternal Ancestor, the Grave Keeper, and the Mirror Lord also paled, unable to accept it.

"Once one grasps the authority of the Heavenly Dao, none can escape its corruption. Everyone is the same. At this point, it all comes down to one’s own destiny."

The Forgetful Immortal spoke with a heavy look in his eyes, never having expected that even the leader of the Defiance of Fate Palace would fall victim to its corruption.

She held the authority over fate; logically, she should have understood the risks and rewards of the authority of the Heavenly Dao.

"Haha…"

"You never expected this, did you? Even the Lord of the Camp will become a great incarnation of Heaven."

"I wonder what you can possibly rely on now. Once the Heavenly Lord intervenes, everything will come to an end."

The Ancient Treasure Emperor burst into laughter, his gaze fierce and filled with mockery. Since he had no connection whatsoever to the authority of the Heavenly Dao, he felt not the slightest fear.

Meanwhile, on another distant battlefield, the Lord of the Hall of Slaughter and the Lord of Life were undergoing astonishing transformations.

The Authority of Killing and the Authority of Life emerged, converging and colliding in the vast, ethereal heights. They erupted with billions of strands of vast, supreme energy, finally coalescing into two torrents of intensely brilliant light that engulfed both of them.

Though they reacted immediately, it was already too late. The power of the Heavenly Dao’s authority coalesced into two massive cocoons, completely enveloping them.

Boundless, overwhelming power surged and raged within, creating a chaotic blur—much like the primordial chaos of creation itself, where destruction and rebirth intertwined as two utterly distinct supreme forces collided.

A deafening roar!!!

The Vast Expanse of all worlds resonated in unison, an unimaginable sound that seemed to traverse billions of realms of time and space, descending upon the boundless masses.

Finally, that realm boiled over completely, only to be swallowed by endless light.

When the light faded.

The figures of the Lord of the Hall of Slaughter and the Lord of Life slowly emerged from within, becoming two more great avatars of the heavens. The aura of destruction and rebirth surged through the cosmos, sending shivers down the spines of all.

"Lord of the Hall of Slaughter, Lord of Life..."

"Ah..."

The Immortal Forgetter, who had maintained good personal relations with both, now witnessed these two—who had battled for countless eons—finally transforming together into great celestial incarnations. A deep sorrow welled up in his eyes, and he let out a long sigh, filled with countless emotions.

In the Vast Expanse of the other realms, the Lord of Black and White, who had been watching this scene intently, took a deep breath and suppressed the slight chill in his heart.

"I knew long ago that the authority of the Heavenly Dao would face trouble. Fortunately, I was prepared..."

"As expected, the Declining Heaven has kept its promise; I have suppressed this wave of transformation and decay."

The Lord of Black and White wielded the authority of Yin and Yang and had historically been known as the Lord of Yin and Yang.

Renowned for his wisdom and cunning, he was a master of intrigue and had detected the flaws within the Heavenly Dao many years ago.

In particular, before Gu Changge, the former leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, had vanished, the Heavenly Will had once used the authority of the Heavenly Dao to send down a call, seducing them and even promising the Way of the Future.

However, the Lord of Black and White chose not to believe it.

"Heh, I won’t lie to you. Those who wield the authority of the Heavenly Dao will ultimately become its puppets. Otherwise, I wouldn’t have devoted myself to researching the method to shatter the ‘Wuzheng’."

"This is merely the beginning of the game."

Beside the Lord of Black and White, a hazy, misty vapor began to spread. From within it emerged a figure clad in a golden robe, with a beard as sharp as a halberd and hair bound by a golden band. The human head in the center bore a face of unspeakable ugliness, as if it were the embodiment of all the evils of the world, while the left and right sides each bore the head of a dragon and a phoenix, respectively.

This was none other than the Three-Headed Sovereign.

He had once grasped the concept of the spacetime of the New Cosmic Order, using it to propose the doctrines of "The End of the Path" and "Supreme Shattering." It could be said that the Three-Headed Lord was one of the earliest sages to embark on the path of Supreme Shattering, and is considered one of the most ancient chaotic beings in this world.

When the aura of the Plague Heaven emerged in the Realm of Sacred Truth, he had appeared there and clashed with the Lord of Black and White, attempting to seize the source of the Plague Heaven, but ultimately failed, and the two eventually reached an agreement.

It was precisely through this agreement that the Lord of Black and White later had the Plague Heaven help him suppress the allure and seduction of the Heavenly Dao’s authority; after all, the Plague Heaven itself is part of the Heavenly Will, and the two share the same origin and lineage.

"Indeed, had I not made that choice back then, I too would likely have become a great incarnation of Heaven."

"Even though the Heavenly Will of the Heavenly Dao is now entangled with the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, it still exerts influence over the entire Vast Expanse—it must not be underestimated."

The Lord of Black and White still felt a chill run down his spine, filled with lingering dread.

"After all, the entire Vast Expanse and its myriad realms were derived from the power of the Heavenly Will. How could they so easily slip from its grasp?"

"However, the Heavenly Will’s grip on the entire Vast Expanse is gradually loosening. The fact that the Supreme Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness can only summon a Heavenly Avatar through his so-called ‘Method of Commanding the Heavens’ speaks volumes. Do you really think he doesn’t want to harness the power of the Heavenly Will?"

"No, it’s because he can’t. He simply can’t do it."

"The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin can barely save himself now. He was too arrogant and overconfident, and he severely underestimated the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance. You must understand that all the remaining power of the Heavenly Will is now being used to counter the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance. What does that even mean? Surely the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin can’t fail to grasp this?"

"Haha, but perhaps he truly cannot comprehend it. The Land Of Truth carries an innate arrogance and contempt toward this side; he cannot accept that such an unimaginable being as the Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance could emerge here."

"You must understand that even the malevolent remnants of the deities left behind by the Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance are beyond his capacity to handle. The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness is merely holding on by a thread. He is already regretting his overconfidence and is likely already considering how to make a clean escape..."

Across the vast expanse of time and space, the Three-Headed Master watched the scene at the front lines of the Ancient Hidden Civilization intently, letting out a series of cold laughs. Every word he spoke was filled with admiration for the Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.
