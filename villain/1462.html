"Who has disobeyed the Lord's orders..."

The figure appeared only for a moment; seemingly wary of something, it vanished just as quickly.

The Hall of No-Life is an extremely mysterious entity. To call it a "faction" is not quite accurate; it is more accurately described as a realm, a sanctuary, or a dimension—it has no fixed designation.

The Lord of the Hall of No-Life believes this realm to be a prison, where sentient beings are destined to lack vitality; it is better to die early and put an end to past attachments.

In his philosophy, life itself is an eternal tragedy and suffering; only death can bring liberation and freedom.

To realize this philosophy, the Hall has long sought suitable candidates, granting them immortality alongside eternal suffering and torment. Only by slaughtering other lives and bestowing liberation upon them can they alleviate their own agony. When their cultivation and power reach a certain stage, they may be granted the opportunity to die.

Members of the Hall of No-Birth are ranked by serial number; they cannot be killed by others, but only granted death by the Hall Master.

Thus, each member ceaselessly completes the Hall’s tasks in pursuit of death…

When Number One dies, a new Number One will take his place, and so the cycle repeats, having gone through countless iterations.

In the entire world, the number of sentient beings aware of the Hall of No Birth’s existence can be counted on one hand. They are, for the most part, truly ancient beings who have survived the cataclysms of the cosmic era—living for untold ages, to the point where the concept of lifespan no longer applies to them.

When the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance engaged in a fierce battle, the appearance of members from the Hall of No-Birth caused many sentient beings in the Chaos Realm to sense that something was amiss.

Yet none dared to reveal themselves; even those who caught a glimpse of them immediately and thoroughly erased every trace of their presence and karmic connection, leaving not a single shred behind.

This was a caution that had become second nature, etched deep into their souls.

The downfall of the Righteous Unity Alliance had become an irreversible trend; it was simply that this trend had arrived too late. For the civilizations of the Vast Expanse of True Realms, the Dark Sacrifice had begun, and no one could escape its grasp.

At this moment, the only hope lay with the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

A cold, blood-red altar stood beyond the realms, vast and boundless.

Boundless dark matter poured forth from it, as if a gash had been torn open in the heavens and earth, and an endless tide of dark energy surged toward the worlds and universes in every direction.

Amidst that dark matter, dark warships drifted and emerged, brimming with a swarm of dark beings. They wielded various dark celestial blades and spears, clad in dark armor, their presence cold and solemn.

Ancient horns were sounded, directing the dark beings as they began to slaughter across all realms.

In other realms, celestial beasts—true dragons, immortal phoenixes, and white tigers—seemingly cast from dark divine metal, carried hordes of terrifying dark beings as they waged war across all realms, gathering offerings for the great sacrifice.

Wherever they passed, the cosmos collapsed and worlds crumbled. Even beings of the Dao Realm could not alter this fate, and could only become sacrifices.

Woo-woo-woo...

The sound of trumpets swept through the air, like the wails of ancient gods and demons, or the heavens weeping blood.

The realm where the Ethereal Sanctuary lay remained shrouded in endless black mist; the various races and realms outside had no idea what was happening within.

Even many of the powerful figures within the Heaven Slaying Alliance were unaware of what was unfolding inside. Could this truly be a conspiracy by the Dark Clans, intended to trap Alliance Leader Gu Changge within?

However, regardless of the circumstances, the most urgent matter at hand was to halt the Dark Beings’ grand ritual.

Ling Yuling, donning armor to act as the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s regent, issued a series of orders to repel the Dark Clans’ grand ritual while simultaneously settling accounts with the Righteous Unity Alliance.

Following the collapse of the Righteous Unity Alliance, the various races and realms of the Vast Expanse finally grasped the gravity of the situation. Setting aside their previous prejudices, they began actively cooperating with the Heaven Slaying Alliance to jointly resist the Dark Rites. Every day, they dispatched numerous powerful members of their tribes, and they readily fulfilled every request for resources.

Even without Gu Changge at the helm, the Heaven Slaying Alliance faced no major issues. After all, Ling Yuling was indeed a venerable figure who had once stood against the Dark Clans, and everyone held her in high regard.

Under her guidance and leadership, everything ran smoothly and orderly.

Meanwhile, the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, having concluded its campaign against the ancient kingdom of Nanzhao, dispatched a massive army at this time to various civilizations across the True Realms to defend against the Dark Clans’ Great Sacrifice.

For a time, the situation was clear amidst the Vast Expanse: all civilizations of the True Realm, led by the Heaven Slaying Alliance, began a period of genuine resistance against the Dark Tribes.

However, with the collapse and disintegration of the Righteous Unity Alliance, the figure of the Emperor of Weiyang was nowhere to be seen throughout the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty; it seemed he, like the high-ranking officials of the Righteous Unity Alliance, had fled and gone into hiding.

Of course, no one paid any attention to such matters at the moment.

Ling Yuling appeared on the front lines every day. Drawing on her past experience fighting the Dark Tribes, she forged the Immortal Great Wall, using the shattered fragments of the cosmos as its borders, and built fortresses there.

Civilizations from all corners of the True Realms continuously sent various resources, dubbing the area “Mountains And Seas Pass” and forging it into the most impregnable and fortified defensive line.

Behind that defensive line, numerous array masters at the Dao Realm level arrived, setting up one terrifying restriction after another. Surging divine light pierced through heaven and earth, stirring the runes of the Great Dao, which intertwined and permeated the area.

Beyond the restrictive formations lay a realm of chaos, personally forged by numerous Ancestral Dao Realm beings.

Beyond the Exile Realm lay one floating star and continent after another, where the various races and True Realms had dispatched their strongest cultivators to maintain a permanent garrison.

Upon the Immortal Great Wall, the high command of the Heaven Slaying Alliance dispatched powerful cultivators daily to patrol and ward off the arrival of the Dark Clans. Simultaneously, they sent out numerous small teams to assist the clans of distant realms in evacuating and relocating within the confines of the Immortal Great Wall.

Under these circumstances, the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s influence has surged beyond its former heights. Unlike when Gu Changge presided over the Alliance, the Heaven Slaying Alliance under Ling Yuling’s leadership has instilled a sense of security—a true peace of mind—in all the races and living beings.

With the Heaven Slaying Alliance serving as the main force to withstand the Great Dark Ritual,

The Dark Tribes, however, were not particularly anxious; they began to focus their efforts on other barren regions of the cosmos, such as chaotic zones, where some Divine Path civilizations were also attacked and annihilated.

Among the divine civilizations, the Tai Xuan Divine Realm was tainted and corrupted by dark matter, becoming a territory of the Dark Clans. Other divine realms hidden within spacetime were similarly invaded by the Dark Clans, who sought to sacrifice the entire divine civilization.

In addition to the Tai Xuan Divine Realm, other divine realms such as the Jiu Ao Divine Realm have also been eroded and attacked; the chaotic battles have raged on for many years. The Dark Ancestral Goddess of Tai Xuan personally leads the subjects of her Dark Divine Kingdom to attack the remaining divine realms.

Following the commencement of the Great Dark Ritual, numerous powerful dark beings have emerged to assist her in her assault on the other divine realms.

The entire Divine Path civilization is now in chaos and turmoil; the other divine realms are on the brink of collapse. Some ancestral gods who rarely manifest in the various realms have been forced to step forward and plead for help from the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

However, the Heaven Slaying Alliance initially offered no response. Ling Yuling had intended to intervene, but was met with unanimous objections from the leaders of all races, who pleaded with her not to meddle.

This is because the Divine Path civilization, both in the past and present, has never interfered in the affairs of other civilizations within the Vast Expanse. Faced with the descent of the Black Calamity, they have consistently adopted an attitude of indifference, refusing to offer any support or assistance.

The Ancestral Gods, who prided themselves on being primordial beings, disdained to be tainted by the “post-heavenly” mundane world; the consequences they face today are entirely of their own making.

Consequently, many civilizations in the True Realm have no intention of offering support. Moreover, while the Heaven Slaying Alliance appears powerful and confident on the surface, it harbors a major internal crisis that remains unresolved.

That is the matter of the Black Lotus Sect.

Many civilizations of the True Realm believe that the Black Lotus Sect is a tool of the Dark Races, a scheme concocted jointly by the former Righteous Unity Alliance and the Dark Races.

The goal was to trap the various races attending the Immortal Dao Alliance’s founding assembly—and to trap the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s leader, Gu Changge, within it—thereby giving the Dark Tribes the confidence to hold their Dark Grand Ritual.

These speculations and views have gained widespread acceptance.

Therefore, the immediate priority remains resolving internal issues; there is simply no time to concern oneself with the fate of the Divine Path Civilization. Given its current state of decline, whether to save it or not makes little difference—the power of each of its remaining Ancestral Gods is, at most, on par with that of beings in the Ancestral Dao Realm.

There were certainly no more than ten Ancestral Gods remaining in the Divine Path Civilization. Such a force was not worth the immense cost for the civilizations of the True Realms to expend in a rescue mission.

Ling Yuling had no choice but to accept this reality. She redirected her focus toward fortifying defensive lines and maintaining control over the Heaven Slaying Alliance. She was well aware of Gu Changge’s capabilities and did not believe he would be trapped within the Ethereal Sanctuary. However, despite reconnaissance efforts by powerful figures from various races, that region remained shrouded in an eerie darkness and endless black mist, rendering it impenetrable. No one could enter, and the true nature of the situation remained a mystery.

The only option was to wait.

Time passed.

With the commencement of the Great Dark Ritual, the entire Vast Expanse entered its darkest and most devastating era. Although the Heaven Slaying Alliance served as the main force to resist and repel the threat, they remained powerless against the nearly indestructible dark hordes.

Vast swathes of territory were ravaged by the dark substance; countless worlds and universes withered away, and all realms were plunged into ruin.

However, in the case of cosmic realms guarded by beings of the Dao Realm, the dark hordes did not completely annihilate them. They merely exterminated and carried away sentient beings of the Immortal King Realm and above; the remaining beings were too weak, lacking sufficient vital energy to serve as nourishment.

Wave after wave of worlds were harvested and carried away, like a field of chives.

Although aided by the Heaven Slaying Alliance, over the years, vast numbers of races have still vanished entirely. The dark altar beyond the Vast Expanse grows ever more majestic and solid, and ancient ritual chants resound there, echoing in the ears of every living being.

During this time, the Divine Path civilization, which had previously put up a desperate resistance, also collapsed and was utterly consumed by darkness. Facing the crushing onslaught of the Dark Clans, it had no chance of defense.

One of the ancient ancestral gods emerged, possessing power comparable to one who had survived nine Celestial Waning Tribulations, and attempted to resist. However, the High Priest himself intervened, piercing the ancestral god’s scapula with chains of darkness to seal the divine fire within its body, then carried the ancestral god back to the Dark Clans’ lair.

Before long, the Dark Clan would give birth to an immensely powerful Dark Ancestral God.

Emotions of despair, fear, and dread swept across the Vast Expanse, infecting all sentient beings, who lived in constant fear and trepidation.

While the Dark Clan devoured and sacrificed the remaining worlds and universes, they never ceased attempting to breach the defenses created by the Heaven Slaying Alliance. Though their efforts were in vain, each attempt sparked a massive war.

As the Deputy Alliance Leader, Ling Yuling felt exhausted and powerless with each such encounter, unsure how long this situation would persist.

The Dark Clans were merely testing the waters; they had not yet launched a full-scale assault against the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Meanwhile, Gu Changge remained trapped within the Ethereal Sanctuary, showing no signs of escape.

After failing to make headway against the Heaven Slaying Alliance, the Dark Clans began to shift their focus to other realms within the Vast Expanse—such as the Nine Heavens, reputed to be the source of immortality, and the Ancient Treasure Civilization, said to control the Great Calamity.

The Nine Heavens and the Ancient Treasure Civilization have always been two exceptionally unique regions within the Vast Expanse.

The Nine Heavens is said to be supremely transcendent, regarded as the origin of the Immortal Dao system. Some claim that it was actually the beings of the Nine Heavens who pioneered the cultivation methods of the Immortal Dao; in ancient times, the Immortal Dao did not exist.

Of course, the truth of such claims remains unknown, but in the eyes of every practitioner of the Immortal Civilization, the Nine Heavens holds extraordinary significance.

As for the Ancient Treasure Civilization, it is home to the Epoch Mother Tree, which gave birth to and shaped the myriad heavens and universes. Naturally, the Epoch Mother Tree is also known as the World Tree.

Every leaf and branch of this tree bears a vast realm; the birth, death, and cycles of reincarnation across all worlds ripple at its base, transforming into a deep, azure sea—a sight of extraordinary mystery.

Some say that behind the Ancient Zang Civilization lies the hidden path to the Realm of Truth. Each time a cosmic cataclysm descends, the Ancient Zang Civilization acts as the vanguard, exercising authority on behalf of Heaven.

And whenever that time comes, the path connecting the two realms emerges. Then, the beings responsible for the cataclysm in the Realm of Truth appear, turning the wheel of destruction to annihilate all worlds and plunge the cosmos into the Eon’s Winter.

After all, an epochal cataclysm differs from minor or major cataclysms; it is a vast calamity that engulfs the entire Vast Expanse. Even the mightiest True Realms cannot escape it; there is no single True Realm that can remain untouched.

Such claims cannot be verified, but the one thing that can be confirmed is that the Ancient Tomb Civilization is indeed connected to the Real Land. Through the vast expanse of time, the legacy and existence of the Ancient Tomb Civilization have never been severed.

It is simply that in each era, it has been known by a different name; its true name should be the Ancient Burial Civilization.

This is a civilization that has witnessed the burial of the ages with its own eyes, bearing witness to the succession of eras and the endless cycle of reincarnation.
