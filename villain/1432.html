The Vast Expanse is vast and boundless, stretching endlessly, containing countless universes and infinite spacetime.

The Tai Xuan Divine Realm is situated within the overlapping dimensions of time and space, where countless brilliant, ancient worlds are stacked like nebulae, surrounding it like stars encircling the moon, supporting the Tai Xuan Divine Realm at its center.

Endless, boundless divine power of faith converges from countless realms and universes, surging forth like rivers and tides, overwhelming all, to form a supreme divine crown and throne.

Surrounding the Taixuan Divine Realm lies the Multiverse Void Sea, composed of countless fragmented spacetime dimensions, existing in no single realm of past, future, or present.

Even beings at the Ancestral Dao Realm would find it difficult to detect its presence or determine its spatial coordinates.

This is also the very foundation upon which every Ancestral God relies to remain safely concealed within the multi-dimensional fabric of spacetime.

Over the course of countless ages, innumerable divine realms have perished and been destroyed, and just as many Ancestral Gods have fallen.

Only those Ancestral Gods capable of evading their natural enemies and navigating fortune and misfortune have endured since time immemorial.

To the countless inhabitants of the Taixuan Divine Realm, the Taixuan Ancestral Goddess is the sole creator deity. Her existence shields all realms, ensuring that the Taixuan Divine Realm remains untouched by foreign invaders and the ravages of war, allowing it to endure to this day.

Yet since the last time the Taishuan Ancestral Goddess returned, the entire Taishuan Divine Realm has undergone earth-shattering changes.

Countless citizens of the divine realms are filled with deep concern, anxiety, and fear.

The divine light shrouding the borderlands of the divine realm has grown dim and hazy, as if corrupted by some terrible erosion. The outlying regions have grown increasingly bleak, as if they might vanish at any moment.

The waves of the Multiverse Void Sea kept crashing in, like a terrifying darkness capable of swallowing everything; any living being that set foot within it would perish, instantly crushed and shattered by the forces between multiple dimensions.

Yet the vast divine light shielding the Divine Realm was the very embodiment of the power of the Ancestral God Taixuan. What did the dimming of this light signify? It meant that the power of the Ancestral God Taixuan was waning, gradually fading away.

Perhaps even she has reached her limit, no longer able to continue protecting the Divine Realm of Taixuan.

For the countless subjects of the Taishuan Divine Realm, this is an indescribably devastating blow, as if the heavens themselves were collapsing.

"Once the Ancestral Goddess’s power is completely gone, the Void Beasts of the Multiverse Void Sea will seize the opportunity to invade and devour our realm. With our current strength, there is simply no way we can withstand so many Void Beasts..."

On the outskirts of the Taishuan Divine Kingdom, figures shrouded in divine light—each bearing a dazzling divine halo, multiple wings on their backs, wielding divine weapons, and clad in divine armor—stared intently at the ceaseless, sky-shattering waves crashing against them.

Though called a tide, it was actually composed of countless shattered dimensions and cosmic fissures.

Each wave was a fissure—the remnants of shattered and decaying realms and universes from the Vast Expanse spanning countless eons.

These remnants not only harbored terrifying power but also gave birth to a race of beings known as Void Beasts. Specializing in devouring the shards of these shattered realms and universes, they possessed immense strength and were nearly impossible to destroy.

To the divine civilization system, these Void Beasts are akin to natural enemies.

Many realms established by lesser deities have been obliterated in the river of time simply because they could not withstand the Void Beasts.

Only divine realms presided over by a Primordial God possess the inherent strength to withstand the Void Beasts and endure through the ages.

"What on earth has happened? Why has the Ancestral Goddess’s power begun to wane since her return? Even her divine might is fading, and corruption is beginning to spread. I fear it won’t be long before the Void Beasts tear open gaps in these realms and storm in..."

"The Nine Ao Divine Realm was recently swallowed whole by the Void Beasts, utterly annihilated and vanishing. Are we, the Tai Xuan Divine Realm, destined to follow in the Nine Ao Divine Realm’s footsteps?"

A female deity spoke, her face filled with fear and trembling.

The Nine Teachings Divine Realm was also a renowned and powerful divine realm within the Divine Path Civilization. Though its foundations were not as deep as those of the Tai Xuan Divine Realm, it was presided over by a Primordial God.

Yet, not long ago, the Ancestral God of the Nine Ao suddenly fell. His divine essence shattered, his faith system collapsed, and the boundless faith energy within the Nine Ao Divine Realm surged forth, tearing apart the protective barrier left behind by the Ancestral God of the Nine Ao.

In an instant, it was as if a dam had burst; the Multiverse Void Sea flooded in, and endless Void Beasts followed in its wake. An ancient and mighty divine realm crumbled and disintegrated.

That event sent shockwaves throughout the entire Divine Path Civilization.

Numerous divine realms, including the Taosu Divine Realm and the Chuwu Divine Realm, were plunged into turmoil and fear. It was at that very moment that the Ancestral God Taoxuan returned from the outside world.

She seemed entirely unsurprised by the collapse of the Nine Ao Divine Realm and said little to her divine emissaries before entering a state of recuperative slumber.

The Ancestral Gods of the other divine realms seemed to be aware of this as well, yet none spoke of it openly, as if they were avoiding or fearing something.

For the deities of the entire divine hierarchy, the fall of an ancient ancestral deity—with their divine essence shattered along with it—meant utter annihilation.

The shockwaves and upheaval caused by this news were, as one might imagine, immense.

However, since even the Nine Ao Divine Realm had perished and been destroyed, no one pursued the matter further.

To the deities of the Taixuan Divine Realm, this matter seemed to have nothing to do with them. Yet no one could have imagined that during the time the Taixuan Ancestral God lay in slumber...

the heavens of the Taixuan Divine Realm had changed…

First, the people noticed that in some of the realms created by the Taoxuan Ancestral God, terrifying anomalies began to appear: black blood fell from the heavens like a torrential downpour, and strange, filthy tentacles seemed to cling to the world’s barriers, writhing as if trying to crawl in from the unknown beyond.

Order began to break down in many realms; in the shadows, corruption bred, gradually corroding and infecting the souls of the people.

At the same time, the protective barrier left by the Ancestral God of Taixuan began to blur. In some places, black spots even appeared, as if struck by some eerie curse, spreading slowly like thick ink staining rice paper.

The entire Taixuan Divine Realm was plunged into panic.

Many deities suspected that the Tai Xuan Divine Realm was being eroded by some fallen dark gods; the forces of darkness were seeping in from unknown sources, threatening to corrode the entire realm.

Yet at this critical juncture, the Ancestral Goddess of Taixuan remained in slumber, recuperating. No deity could approach her palace, nor could any rouse her from her rest.

For all the deities of the Taixuan Divine Realm, this was nothing short of despair.

Rumble!

Thick, boundless bolts of black lightning—like a black sun falling from the sky—struck down with torrential force across the Tai Xuan Divine Realm.

As the lightning streaked across the sky, clouds gathered in the heavens, and then a torrential downpour of black rain began to fall.

This black rain seemed to possess some kind of eerie vitality; the moment it hit the ground, it spread and permeated the surrounding area.

Many ordinary citizens of the divine realms were drenched by the black rain and soon fell gravely ill. After lying unconscious for several days, they began to awaken, only to find an inexplicable power—one distinct from divine power—emerging within them.

They could not control this power, yet it influenced them, turning them mad, cruel, bloodthirsty, and tyrannical.

The same happened to the ordinary vegetation; it seemed to have been given a sudden burst of growth. Ordinary green grass grew in an instant, becoming as massive as mountains, utterly frenzied—a single flick of its stem was enough to cause a city to collapse and shatter.

As for the rest of the living beings, the same was true...

The black rain fell harder and harder, quickly engulfing one realm after another, plunging all the gods into despair.

The Tai Xuan Divine Realm was created single-handedly by the Tai Xuan Ancestral God. For such a terrifying and bizarre upheaval to occur meant that a grave problem had arisen within the Tai Xuan Ancestral God herself.

Perhaps she was not unwilling to awaken, but simply unable to do so.

"Although I knew long ago that the bait was tainted, I still took the risk and brought it away, thinking I could suppress it for the time being. I planned to carefully refine and expel it once I returned to the Divine Realm—even if it meant sacrificing part of my primordial essence, I could accept that."

"But I truly never expected that the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance would be so ruthless and terrifying. Merely coming into contact with a trace of it at the time has brought upon me endless calamity."

In the vast, abyssal void, the Ancestral God Tai Xuan let out a soft sigh.

How could she possibly fail to notice the changes within herself? From the stump of her severed arm, wispy strands of black mist were spreading upward, already completely saturating half of her shoulder.

This power was eerie and overbearing, like maggots gnawing at the bone—utterly impossible to expel.

Back in that sea of spacetime, she had forcibly fused and reattached her arm in front of the Primordial Ancestral Gods—Taisu, Chuwu, and Haoshang—and others.

Fully aware that Gu Changge might have left some trap there, waiting for her to take the bait, she still forcibly fused and reattached it.

And it was precisely this that brought about her current calamity. After leaving that sea of spacetime, she severed the severed arm, but in the briefest of moments, that eerie power had already corroded its way to her shoulder.

Consequently, the moment she returned to the Tai Xuan Divine Realm, she fell into a deep slumber, intending to completely expel and purify the force.

Yet she never imagined that during this process, not only would she fail to expel it, but that the eerie power would instead take root ever deeper.

The Ancestral God of Taixuan felt deep regret. If only she hadn’t spied on Gu Changge and offended him back when the Heaven Slaying Alliance was first established, perhaps none of this calamity would have befallen her today.

"This eerie power bears a striking resemblance to the source of darkness. Who exactly is he?"

It seemed the only solution now was to find Gu Changge herself and ask him to intervene.

However, her consciousness was trapped within this space—dark and profound, like a prison. The Divine Ancestor Tai Xuan could clearly sense that a new consciousness seemed to be taking shape within her physical body.

If she did not find a way to escape this space in time, her physical body might be taken over by another consciousness from this day forward.

This prospect sent a chill down her spine, and she couldn’t help but think of those clans and beings who had fallen into the darkness.
