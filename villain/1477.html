With majestic mountain ranges and towering palaces, this realm resembles a pristine paradise. Silver waterfalls cascade down, while sacred peaks and ancient mountains are shrouded in a chaotic mist, creating a world of its own, suffused with a profound aura of primordial energy.

Yet at this very moment, the land trembles violently, the earth rumbling as if being pushed relentlessly by a giant hand.

Mountain range after mountain range trembled; every tower, palace, cave, and secluded chamber seemed like a fragile boat tossed in raging waves, swaying ceaselessly. The array patterns etched into the surroundings emitted dazzling, blinding light, erupting with an unprecedented, violent turbulence.

Figures shot out from all directions at breakneck speed, and many beings clad in ancient attire, their bodies imbued with the essence of time, appeared one after another.

That terrifying presence, as if the heavens were watching, swept in like an unstoppable, terrifying storm. The entire Nine Heavens region churned violently, and one spiritual artifact after another was swept uncontrollably toward the heavens.

This was the depths of the Nine Heavens, the ancestral lands of the Gu Clan.

"Who is spying on our ancestral lands?"

"This presence seems to come from the void, yet it is in fact an invasion from the outside world, having penetrated the vast array barriers our clan has established. It is simply inconceivable."

A boundless, overwhelming aura surged forth, as if awakening from the river of time, disrupting the heavens of ages past.

An elderly man with snow-white hair and a stooped, aged figure, clad in a loose gray robe, appeared, visibly shaken. Even he was swept up uncontrollably by that surging mental force, his form carried skyward toward the heavens.

"There is no need to panic."

"And do not resist."

Hum!

In the blink of an eye, the spacetime before the old man collapsed. A figure stepped out—tall and graceful, of unparalleled beauty—with countless fragments of time swirling around her.

At her feet, the shadows of the river of time and the river of the ages converged, then gradually faded away.

"—Ancestor..."

The formidable figures from the Gu family who had rushed to the scene all looked upon this figure with deference, their eyes brimming with respect.

The woman stood there calmly, and all the surging energy around her, as vast as the ocean, began to subside.

"This thought originates from the Lord of the Heavens; he is searching for something..."

She observed for only a moment before comprehending the situation, and spoke up to reassure everyone.

As her words fell, within a vast hall, a white-robed woman shrouded in celestial mist and rosy light—her cold, jade-like features now filled with astonishment and shock—was propelled uncontrollably into the air. The very thought that had enveloped and swept across the entire Nine Heavens now descended upon her.

Flying out with her was an ancient bronze mirror, suffused with a hazy radiance.

At that moment, the bronze mirror burst forth with countless rays of light, as if capable of reflecting all of history, with boundless mysteries unfolding within it.

"Is it her?"

"She was brought back with Xian'er at the time. I sensed then that she held some secrets and had a connection to someone in the Fate-Defying Palace, but I didn’t investigate further."

"I never expected her to attract the attention of a figure like the Ruler of Heaven..."

Witnessing this scene, the few supreme experts of the Gu family present were filled with astonishment and shock.

"This must be the 'Wheel of Reincarnation,' the treasure of Civilization that originally sealed the Ruler of Heaven. I never expected it to be in her possession."

"As the cause was sown, so is the effect reaped."

The First Ancestor of the Gu family looked over, unsurprised, but after a moment’s thought, she reached out a hand and intercepted the woman in white.

She had only glanced at the Mirror of Reincarnation, but she knew that intercepting it would inevitably lead to unimaginable consequences, so she held back.

As the Mirror of Reincarnation flew away, a strange transformation seemed to take place within it.

A crisp crackling sound rang out from within the mirror’s surface; certain markings began to dissipate and collapse. Rays of divine light pierced through the heavens and earth, followed by threads resembling the threads of cause and effect, which swiftly vanished into the void.

One of these mist-like strands of causality landed directly on the white-robed woman, poised to surge into the sea of consciousness at her brow.

"What is this?"

Several of the Gu family’s most powerful figures noticed this scene and were slightly startled.

The First Patriarch of the Gu family furrowed his brow slightly, then mused, “These are her memories. She sealed some of her memories within that Mirror of Reincarnation—perhaps out of fear that some unforeseen accident might occur.”

"Or perhaps she feared that certain secrets might be uncovered by others..."

There were many ancient beings who had also noticed this scene.

However, since this was the Gu Clan’s ancestral domain, many figures from the Nine Heavens could not peer in.

Only a few, such as the Yellow Lord and the Eternal Ancestor, looked over. From a great distance, they nodded to the First Ancestor of the Gu family in greeting, clearly recognizing her.

Their gazes fell upon the woman in white, but they only glanced at her briefly before moving on. Instead, most eyes turned toward the Mirror of Reincarnation, which had been enveloped by a terrifying presence and was now flying away.

“Could it be that the Lord of Heaven is after those six ancient treasures of Civilization?”

“They’re called Treasures of Civilization, but their true origins are impossible to trace—we don’t even know where the blueprints for their forging came from.”

A million thoughts flashed through their minds in an instant as they tried to deduce the origins of the Mirror of Reincarnation, but they quickly failed; the six Treasures of Civilization were all shrouded in a hazy mist.

To uncover the secrets of the Mirror of Reincarnation before them, they would have to seize it by force—but doing so would clearly incur the wrath of the Ruler of Heaven.

The woman in white was enveloped in a haze of rosy mist. Her face, as luminous and white as jade, was now filled with a dazed expression—at times bewildered, at times pained…

Deep within the Nine Heavens, at a certain point in space and time, everything seemed frozen in eternal stillness, like a smooth mirror. Yet now, ripples spread across its surface, and a blurred figure emerged silently, standing upon the mirror’s surface—her robes fluttering, her bare feet as white as snow.

She looked at the woman in white outside and sighed softly.

The void suddenly split open. The figure extended a hand as white as jade and swept the woman in white away in a single motion.

As if sensing a familiar presence, the bewildered expression on the woman in white’s face eased slightly, and she murmured softly, “Master…”

Watching this scene, the First Ancestor of the Gu Clan, standing within the Gu family’s ancestral grounds, was momentarily startled, but quickly understood and made no attempt to intervene.

The other supreme figures of the Gu family also watched as the woman in white was carried away by that snow-white hand, and not a single one spoke.

Nearly every member of the Fate-Defying Palace knew that the Palace had three leaders, and that the Gu family’s distant ancestor was, in fact, one of them.

However, during that cataclysm that engulfed all worlds, he chose to sacrifice himself to become a realm, shielding all living beings…

Today, the current Patriarch of the Gu family held a status and influence within the Fate-Defying Palace that rivaled that of the other two leaders.

Of course, due to the selfless act of the Gu family’s distant ancestor, the Gu family held a unique status within the Fate-Defying Palace, and even the members of the other paths held them in high regard.

“Was that aura just now from the Palace Master?” a supreme figure of the Gu family couldn’t help but ask aloud.

The First Ancestor of the Gu Clan seemed to furrow her delicate brows slightly before replying, “I’m not entirely sure. I cannot discern the relationship between that woman and the Palace Master.”

"The Fate-Defying Palace now has only two leaders remaining, and it seems both have appeared today."

"But when will our Ancestor return?"

The Gu family’s elite figures, their minds somewhat calmed, turned their gaze toward the deepest recesses of their ancestral lands.

Amidst the endless mist-shrouded mountains, a mighty force seemed to be surging. The terrain there was more majestic than any mountain range, resembling an inverted plateau.

The plateau stretched across the boundless mist, and faintly visible was an ancient ancestral hall situated there, as if overlooking the entire cosmos. A certain supreme and vast aura permeated the air, while a chaotic waterfall cascaded down like an ocean, causing the surrounding laws of time and space to warp.

It seemed to be the very source of all life in the heavens; even though shrouded by that terrifying, supreme aura, a rich vitality still permeated the air.

This aura far surpasses that of any being at the Ancestral Dao Realm. Even those who have survived nine Celestial Waning Tribulations and approached the End of the Path tier would, upon reaching that place, have their spines crushed and be unable to take a single step further.

Deep within that ancient shrine lies a temporal vortex carved by unimaginable power—a realm independent of past, present, and future, untouched by the disturbances of the present world. Those who cultivate and gain enlightenment within it can, to the greatest extent, reclaim their true selves and powers across all timelines, unifying the causal threads and timelines of all planes and spacetime into a single, singular whole.

However, they had no idea how long this process would take.

High above the Nine Heavens, the Mirror of Reincarnation emitted a hazy glow. As the vast army of the Dark Clan, along with the sentient beings and cultivators from every layer of the heavens, was swept up and carried away, all watched the scene with solemn gravity; no one dared to speak up to stop it.

Not even the two "End-of-the-Path" beings, the Yellow Sovereign and the Eternal Ancestor, spoke a word; they merely watched all this in silence.

That boundless, surging stream of consciousness that had reverberated through the Nine Heavens finally receded as swiftly as a tide. The heavens, earth, and spacetime returned to calm, and the trembling earth and mountains settled once more.

The fractured universes and spacetime, still trembling, also returned to peace; the vast, sweeping aura that had just engulfed the cosmos seemed as though it had been nothing more than an illusion.

The Dark Horde retreated in its wake, a boundless, black tide flowing along the newly opened spacetime passage. The sight obscured everything, weighing down upon all creation—its sheer magnitude was enough to make one’s heart tremble.

"The Dark Ones have finally retreated..."

"The Ruler of Heaven has truly only just awakened. Beneath that colossal, terrifying intellect, it seems as though a single thought could tear the Vast Expanse apart, and all living beings would perish in an instant."

"This is simply too terrifying."

Even now, many of the most powerful beings could not calm their minds, their hearts still filled with a sense of dread.

Meanwhile, the myriad civilizations and realms within the Vast Expanse seemed to have fallen into a kind of deathly silence. Only those at the pinnacle of power could perceive this scene; ordinary beings were completely unaware of what had transpired.

But… even sensing the slightest trace of it brought them a sense of oppression and fear as if the world were ending.

Not to mention the Nine Heavens, which had just witnessed it all firsthand; it felt as though the slightest disturbance would immediately trigger an unimaginably terrifying battle.

"The final test..."

"What did it reveal? The true nature of the Ruler of Heaven, or his relationship with the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance?"

"And what secrets do those six treasures of the First Civilization hold? That the Lord of Heaven must personally intervene to seize them?"

The figures of the Yellow Sovereign and the Eternal Ancestor vanished silently into the void, reappearing in another realm of time and space. There, a vast and majestic hall stood, seemingly as vast as the heavens and earth themselves.

The hall was boundlessly vast, as if multiple universes had been layered together. At its very center hung a brilliant, radiant sun. Though called a sun, it was not quite so; it resembled a boundlessly massive fireball, constantly radiating a scorching, surging, and terrifying energy. It seemed as though another realm existed within its depths.

At the very center of this hall, a figure of blazing radiance sat cross-legged deep within the Great Sun, seemingly in meditation. As the Yellow Sovereign and the Eternal Ancestor approached, the figure opened its eyes. The gaze was so blindingly bright it was as if two actual Great Suns were present; boundless energy and matter surged behind it like a tidal wave.

Of course, for beings of their caliber, transforming their eyes into great suns was, in itself, a trivial matter.

"Lord Ju..."

Huangzhu and Yongzu came to a halt within the hall.

“This era is truly chaotic. All manner of demons and monsters have emerged…”

The ancient, resonant voice that had just echoed across the Nine Heavens and the Earth rang out once more. It was clear that the being who had intervened to halt the Dark Clan’s massive army was the figure standing before them.

Even the members of the Fate-Defying Palace knew him only by the name "Ju," addressing him as Lord Ju.

And now, the ruler of the Nine Heavens, the Eternal Life Dao Lord, had once met the Master of Confinement and received his guidance.

“Does the Master of Ju refer to the Lord of Heaven? He is indeed very powerful; he single-handedly ushered in the Age of Black Calamity. At that time, the Lord of Si took notice of him, but chose not to have the Fate-Defying Palace interfere, opting instead to wait and see how things unfolded,” said the Lord of Huang.

Master Ju’s form blazed with boundless radiance; the surrounding matter had transformed into energy particles swirling around him. Yet upon closer inspection, one could discern that these energy particles seemed to contain a vital force, with countless microscopic microcosms evolving within them.

“It’s not just the Lord of Heaven. Just now, I sensed a gaze emanating from the depths of the Ancient Hidden Civilization.”

“I suspect the Realm of Reincarnation is about to make its move as well. With the Great Calamity imminent, if the Nine Heavens fail to prepare this time, it will be difficult for them to remain untouched by the chaos,” the Ruler of Confinement said calmly.

The Yellow Sovereign and the Eternal Ancestor had long anticipated this situation. Before the previous Great Calamity, how could so many figures at the end of their paths have appeared? Everyone had hidden themselves well, doing their utmost to conceal their auras.

Even the Supreme Shattered Beings, whose power rivaled that of the rulers, dared not appear or act so casually, leaving traces of karma behind…

In the past, before the Great Calamity, even those at the Ancestral Dao Realm would carefully conceal their auras, doing their utmost not to stir up any commotion or turmoil.

Yet in this era, not only have the "End-of-Path" figures been forced into the limelight, but even the "True Path" beings who wield authority have been driven to their ends one after another.

One can only say that the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance is truly cunning—constantly stirring up trouble, then using various direct or indirect means to force these beings to reveal themselves.

Today, before the Nine Heavens, had the Ruler of Confinement not intervened, the Nine Heavens might truly have been brought down by the Ruler of Heaven.

Rather than saying they were testing the Provisional Heavenly Lord, it would be more accurate to say that the Provisional Heavenly Lord actively forced them to act, compelling them to reluctantly test his stance.

Had no entity of the appropriate caliber stepped forward, the Lord of the Heavens would never have retreated so easily.

"The upheavals of this era are too numerous; they have long surpassed our expectations."

“Perhaps the grand plan of the Fate-Defying Palace will be brought forward in this era, but are we truly prepared?” the Ruler of Restraint sighed.

Both the Yellow Lord and the Eternal Ancestor felt a chill run through them, knowing exactly what the Restraint Lord meant.

In a sense, the very existence of the Fate-Defying Palace was to resist the Realm of Truth and defy Heaven to rewrite destiny.

For this cause, countless sages of the past have sacrificed themselves, one after another, throwing caution to the wind and fearing neither life nor death...

But to achieve this great feat, who knows how much power accumulated over the ages would be required? Although the Fate-Defying Palace is already very powerful now, whether it can overthrow the Realm of Truth remains an unknown.

"Acting on Heaven’s behalf, yet engaging in Heaven Slaying…"

"Perhaps his goal is truly the same as ours," Huang Zhu said, shaking his head slightly.
