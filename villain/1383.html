The Righteous Unity Alliance: a tranquil haven untouched by the outside world.

Even as war rages and chaos reigns in the outside world, this place remains utterly untouched, as if suspended beyond the heavens, transcending the mundane world.

Shrouded in mist and bathed in ethereal radiance, a magnificent bronze hall stands deep within the mountains and islands.

Surrounded by swirling stars and diffusing chaotic energy, the air is permeated with the aura of the Dao, creating a scene that shifts between chaos and hazy obscurity.

Within the hall, numerous indistinct figures stood tall, shrouded and obscured by thick mist.

Only in the blink of an eye could one witness the dissolution of all beings, the collapse of past and present—even the runes of the Great Dao were shattering. Their existence was akin to an eternal, indestructible order and law, standing alone at the pinnacle of the Immortal Path, overlooking the masses of sentient beings.

Although the Righteous Unity Alliance was established in emulation of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, it did not have the position of Alliance Leader.

Civilizations and lineages from all quarters have selected their own spokespeople to represent the forces behind them, granting them a certain degree of decision-making power within the Righteous Unity Alliance.

Now, the beings within the grand hall were all peerless figures amidst the vast expanse; not a single one possessed cultivation below the Ancestral Dao Realm.

Before them, a rippling wave spread out, taking the form of a round mirror—crystal clear and translucent, with the laws of the Great Dao circulating within. The vast expanse of time and space, the boundless cosmos, seemed to be reflected within it.

Within this circular mirror, one could witness the scenes of fierce battles currently raging across the Righteous Unity Alliance—the flames of war spreading and ravaging every corner of the cosmos.

Not only were there scenes of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s armies waging war, but also the fierce clashes with dark creatures outside the embankments and city walls across the realm.

Beyond each embankment, the various races and factions, having received their orders, fought to the death, holding back wave after wave of the dark tide.

Endless blood and bones are spilled beyond the heavens, exploding across the starry realms. The vast, desolate sands of the Gobi are strewn with corpses, shattered armor, and blunted weapons.

Thick black mist continues to surge from distant realms, like wave upon wave, overwhelming and endless, a sight that brings suffocation and despair.

Amidst that overwhelming black mist, which completely obscured the heavens, the terrifying shadows looming against it were even more chilling and despair-inducing; there was no sign of life to be found.

Some of the dams had already shattered; runes were worn away, corners crumbled, and signs of collapse were evident, placing them on the brink of ruin.

Despite this, countless cultivators and living beings still surged out from the other side of the embankments, their eyes bloodshot, charging forward like moths to a flame.

"It seems we can’t hold out much longer, but that’s all right. As long as we buy some time, it will all be worth it."

These figures watched the scene reflected in the round mirror with cold detachment, their eyes utterly unflinching.

In their eyes, those sentient beings and cultivators charging forward without regard for life or death, utterly fearless, were no different from so-called ants.

If they could delay the collapse of the Righteous Unity Alliance’s front lines even a little longer, then the sacrifices of these living beings and cultivators would be worthwhile.

For behind them, more tribes and factions were on the move, rushing toward the heartland of the Righteous Unity Alliance.

To gain something, one must inevitably lose something; this is an eternal and unchanging law of nature.

However, amidst the thick, chaotic mist, a figure spoke up, their voice tinged with a hint of reluctance.

"The Weiyang Immortal Dynasty coming to our aid at this very moment presents a golden opportunity. Why not seize this momentum to strike back and teach the Heaven Slaying Alliance a profound lesson?"

“If we continue to retreat like this, our territory will be eroded further and further. Before long, the Righteous Unity Alliance may not even have the right to stand here.”

A figure beside him shook his head and said coldly, “Now is not the right time. The Righteous Unity Alliance has no need to shelter the weak. In the face of great calamity, only the fittest who survive are destined to go the farthest.”

"The outside world sees the Righteous Unity Alliance as weak and in constant retreat—how could they possibly understand our true intentions? When a great war erupts and the Black Calamity descends, it is simply the natural law of the jungle: the weak are destined to perish in this turmoil, while only the strong will remain.The campaign against the Heaven Slaying Alliance is merely a tool for the Righteous Unity Alliance to purge the weak and inferior."

"Do you not know, gentlemen, that every so often, a Great Purge descends upon us? Is this not the natural law upheld by the Way of Heaven—the cyclical principle that eliminates the weak and supports the strong? We are merely following nature."

A cold, heavy silence fell over the hall, and the speaker fell silent as well.

On the surface, the Righteous Unity Alliance appears to have been established to uphold justice and purge evil.

But in essence, how different is it really from the Heaven Slaying Alliance?

For the Righteous Unity Alliance to have grown to such a massive scale in such a short time, it is absolutely impossible to say that there is no one with ulterior motives driving it from behind the scenes.

Though they are beings of the Ancestral Dao Realm, revered as revered figures in the True Realms of Civilization behind them,

Yet even within the Righteous Unity Alliance, they remain constrained; they are not truly free from restrictions.

Everyone knows that behind the Righteous Unity Alliance stands an equally mysterious and immensely powerful entity.

It is precisely that being who single-handedly brought about the Righteous Unity Alliance as it stands today.

Many even speculate that this being likely hails from the same era as Ling Yuling, the founder of the Imperial Immortal Palace—or perhaps is even older—possessing both the qualifications and the strength to challenge Gu Changge, the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Otherwise, that mysterious entity would not have emulated the Heaven Slaying Alliance by establishing the Righteous Unity Alliance as a rival power.

However, that mysterious entity has never revealed itself, only occasionally manifesting to employ unimaginable methods to command its subordinates and carry out its will.

Even figures at the level of Patriarchs from the main sects of the Immortal lineages obeyed their commands.

"In that case, issue the order: let all races hold the dams at all costs. Every day we can buy is a day gained."

“As for the Heaven Slaying Alliance, we should avoid them whenever possible. What’s the harm in ceding those useless territories to them?”

The assembly in the hall began to discuss the matter once more, their words betraying no concern for the approaching dark beings or the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

The boundaries of the Righteous Unity Alliance were actually quite vague, existing only as a rough outline.

As more factions joined the Righteous Unity Alliance, its territory continued to expand, resulting in a lack of any fixed concept regarding its borders.

Even the high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance were unable to clearly define the exact boundaries of its territory.

Which factions and lineages have joined the Righteous Unity Alliance during this period?

Under these circumstances, as long as the situation within the Righteous Unity Alliance remains within their control and does not affect the True Realm of Civilization behind them,

why should they care? And why should they concern themselves with how many members of the Righteous Unity Alliance have been wiped out, or how much territory has been eroded?

Perhaps the factions on the front lines, fighting off the dark beings, were still waiting for aid from the Righteous Unity Alliance, still holding their ground with desperate resistance, clinging to a glimmer of hope.

But the high-ranking officials of the Righteous Unity Alliance had, in fact, long since abandoned them.

The Lord of the Zou Mie Manor watched all this coldly, saying nothing. His true form stood

amidst the mist, concealing his aura and origins just like the others.

He is now the de facto ruler of the Yan Yang True Realm; factions such as the Jie Sheng Hall and Wunian Mountain have all been subjugated and brought under his control.

After surviving eight Celestial Waning Tribulations, he had found no equal within the Yan Yang True Realm.

Relying on his past foundations and prestige, controlling the Yan Yang True Realm was not difficult.

After all, the Yan Yang True Realm had long since fallen from its peak and could not compare to a supreme Civilization like the Yuan Hope Civilization.

Yet, even after securing control of the Yan Yang True Realm, he too began to find himself increasingly constrained.

His former ambition was to restore the Yanyang True Realm to its ancient glory, establishing it as the sole True Realm, thereby achieving true transcendence and escaping the reckoning of the Great Calamity.

There were also rumors that the Source World had actually evolved and transcended from the former Sole True Realm.

If, under his leadership, the One True Realm were to emerge, would he not then become a figure on par with those supreme beings in the Source World?

For the sake of this ambition, he governed with unwavering dedication, poured his heart and soul into his efforts, and laid out numerous contingency plans, ultimately making the Zou Mie Mansion the dominant power within the Yan Yang True Realm.

Alas, the good times were short-lived. Before the Yan Yang True Realm could restore its ancient glory, this dark calamity descended—faster even than the reckoning of the Great Calamity.

The entire vast expanse plunged into turmoil, and a prolonged atmosphere of panic and dread began to spread throughout the various realms of Civilization.

This caught him off guard as well, and he began to worry. Given the Yanyang True Realm’s current foundations, it was virtually impossible to stand against the dark beings and survive this catastrophic calamity.

Even at the very onset of the Black Calamity, supreme realms had already fallen, swallowed by the forces of darkness and plunged into eternal silence.

If no solution is found, those fallen supreme realms will serve as a cautionary tale for the Yan Yang Realm.

The entire populace of the Yan Yang True Realm, along with the sentient beings across countless timelines and universes, would be swallowed by the darkness.

It was precisely when he was desperately in need of power to resolve this predicament that a mysterious benefactor appeared. It was this benefactor who bestowed upon him a Daoist text, guided him on his path forward, and dispelled the fog of uncertainty from his journey.

From that point on, he successfully weathered eight Celestial Waning Tribulations and unified the entire Yan Yang True Realm.

Subsequently, under his deliberate guidance, the embryonic form of the Righteous Unity Alliance emerged. Leaders from other civilizations and realms also rallied to the cause, deciding to join forces and establish a covenant.

The Lord of the Zou Mie Mansion’s mind was in turmoil; the emergence of the Righteous Unity Alliance had resolved his most pressing crisis.

However, this also turned him into a puppet controlled by others, with no freedom of action.

The assistance from that mysterious benefactor came with corresponding karmic consequences; in this world, there are no such things as unearned benefits or free rides.

Suddenly, outside the hall, a divine radiance flashed, illuminating the entire sky in a dazzling, sacred glow.

A Azure Dragon and a Azure Phoenix descended, streaking abruptly across the cosmos to appear here in an instant. They then landed and transformed into two child attendants, one boy and one girl.

In the boy’s hand, he held an edict shrouded in a hazy, radiant glow. As he approached the hall, everyone who had been discussing strategies fell silent, their gazes all turning to the edict.

Clearly, they recognized the pair of children, and their expressions were filled with respect and deference, daring not to show the slightest sign of disrespect.

“This is the Lord’s edict. Lord of the Zou Mie Manor, receive the edict.”

The boy’s gaze was detached and world-weary; though separated by thick, chaotic mist, he spotted the Lord of the Zou Mie Manor at a glance.

Everyone in the hall was utterly astonished and shaken; they had all been concealing their auras and origins, unwilling to reveal their true forms.

Amidst his astonishment, the Lord of the Zou Mie Manor dared not delay; he hurried forward to receive the edict.

Even though he was a being who had survived eight Celestial Waning Tribulations, in the presence of these two young boys, he still felt a sense of pressure and dread.

In terms of strength, these two were certainly no weaker than he was.

"This is the Master’s will. He hopes that the Lord of the Zou Mie Manor will assume the position of Deputy Alliance Leader of the Righteous Unity Alliance in the coming days. The Emperor of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty will lead his troops here shortly, at which time he will take over the role of Alliance Leader."

The young boy didn’t even lift his eyes, speaking indifferently, paying no heed to the crowd within the hall.

After saying this, he and the girl left without a moment’s delay.

The Lord of the Zou Mie Manor and the others in the hall were left dumbfounded. Many exchanged glances, their eyes flickering with uncertainty, wondering what this meant.

The head of the Zou Mie Mansion is to take over as Deputy Alliance Leader?

Would the Emperor of Weiyang arrive next? Could it be that the Emperor of Weiyang was also connected to that mysterious entity?

“What does the imperial edict say?”

Many were curious about the edict, their eyes falling upon it with a mix of contemplation and scrutiny.

The Lord of the Zou Mie Manor frowned as well, but he didn’t hesitate. Right in front of everyone, he unfolded the imperial edict.

Upon seeing the contents of the edict, his expression—along with everyone else’s—changed. They looked as though they could not believe their eyes, standing frozen in place.

Even those who had already made numerous decisions and planned to retreat to Jiangdong stood frozen in place, unable to believe what they were seeing.

"Battle reports have arrived: the Thirteenth Dam, defended by the Great Wasteland Sect, the Thirty-Three Star Mountains, and the Nine Paths Sect, has fallen. Dark entities have swept through unimpeded, penetrating deep into the heartland. The Great Wasteland Sect and the Thirty-Three Star Mountains have both fallen, swallowed by black mist and transformed into a wasteland…"

"There are far too many dark beings over there. Several extremely terrifying dark beings have intervened; even several Dao Realm beings have been killed outright. Ordinary cultivators simply cannot withstand their onslaught." "The Ancestral Dao Realm beings have long since gathered the sect’s foundations and core seeds and fled to other realms, leaving the others behind."

"If reinforcements from the Righteous Unity Alliance don’t arrive soon, our Dam No. 17 will fall as well."

Meanwhile, within the Crescent Moon Sect, the atmosphere was incredibly heavy.

One piece of heart-wrenching, despair-inducing news after another poured in from all directions—reports of dams falling, the sect’s heartland being overrun, and dark beings infiltrating to work from within, coordinating with external forces to breach the defenses.

These powerful dark entities employed unpredictable tactics, and the manner of their arrival was truly baffling.

They could emerge from the depths of practitioners’ hearts by feeding on their spreading fear and despair; even the elders of various sects and lineages could not guarantee that their minds would remain steadfast, free from contamination and corruption.

Ordinary disciples, needless to say, are riddled with vulnerabilities.

Furthermore, within those rifts in space-time and fractured cosmic tunnels, dark entities easily find openings to seize the opportunity and descend.

A single moment of inattention could allow vast hordes of dark beings to pour in, like a swarm of locusts sweeping across the land—endless and overwhelming, devouring everything in their path.

Wherever they pass, rivers run dry, mountains and valleys lie barren, cities fall silent, nations perish, and all life is wiped out.

"As long as we hold out until the Righteous Unity Alliance’s reinforcements arrive, we still have hope." Across the peaks of the Crescent Moon Sect, on battle platforms everywhere, lights flickered and spatial ripples spread. One squad after another, along with ancient war chariots, sped off into the distance, vanishing rapidly as they raced toward the front lines.

All the disciples and elders still clung to a sliver of hope, hoping to hold out until reinforcements arrived.

Outside Dam No. 17, the fighting was incredibly fierce; even descriptions of rivers of blood and mountains of corpses fell far short of capturing the reality.

The air was thick with the stench of blood, and the ceaseless rain of blood staining the sky had turned the heavens crimson; as far as the eye could see, the entire world was a sea of scarlet.

On the massive, imposing city walls, runes flickered. Ancient symbols lit up, surging with power, then transformed into pillars of energy that crashed down with a thunderous roar. The tide of dark creatures was instantly scattered, but before long, they regrouped and charged forward once more.

Such a scene was despairing and helpless, offering not a shred of hope.

The dark creatures seemed inexhaustible, surging forward in wave after wave, their bodies crashing against the city walls. Even as they were reduced to dust, they were determined to tear a gap in the defenses.

In the distant, secluded star domains, the battle was equally earth-shattering and brutal. The surging energy and aura were so terrifying they shook the ages, disrupting the river of time and causing countless shards and afterimages to appear.

That was a battlefield for beings of the Dao Realm—a realm beyond the reach of Immortal Realm cultivators.

Gu Xian’er, Lan Xin, and the others all wore expressions of deep exhaustion, their bodies bearing numerous wounds and stained with blood.

Yet even so, they had not retreated a single step, steadfastly defending the city walls and embankments.

Such a fierce battle had raged on for days on end; anyone would feel exhausted.

Especially in a place like this, it was difficult to heal injuries or draw upon the energy of heaven and earth immediately, causing their magical power to deplete rapidly, leaving them unable to keep up.

Apart from those at the Dao Realm, any Immortal cultivator of a lower tier facing such a massive battle would likely meet a fate of dying from exhaustion.

"Junior Sister Xian'er, I have a bad feeling. It seems we might truly die here today."

Lan Xin was clad in plain black robes, her head draped in white cloth—though now it was soaked in blood: her own, her enemies’, and that of her companions.

She watched as Gu Xian’er swung her sword, slaying a dark creature that had charged toward her, and a bitter expression crossed her face.
