With the formation of the Immortal Dao Alliance imminent, three months of preparation time is actually far from sufficient for the Piao Miao Sacred Realm.

And the Piao Miao Holy City would rival the headquarters of the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the central region of the Righteous Unity Alliance.

Consequently, in recent times, many clans and factions within the Piao Miao Sacred Realm have been seeking ways to relocate to Piao Miao Holy City.

Time-space tunnels were being opened one after another across various realms and universes. As ancient warships arrived one after another, shuttling back and forth, the spectacle was grand and truly awe-inspiring.

Amidst the towers, palaces, and sacred mountains of the Piao Miao Holy City, the figures of many civilizations and clans who had come to witness the ceremony could already be seen.

The crowds were surging, shoulder to shoulder, with people everywhere.

The Deputy Leader of the Righteous Unity Alliance had arrived in the Ethereal Holy City several days prior, leading a delegation of the Alliance’s high-ranking officials.

The heads of the major clans were in attendance, accompanying him as they toured the city, creating a rather leisurely atmosphere.

However, at this very moment, the ancient Xiao Clan within the Piao Miao Holy City was shrouded in a desolate and silent atmosphere.

Even the current head of the Xiao family, upon receiving the news, had no choice but to take his leave and return to the Xiao residence.

"It seems the Holy City of Piaomiao won’t be peaceful for a while."

The head of the Zou Mie Manor, Zhao Tianqi, who also served as the Deputy Alliance Master of the Righteous Unity Alliance, had now led a group of high-ranking officials to the Piao Miao Holy City to participate in the ceremonial observance.

On a crystal-clear cloud vessel bathed in the radiance of celestial hues.

With his hands clasped behind his back, he surveyed the sights of the Piao Miao Holy City, a faint smile playing on his lips as he spoke with an air of implied meaning.

Behind him, the senior members of the Righteous Unity Alliance all looked pensive.

The heads of the other families in the Ethereal Holy City, though accompanying them, wore expressions of deep concern at that moment, far from the calm they projected on the surface.

"Is this related to the Heaven Slaying Alliance? Or is it a conspiracy by dark beings targeting the Immortal Dao Alliance?"

Behind Zhao Tianqi, Zhao Xiuyan furrowed her delicate brows slightly, equally lost in thought.

She was clad in immaculate white robes, and her face made her appear to be only about twenty years old.

Her features were exquisite and stunning; her long black hair fell loosely, partially obscuring her face.

Yet her expression was utterly cold, as if she held no regard for any living being in the heavens or on earth.

She exuded a profound air of darkness.

The young disciples of the major clans in the Ethereal Holy City dared not meet her gaze, feeling a deep sense of dread.

Lately, strange and eerie events have been occurring in the Holy City of Piaomiao—no, indeed, throughout the entire Holy Realm of Piaomiao.

It wasn’t just the younger generation; even many ancient figures with profound cultivation couldn’t escape this corruption…

Many suspect this might be a harbinger of an impending attack by dark entities…

The ancient Xiao clan.

Numerous elders and young disciples gathered in the council hall, gazing with deep concern at the handsome young man lying unconscious on the floor—black mist swirling between his brows, his eyes tightly shut.

“What on earth is going on? Why has Fan’er, who was perfectly fine just moments ago, fallen into this state?”

A dashing, middle-aged man in a golden robe, his brow furrowed, asked with a look of sorrow.

His features bore a striking resemblance to the young man lying unconscious on the floor; he was the latter’s father and the current head of the Xiao Clan.

The ancient Xiao clan was a reclusive family of the region, whose strength and heritage ranked among the top five in the entire Piao Miao Sacred Domain, with three Ancestral Dao Realm elders presiding over the clan.

The young man lying unconscious on the ground was none other than his own son, Xiao Fan.

He was also the current leader of the younger generation of the Xiao family, with strength nearing the Dao Realm.

Hearing the head of the Xiao family ask this, the several young men and women who had accompanied Xiao Fan back to the Xiao family estate replied with concern and confusion, “We don’t know what happened either. Why would Senior Brother Xiao Fan, who was perfectly fine just moments ago, end up like this?”

“Over the past few days, we’ve been carrying out a mission assigned by our sect, welcoming the numerous Daoist lineages attending the Immortal Dao Alliance’s ceremonial assembly from all corners of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm.”

“Senior Brother Xiao Fan was fine at first, but we don’t know what he came into contact with. A few days ago, his body suddenly seemed to develop a problem, and he started muttering about the ‘Black Lotus descending to save all beings’…”

"Then, yesterday, Senior Brother Xiao Fan suddenly went mad. He knelt and bowed toward a spot outside the courtyard, shouting 'Lord of the Black Lotus' with intense fervor. A massive amount of black mist began to emanate from his body. We had no choice but to force him unconscious and seal him away."

"We suspect that Senior Brother Xiao Fan may have come into contact with those strange cultists."

Both they and Xiao Fan hail from the same sect, the Piao Miao Holy Sect.

The Piao Miao Holy Sect is the true ruler of the entire Piao Miao Holy Realm.

Even the major clans and factions send their clan members and disciples to join the sect for cultivation.

"Another Black Lotus has appeared?"

"How many times has this happened recently? How did even the Young Master get caught up in it?"

"What exactly is the origin of this Black Lotus? It appeared out of nowhere, without even the slightest warning."

Hearing the words of these young disciples, the expressions of the Xiao Clan elders gathered in the council hall all changed. There was a hint of fear, but more than that, there was anger and unease.

"The Black Lotus Sect?"

The head of the Xiao Clan furrowed his brow, clenched his fists, and exuded a terrifying aura. A horrifying spectacle played out in his eyes, though he was currently suppressing his rage.

In fact, over the past period, practitioners and living beings “brainwashed” and corrupted by the Black Lotus Sect had begun to emerge in many parts of the Ethereal Holy City—even the major clans were no exception.

At first, no one took it seriously, believing them to be nothing more than ordinary cultists—a rabble not worth paying attention to.

But when the Black Lotus Sect’s “doctrines” began to spread and erode like shadows, reaching even them, they finally realized the danger—but it was already too late.

In the major clans of the Ethereal Holy City, clan members corrupted by the Black Lotus Cult’s doctrines began to appear one after another. Without exception, these individuals fell into a kind of inexplicable nightmare.

In a state that seemed like both sleep and wakefulness, they would mutter phrases such as “The Black Lotus has descended to save all beings.”

At the same time, a power resembling the very source of darkness surged within them, greatly increasing their strength.

During this process, they even became unrecognizable to their own kin, seeing only the Black Lotus Sect and that so-called “Lord of the Black Lotus” of unknown origin.

The major clans tried every means to probe the spiritual seas of these clan members, but to no avail; they could find no root cause.

This plague-like calamity, once contracted, is nearly impossible to eradicate.

Although it is not as terrifying as that dark substance—which is highly contagious and invasive—if left unchecked for long, the entire Piao Miao Holy City may not escape its grasp.

Especially now that the Immortal Dao Alliance’s ceremonial day is fast approaching.

The Piao Miao Sacred Realm has opened its gates wide to the entire Vast Expanse, and forces from all major Daoist lineages and civilizations are pouring in—a motley crew where every kind of being can be found.

This calamity is simply impossible to contain.

It is as if a ball of ink has been thrown onto a breached dam, and the corroded seawater is now surging out in all directions, contaminating the entire Vast Expanse.

Many of the guests present have now taken notice of this, and panic and fear are beginning to set in.

Under investigation by the major clans of the Piao Miao Holy City, it was discovered that in the other cosmic territories of the Piao Miao Holy Realm, numerous shrines and temples associated with the Black Lotus Sect had already appeared as early as half a month ago.

However, at that time, the Black Lotus Sect remained hidden and dormant, merely incubating its spores, waiting for the day they would burst forth and spread.

This was undoubtedly a premeditated conspiracy, targeting not only the Ethereal Holy City but the entire Vast Expanse.

"This matter is undoubtedly linked to the Dark Tribes. I had thought that with the Dark Source Land about to descend and border the Vast Expanse, these tribes would hold back for the time being. There has been no movement from them during this period; it seems they were waiting for this grand move..."

The head of the Xiao clan looked grim, and his heart ached even more as he gazed at Xiao Fan, who lay unconscious on the ground with black mist swirling around his features.

For now, the cultivators and sentient beings corrupted and brainwashed by the Black Lotus Sect’s doctrines could only be temporarily sealed away; otherwise, allowing them to roam freely would only invite even greater trouble.

No one knew how these people had been brainwashed by the Black Lotus Sect’s followers.

He had always believed his son possessed exceptional talent, a steady temperament, and an unshakably firm Daoist resolve—making it impossible for external influences to disturb his mind.

Yet even a prodigy like Xiao Fan had fallen victim to the Black Lotus Sect’s influence; ordinary beings and cultivators would likely find it even harder to resist, and those with unstable Dao hearts would have virtually no escape.

"Head of the House, this matter has already been reported to the Sect Master and the others. As things stand, it is impossible to postpone or cancel the Immortal Dao Alliance’s ceremonial gathering. To do so would only play into the schemes of the Dark Clans."

"We can only hope that the Sect Master and her council have a solution—such as locating the Black Lotus Sect’s source and lair to destroy it. Otherwise, the consequences would be utterly unimaginable."

Several of the Xiao Clan’s most senior elders, their expressions grave, spoke in solemn tones.

"Sigh..."
