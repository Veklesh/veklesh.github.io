Cheng Tian stood amidst the shattered Ninth Heaven, with endless void stretching out behind him.

As his words echoed out, an inexplicable force resonated across the river of time, spanning past and present. A rust-stained war chariot, exuding an air of ancient weathered grandeur, tore through the void. An ancient battle standard fluttered in the gale of the Dao, its fabric flapping in the wind.

Where the reins should have been, there was originally nothing but void. Yet as the ancient chariot descended with a rumbling roar, the ethereal forms of ancient sacred beasts—the Azure Dragon, the White Tiger, and the Vermilion Bird—began to materialize and coalesce.

A shower of light rained down from the heavens, sparkling and brilliant. Upon the skeletal framework of the void, the Four Sacred Beasts took physical form, roaring toward the sky as they took the reins of the chariot.

The sheer force of their roars alone sent the blood and qi of the Ancestral Dao Realm beings surging, causing them to stagger and lose their footing, their eyes filled with sheer terror.

"Where is the commander?"

"Where are the warriors?"

Cheng Tian took a step forward, radiating the divine light of the Great Dao. Trampling it beneath his feet, he leaped onto the ancient war chariot, sword in one hand and reins in the other. Upon his heroic imperial robes, rust-stained and blood-spattered armor materialized, wrapping itself around his body.

Each piece of armor was incomplete, bearing the marks of centuries of war—sword gashes, arrow holes, and knife wounds. Now, as they clung to his body, they rang out ceaselessly, like the very voice of the Heavenly Dao.

Those enemies who had once fallen at his hand—those who had left these sword gashes and knife wounds—seemed poised to rise again from the abyss, their blurred, ethereal forms flickering at his side.

His stature seemed to grow even more towering; merely standing there, he inspired an instinctive urge in Zhe Huan to kneel and pay homage.

In life, he was the First Emperor who established an immortal empire and nearly unified the Vast Expanse; in death, he is the incarnation of Heaven, embodying the Heavenly Will.

At this moment, he resembled a supreme emperor leading his own troops into battle to subdue the rebellious dust.

Before the shattered Nine Heavens, the Grave Keeper, the Yellow Lord, the Eternal Ancestor, and others all paled. They sensed that the power emanating from Cheng Tian, who had just awakened, did not at all resemble that of a newly revived being; he could even single-handedly suppress the three of them, holding his own without faltering.

Moreover, his power continued to surge, potentially surpassing his former peak.

"Strike now. We cannot let him summon back his Abhorrent Followers."

"Delay and things will change. Cheng Tian’s power is growing; he might just become the Great Avatar of Heaven."

"Since time immemorial, no Great Avatar of Heaven has ever appeared."

Without a moment’s hesitation, the three stepped forward in unison. Endless radiance manifested, as if billions of realms were roaring, or as if countless universes were emerging in tandem with their movements.

Before them, Dao symbols flickered like a torrential flood, transforming into rivers and oceans that engulfed the heavens and earth, obscuring the entire cosmos.

In a single instant, they transformed into countless beams of Dao symbols, each containing a power that transcended the Ancestral Dao Realm. Beneath the light of these symbols, one could see the myriad forms of sentient beings, the rise and fall of Civilizations, and even the clash of Daoist doctrines as they returned to the true essence of the Dao.

As beings at the End of the Path, within the spacetime of the old cosmic order, they were the Ancestors of the Dao. What is an Ancestor of the Dao? The progenitor of the Great Dao.

In their comprehension of their own Dao, they have reached the pinnacle where they may be called Ancestors and revered as sovereigns.

When the three of them acted, the scene was akin to the very moment of creation, enlightening all beings and expounding to them the nature of the Great Dao of Heaven and Earth.

This transcends the very concept of divine powers and magical arts; it is the ultimate understanding of the Dao.

Not even beings at the Ancestral Dao Realm could hope to see this scene clearly; it was obscured by the vast, boundless, and overwhelming aura of the Dao before their eyes.

Cheng Tian stood tall before the ancient war chariot, holding only the Imperial Sword. With a single, effortless swing forward, a dazzling sword light seemed to pierce through the ancient history of heaven and earth, obscuring all past and present, overwhelming the myriad heavens of the cosmos, and transcending the very limits of the Dao. With ease, it shattered the torrent of the Great Dao unleashed by the combined might of the three.

With a thunderous roar, one Dao symbol after another shattered there, reduced to dust—a sight too terrifying to behold.

It seemed as though the ultimate essence of the surrounding heavens and earth had vanished in an instant, every trace erased into nothingness.

Even Chaos could only shatter under such power; it simply could not withstand it.

And this was merely the first strike of their clash.

Soon, the Eternal Ancestor struck again. With a light command, a beam of eternal light appeared above his head, transforming into a purple-gold Dao gourd. Eternal light swirled from the gourd’s mouth, spreading to obscure the heavens ahead, enveloping them and turning them into substance within the light of eternity.

"Return…"

Beneath Huang Zhu’s wide robes, a vast fragrance of medicinal herbs filled the air. Countless sacred herbs and immortal elixirs manifested, coalescing into a river of vital energy that coiled around him.

This river of vital energy spread out in all directions, transforming into countless strands of divine light that instantly extended into the unknown realms of time and space. Like a series of intertwining threads, they drew forth one powerful figure after another, binding them to fight for him.

He had attained the Dao through Huang Qi; his medical skills could heal the dead, reverse the balance of yin and yang, and even borrow the corpses of the deceased from unknown realms of time and space to temporarily “revive” them.

The Grave Keeper’s methods were even more terrifying. Behind him, a boundless, desolate graveyard materialized, filled with blood rivers reaching to the heavens, where sword light danced and living beings clashed in battle.

With a sweep of his hand, endless streams of bloodlight shot forth; within each beam, it seemed as though a living being sat in meditation. From that desolate graveyard, coffins emerged like swords, then split open in the void. Amidst the hazy glow that billowed forth, supreme beings stepped out.

In an instant, a battle of unparalleled ferocity—surpassing all that had ever been—erupted here. The light of flying swords and the glow of blood intertwined, while the light of eternity pierced through it all.

Cheng Tian fought against three figures of the End of the Path tier, and not once did he fall behind.

This spectacle sent a wave of shock and dread through all the cultivators and beings of the Nine Heavens. These were three figures at the End of the Path—any one of them could establish a super-multiverse of their own, and even the Ren Dun Era might not have produced a Sun Sovereign.

Yet now, they could not even suppress a single Heavenly Avatar.

"In the Nine Heavens, from ancient times to the present, a total of nine Heavenly Miniature Avatars have been sealed away. Should they ever emerge, the consequences would be simply unimaginable."

"Who in this world could possibly subdue them now?"

"To seal these Heavenly Miniature Avatars, the sages of old paid an unimaginable price..."

Many beings turned pale and could not help but tremble; the younger and middle-aged cultivators, in particular, shuddered slightly, sensing an inescapable sense of hopelessness.

In their eyes, the Immortal Dao Lords at the Ancestral Dao Realm were already figures so far beyond their reach in this lifetime that they could only look up to them in awe.

As for the "End of the Path" figures, before this era, they had never even heard of them—they were beings that existed only in ancient myths and legends.

Now, in this era, they have come to know of the existence of the "End of the Path" figures, and they have come to understand the cruelty and despair of this age.

If the Heaven and Earth Era were to come to an end, all the Minor Avatars of Heaven would awaken—and according to legend, even the Great Avatar of Heaven, whom not even the figures at the End of the Path had ever seen, might emerge.

Who, then, could stop their reckoning?

In the grand hall of the Eternal Dao Lord’s sanctuary, many returning members of the Palace of Defiance wore grave expressions. Now that the Palace of Defiance had only two leaders remaining, if the Great Avatars of Heaven were to appear, they would have no choice but to rely on the Master of Confinement to intervene.

After all, like the other True Path-level figures, the Master of the Path wields the Authority of Destiny among the Nine Great Heavenly Dao Authorities; if she were to intervene, she would inevitably suffer backlash.

"The Heavenly Favored of Chengtian is still on the way..."

"If the Heavenly Favored ones arrive, even we will be drawn into the great war. It seems the Nine Heavens cannot be defended."

Many in the grand hall were deeply concerned, sensing that the Nine Heavens would soon face unimaginable calamity. Before that happens, they must find a way to leave this place.

The Nine Heavens Tomb is impossible to defend.

Once Cheng Tian emerges, the other minor avatars of the heavens will likely be close to breaking their seals as well...

Whoosh!!!

Suddenly, space and time warped within the hall, shattering like a mirror. A slender, graceful figure clad in a moon-white robe stepped forward from the center. Her features were indistinct, yet her eyes were as clear as a mirror, seeming to reflect all the profound truths and true scenes of the world.

Around her, an endless array of dimensions seemed to shift and intertwine like mirror surfaces at every moment, making it difficult to discern where her true form resided.

Upon seeing her appear, all the members of the Palace of Defiance within the hall—regardless of their cultivation level—spoke respectfully, “We pay our respects to Lady Mirror.”

The Mirror Master was a figure at the End of the Path who had long served at the Lord’s side within the Palace of Defying Fate.

No one knew her origins; even members of the Palace of Defying Fate rarely saw her.

Generally speaking, the Mirror Master served as the Palace Master’s messenger; her appearance likely signaled the emergence of a matter so grave and troublesome that even the Palace Master himself felt compelled to take it seriously.

Those who wield the authority over destiny, in a certain sense, can already glimpse glimpses of the true future, forewarned of impending catastrophes, and thus navigate to avoid misfortune.

"The Heavenly Will has awakened prematurely, and it is far more wrathful than before. The Celestial Tomb, sealed within the Nine Heavens, will also be broken open ahead of schedule..."

"Following the awakening of the Celestial Heaven, the remaining Nine Heavens—such as the Shen Heaven, Xian Heaven, Kuo Heaven, and Tian Heaven—will all awaken and descend one after another."

"Once the Heavenly Favored Ones of the Nine Heavens have gathered, the Era of Heaven and Earth will also begin ahead of schedule."

"Lord Si fears that the Ancient Hidden Civilization may have already received some signal or intelligence. It is likely that they will collaborate with the Nine Heavens’ Chosen to summon the Great Avatar of Heaven, bringing about an early reckoning.

"At that time, the Reckoning Envoy of the Living Treasure Civilization will appear."

"The Vast Expanse will be plunged into the greatest catastrophe in its history."

The Mirror Lord’s crimson lips parted slightly; her voice was calm, yet her words caused the members of the Palace of Defiance gathered in the hall to turn pale, sensing an overwhelming sense of oppression and dread.

"How could this be..."

"Could it be that this time, we must abandon the Nine Heavens?"

The Eternal Dao Lord’s expression darkened. In the midst of such a cosmic catastrophe, even those at the Ancestral Dao Realm might end up as mere cannon fodder.

Every Reckoning Envoy possessed strength comparable to that of a True Path-level domain.

In past Great Reckonings, when Reckoning Envoys arrived, they would merely oversee the situation without interfering.

However, the Nine Heavens was unique—guarded by figures possessing True Path-level domains and housing the Heavenly Avatar’s seal. Even if an Enforcer were to search, they might not be able to pinpoint its exact location.

The other super-multiverses are also deeply concealed.

Thus, all coexisted peacefully until now.

But with the end of the Earth-Heaven Era approaching, it is highly likely that more than one Reckoning Envoy will appear.

At that time, who could possibly escape?

For a moment, the grand hall fell deathly silent; everyone was lost in silence, feeling utterly powerless.

"All along, the sealing and guarding of the Heavenly Avatar have been the responsibility of our Nine Heavens. The other super-multiverses have kept their distance, refusing to help and unwilling to be tainted by even a shred of karmic entanglement."

A moment later, a being with an aura of profound age and weariness spoke. His gaze held a mix of grief and indignation, yet also a sense of helplessness.

"We have truly done our utmost. Now that the Celestial Being has broken the seal and revived, the major super-multiverses have long taken notice of this scene, yet they remain indifferent, standing idly by, merely watching the spectacle."

"In this great calamity of heaven and earth, all beings are equal; no one can truly escape unscathed. So rather than letting us bear all the consequences alone, it would be better to let go… "Whatever the consequences, let us bear them together. We will no longer guard the Nine Heavens Tomb."

"With the situation having come to this, yet you still seek personal gain? If that is the case, then let’s overturn the table—no one should

spectators anymore. We must find a way—whatever it takes."

The others were filled with grief and indignation as well.

The Three Purities Dao Realm prides itself as the source of the Dao and the progenitor of all paths, yet at this critical juncture, it merely stands by with a sanctimonious air.

The Tathagata Buddha Realm claims to save all sentient beings and relieve suffering, yet isn’t it still just watching the spectacle unfold?

And what of the other super-multiverses where the End of the Path exists and holds sway? Are they not the same?

As for those Supreme Shattering Fragments, or True Path-level figures who wield the authority of the Heavenly Dao—what on earth are they doing?

"Flip the table..."

The Mirror Lord seemed not the least bit surprised by everyone’s reaction.

She also knew that, amidst the Vast Expanse of the cosmos, countless beings and entities were watching the Nine Heavens.

Yet, even now, no one has made an appearance.

Since that is the case… Soon, she shook her head slightly. Her crimson lips parted, and her calm voice, enveloped in supreme power, swept out from the Nine Heavens toward the vast worlds of the Vast Expanse.

"The heavens and earth are undergoing a great upheaval. We of the Nine Heavens have guarded the Nine-Layered Heavenly Tomb for generations, and we have long since done our utmost. Faced with such a great calamity, the Vast Expanse must unite in heart and effort, helping one another, for only then is there a glimmer of hope to safely weather this crisis..."

"Since you fellow Daoists are only concerned with your own petty, short-sighted interests and are unwilling to come to our aid, then let the Nine Heavens Tomb shatter on its own. Once the Nine Heavens emerge into the world, the consequences will be beyond the control of the Nine Heavens Realm."

It is clear that this matter has already received the approval of the Lord of the Bureau and the Lord of Confinement.

Otherwise, the Mirror Lord would not have stepped forward to speak these words before the Vast Expanse.

Everyone wants to watch the spectacle but refuses to help. Very well, then let everyone step aside. After all, this great calamity of heaven and earth engulfs the entire vast cosmos, and is by no means a private affair of the Nine Heavens.

Of course, the Nine Heavens Realm is indeed ill-equipped to handle this matter at present. No matter how powerful the Palace of Defiance of Fate may be, or how profound its foundations, it cannot possibly stand against the great tide of the cosmos.

The moment the Mirror Lord’s words were spoken, a thunderous roar echoed throughout the Vast Expanse—resounding across all true civilizations and major super-multiverses. In the Yuan Realm, the Ru Realm, the Man Realm… the expressions of many figures at the End of the Path shifted slightly.

"The Nine Heavens... they are forcing everyone to take a stand..."

In the Three Purities Dao Realm, the Lord of the Three Purities also furrowed his brow slightly, and his expression quickly darkened.

In the Tathagata Buddha Realm, within the Great Hero Hall, brilliant Buddha-light shone, surrounded by countless Buddha-realms. Vast, resonant Buddhist chants filled the air, and Bodhisattvas, Buddhas, and Arhats gathered in the sky.

= "The return of heaven and earth to the void is not necessarily a great calamity. Within destruction, new life is born; in the midst of despair, hope is revealed. The path forward may well be contained within this great calamity..."

The Buddha sat cross-legged upon the Nine-Colored Platform, chanting Buddhist verses with a face full of compassion, as if illuminating the suffering of all sentient beings.

Behind him, the Three Bodies—Past, Present, and Future—simultaneously emitted a vast radiance of Buddha-light, with Dharma Fruits coalescing. Within each of these Dharma Fruits, it seemed as though billions of cycles of reincarnation were imprinted in a flickering glow.

At this moment, the Buddha’s eyes grew profoundly deep, containing supreme wisdom, as if he had pierced through the mist to glimpse the bed of all knowledge.

Seated within the Tathāgata’s Buddha-realm, he extended a massive hand, radiant with Buddha-light. Within each finger, it seemed as though billions of Buddha-realms were taking shape, each teeming with countless Buddha-realm inhabitants chanting sutras, offering him boundless faith.

Behind him, countless Buddhas and Bodhisattvas manifested, chanting Buddhist verses.

The Vast Expanse of countless ages and boundless darkness was instantly illuminated by this single ray of Buddha-light, becoming as bright as day in the blink of an eye.

The Buddha intervened. Seated within the Tathagata’s realm, he brought down a palm strike beyond the Nine Heavens—like a mountain of five-fingered Buddha, vast, supreme, majestic, and towering—joining forces with the Eternal Ancestor, the Yellow Sovereign, and the Grave Keeper to collectively suppress Xiang Chengtian.

With the Buddha’s intervention, the situation shifted instantly; even Ruo Chengtian felt the crushing pressure of overwhelming might.

"Good. Let us subdue Cheng Tian first."

Eternal Ancestor’s gaze was profound as he struck again, unleashing a beam of Eternal Divine Light that pierced through the air.

The Yellow Lord and the Grave Keeper followed closely behind. Their techniques shifted in an instant, transforming into two golden Dao seals. Within them lay the intent to seal and subdue all things, as well as the surging resolve to conquer the heavens.
