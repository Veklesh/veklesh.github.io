Beyond the Ancient Hidden Civilization, the void churned ceaselessly as a Vast Expanse of ethereal energy erupted, giving rise to countless chasms of nothingness from which ancient warships surged forth, one after another.

These ancient warships were larger than the stars themselves; as they raced through space, the stars trembled, and one world after another shook ceaselessly in their wake.

Jiang Weiyang leaned against the railing, gazing into the distance. A divine light began to surge from her body. Piece after piece of ancient armor burst forth from the weapon chests carried by the maidservants before her, enveloping her form. As her black hair flowed freely, her gaze remained steady and composed, radiating an aura of an empress that rose from her.

In the background, the might of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty’s army also gathered and surged forth.

Ji Han Jing stood behind Jiang Wei Yang, seated upon a chair crafted of black obsidian inlaid with gold. Observing the shift in Jiang Wei Yang’s aura, he nodded approvingly to himself.

Her teachings over the past period had not been in vain.

"The Ancient Hidden Civilization harbors profound secrets, but since he intended for Wei Yang to come here in search of the Master of Destiny, he must have had a plan all along."

“If they’re setting a trap, then what’s wrong with being the bait…”

As these thoughts flashed through her mind, Ji Hanjing gazed toward the Ancient Hidden Civilization. Wave after wave of overwhelming and domineering divine senses swept toward her, causing the surrounding star domains to sway and tremble like a lone boat amidst giant waves, as if on the verge of plummeting into the abyss.

She let out a cold snort. A blood-red vision of a setting moon and falling stars unfolded in her eyes. The divine senses sweeping toward her were instantly struck as if by lightning; in a daze, they seemed to be confined within a bizarre, blood-red Daoist text, letting out agonized screams.

Having spent so many years as the spirit of a scavenger’s book, Ji Hanjing had indeed lost her way and forgotten much, but her strength at the peak of her power was by no means to be underestimated.

Though she had not yet fully recovered to her former self, ordinary figures at the Ancestral Dao Realm were no match for her.

Waaah…

Ancient trumpets sounded from one ancient warship after another, their voices echoing in all directions—a desolate yet majestic resonance.

Zhu Cangning, the Divine Martial General of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, stood there clad in armor, radiating an aura as vast as the Dao itself, leading his massive army into battle as they descended upon the outer realms of the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

Behind him, a vast and mighty army surged forward like a torrent, its end nowhere in sight, as more troops continued to pour through the fissure passages.

This was a force of unimaginable terror. Among them, besides General Zhu Cangning, were numerous commanders of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, leading squad after squad as they charged from other realms. Hidden within the shadows, the aura of beings at the Ancestral Dao Realm lingered.

"What has happened?"

This vast wave of energy swept across many realms; in numerous neighboring universes, powerful auras began to awaken one after another.

On life-sustaining stars radiant with vibrant hues, ancient figures stepped forth, their presence shaking the heavens and earth as they gazed at one another from afar.

"People from the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty?"

"Why have you come to our Ancient Hidden Civilization?"

An old man, reeking of decay, bellowed the question. He awoke from a cliff overgrown with withered grass and, with a single stride, appeared beyond the heavens.

Yet the only response was a cold snort that echoed throughout the heavens and earth: “Have the Master of Destiny show himself.”

Ji Hanjing sat upright within the ancient warship, gazing at the scene before her, her expression unreadable.

Her words carried an indescribable weight of meaning, reverberating through the Vast Expanse of heaven and earth. They were so terrifying that the void trembled and hummed like a pile of scattered paper.

Countless living beings and cultivators felt their eardrums ringing in waves, as if they were about to be torn apart.

"What does the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty mean by this? Could it be that they intend to wage war against our Ancient Hidden Civilization?"

However, Ji Hanjing’s words received no response. Another figure, clad entirely in black with wings on his back, stepped out from a star, his gaze cold and indifferent.

From the star behind him, boundless light erupted, with pillar after pillar of light shooting skyward, as if they were about to tear the cosmos apart.

At the same time, across various regions of the Ancient Hidden Civilization, a succession of overwhelmingly powerful auras began to awaken and emerge.

Unlike the other realms in the Vast Expanse, the Ancient Hidden Civilization has always been exceptionally mysterious. Even beings of the Dao Realm dare not act recklessly within it, for they might at any moment provoke unimaginably powerful figures.

Even as the Dark Clans wreaked havoc across the Vast Expanse, they have yet to lay a finger on the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

In the eyes of many beings, this is because they fear the overwhelming might of the Ancient Hidden Civilization and are unwilling to provoke it lightly.

Although the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty enjoys a formidable reputation within the Vast Expanse, it remains merely a regional power. While it possesses foundations comparable to those of the mightiest True Realms, it pales in significance when set against the ancient and mysterious Ancient Hidden Civilization.

Even just one of the five great royal houses of the Ancient Hidden Civilization possesses the strength to easily sweep away the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty.

To the beings and cultivators of the Ancient Hidden Civilization, the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty’s actions seemed utterly laughable.

Nevertheless, many ancient figures furrowed their brows upon awakening. While the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty itself posed no real threat, it was clear that it had a powerful backer: the Heaven Slaying Alliance, which was now on the verge of unifying the realm.

The Weiyang Immortal Dynasty’s invasion, appearing as a vanguard force—could this be a test by the Heaven Slaying Alliance?

After all, the Dark Clans are currently launching an invasion against the Nine Heavens, and now the Heaven Slaying Alliance is targeting the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

Could this be a coordinated effort?

However, in the Vast Expanse of the cosmos today, aside from the scattered True Realm civilizations hiding within the chaotic currents of spacetime, only the Ancient Hidden Civilization and the Nine Heavens Realm remain—these two places are considered sanctuaries.

But now, even these sanctuaries are beginning to suffer the ravages of war, and there is no longer any peace to be found.

"The Weiyang Immortal Dynasty has no intention of going to war with the Ancient Hidden Civilization, but we demand that the Ancient Hidden Civilization hand over the mastermind behind the Righteous Unity Alliance—the Master of Destiny."

"The Master of Destiny has colluded with the Dark Clans to wreak havoc upon our Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, and has even manipulated the life and death of our Weiyang Emperor, imprisoning and carrying him away..."

In the vast void, Jiang Weiyang’s voice suddenly rang out.

Her tone, crisp yet imbued with an extreme resolve, responded to the figures that had appeared in the void.

"Ridiculous."

"Who is this 'Master of Destiny'? We have never heard of him. If the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty knows what’s good for them, they should withdraw immediately. Given the current Vast Expanse situation and the hard-won peace and stability, we will overlook the offense committed against the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty."

"If not, then do not blame us for being ruthless."

The figures standing in the void were all beings of the Dao Realm.

Although they could sense the presence of figures at the Ancestral Dao Realm level concealed within the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty’s army, they showed no restraint whatsoever, and their words were filled with contempt and disdain toward the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty.

Jiang Weiyang’s expression remained unchanged, her voice as cool and decisive as ever. “In that case,” she said, “the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty will search for the Master of Destiny ourselves.”

“Hah. Little Princess of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, with your meager strength, you dare to act so brazenly in our Ancient Hidden Civilization?”

“You truly don’t know when to back down.”

Several of them let out scornful, cold laughs, completely unconcerned about the vast army of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty before them.

Jiang Weiyang’s lovely face was frozen in a frosty expression; she clenched her jade-like hands and then coldly ordered, “Attack.”

The great battle erupted, as inevitable as it was. The Weiyang Immortal Dynasty had opened a spacetime passage to arrive here, fully prepared for a bloody clash.

“Charge!”

As the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty’s army charged forward, they surged like a rolling tide, seemingly as vast as a billion troops.

In many of the Ancient Hidden Civilizations’ universes, portals also split open. Streaks of dazzling light illuminated the pitch-black star fields, revealing the long-depleted and lifeless worlds beyond.

And within those worlds, an inexplicable aura descended—a faint, white aura of malevolence, drifting like mist.

Those barren, lifeless lands began to crack open, and skeletal corpses emerged one after another. The white, malevolent aura swirled around them, instantly transforming into warhorses, armor, black celestial blades, and spears…Deeper within, a terrifying aura surged. One after another, bronze war chariots gleaming with a rusted light burst forth from the ground. From within them emerged generals, commanding vast armies as they charged forth.

It was a scene of unimaginable awe, sending shivers of dread through the heart.

In this world of the dead, an unknown force descended, imbued with a certain law. It caused the souls of those who had perished in the great war to reappear, donning armor once more as if brought back to life. Unafraid of death and oblivious to pain, they clashed fiercely with the vast armies of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty.

Around the Ancient Hidden Civilization, more and more fissures appeared, each leading to one world after another. Buried in the void of time and space for untold ages, the moment they emerged, they exuded an ancient and decaying aura.

Within one vast realm, a terrifying giant hand reached out, striking directly toward the location where the generals and commanders of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty were gathered.

Ji Hanjing’s face was as cold as frost; he struck out with a palm of his own, colliding with it. Endless particles of spacetime erupted and swirled there, causing relentless tremors, as if a terrifying storm capable of sweeping across all worlds had emerged.

Cracks spread into the void, devouring the shattered universes.

The Dao Realm experts of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty also joined the fray, and the Imperial Ancestral Dao Realm, hidden in the shadows, did not hesitate for a moment.

The chaos and turmoil here were immense, the scale of the battle comparable to the clash between the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance in the past, though the scope of its impact was far less vast and expansive.

At the same moment, the commotion here instantly drew the attention of numerous Ancestral Dao Realm beings amidst the Vast Expanse.

It could be said that this battle came far too suddenly; many had not anticipated that the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty had been mobilizing such a massive army during this period.

It must be said that since the formation of the Vast Expanse unified order, few had paid attention to the activities within their respective member realms. Unless they received a decree from the Alliance Master, who would have anticipated that a major battle would erupt so suddenly?

Among the True Realms of the various member civilizations of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, their respective leaders also began to take note of the Wei Yang Immortal Dynasty’s assault on the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

Some have attempted to contact acquaintances within the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty to determine if the Alliance Leader’s guidance lies behind this, but have received no response.

And now, more people are actually focusing their attention on the Dark Clans.

With the seal on the Source of Darkness broken and the Ruler of Heaven’s revival, it is now inevitable that they cannot be stopped.

Once the Dark Clans have overrun the Vast Expanse, will the current state of relative calm be shattered by upheaval—such as the Dark Clans and the Heaven Slaying Alliance tearing up their previous pact and plunging into all-out war?

This great war erupted suddenly and, once it began, raged on for years with no end in sight.

Deep within the Sea of Reincarnation, the Master of Destiny remained impassive. The Primordial Void and the Master of Calamity, standing by his side, both cast meaningful glances his way but said nothing.

Within the Heaven Slaying Alliance, Gu Changge, clad in snow-white robes, sat quietly in the courtyard.

Before him, a jade-green pool lay like a mirror, reflecting the Vast Expanse of all realms: the fierce slaughter between the Nine Heavens and the Dark Clans; the four High Priests cultivated by Wu Yue joining forces to tear open the Void, ushering in the vast, surging hordes of the Dark Clans; and the Third Heaven of the Nine Heavens, crumbling and collapsing layer by layer.

There was also the scene before the Ancient Hidden Civilization, where the vast armies of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty surged forward like a roaring tide, unleashing a massive battle.

And within a dark, lightless underground palace, a blood-red pool churned, shrouded in a hazy mist.

That figure clad in armor sat cross-legged there, employing various methods to attempt to refine the pulsating mass of flesh before him, which rose and fell like a heart.

Before him, black mist swirled, engulfing the underground chamber, while he breathed heavily, his gaze filled with longing and intense fervor.

In the corners where the Black Lotus Sect still thrives, some continue to seek the truth and investigate the sect’s origins.

She was a radiant young woman in the prime of her youth, as dazzling as the sun, clad in a loose, navy-blue cloak. Her eyes, as clear as autumn water, bore a calm and composed demeanor uncharacteristic of her age.

Across the realms where fate and destiny have scattered their seeds, those blessed with great fortune rise rapidly. In each universe, there are beings known as protagonists who, bearing the mandate of heaven, swiftly stir up the winds and clouds in all directions.

In every corner of the Vast Expanse, the diverse tapestry of life unfolds.

"As for Wu Yue, she cannot inflict any serious damage on the Nine Heavens. Compared to the Nine Heavens’ profound foundations, her strength is still far too weak."

"However, this is of little consequence. With the reawakening of my identity as the Ruler of Heaven, sooner or later, someone deep within the Nine Heavens will be unable to contain themselves..."

Gu Changge’s gaze fell upon the Nightmare Demon Realm.

Before the Hall of the Celestial Regent, a vast, dark horde knelt prostrate. Endless crimson matter surged forth from the Coffin of the World’s Demise, and from within, a breath as terrifying as the Mountains And Seas seemed to resound.

The power from the Source of Darkness and that within the Coffin of the World’s Demise were merging, converging.

As Gu Changge’s gaze fell upon it, the misty, heart-like substance within twisted ceaselessly, growing increasingly indistinct. Countless dark masses surged forth like a bursting dam, engulfing the entire Nightmare Demon Realm.

Thud, thud, thud…

Across the Vast Expanse of the cosmos, countless true realms of the universe now heard the sound of a heartbeat—as if billions of gods and demons were beating drums, standing at the very edge of the heavens. The reverberations of the sound pierced through countless layers of the void.

"We humbly greet our Lord..."

"We welcome our Lord..."

The fervent, frenzied shouts of the Dark Clans echoed throughout the realm. Regardless of their cultivation level, all beings trembled deeply as they knelt there, their souls quivering and every bone in their bodies emitting a sound of trembling.

Whoosh, whoosh, whoosh...

Countless chaotic energies filled the air. At that moment, a terrifying, all-encompassing pressure seemed to awaken, warping the very fabric of the Vast Expanse and time. The fabric of reality collapsed relentlessly, and even the river of time began to evaporate and vanish.

In the next instant, the darkness that had existed since the very beginning of creation was torn apart.

Amidst the boundless, eternal darkness, a pair of cold, silent eyes materialized from within. Vast beyond measure, they obscured the heavens, as if suspended high above the Vast Expanse.

Those pupils were so vast and majestic that it seemed as though all beings were fading into nothingness, the cosmos was being obliterated, and endless life and chaotic matter were rushing through them.

The scene behind it was utterly indistinct, as if no space-time existed at all—blurred and chaotic to the extreme. Only those indifferent eyes, filling the entire expanse beyond the world, were reflected there.

"The Ruler of Heaven has awakened..."

The mightiest beings across the civilizations of the True Realms all felt a sudden pang of dread and trembling in their hearts. No matter where they were, they were seen clearly by that single pupil; the boundaries between all worlds had become crystal clear.

If the Vast Expanse were likened to a boundless ocean, then the True Realms born within it would be the isolated islands and lands within that ocean. Now, as that eye opened, countless beings that had been slumbering in the depths of the sea were successively awakened.

This aura is indescribable—overwhelming, domineering, supreme, and yet inconceivable. Neither before the birth of the heavens and earth, nor after, nor even in the distant future, has such power ever seemed to exist.

As those eyes opened, an endless storm of the Great Dao began to sweep forth from that place. A sound like a breath swept past countless great worlds and universes. Under such a colossal aura, even the True Realm trembled, like a withered leaf in a gale, ready to shatter at any moment.

At that moment, the Vast Expanse seemed to fall silent, then plunged into deep dread and icy chill.
