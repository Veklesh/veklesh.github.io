In this secluded world, the mother and daughter sat in silence, lost in thought for a long time.

At that moment, Nan Qing couldn’t help but reflect on many things.

Li Hanying, bearing a fragment of the Lord of Heaven’s flesh and blood, had arrived in the Nightmare Demon Realm at this critical juncture, thwarting the schemes of the Ancestral Void and ultimately unleashing the malevolent intent of the Lord of Heaven from the Coffin of the World’s Demise.

Meanwhile, the information she had received from Gu Xian’er revealed that Li Hanying was the Holy Maiden of the Ethereal Holy Realm—a being of dual bodies and three coexisting souls—who had been sent there long ago, at the very founding of the Ethereal Holy Realm.

Could it be that her mother, the Southern Venerable, had also been influenced by the Western Saint when she first ventured into the Nightmare Demon Realm?

Behind all these events, it seemed an invisible web was unfolding, casting a heavy net over everything in sight.

"What exactly is the Western Saint trying to do?"

Nan Qing took a deep breath, a chill running down her spine.

Could it be that even her very birth was the result of the Western Saint’s scheming?

"The waters of the Vast Expanse are far murkier than they appear on the surface. What you see is merely the tip of the iceberg floating above the water. As far as I know, aside from the Western Saint—that enigmatic entity—there is another figure of unknown origin lurking in the shadows..." Southern Venerable Nan said.

"Who?"

Nan Qing asked.

"The world calls him the Earth Ancestor."

"The Palace of Defiance and this Earth Ancestor are intricately connected. In the dawn of the Vast Expanse, there was a sect known as the Heaven-Binding Sect. Its founder is believed to be the fourth leader behind the Palace of Defiance," Southern Venerable Nan said softly.

Before this, she would never have discussed these ancient secrets with Nan Qing.

But now the situation was different. What a chaotic mess the Vast Expanse had become!

It could be said that all manner of demons and monsters have emerged. If he were unaware of the stakes involved, she feared Nan Qing might get caught up in this vortex while out in the world, leading to the annihilation of his body, mind, and spirit.

“There’s actually a fourth leader behind the Palace of Defiance?”

Nan Qing was stunned once again.

She had once traveled to the Nine Heavens with Gu Xian’er and stayed there for a while, so she had some understanding of the Palace of Defiance.

Historically, the Palace of Defiance had always had only three leaders: the distant ancestor of the Gu family, the Lord of Restraint, and the Master. There was absolutely no information regarding this fourth leader, nor had anyone ever mentioned him.

"Be wary of anyone connected to Western Saint."

"Be wary of Western Saint." Southern Venerable urged her earnestly.

Upon hearing this, Nan Qing took a deep breath and said, “I understand, Mother.”

The mother and daughter did not dwell on the past any further.

After all, the outside world was in chaos. Once the great battle between the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness and the deities of the Lord of Heaven’s evil intentions concluded, an even greater storm would sweep across the Vast Expanse.

Moreover, the Ancient Altar of Reckoning, cast in light, still hung at the very edge of the vast, shadowy void beyond the Realms. Its light of reckoning radiated across the cosmos, and its waves of judgment could sweep in at any moment.

In the midst of this great upheaval, no living being will be spared.

……

The chaotic front lines of the great war between the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

Blinding spearlight sliced through the void and the cosmos, accompanied by a shocking crimson tide of blood, piercing one of the Heavenly Lords of Absolute Darkness’s followers once more and pinning him deep within the void.

Terrifying shards of spacetime crumbled, endlessly spattering and swirling, tearing apart the cosmos.

Yue Mingkong stood suspended in midair, her black hair stained with blood. Drops of blood trickled down her Six Heavens and Abyss armor, and her entire body radiated a fierce, dazzling glow, exuding unparalleled might.

At this moment, she was truly like an unrivaled Empress, overshadowing all the heavens and the cosmos. Her aura was terrifyingly chilling; even the followers from the Land Of Truth felt a deep sense of dread.

Three!

Three of their comrades had already vanished into eternal silence, fallen on this side—something they could never have imagined before their arrival.

"Just relying on the troops and armor left behind by the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, and yet it’s this terrifying..."

"This defies all reason."

The remaining four beings felt a chill run down their spines; they had already been thoroughly terrified by Yue Mingkong’s killings.

Especially now that the Heavenly Lord of the Absolute Yin seemed to be facing immense trouble himself and had no time to spare for them; otherwise, he wouldn’t have allowed Yue Mingkong to slaughter three of them right before his eyes.

Even if the four of them joined forces, they would be unable to kill Yue Mingkong, for his armor was far too sturdy—forged through unknown methods of refinement.

Even attacks with aura fluctuations surpassing the True Path realm would be blocked by it, unable to harm Yue Mingkong.

Unless the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness intervenes personally.

"Kill."

Under the stunned gazes of countless beings on the battlefield, Yue Mingkong struck again. The Eight Desolate Demon Halberd hummed as it cleaved downward, its terrifying radiance seeming to sever the fabric of time and space.

The being bearing the ancient coffin, filled with shock and rage, fiercely resisted.

A torrent of overwhelming corpse energy surged forth, as if capable of drowning the heavens, sweeping toward the descending spear.

The other three beings struck simultaneously. A dense barrage of light erupted, weaving patterns that transcended the True Path realm. Intertwining and coalescing, they channeled the power from the Heavenly Seal Talisman, transforming it into a surging torrent of overwhelming energy that poured forth.

In an instant, the chaos qi in this place boiled, the light of the Great Dao scattered, and the blinding spearlight tore through everything, obliterating all in its path.

Even a single ripple that escaped effortlessly split the vast cosmos in the distance in two.

Such a scene sent shockwaves through the Vast Expanse of all worlds.

As Yue Mingkong struck, he also deliberately targeted the armies of the Ancient Hidden Civilization. The Absolute Yin Beings created by the Absolute Yin Box were caught in the crossfire; in an instant, vast numbers perished, completely dissolving and exploding into fragments.

The Absolute Yin Realm, which the Ancient Hidden Civilization had originally established as its first line of defense, was also torn apart.

The Bagua Formation deployed deep within erupted with a celestial domain field; countless runes shone brightly as they put up resistance. Yet, under the strike of that terrifying spearlight, it disintegrated and ultimately collapsed.

Finally, the runes filling the sky in that region dimmed. The ancient banners positioned at the eight cardinal directions lay in tatters, and with a thunderous roar, the Bagua Formation was utterly destroyed.

With that, the two defensive lines of the Ancient Hidden Civilization were completely torn apart.

The Grave Keeper of the Nine Heavens, who had been trapped within this formation, suddenly let out a furious roar, unleashing a terrifying aura of the End of the Path tier. A vast, overwhelming energy surged toward the heavens, like a blazing sun rising into the sky, as he burst forth.

When the Ancient Hidden Civilization’s army launched their sudden ambush, he had charged forward alone to block the advancing hordes. Ultimately, outside the frontline zone, he was lured by the Lord of Calamity and his cohorts and trapped deep within the Land of Utter Darkness.

He was naturally aware of the situation unfolding outside, but unfortunately, despite his strength, he was unable to break free from that ancient formation.

It contained a thread of mystery transcending the True Path realm; even a True Path-level figure would find it difficult to escape.

"Charge!"

Seeing this, the armies of the Heaven Slaying Alliance were greatly encouraged and charged through the shielded zone to kill their way through.

Within the ranks of the Dark Clan, the four High Priests commanded a vast, tide-like army of darkness, flanking the sides like wings and striking like sharp swords as they advanced into the heartland of the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

"How could it have come to this?"

Existences such as the Lord of Calamity, the Lord of Longevity, and the Lord of Extinction were under siege; the situation was dire, leaving them no time to concern themselves with what was happening over there.

Opposite them, figures at the End of the Path from every corner of the Vast Expanse of multiverses were striking with ruthless ferocity, showing no mercy.

This final reckoning had actually compelled the various figures at the End of the Path—who had previously chosen to mind their own business—to step forward and band together, a development they had never anticipated.

Of course, a small minority still chose to watch from the sidelines, but essentially no beings at the End of the Path defected to side with the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

Whoosh!

A dazzling beam of light pierced through the air, and with a thunderous roar, countless stars along its path were obliterated and exploded.

This stream of arrowlight pierced through the heavens and earth to the very end, like an immortal arrow shattering the cosmos. On either side of the arrow, scenes of the creation of the world unfolded: chaos arising and vanishing, ancient ancestors descending, and billions of realms offering prayers and chanting ritual hymns.

This was Qi Jian—an existence who had attained the Way through the arrow, reaching the pinnacle of Ascension at the End of the Path.

This single arrow was as if gods and demons were driven back, and the heavens were vast and boundless, possessing the overwhelming might to carve out a path of life for future generations.

He stood in the distance, shoulder to shoulder with the Eternal Ancestor, the Yellow Sovereign, and others, his bow drawn like a full moon, as endless symbols of the Great Dao rose and swirled around him.

Whoosh…

Blood spattered through the void; every drop seared so fiercely that space and time fractured, and myriad Dao forces retreated.

The Lord of Calamity let out a agonized cry as half his body exploded. The armor on his shoulder shattered into pieces, scattering in all directions—the impact was immense.

Though he stood within the End of the Path Ascension Realm, he stood no chance against the combined might of a group of End of the Path-level figures.

BOOM!!!

Following the Arrow Lord’s strike, the Eternal Ancestor and the Yellow Lord also moved to attack.

Their techniques shifted in an instant, transforming into two golden Dao seals. Within them lay the intent to suppress and seal all things, along with a surging resolve to defy the heavens.

The Dao Seals descended like an immortal mountain, crushing down upon the Lord of Calamity. He was struck, spewing blood once more, and his body was nearly torn to pieces.

On the other side, the Lord of Longevity, the Lord of Extinction, and the others were also in dire straits, facing a combined assault from the Tathagata Buddha Realm, the Five Elements True Realm, and the Light and Darkness Divine Realm.

The Tathagata Buddha, radiant in golden light, embodied the laws of heaven and earth. With a single finger pointing like a blossoming flower, an endless host of Buddhas and Bodhisattvas manifested behind him, chanting Buddhist verses.

The Vast Expanse of the cosmos, shrouded in endless darkness, was instantly illuminated by this beam of Buddha’s light, becoming as bright as day in the blink of an eye.

The Lord of Longevity coughed up blood. His true form was unstable, merely a mass of strange mist; yet under the illumination of the Buddha’s light, it became crystal clear. Ultimately, even his Dao Fruit began to crack, revealing his grievous injuries.

The master of the Five Elements True Realm was likewise a being of the End of the Path tier, known simply as Bing Wuxing Daosheng.

Around him, the Primordial Five Elements Dao unfolded, transforming into five surging rivers of the Dao. Metal, Wood, Water, Fire, and Earth intertwined, collectively engulfing the skies above the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

The master of the Realm of Light and Darkness commanded both the paths of light and darkness, at times dazzling and brilliant, at others deep and pitch-black, as he clashed with the Lord of Extinction.

This great battle was exceptionally fierce; a rain of blood spattered high into the air, scattering across the cosmos.

On the other side, the Lord of Reincarnation, the Lord of the World, and the Ancient Treasure Emperor were faring no better; all bore wounds and were covered in blood, besieged by the Lord of Power, the Lord of Restraint, the Lord of Governance, and Gu Xihan, the First Ancestor of the Gu Clan.

The tide of battle turned, and a cry of despair rang out from the Guest Realm.

The Ancient Hidden Civilization finally faced its decline.

All high-ranking officials and powerful figures were under siege, and countless clans and living beings were annihilated and met a gruesome death.

Across the boundless, vast expanse of the cosmos, the armies of the Heaven Slaying Alliance charged in with relentless ferocity, sweeping through like a cosmic torrent and shattering the numerous defensive lines in their path.

One by one, the stars of life were extinguished and shattered.

Many tribes that had aligned themselves with the Ancient Hidden Civilization were subjected to a purge; the path was strewn with blood, leaving no life behind.

Just as the many supreme beings on the Vast Expanse’s side were rejoicing,

From the farthest reaches of the abyssal void, deep within the shroud of infinite dark matter, a deafening, earth-shattering roar suddenly echoed out. The multiverse was thrown into turmoil, and the river of time itself was thrown into chaos.
