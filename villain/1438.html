Half a month passed in the blink of an eye. At Gu Changge’s command, the entire Heaven Slaying Alliance sprang into action.

One ancient warship and ancient war chariot after another rumbled into view, crushing the heavens beneath them as they tore open tunnels through space and time, rushing in from all directions.

Numerous high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance set out from their respective cosmic abodes, joining Gu Changge to attend the Immortal Dao Alliance’s ceremonial observance.

For the high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, Gu Changge rarely made an appearance, but whenever he did, it would cause great upheaval in the Vast Expanse.

Thus, many high-ranking members secretly hoped to see if the situation would once again be set in motion. The establishment of the Immortal Dao Alliance this time held epoch-making significance for the Vast Expanse.

Meanwhile, Chu Heng, Hun Yuanjun, Zhuo Fengwei, and the other beings from the Forbidden Zone—who had received Gu Changge’s orders long ago—were secretly rallying the vast armies from the various universes of the Heaven Slaying Alliance during this time, determined not to give the Righteous Unity Alliance any chance to catch its breath.

A surging undercurrent coursed through the entire Vast Expanse, and civilizations across the True Realms could distinctly sense a suffocating pressure, as if a storm were brewing.

This period could truly be called a time of turmoil; even the Divine Path Civilization, which had always remained in the depths of spacetime and refrained from intervening in the feuds among the civilizations of the Vast Expanse, was thrown into chaos.

The Great Ancestral God of Taixuan has appeared in the various divine realms, leading his numerous deities in a bloody slaughter. According to the other ancient ancestral gods, the Great Ancestral God of Taixuan has already fallen into darkness, and the entire Taixuan Divine Realm has long been filled with darkness and the uncanny.

Even a being as powerful and ancient as the Great Proclamation Ancestral God had met such a fate, which sent shivers down the spines of the ancestral gods across all realms.

Dark matter truly seeps into every crevice; who knows when it had already infected and corrupted everything…

This incident served as a wake-up call for the other civilizations. Though the Dark Clans appeared to have laid low, they were in fact lying in wait; even the Divine Path Civilization had been infiltrated by them. In the Vast Expanse, there truly were few safe havens left.

The true realms of all civilizations are also rallying and joining forces, intent on jointly resisting the Dark Clans.

In their view, the establishment of the Immortal Dao Alliance is not only urgent but also an inevitable trend—one that no one can stop.

To oppose it would be to stand against the entire civilization of the Vast Expanse.

The source of the darkness is rapidly approaching, poised to plunge into the Vast Expanse and merge with the various realms. There is little time left for the civilizations of the True Realms.

During this time, the various factions closely monitoring the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s movements also noticed that the runaway princess of the Wei Yang Immortal Dynasty had unexpectedly appeared within the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

She appeared openly in an ancient city within the Heaven Slaying Alliance, strolling with several maidservants as if shopping for supplies.

Having restored her true form, she wore a gauze veil, making no attempt to conceal her identity. She seemed unconcerned that her presence might attract the covetous gazes of those with ulterior motives within the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

As soon as this news spread, it naturally sparked speculation and caused quite a stir among many interested parties.

The Righteous Unity Alliance and the Heaven Slaying Alliance were as incompatible as water and fire; they had only just ceased hostilities and were in a temporary truce. Yet for the Princess of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty to appear in the Heaven Slaying Alliance like this—what did it signify?

It must be noted that her father, the Emperor of Weiyang, is currently the leader of the Righteous Unity Alliance, presiding over the alliance and commanding its various factions.

Could this be a deliberate ploy by the Heaven Slaying Alliance to spread this news, intending to make the leader of the Righteous Unity Alliance hesitate to act?

However, Jiang Weiyang’s elopement is shrouded in mystery, and various factions are speculating whether there might be some deeper meaning behind it.

Meanwhile, within the Heaven Slaying Alliance, some have noticed that the princess of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty seems to be highly regarded by the Alliance Leader. She is often seen entering and leaving the courtyard where the Leader meditates, and more often than not, she leaves with a smile on her face.

This news has caused a sensation, sparking widespread speculation.

It must be noted that even for the high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, securing an audience with the Alliance Leader is no easy feat; for others, it requires accumulating sufficient military merits.

To be able to come and go freely from that courtyard and see the Heaven Slaying Alliance Leader, Gu Changge, at any time is the ultimate proof of one’s status within the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Many began to speculate whether Jiang Weiyang’s appearance might have been orchestrated by the Alliance Leader.

After all, accompanied by only a few maidservants, how could she have evaded the all-out manhunts launched by the various factions of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty and the Ancient Kingdom of Nanzhao, safely traversed the vast expanse of time and space, and arrived at the Heaven Slaying Alliance?

Without the protection of the Heaven Slaying Alliance Leader, could she truly have made it to the Heaven Slaying Alliance?

The group of powerful warriors dispatched by the Ancient Kingdom of Nanzhao to retrieve Jiang Weiyang halted at the border of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, daring not to venture a single step further.

Upon learning that Jiang Weiyang had fled to the Heaven Slaying Alliance, the one who felt the most aggrieved was, naturally, the Ninth Prince of the Ancient Kingdom of Nanzhao, Ye Wudao.

"Is this Emperor Weiyang playing games with me? How could he have let Jiang Weiyang escape all the way here?"

On the summit of a steep, renowned mountain, a man clad in gold stood, flanked by numerous powerful figures, his expression grim.

He possessed a commanding presence, his jet-black hair flowing freely, exuding a sharp, domineering aura. A faint current of the Dao of Heaven and Earth swirled around him—a natural, peerless might that had existed since time immemorial. This was Ye Wudao, the Ninth Prince of the Ancient Kingdom of Nanzhao.

"Moreover, that Jiang Weiyang is my fiancée. How dare she come and go so freely in the courtyard of the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance? What does this mean?" Ye Wudao’s eyes flashed as chaotic mist swirled within them. He clenched his fists, a deep sense of displeasure welling up within him.

"Your Highness, this is the border territory of the Heaven Slaying Alliance. Please be cautious with your words. The Alliance Leader possesses immense power; if he were to hear this, we might all meet our end here."

“As for Jiang Weiyang fleeing her marriage like this, without the tacit approval of the Emperor of Weiyang, I fear she wouldn’t even be able to escape the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty. It would be best to return to Nanzhao first, report this matter to the King, and let him decide.”

A powerful figure standing beside Ye Wudao spoke respectfully.

“I’m eager to see just what tricks the Emperor of Weiyang is playing. The moment I learned he was bestowing the jewel of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty upon me through this marriage, I knew he was doing so reluctantly. But this was also my Master’s will…”

Ye Wudao narrowed his eyes, his sense of resentment growing stronger. He felt as though he were being treated like a monkey.

He gazed at the vast, majestic city walls stretching before him—resembling a true dragon lying across the landscape—yet dared not set foot there lightly.

Although the area appeared almost completely deserted from this vantage point, he understood all too well that the Heaven Slaying Alliance dared to leave the borderlands so brazenly unguarded precisely because they feared no external power would dare to set foot there.

Not even the Dark Clans—whose chaos and destruction strike fear into the hearts of civilizations across the Vast Expanse—dare to act against the Heaven Slaying Alliance lightly.

And the source of all this confidence and reliance was entirely due to the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

“How was Jiang Weiyang able to arrive at the Heaven Slaying Alliance safely back then?”

Ye Wudao’s fists clenched so hard they cracked. As he learned of all that had happened to Jiang Weiyang during this time, he felt increasingly humiliated, realizing that he, her nominal fiancé, had become a laughingstock to everyone.

Yet, he dared not harbor even a shred of hostility or resentment toward the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Such a being could sense even the slightest mention of him in daily conversation; if he were to harbor hostility toward him, a single thought from the depths of the unknown might cause him to die silently and without a trace.

"Never mind. I’d rather not have this engagement. Once I return to Nanzhao, I’ll have my father step in personally to annul the marriage alliance with the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty." Ye Wudao felt his spirits sink; he waved his hand and turned to leave.

Although his betrothal had been arranged by the Yinxu Cave and the Emperor of Weiyang had reluctantly agreed to it,

In his view, as long as his father intervened, the Weiyang Immortal Court would now be more than willing to cancel the engagement.

Otherwise, he would only become a laughingstock to everyone.

No matter how highly the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance valued Jiang Weiyang, and no matter the nature of their relationship.

Even if Jiang Weiyang were ultimately taken in by the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, he, as her nominal fiancé, would have no choice but to grit his teeth and accept it—and he couldn’t even harbor a single thought of jealousy or resentment.

What man could possibly endure such a situation?

Now, Ye Wudao strongly suspected that the Emperor of Weiyang had long foreseen this day. Only he had been so eager at the time, deluding himself into believing he could claim this jewel of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty.

"Now I’m just afraid my Master won’t agree. Since the Emperor of Weiyang is connected to the Yinxu Cave, why would he orchestrate this?"

As Ye Wudao and the others departed, in a void far off, space twisted, and a figure with a face covered in scars—a hideous, terrifying visage—materialized.

At his side walked a massive black dog.

"This Ninth Prince of the ancient Nanzhao Kingdom is actually quite clever and knows his place—not the complete fool I imagined. Still, the fact that Jiang Weiyang really did escape to the Heaven Slaying Alliance is quite unexpected."

"I thought she’d stop at the outer perimeter at most, but I never expected her to actually follow that girl in the red dress into the Heaven Slaying Alliance and even get involved with the Alliance Leader."

"I suspect the person who startled the Star-Illuminating National Preceptor into fleeing was none other than the Heaven Slaying Alliance Master. That girl in the red skirt is likely the little witch from the Heaven Slaying Alliance—rumor has it she is the Alliance Master’s disciple…"

The big black dog’s dark, brooding gaze fixed intently on the distant border of the Heaven Slaying Alliance. It, too, dared not take a single step beyond it, as if that place were a forbidden zone even for the gods.

Its eyes gleamed with a human-like intelligence as it spoke in human language, offering its conjecture.

"We’ve been following Jiang Weiyang this whole time. We can’t possibly keep trying to sneak into the Heaven Slaying Alliance now, can we? The Heaven Slaying Alliance isn’t like the Nine Heavens, where you can leave at any time. Once we get inside, the Alliance Leader will likely spot us immediately, and then it’ll be hard to get out..." The man beside the big black dog frowned and said.

The man and the dog had already discussed leaving earlier and not continuing to tail Jiang Weiyang’s movements.

However, news from another source had prompted them to keep watching, monitoring Jiang Weiyang’s movements. It wasn’t until they saw her actually enter the Heaven Slaying Alliance that they dared to stop following her.

"As for that Ji Yu’s revival plan, it looks like it’s going down the drain now. Neither the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty nor the Heaven Slaying Alliance is a place one can set foot in lightly at the moment." The man with the scar on his face shook his head, looking somewhat exasperated.

“Ji Yu must have returned to the Nine Heavens by now. He went by the alias Song Yu and spent all those years at the Crescent Moon Sect—who knows what he was scheming there…”

“Well, look at that now—the Crescent Moon Sect has been completely wiped out by the Dark Clans. It’s as if he wasted all those years there for nothing.”

At this point, the scarred man wore a look of schadenfreude.

The big black dog shot him a glance, shook his head, and said, “After all, Ji Yu is the person our master has chosen. Since our master told us to help him, we shouldn’t worry about the rest.”

The Ji Yu he referred to was a direct descendant of the Yao Guang Ancestral Court.

For all these years, he had been plotting the revival of the Yao Guang Ancestral Court.

As Jiang Yun’s daughter, Jiang Weiyang was the most accessible target for Ji Yu. He hoped to gain influence over the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty by gradually infiltrating it through her.

Unfortunately, during his time in the Ancient Realm of Yao Guang, his advances were met with cold indifference; from start to finish, he never managed to exchange a single word with Jiang Weiyang.

Later, forced to return to the Crescent Moon Sect, he encountered the emergence of the Dark Clans, which swept across the land, wreaking havoc everywhere.

The Crescent Moon Sect and other sects bore the brunt of the attack and were annihilated by the Dark Clans.

Ji Yu was forced to abandon his original goals and plans, fleeing all the way back to the Nine Heavens.

Upon learning that Jiang Weiyang had eloped and left the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, Ji Yu specifically contacted them, asking them to keep an eye on Jiang Weiyang’s movements, to be dealt with at a later date.
