Qingyi did not hesitate. Although she had not inquired about Gu Changge’s intentions or plans, she was well aware that the entire Ruomang region was likely to undergo changes unprecedented in history; without formidable cultivation and strength, it would be difficult to survive there.

Gu Changge’s attempt at Heaven Slaying would undoubtedly face unprecedented obstacles.

Those beings and entities that once existed only in ancient texts and legends would all emerge one after another. The Ji Chan clan possessed a clear understanding and awareness of the workings of the cosmic order.

Gu Changge had planted the Epoch Mother Tree within the Heaven Slaying Alliance. In the short term, it had caused no apparent repercussions; in fact, it had even caused the Alliance’s fortune to surge and rise.

But Qingyi knew full well that this was Gu Changge using his supreme power to distort the will of Heaven and eliminate many adverse effects; yet this hidden danger remains, and it will undoubtedly erupt in the future.

Gu Changge had helped her immensely. Though he had never asked for anything in return, she longed to assist him in some way.

Qingyi went into seclusion to refine the Destiny Master Pill.

Gu Changge, meanwhile, returned to the central hall of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, where a figure had been waiting for him—clad in black robes as graceful as lotus petals, with a cold, striking beauty. It was none other than Ji Ziyi.

She bowed her hands in respect, her eyes brimming with undisguised reverence, and reported, “The Ji Chan clan has now settled down. Second Sister holds the reins of power, and the elders have ceased their objections.”

She suddenly hesitated, as if she wanted to say something but held back.

“Is there a problem with the Ji Yuan Mother Tree?” Gu Changge remained calm, as if she had anticipated this.

Ji Ziyi explained, “My second elder sister discovered black spots appearing on some of the main trunks of the Primordial Mother Tree, showing signs of gradually corroding and spreading. These insects have no physical form; cultivators cannot see them, and they only manifest a body when they come into contact with the essence of the world. "
