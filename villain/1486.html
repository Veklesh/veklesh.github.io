Outside the Ancient Hidden Civilization, as they witnessed this scene, the members of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty trembled uncontrollably—not out of fear, but out of excitement and elation.

Although Ji Hanjing had long anticipated that Gu Changge might intervene, she never imagined he would personally descend upon this place. With a single move, he had silenced the entire Ancient Hidden Civilization, and from the depths of infinite time and space, he had snatched the Ruler of Destiny out with a single palm strike.

Such prowess and power far exceeded her imagination; it seemed that Lu Jin, in Gu Changge’s hands, was now as insignificant as an ant.

“Just how powerful is he? Has he been recovering all these years? Or has he always been this strong?” Ji Hanjing murmured.

When she first met Gu Changge as the spirit of the Scavenger’s Book, she had merely found him terrifying, but she had never imagined he could be this powerful.

It seemed that over the years, he had rarely intervened, yet every time he did, it was earth-shattering and awe-inspiring, a spectacle that would have stunned the universe throughout all of history.

"The Alliance Leader has intervened personally..."

Jiang Weiyang’s bright eyes fixed intently on the scene, her gaze finally settling on the Master of Destiny, revealing deep hatred.

The greatest mastermind behind the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty had finally been caught.

If it weren’t for Gu Changge, perhaps she would have found it difficult to exact revenge in this lifetime based solely on her own cultivation and strength, let alone rescue her parents from his clutches.

"If you had simply established the Righteous Unity Alliance and followed the destiny I had arranged for you, you might well have obtained what you’ve always dreamed of in the future. Alas, you chose to be clever, breaking free from the fate I had laid out for you, and embarked on a path you knew was dead-end—yet you insisted on opposing me."

Gu Changge stood tall in the air, gently withdrawing her hand. The boundless expanse of time and space began to heal, and everything that had just happened seemed to have turned into an illusion.

Only the Master of Fate was snatched before him, his entire body bound by an invisible force, unable to move a muscle.

Upon hearing these words, his face—which had previously appeared somewhat refined—suddenly contorted, as if filled with a rage that could shake the heavens.

"I hate it, I hate it..."

In that instant, countless scenes and images flashed through his mind; it seemed that Gu Changge’s words had revealed many truths he had never understood before.

He took "fortune" as his path and was known as the Master of Fortune, yet the world’s fortunes encompassed everything—beyond mere luck, there was destiny and more.

At this moment, he felt as though the fog that had long obscured his vision had lifted, and his understanding of fate suddenly reached a realm he had never before attained.

Of course, this was not a sudden breakthrough in cultivation; reaching this stage required countless years of accumulation, countless insights, and the right opportunities to advance through each level.

The Master of Fate simply sensed that his death was now inevitable, with no possibility of escape. Thus, in that final flash of clarity before death, he came to understand many truths he had never known before.

In fact, from the moment he had dispatched the Old Woman Xingying to appear in the vast lands of Xianchu, he had already been bound by an invisible net of fate.

From that moment on, Gu Changge had already foreseen the future. The reason he allowed the Master to establish the Righteous Unity Alliance in secret to oppose him—despite knowing of his existence—was to stir the waters of the Vast Expanse, forcing all creatures hidden at the bottom of the pond to reveal themselves.

From the very beginning, he, the Master of Destiny, had been nothing more than a pawn—a role that had never changed.

And now, everything within the Vast Expanse is unfolding exactly as Gu Changge intended. As a pawn, he sensed some impending danger in the depths of his being, which is why he took the risk of collaborating with the Ancestor Xu. Yet, had he followed his instincts back then and not resisted that thread of fate, perhaps the situation would not have turned out this way.

"I regret it. If only I had sensed your existence before the Xianchu Haotu was destroyed, I would have used every means at my disposal to make you pay the price. But I hesitated then, I was afraid. From that moment on, I should have foreseen today’s outcome. I should never have allowed you to regain your former power, to the point where I now stand before you with no power to resist."

"You monster—you monster who should never have existed in this world—sooner or later, the heavens will exact their retribution upon you. No one can evade the laws of the universe or break the rules. Even someone as powerful as you is far from capable of doing so."

The Master of Fate’s eyes turned blood-red, and his hair fell loose.

At this moment, he bore no resemblance to a being who had reached the end of the path; rather, he looked like a madman who had lost everything. He raged and cursed, fully aware that the outcome was already sealed, yet he still refused to accept it.

Above the Sea of Reincarnation, a deathly silence hung.

Whether it was the Ancient Treasure Emperor or the rulers of the Five Great Royal Clans, all fell silent at this moment. No one would dare offend Gu Changge for the sake of the Lord of Destiny; this was no longer a matter of reason or irrationality.

If anyone were to intervene and obstruct him at this moment, Gu Changge would likely be determined to turn the Ancient Hidden Civilization upside down.

Moreover, judging by the Fate Master’s words, the karmic bond between him and Gu Changge seemed to have existed for a very long time. Yet, for some reason, Gu Changge had not taken action against the Fate Master. Instead, fully aware of his purpose, he had deliberately arranged his destiny, waiting for him to walk this path to the end.

Throughout his life, the Master of Fate had used countless factions, nations, and living beings as puppets, manipulating their destinies. Yet in the end, he himself had become a pawn in someone else’s game.

This is indeed a case of "what goes around comes around." The karma he inflicted upon others eventually returned to haunt him.

“Do you still believe, even now, that you have any chance of escaping all of this?”

Gu Changge merely smiled. With another wave of his hand, a dense web of causal threads instantly emerged from the Master of Destiny’s body. Those threads shot out into the infinite realms of spacetime, seemingly settling upon certain sentient beings.

However, as those threads of karma spread out in all directions, the sound of figures shattering soon echoed from the void of spacetime. In the next instant, three luminous figures, entangled by the threads of karma—which had transformed into chains—were all bound together and cast here.

One of them wore a white robe; he had once appeared on the battlefield during the great war between the Righteous Unity Alliance and the Heaven Slaying Alliance, holding the ancient Fourth Killing Array in his hand.

High above the Ancient Hidden Civilization, Ji Hanjing gazed at this figure with a complex expression, recognizing him as the descendant of her blood-related younger sister—though he had been transformed by the Master of Destiny into one of the Five Essence Bodies.

As for the other two figures, one wore a black robe, radiating a murderous aura; his eyes were filled with madness and malice, bound by countless chains and unable to move. The other was an ordinary-looking man with an unremarkable aura, appearing just like any mortal.

These were all contingency plans left by the Master of Destiny. Each of his remaining Five-Element Bodies could transform into his true form to shield him from calamity should he face disaster.

Apart from these three figures, a miniature world resembling a bubble suddenly flew out from the Master of Destiny’s brow. It then split open, revealing two figures.

Jiang Weiyang, watching from above the Ancient Hidden Civilization, saw the two figures, and tears immediately blurred her vision.

"Thank you, Alliance Leader, for saving my life."

Emperor Jiang Yun of Weiyang, who had not yet been refined into one of the Five Essence Bodies by the Master of the Universe, immediately grasped the significance of this unexpected rescue. He hurriedly pulled the woman beside him to her feet and knelt, his eyes brimming with gratitude and reverence.

Gu Changge merely nodded slightly, showing no intention of saying more.

"I’ll fight you to the death..."

All his remaining options had now been thrown into this battle; there was no longer the slightest possibility of resistance. The Master of Destiny suddenly roared in fury, intending to destroy everything along with himself—shattering his own Dao Fruit to deliver a devastating counterattack against Gu Changge.

"Destroy everything along with yourself? Go down fighting? But in my eyes, you don’t even qualify as a fish anymore."

Gu Changge merely smiled, his eyes devoid of any emotion. He raised his wrist, and that massive hand appeared once more, seizing the Master of Destiny.

"Having come this far, it would be a pity to simply kill you now. It would also be a waste of all the cultivation and cultivation you’ve accumulated over the years. With the Great Calamity approaching, I have no desire to cause any more senseless bloodshed."

“It’s also a good opportunity for the Ancient Hidden Civilization to see what fate awaits those who oppose me.”

"I refuse to accept this..."

Trapped within that colossal hand that obscured the sky, the Master of Destiny felt the immense power surging in from all directions, endless and boundless, and let out a furious roar.

Above his head, a Dao Fruit—crystalline as jade yet veiled in mist—seemed to materialize. It now emitted countless rays of light, each radiating unimaginable brilliance: the great wisdom, the ultimate perfection, and the most transcendent knowledge and power of one who has reached the end of the path—the boundless divine essence of a lifetime.

But now, the Dao Fruit was covered in dense cracks, spreading like a spider’s web or the fissures in porcelain, on the verge of shattering.

Above his head, boundless waves rolled ceaselessly—a river of cosmic destiny that seemed to stretch into infinite dimensions and traverse countless realms of time and space. In that instant, all the Daoist arts he had cultivated throughout his life coalesced into an unimaginable stillness; he appeared to have become that very "Destiny" itself.

At this moment, any being who witnessed this scene could not help but feel their heart tremble with awe.

Throughout history, for a figure who had reached the end of the path to shatter his own Dao fruit was simply unimaginable; such a thing had never occurred before. If it were to truly explode, the resulting energy would affect countless universes and spacetime—it would be nothing short of a cataclysm.

"Refine."

Witnessing this, Gu Changge merely clasped his hands together. The river of destiny that had been swirling around him seemed to be ignited by an indescribable flame, which then began to circle the Master of Destiny’s Dao Fruit, constantly licking and enveloping it.

In an instant, it enveloped all the energy and the unimaginable spectacle within its embrace.

All around, countless profound truths emerged—time and space, fate, cause and effect, the passage of time… all manner of supreme Dao and cosmic principles converged and collided.

The Vast Expanse echoed with a rumbling roar; in every corner of the universe, one could hear this immense clamour, as if worlds were colliding, about to converge and give birth to a power beyond the imagination of all sentient beings.

"Ahhh..."

The Master’s eyes turned red as he watched his Dao Fruit being refined away. From within it seemed to emanate the delicate fragrance of an elixir—rich and pure, far more precious than any celestial elixir or ancient treasure. Even a single wisp of it could seemingly cause an ordinary person to ascend on the spot, entering an unimaginable realm.

"This is my Dao Fruit; no one can refine it away. If you wish to take it, then I’d rather simply cast it aside myself..."

He roared in fury. At that moment, both his physical body and soul seemed to enter a state beyond human comprehension. A indistinct path materialized before him, but now the Master of Destiny, relying on his supreme will, shattered that path—inch by inch it collapsed, turning to dust.He seemed to have transformed into a state that existed only beyond the Three Realms and Six Paths—the realm of transcendence sought by all—leaving behind only what might be called an "appearance."

"This is the trend toward achieving supreme shattering..."

"Could it be that this calamity has, paradoxically, served to elevate the Master of Destiny, enabling him to cast aside all else and forge his own path with supreme resolve?"

Many beings witnessing this scene were shaken to the core, finding it almost unbelievable.

But in the next instant, this state did not last long. The Master of Destiny’s physical body and soul seemed to shatter completely, transforming into a wisp of pure energy that fell into that Dao Fruit.

Achieving supreme shattering is an incredibly arduous feat; he had merely caught a fleeting glimpse of a glimmer of opportunity, yet he had clearly failed. All paths ahead lay in ruins; neither his physical body nor his soul remained. Only the Dao Fruit he had relinquished survived, still being scorched and refined by the flames of the Way of Destiny.

"Hahaha, though I no longer exist, in that single instant, I glimpsed a future that has never before been seen. Everyone will die; the future has been severed. There can be no future."

"The Great Calamity? Hahaha, it’s not the Great Calamity at all. This is something that has never appeared throughout all of history, and could never possibly appear. Everything will be overturned; everything will be restarted…"

"Ancestor Xu, you have schemed against me, but soon, you too will join me."

The Ruler of Destiny’s furious roar and heart-wrenching cry, in the very instant his soul and physical form were utterly annihilated, resounded like the tolling of great bells, echoing throughout countless cosmic realms.

"This fellow is already dead, yet he still raves. Did I not foresee all this long ago? The future you see may not be the future, nor is what we see necessarily so. But I have long grasped the truth within his flesh and blood. As long as I possess this sliver of truth, even if you all die and everything starts anew, I can still reappear."

In a distant realm, the Ancestor’s ethereal form was bathed in a light where illusion and reality intertwined.

Hearing the agonized roar of the Fate Master in his final moments, he gave a cold smile. Without hesitation, he once again tore through countless layers of space, leaping through them a million times, and emerged in a mysterious, unknown land.

Above the Sea of Reincarnation, all traces of energy and fluctuations quickly dissipated. A spinning pill appeared there, no larger than a fist, yet containing the boundless wisdom and mysteries of the world. It seemed as though a sage were expounding the Great Dao of Heaven and Earth within it at every moment.

No one doubted the efficacy of this elixir. It had been forged from the lifetime of cultivation and spiritual fruits of a figure who had reached the pinnacle of the path. Throughout history, and perhaps even in the distant future, there could be no more than this single one.

Countless gazes fell upon the elixir, brimming with undisguised admiration and fervor, yet they immediately suppressed the greed in their hearts, daring not to reveal any unusual emotion.

The Ancient Treasure Emperor and the others were at a loss for words. As the rulers of the five great royal clans within the Realm of the End, they all felt a chill run down their spines. If Gu Changge could so casually strip a Fate Master of their Dao Fruits, then dealing with them would naturally be a trivial matter.

Such power was something even the Lord of Reincarnation, who held dominion over the cycle of rebirth, could never achieve.

“Does the Alliance Leader truly intend to force the Ancient Hidden Civilization to submit?” the Emperor of Gu Cang asked, his eyes brimming with deep apprehension. Gu Changge’s methods had surpassed his wildest imaginings; in his view, Gu Changge had reached a state… one that the Vast Expanse itself would not acknowledge or tolerate.

Perhaps only one who truly wields the full authority of the Heavenly Dao would be qualified to stand against him.

"Do you really think what I just said was a joke?" Gu Changge put away the elixir formed from the Fruit of the Sovereign’s Dao and declared, "Heaven will cease to be heaven, and humanity will cease to be humanity. Therefore, I intend to engage in Heaven Slaying and revolutionize everything. Whether it be the Ancient Hidden Civilization or the Nine Heavens, in the face of this overwhelming tide, if you fail to stand firm and understand your own place, then I shall have no choice but to intervene and force you to make your own choice."

"It is not merely the Ancient Hidden Civilization. Next will come the Nine Heavens, the Nightmare Demon Realm, the Mountain of Forgotten Immortals, the Hall of the Unborn… All the orthodox factions within the Vast Expanse—regardless of how long they have existed, whether from the dawn of creation or after the birth of the Vast Expanse—must all submit to my Heaven Slaying Alliance."

"This era of the False Heaven must come to a complete end."
