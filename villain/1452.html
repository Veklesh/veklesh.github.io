The black lotus, as vast as a mountain, drifted slowly toward them, casting its shadow over the entire world.

Each lotus petal was vast and boundless, resembling an apocalyptic millstone that obscured the sky and blocked out the sun. Imbued with a certain apocalyptic aura and entwined with the essence of the Dao, they descended from beyond the realms of the world.

Within each black lotus lay a shrine shrouded in black mist.

Dense, endless crowds of devotees, a dark, surging tide, sat cross-legged around the black lotuses, forming tiered steps; on every tier, devout prayers were offered.

They chanted obscure, incomprehensible words, their eyes burning with fanatical fervor. Wisp after wisp of black mist coiled around them, then swiftly converged upon the shrine like waves and tides.

The heavens shifted; rolling black clouds accompanied a torrential downpour of pitch-black rain, which poured mercilessly down upon the world.

Universes shattered in every direction; the black rain fell, mountains collapsed, and the world decayed.

Vast swathes of land cracked open; mountains, rivers, and streams all withered and dried up, and endless death energy rose from every corner.

And this death energy, as if drawn by some force, began to spread in all directions from the Ethereal Sanctuary, rapidly eroding the surrounding cosmic realms.

Amidst this, vast swathes of continents could be seen collapsing.

Cities crumbled, and kingdoms were annihilated.

No matter what race or kind of living being, the moment they were touched by this death energy, they fell into a frenzy of slaughter, heedless of everything, killing anyone they saw—whether former friends, loved ones, or even their own children and grandchildren.

The land was filled with wailing and despair. Although the four great families of the Ethereal Holy City immediately dispatched their strongest warriors to block and eradicate the threat, their efforts proved utterly futile.

The Black Lotus Sect had been waiting all along. They had refrained from acting during the days leading up to the grand gathering, biding their time and waiting for this very moment.

The scenes of carnage were etched into the eyes of the many powerful figures within the Ethereal Holy Realm.

"Damn it, these Black Lotus Sect followers are truly despicable."

"They’re deliberately trying to destroy our Ethereal Sanctuary and turn it into a wasteland!"

Many native beings and cultivators of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm were filled with rage. Without a second thought, they charged toward the vast, boundless black lotuses, attempting to destroy them.

However, upon those black lotuses sat numerous shadowy figures, shrouded in a terrifying aura of death. Their gazes were cold and indifferent, as if they held all living beings in the world in utter contempt.

As they watched the figures charging toward them from all corners of the cosmos, they merely cast a detached glance in their direction.

Then, with a single finger, they chanted the ritual incantation for the descent of the Black Lotus to save all beings. Boundless black divine light surged from their sides, containing unparalleled destructive power, and every charging figure disintegrated and collapsed in an instant.

The black lotus bloomed slowly; each petal appeared eerie and sinister, yet at that very moment, it also carried a certain aura of sanctity.

It was as if a vague yet supreme figure truly sat cross-legged within the shrine, eyes filled with compassion, intent on saving all sentient beings.

Across the vast cosmos, some had even heard the strange and eerie tones of the ritual chant—a sound so unsettling it made one’s entire body shudder, as if one’s very soul were about to be torn from one’s body.

"The Immortal Dao Alliance gathering must be suspended for now. The immediate priority is to expel these followers of the Black Lotus Sect."

Numerous elders and disciples of the Piao Miao Sect transformed into streaks of divine light and joined the fray.

Alas, even within the Piao Miao Holy City before them, chaos had already erupted. In many clans, members were being corrupted without their knowledge, attacked directly from behind.

Chaos, screams, grief, and fury… a cacophony of emotions began to spread throughout the Piao Miao Sacred Realm.

The various True Realm civilizations that had been closely monitoring the situation in the Ethereal Holy Realm were equally shaken and horrified.

If anyone were to look in from outside the Ethereal Sanctuary at this moment, they would see a layer of mist-like black mist drifting in from all corners of the cosmos. It grew increasingly dense until it finally coalesced into an endless, wave-like black fog, completely shrouding the realm where the Ethereal Sanctuary stood, making it impossible to discern what lay within.

That black mist contained a strange power capable of blocking divine perception and obscuring the workings of heaven, making it difficult even for physical bodies to break through and enter.

"Could it be they intend to seal off the Ethereal Sanctuary? They’ve truly lost their minds..."

An ancient figure could only feel a sense of dread and disbelief. How dare the Black Lotus Sect be so audacious? Just how many eyes from the civilizations of the True Realm were now focused on the Ethereal Sanctuary?

Not to mention the countless powerful figures stationed there—this was tantamount to declaring war on the entire Vast Expanse.

Furthermore, even Gu Changge, the current leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, is present there. How dare the Black Lotus Sect do such a thing?

"The Divine Calamity Curse Array of the Xuan You Capital Prison once aided me in refining an entire Formless Heaven, earning me the title of 'Formless.' Today, I shall use your True Soul Dao Fruits to help me forge the Black Lotus Sacred Realm and restore the former glory of the Formless Heaven..."

Deep within the dark, abyssal underground palace, the blood-red pool churned, shrouded in a hazy mist.

The figure clad in armor sat cross-legged there, breathing heavily as he stared intently at the flesh and blood rising and falling like a beating heart before him.

Before him, black mist swirled, engulfing the underground palace, yet the murky pool clearly reflected the scene of the current ethereal sacred realm.

Thousands upon thousands of threads of faith converged, only to be corrupted by the filth within the pool, before transforming into a torrential downpour of black rain that poured down from the sky.

He watched as the ceaselessly encroaching black mist eventually enveloped the Ethereal Sanctuary entirely, forming a vast, colossal region resembling a giant black cocoon, his gaze growing ever more intense.

The Black Lotus Sacred Realm was the very foundation upon which he now sought to establish himself as the Lord of the Black Lotus.

The Immortal Dao Alliance Grand Assembly in the Ethereal Sacred Realm had gathered nearly every notable civilization from the True Realms across the Vast Expanse, as well as the most influential figures from various Daoist lineages and factions—truly the most perfect nourishment.

Not to mention the presence of figures like the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

"The Ancestor Xu has always been cautious. He not only wants me to deal with the Heaven Slaying Alliance but also to shift the calamity caused by the Black Lotus Sect onto the Alliance Leader. I wonder what he’s thinking?"

“That leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance is now trapped within the Black Lotus Sacred Realm, reduced to nothing more than nourishment for me.” His expression was impassive, yet a cold smile curled at the corners of his mouth.

In a void of time and space beyond the outer realms of the Xiao Xi Sacred Domain.

A vast hall stood deep within this void, its boundaries invisible to the eye. Only swirling clouds and mist filled the air, as if one had truly arrived at the dwelling of ancient immortals.

A vague, indistinct shadow sat cross-legged in the center of the hall, surrounded by the resounding sounds of ritual chanting, as if all the gods and Buddhas of the heavens were chanting prayers for him.

This shadow seemed to reflect the passage of time, existing amidst the void and the void’s dissolution—part real, part illusory, like a dream, a bubble, a shadow, like dew, and like lightning.

“Ancestor, over at the Ethereal Sanctuary, the Formless should have already taken action according to your instructions.”

Wuxuzi of the Heaven’s Decline Clan stood respectfully within the grand hall, showing great reverence toward that indistinct figure.

The "Formless" he spoke of was the leader of the Formless Clan within the Eight Clans of the Celestial Realm. He had once followed the Lord of the Celestial Realm, but with the collapse and disintegration of the Celestial Realm, Formless had taken a portion of its essence and departed to live in seclusion.

For countless years, no one has known his whereabouts or heard any news of him, for Formless, true to his name, remains untouched by the world’s five aggregates and the myriad forms of mortal existence. He has no fixed abode and no fixed form; finding him is an incredibly difficult task.

"The Formless has always feared the power of the Lord of the Celestial Realm. Upon learning that he is about to awaken, he certainly won’t hold back for long."

"He doesn’t have much time left. If this pawn isn’t deployed soon, it will likely be destroyed by the Ruler of Heaven before long, wasting all the effort I’ve put in over the years."

The Ancestor Xu’s face kept shifting, eventually transforming into a featureless, flat visage—a truly eerie sight.

Wuxuzi asked in confusion, “Ancestor, if that is the case, why must Wuxiang take action against the Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance? Have you not already confirmed his identity?”

The Primordial Void’s eyes were a blank silver, devoid of pupils, with fleeting glimpses of time passing within them.

He looked at the void before him and said calmly, “I need him as a pawn to confirm something for me.”

“The Lord of Destiny made a deal with me. He does not know the identity of the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, but he is always cautious and refuses to step forward and reveal himself lightly. Therefore, he wishes to use my hand to test the depth of the Alliance Leader’s strength, so that he may make a decision.”

Upon hearing this, Wuxuzi suddenly understood and remarked with admiration, “That Master of Fate is nothing but a cowardly wretch who clings to life and fears death. Yet he dares to strike a deal with the Ancestor—does he not fear that in the end, the deal will backfire, leaving him utterly annihilated?”

The Ancestor Xu replied calmly, “The Lord of Destiny is a bold man, but he doesn’t have much time left now. If he doesn’t step forward soon, once the Vast Expanse becomes completely murky and certain things emerge, he’ll have no chance. That’s why he’s so anxious right now.”

“It’s just a pity that his path was wrong from the very beginning. No matter how far he goes, he’s merely walking down the wrong path, never to return, until the road comes to an end.”

Wuxuzi said with a mocking look in his eyes, “Someone has already walked that path before the Master of Destiny. You, the Ancestor, kindly warned him, yet he insisted on continuing down it. Isn’t that courting his own destruction?”

The Ancestor Xu fell into deep thought, then shook his head and said, “If he were willing to let go of certain things, there would likely be a place willing to accept him. He wouldn’t have to meet such a fate of physical death and the extinction of his path. But since he insists on continuing down this path, I fear what he truly seeks is power…”

“Power…” Wu Xuzi’s expression changed; he glanced furtively toward the heavens. Seeing nothing amiss, he let out a sigh of relief.

But in the Vast Expanse of today, how much power remains?

Virtually all known sources of power have either been seized or abolished.

No matter how long the Master of Destiny plots, if he seeks power, he will likely meet a gruesome end at the very first opportunity.

"Ancestor, there is one thing I still do not understand. As for the Black Lotus Sect, we need not concern ourselves with them, is that correct? Are we simply waiting for news from the Master of Destiny before deciding whether to intervene?" Wu Xuzi asked.

The Ancestor Xu closed his eyes, his aura seeming to dissolve into stillness and nothingness.

“I bestowed that fragment of flesh upon him as a token of my sincerity, to ensure his loyalty—all for this very day. Formless has a rebellious nature; he will not bow to anyone, save for the Lord of Heaven. Once he learns that fragment of flesh came from the Lord of Heaven, he will become even more unrestrained. After all these years, he should have already figured out how to harness the power within it.”

"I, too, am curious to see just how much power the Lord of Heaven still possesses."

"Is he truly in trouble, or is he just biding his time?"

On Piao Miao Peak, chaos had long since erupted. With the grand gathering disrupted in this manner, the expressions of the guests from all quarters were grim, especially those of their many disciples, who had all, to varying degrees, fallen victim to the Black Lotus Sect’s corruption.

They had assumed this grand gathering would proceed smoothly, with no further surprises.

Yet surprises always strike unexpectedly. Everyone had previously been concerned about the Heaven Slaying Alliance causing trouble, and had paid no attention to other sects and factions within the Vast Expanse.

While the Black Lotus Sect’s rapid rise and expansion can be attributed to the current chaotic state of the Vast Expanse, many suspect that dark factions are undoubtedly backing them.

Otherwise, they wouldn’t dare act so brazenly.

"What exactly is the Black Lotus Sect? Knowing full well that we are holding this grand gathering here, they still deliberately cause trouble. This is nothing short of a blatant provocation."

"I suggest that today, rather than waiting, we all join forces to eradicate this so-called Black Lotus Sect once and for all."

"The dark factions are so used to acting with impunity that they actually thought we feared them. Now that they see us forming the Immortal Dao Alliance to settle accounts together, I suppose they’ve finally realized they have something to fear..."

A figure of immense power from a supreme True Realm spoke up.

His power reached the Ancestral Dao Realm. Witnessing this scene, his gaze grew icy as he rose to his feet. His true form shone brilliantly like a sunset, expanding in an instant to span tens of thousands of zhang, obscuring the sky and blocking out the sun.

With a single command from his mind, a majestic divine sword, as imposing as a mountain range, materialized behind him. It severed the Milky Way and obliterated the cosmos, striking directly at a massive black lotus in the distant horizon.

"This Black Lotus Sect is incredibly mysterious; they will likely be difficult to deal with."

On Piao Miao Peak, the many guests gazing at this scene were all slightly shaken.

Many of their expressions darkened as the scene they had imagined—the massive black lotus being destroyed in a single strike—failed to materialize.

Amidst the sky filled with black light, a terrifying black hand, covered in scales, simply grabbed the sword as it struck.

The shockwaves obliterated the countless stars in the surrounding sky, yet they had no effect whatsoever on the black lotus. The believers gathered there remained oblivious, their expressions still devout and fervent.

Where the black lotus scattered, torrential rain poured down, carrying a corrosive and eerie aura that polluted this world.

This scene left all the native tribes of the Ethereal Sanctuary looking extremely grim.

"Alliance Leader, the Black Lotus Sect has struck at this moment. It seems they intend to wipe us all out at once?"

Several high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance watched the scene, their hearts shaken and filled with confusion.

What had been a perfectly fine grand gathering was forced to end prematurely due to the Black Lotus Sect’s interference.

In truth, no one had paid much attention to the Black Lotus Sect—this suddenly emerged heretical cult—at first, but who could have imagined it would dare to act with such audacity?

In the vast and spacious hall, many ancient beings frowned as they watched the scene unfolding in the distance. They did not intervene nor make any move, choosing instead to wait and see how things would unfold.

Although the Immortal Dao Alliance had been established, it was by no means certain they would come to the aid of the Ethereal Sanctuary to resolve this trouble. The Black Lotus Sect’s origins were strange and mysterious; acting rashly before fully understanding the situation would only invite unnecessary casualties.

Especially on the territory of the Piao Miao Holy Realm—since the Piao Miao Holy Ancestor hadn’t even moved yet, what was the rush?

"The Black Lotus Sect is quite bold, but they don’t have that much power."

Gu Changge, clad in a white robe with wide sleeves, sat upright on the jade-colored platform and remarked casually.

He had anticipated this, so he was not surprised. If the Black Lotus Sect were to make a move, the Lord of Destiny would likely be unable to contain himself much longer.

However, judging by the current situation, the Black Lotus Sect was merely a pawn being pushed forward; the true mastermind remained hidden behind the scenes.

Whether it was the Lord of Destiny or the Ancestor Xu, they were still lying in wait—did they intend to use this to force him to reveal himself? Or were they testing his strength?

"Their plans are, in fact, in perfect alignment. It’s just a pity that my current target isn’t them." Gu Changge saw right through it all, his eyes cold and indifferent.

“Hmph, this Black Lotus Sect is truly audacious. Do they really think this Ancestor doesn’t exist?”

At that moment, a cold snort rang out, shaking the heavens of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm until they rumbled. The stars trembled, and the entire cosmos grew dark.

The Holy Ancestor of Piaomiao had already been harboring considerable resentment throughout this grand gathering. Now, he swept his sleeve aside and struck at a massive black lotus blooming in the distant horizon.

From within his sleeves, the multiverse surged forth, with countless starlights shimmering as if myriad Daoist arts were unfolding. Endless chains of laws and order intertwined, cascading down like a waterfall of cosmic laws, crushing and engulfing everything in their path.

"The power of the Ethereal Holy Ancestor is truly unfathomable; it far surpasses that of the ordinary Ancestral Dao Realm."

"With him stationed here, it seems the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance won’t even need to intervene to resolve this crisis caused by the Black Lotus Sect..."

"I wonder what plans the mastermind behind the Black Lotus Sect has in store. The Dark Clans haven’t made an appearance either."

The influential figures present, sensing this disturbance, all felt their hearts tremble slightly.

As the backbone of a True Realm Civilization, their strength was naturally not to be underestimated; even the weakest among them had survived six Celestial Waning Tribulations.

The vast majority were at the Ancestral Dao Realm. Such power, even across the entire Vast Expanse, could be said to tower over the masses, with few rivals.
