Though the Mother Tree of the Era had cracked and no longer possessed its former boundless vitality, as it trembled and rose into the sky, it still radiated an unimaginable divine might.

The spectacle was so immense and awe-inspiring that even the Vast Expanse of the cosmos trembled in response, drawing countless gazes from every corner of the universe.

In that moment, it was as though an ancient divine tree had risen from the depths of an endless ocean, setting off boundless waves—each ripple powerful enough to overturn and shatter an entire realm.

In that place, a boundless storm raged—a hurricane seemingly forged from the very power of the world, affecting and sweeping everything in its path. Even a single ripple was enough to make figures at the Ancestral Dao Realm cough up blood and retreat; they dared not approach at all.

For this is the Epoch Mother Tree, also known as the World Tree. It contains the Vast Expanse of countless ages, endless years, the succession of epochs, all living beings, the cycle of reincarnation, and the birth of worlds… countless secrets. It can be said that the Epoch Mother Tree is the cornerstone that nurtures all living beings within the Vast Expanse.

The particle-like substances, resembling bubbles, that it constantly exhales and inhales through its branches and leaves scatter and drift into the Vast Expanse. Taking root across endless time and space and across countless dimensions, they evolve into real, microscopic worlds.

And it is precisely these worlds that have come together to form the Vast Expanse we know today.

The Epoch Mother Tree has always been an exceptionally unique entity. It is no exaggeration to say that the place where it takes root is the very core of the Vast Expanse; the cosmic fortune and resources of the Vast Expanse, along with all manner of divine and wondrous phenomena, gravitate toward it.

Originally, it leapt ceaselessly through the endless dimensions of time and space and the fractures between planes; no one could grasp it, and no Civilization of the True Realm could cause it to pause or remain.

For ordinary supreme realms were simply incapable of bearing its power or sustaining its boundless, colossal fortune.

It was not until eons ago that a majestic, supreme, and supreme power descended, intercepting it and causing it to take root within the Ancient Hidden Civilization. Only then did the Epoch Mother Tree truly come to rest.

After the Great Calamity, as civilization began to emerge, the shattered remnants of those ruined worlds and the fragmented primordial sources of the True Realms all converged toward the Ancient Hidden Civilization, drawn by the existence of the Epoch Mother Tree.

In this process, the Ancient Hidden Civilization relied on this accumulation to continuously expand, eventually developing into a realm far surpassing even the mightiest True Realms.

It can be said that the existence of the Epoch Mother Tree holds extraordinary significance, whether for the Vast Expanse or for the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

If the Eon Mother Tree takes root, with the sun and moon absorbing and expelling the substance of the world, condensing one world particle after another, and evolving them into various cosmic realms, the benefits for the fortune and development of a True Realm are truly beyond description.

However, without immense and boundless power, it is absolutely impossible to control the Epoch Mother Tree. Even those in the True Path who hold authority are powerless against it, for although the Epoch Mother Tree itself possesses no will, its level of existence is absolutely comparable to the Heavenly Dao within the Vast Expanse—it is a manifestation of the rules themselves.

It is akin to the Sea of Reincarnation, which allows all futures to unfold in an orderly manner—a manifestation of the rules of the Vast Expanse.

True Path-level figures wield authority, yet they can only infinitely approach this level. They can condense worlds comparable to the mightiest True Realms, but they cannot create an endless universe rivaling the Vast Expanse. To them, this is something far beyond the capabilities of human power.

Thus, at this moment, witnessing the Epoch Mother Tree being gradually lifted and carried away from the Ancient Hidden Civilization by a vast and majestic force—one beyond words—nearly all the powerful, ancient beings were left speechless with astonishment.

Even the World Ruler and the Lord of Reincarnation had their pupils violently dilate, unable to hide the shock in their eyes.

Above the Ancient Hidden Civilization, an endless rift in space-time split open, revealing a vague passage that stretched boundlessly. Any force that entered it would be suppressed; even something as mighty, vast, and ancient as the Mother Tree of the Vast Expanse was crushed by that force, sinking into it bit by bit.

Witnessing this scene, not a single soul dared to intervene at that moment. Under the weight of that vast, boundless power, even those at the Ancestral Dao Realm felt as insignificant as a speck of dust.

To attempt to stop it would be as absurd as an ant trying to move a mountain—utterly laughable.

The palaces and towers of the Ji Chan clan, which thrived upon the Eon Mother Tree, began to shake violently. Nearly every member of the clan wore expressions of horror and alarm, unsure of what was happening.

Some emerged from the quiet chambers of their cave dwellings, intending to investigate, but the aftershocks of the violent tremors swept over them like ripples, instantly crushing them so that they dared not even twitch.

“Has Ziyi succeeded?”

“It seems her choice was not wrong. This boundless power has already reached a realm beyond my imagination.”

Ji Xianmiao, clad in colorful robes, stood before the Ancestral Court. As she watched the scene, her body radiated an aura that seemed to reach to the farthest corners of the universe, attempting to calm the trembling members of her clan.

Within the ancestral courtyard, numerous ancient figures of the Ji Chan clan were roused from their slumber and seclusion, forced to emerge.

The commotion was simply too immense; even Ji Xianmiao’s attempts to conceal it were futile—it was impossible to hide.

"Why has the Mother Tree of the Era become like this? Since taking root in the Ancient Hidden Civilization eons ago, the Mother Tree of the Era has never left..."

One after another, imposing figures swiftly appeared outside the Ancestral Court, their bodies radiating an air of ancient age and timelessness, as if they had lived for untold ages.

Some closed their eyes to attempt a calculation, faintly glimpsing a thread of the cause and effect behind it all, and their faces were filled with shock.

"Ji Xianmiao, it was you who intervened to obscure and block our perceptions. This act is an act of defiance against Heaven. The Ji Chan clan will bring upon itself boundless calamity. Our clan was born by Heaven’s mandate, nurtured by the Mother Tree of the Eon, bearing the succession of eras, chronicling the passage of time and the evolution of Civilization—we have never once faltered." Someone roared angrily.

“You are seeking to destroy the Ji Chan clan, causing our legacy of ten thousand generations to collapse and reducing us to a truly sinful clan!” These ancient beings, having grasped the cause and course of events, were filled with unbridled fury as they roared in anger.

A radiance still emanated from Ji Xianmiao as she soothed the turbulent hearts of the Ji Chan clan, saying, “Why should you elders be so angry? From the moment the Mother Tree of the Eon collapsed, the destiny of our Ji Chan clan has already changed. Why cling to outdated traditions?"For countless ages, our Ji Chan clan has believed in the will of Heaven, adhered to the laws of Heaven, organized the world’s matter, and maintained the rotation of the Heavenly Dao, ensuring an orderly future—we have never erred."

“But now, our eldest sister has been sacrificed, her fate unknown. Our Ji Chan clan has been branded as the sinful clan of the Ancient Hidden Civilization. Many of our people have been conscripted and reduced to slaves of the Five Great Royal Clans. Generations of merit and service have been completely erased. This so-called destiny is nothing but a joke.”

"If this is the will of Heaven, then what of defying it?"

Though she was a woman, the aura surging from her at that moment was as majestic as mountains and as immortal as the heavens, overwhelming the elders of the Ji Chan clan to the point where they could scarcely speak.

"Nonsense! Ji Xianmiao, you’ve gone mad. We entrusted you with the leadership of our clan, not so you could destroy our ancestral legacy." The ancient elders roared with fury.

“Xianmiao, I always believed you understood our clan, but it seems your heart has long been corrupted by darkness.”

"Why does the Ji Chan clan exist? It is to conform to the laws of Heaven. Whatever Heaven’s will may be, the reason for our clan’s existence is set in stone—unchangeable, and it has never wavered since time immemorial."

Deep within the ancestral hall of the Ji Chan clan, a beautiful woman appeared—crowned, voluptuous, and clad in a magnificent, flowing palace gown. She was none other than the former Empress of the Ji Chan clan, and the mother of Ji Xianmiao and the others.

Her face betrayed disappointment as she sighed softly, attempting to persuade Ji Xianmiao to see the error of her ways and return to the right path.

However, Ji Xianmiao seemed to have anticipated this outcome all along. Her expression unchanged, she said, “Mother, I have done nothing wrong. It is you who have long been bound by the shackles of fate.”

“Was the disturbance of the Ji Yuan Mother Tree caused by Zi Yi? Are you merely covering for her?” Empress Ji Chan sensed the situation with just a fleeting glance and understood the events that had unfolded, her face growing even more disheartened.

“It has nothing to do with Zi Yi. I insisted she act this way. The fate of the Ji Chan clan must remain in our own hands.” Ji Xianmiao’s tone remained calm.

“Zi Yi has been away for many years; without a sound, her will has already been corrupted and she’s been manipulated by others. Why have you become like this as well?”

“The clan elders and I have sensed the will of Heaven in the cosmos. In the near future, the Heaven Slaying Alliance will invite boundless calamity. To defy Heaven’s will is to go against the great cosmic order. From ancient times to the present, whoever does so will be reduced to dust in that surging torrent.”

"All those who have been called upon will follow Heaven’s will and destroy the Heaven Slaying Alliance. If this endeavor succeeds, not only will we earn great merit, but we can also quell the Great Calamity and delay the reckoning. Do not act on impulse." Empress Ji Chan sighed, still trying to persuade him.

"Mother, there is no need to urge me further," Ji Xianmiao said. "Since you lack the resolve and courage, I shall take action."

“Ji Xianmiao, do not be so selfish…”

The elders of the Ji Chan clan were furious. Having lived for ages, they had come to understand many of the secrets of the universe. They knew that anyone who defied the Heavenly Dao—no matter how powerful—would ultimately meet their end; there could be no exceptions.

"It’s too late." Ji Xianmiao’s expression remained calm, even carrying a faint smile.

BOOM!!!

The heavens and earth shook violently. Endless rifts tore open in space and time. The fissures spread outward, devouring everything in their path, as if intent on swallowing the entire Ancient Hidden Civilization whole.

Under such colossal might, all the elders of the Ji Chan clan could barely stand; even their auras were thrown into chaos. It was as if autumn cicadas, having long dwelled upon a towering ancient tree, foolishly attempted to shake the tree—a feat utterly unrealistic. Yet now, the very force they sought to challenge effortlessly uprooted that ancient tree…

In the Sea of Reincarnation, the Ancestor Xu sat cross-legged upon a reef, his gaze flickering repeatedly. It seemed he had long anticipated and guessed at this scene, yet his astonishment and shock were no less than those of the others.

"I knew he left the Master of Destiny behind to use as live bait, but since no one took the bait, the Master of Destiny has naturally lost all value."

"Within this Ancient Hidden Civilization, the only thing that could possibly catch his attention is this Epoch Mother Tree."

Ancestor Xu’s form dissolved into a streak of light and vanished above the Sea of Reincarnation.

"Even you and I are far from capable of controlling the Epoch Mother Tree, let alone stirring it. It is, in itself, a manifestation of the Vast Expanse—a vessel representing a part of the Vast Expanse. Yet now, it is being stirred by someone and is about to be taken away. It’s as if a fish living in a pond could actually carry off a portion of the pond itself—it’s nothing short of a fairy tale…"

"How is this possible?"

Both the Lord of Reincarnation and the Lord of the World were deeply shaken. They remained cautious and did not attempt to intervene lightly, for they already understood who was behind this. In the current Vast Expanse, aside from that figure from the Heaven Slaying Alliance, who else could possibly do such a thing?

The turmoil in their hearts at that moment could truly be described as a raging storm.

"But once the Mother Tree of the Eon is taken by him, the fate of the Ancient Hidden Civilization will dissipate for the most part. The Five Great Royal Clans and the Ancient Hidden Imperial House will not tolerate this."

“The Great Calamity is approaching, and our grand plan lies ahead. We must not act rashly. The Heaven Slaying Alliance is defying the heavens—they are not our concern.”

"Let us observe the situation and wait for an opportunity. If he intends to take the Epoch Mother Tree, then let him take it." The Lord of Reincarnation’s eyes flickered as he forcibly suppressed his urge to test the waters.

The World Ruler nodded in agreement. In that instant, both men gained insight into a greater trend.

At this moment, it was not just the Ancient Hidden Civilization; countless gazes from across the Vast Expanse of the cosmos were peering through layer upon layer of void and spacetime.

But soon, the scene there grew blurry, as if boundless chaotic energy had erupted, raging and engulfing everything.

Within the Ancient Hidden Civilization, countless living beings and cultivators trembled in fear, their terror reaching its peak. The entire world shook as if struck by a massive earthquake; it was as if the heavens and earth had been turned upside down.

"Who has awakened me?"

Inside the sacred temple, an old woman seated upon an ancient jade throne suddenly opened her eyes, her gaze icy and ruthless.

She was draped in a robe that seemed embroidered with starlight and wore a ceremonial crown, giving her an ancient appearance. Outside the temple, a being at the Ancestral Dao Realm trembled as they hurried forward to report.

"High Priestess, the Ji Chan clan has rebelled. They have conspired with outsiders to steal the Eon Mother Tree. We implore you to intervene. Otherwise, the fortune of our Ancient Hidden Civilization will wane, and we may face the same endless wrath from the other side as we did last time."

The Ancestral Dao Realm being spoke rapidly, relaying the situation.

The High Priestess held a lofty status within the Ancient Hidden Civilization, for she possessed the means to communicate with the Other Side.

Whenever a Great Calamity descends and the Enforcers of Reckoning appear, it is she who guides and receives them; even the imperial family of the Ancient Hidden Civilization holds her in the highest regard.

“The Ji Chan clan—how dare they?”

“They were entrusted to guard the Abyss of the Eon, watch over the Mother Tree of the Eon, and organize the world’s matter—yet they have conspired with outsiders to betray the will of Heaven.”

The High Priestess’s face darkened as she appeared outside the temple. This vast, boundless continent stretched endlessly into the distance, its surroundings teeming with countless layers of dimensional currents, where with every breath, energies akin to the very essence of creation collided.

As she materialized, figure after figure emerged from the surrounding temples and sacred mountains—all of them overwhelmingly powerful, ancient as if they had existed since time immemorial. As their divine light flickered, it seemed as though they would crush the heavens and earth, leaving one breathless.

"The will of Heaven cannot be defied. The Heaven Slaying Alliance acts against Heaven’s will and has, in the depths of the unknown, already invited boundless calamity."

"Soon, the will of Heaven will issue its decree, annihilating the Heaven Slaying Alliance. This act may perhaps quell the impending Great Calamity."

The High Priest’s eyes were profound and boundless, with countless starry rivers converging within them. In a mere instant, he had already penetrated the essence of the events unfolding before him.

She stared intently at the scene unfolding deep in the heavens, daring not to make a single move. The Mother Tree of the Eon was not something she could shake; under such majestic power, no one could stand in its way—unless the Vast Expanse manifested to resist it.

But how vast and boundless was the Vast Expanse? How could it possibly manifest so easily? Otherwise, wouldn’t the heavens and earth truly descend into chaos? For now, she was utterly helpless, completely incapable of stopping it.

Soon, several terrifying figures appeared beside the High Priest. They seemed to have lost their true physical forms—some wore masks, others hoods—surrounded by a haze of chaotic light, appearing as mere masses of matter capable of shifting shape at will.

“Once the Mother Tree of the Era is taken away, our Ancient Hidden Civilization will likely face catastrophe as well. We cannot stand idly by.” One of them spoke, their eyes displaying a pattern of shattered crosses, filled with the aura of worlds being obliterated and the cosmos collapsing.

"We may not be able to defeat the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, but the Ji Chan clan must pay the price..."

"The Ji Chan clan dared to defy Heaven’s will—this is the consequence."

The High Priestess’s gaze was icy cold. In an instant, she pierced through countless spacetime dimensions, fixing her gaze on the Sea of Reincarnation.

As her gaze fell upon it, the previously still Sea of Reincarnation suddenly churned with boundless waves. Layer upon layer of spacetime twisted like shards, torn apart by some terrifying force. Her gaze materialized in the sky, as if transformed into an immortal divine statue, filling every inch of the void and the heavens with an eternal, indestructible aura of authority, indifferent to all.

"Follow me..."

Boundless waves surged within the chaos. Ji Qiyi, clad in a black gown, stood immovable as her long hair danced in the wind.

Watching the Eon Mother Tree tremble as it slowly rose into the sky, though her body bore numerous scars, her tone remained unwavering.

"Third Sister..."

"What is happening?"

The figure in the green robe, slender as a bamboo, stood transfixed, still unable to comprehend what had happened. Why had the Mother Tree of the Era suddenly stirred so violently, as if it were being seized and carried away by a boundless, mighty force?
