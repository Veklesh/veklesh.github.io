The moon hung high in the sky, bright as a disk, its pure radiance cascading down, as cold and crisp as falling snow.

The buildings and pavilions, resembling celestial palaces and towers, were constructed with grandeur and magnificence. The scenes within were hazy and indistinct, shrouded in ethereal mist and enveloped within the heavens.

The banquet was incredibly lively; many prominent figures from the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty had come, giving Ye Wudao ample face, and it could truly be said that both hosts and guests were thoroughly delighted.

"Father…"
