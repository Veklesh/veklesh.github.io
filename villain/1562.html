From the majestic platform, a figure shrouded in dark golden flames descended rapidly. Clusters of flames, like a shimmering world, formed a divine halo that floated behind the figure’s head, radiating a brilliant glow that exuded an aura of timeless immortality.

Such a colossal and terrifying aura instantly sent shockwaves rippling through the vicinity of the Hexu Abyss.

Many beings fled to a safe distance, their eyes filled with fear.

This was clearly a being who had entered the Heavenly Seal Realm—meaning they were a follower of the Supreme Yin Heavenly Lord, and the magical power they had accumulated far exceeded that of a single Chaos Heaven and Earth Eon.

Another figure, shrouded in eerie black mist and resembling a ferocious bird, was also rushing toward this direction.

That figure was particularly unsettling. It spread its wings, which resembled clouds hanging from the sky, stirring up raging waves, while its jawline was as sharp as a blade.

Each head was that of a peerless, ferocious bird that had once appeared in the Vast Expanse of ancient ages; their eyes were like blood-red moons, and their beaks wide enough to swallow the cosmos. At the base of the tenth head, bloodstains were splattered, and layers of ghostly mist billowed forth, obscuring the sky and the sun, as if a brand-new head were about to grow from within.

"Master."

The two figures rushed to the side of the Absolute Yin Heaven Heavenly Lord and immediately knelt before him.

“Master, the aura emanating from you…”

The figure with the radiant divine halo behind him spoke with a voice trembling with fear.

The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness waved his hand, blocking all external perception and surveillance of the platform. No one knew what was happening here.

He said expressionlessly, “I suffered a heavy loss on the other side and sacrificed the magical power accumulated over an entire Chaos Heaven and Earth Eon. Now I urgently need to restore my cultivation…”

In truth, he was not telling the whole truth, for the actual loss was far greater than the magical power accumulated over a single Chaos Heaven and Earth Eon.

To be precise, if he could have presided over this Eternal Grand Sacrifice within this Chaos Heaven and Earth Eon without disruption and settled accounts with the Vast Expanse,

he could have solidified the vast magical power accumulated within this Chaos Heaven and Earth Eon, transforming it into the foundation of his Heavenly Lord’s position, thereby building upon his existing achievements.

In other words, once this chaotic cosmic era concluded, he would have possessed the magical power equivalent to five such eras.

But precisely because of the failure of this Eternal Grand Sacrifice, coupled with his sacrifice of his originally stable divine power and status to counter the malevolent intentions of the Lord of Heaven,

Everything he had accumulated during this Chaos Heaven and Earth Era was also swallowed whole by the Heavenly Seal Talisman, leaving him with only a mere fraction...

This is the source of the Deep Yin Heavenly Lord’s profound bitterness; unless absolutely necessary, he would never have taken that step.

It was equivalent to losing nearly two Chaos Heaven and Earth Eons’ worth of magical power.

"I am willing to offer everything to my master."

Hearing these words, the figure behind him—surrounded by a radiant divine halo—knelt without hesitation, his tone devout and fervent, willing to sacrifice everything for the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin.

"Ju Ling, you still have a great purpose. The followers who accompanied me to the other side this time were all wiped out; not even the Heavenly Seal can resurrect them."

"If you were to fall from the Dao Realm, my situation would become even more dire."

The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness shook his head, then turned his gaze to the nine-headed figure also kneeling on the ground, speaking in a calm tone, “Ghost Chariot, you have only followed me in this Chaos Heaven and Earth Eon. You have cultivated by my side for over three hundred billion billion years and have nearly accumulated a layer of magical power…”

The “layer of magical power” he referred to was, of course, the power accumulated over a single Chaos Heaven and Earth Eon.

Once this Chaos Heaven and Earth Eon came to an end, the Ghost Chariot before him would be able to solidify his position at the first level of the Heavenly Seal Realm and become a Heavenly Being.

"Master..." Ghost Chariot transformed into human form, his body billowing with ghostly mist. A savage aura filled the heavens, and drops of blood dripped ceaselessly, creating a truly chilling sight.

He looked hesitant, clearly unwilling to part with the magical power he had painstakingly accumulated over such a vast span of time.

A Chaos Heaven and Earth Era is an incredibly long and ancient span of time. Even in the Land Of Truth, where life-sustaining energy is incredibly dense and longevity is never in short supply, it could wear down and kill countless beings who have reached the End of the Path in their Dao Realm journey.

"Ghost Chariot, it is your honor to offer everything to your master. Had my master not taken pity on you back then and shared with you the immense power summoned by the Heavenly Seal Talisman, you would have long since turned to dry bones and become a handful of dust."

Ju Ling’s eyes flashed with ferocity, radiating an overwhelming aura of power that forced Ghost Chariot to lower his head; all nine of his heads bowed to the ground.

"Yes, I am willing to offer everything to my master." Ghost Chariot hesitated only for an instant before hastily replying.

By sacrificing a portion of his magical power, he would merely fall to the lowest tier of the End of the Path in the Dao Realm. As long as he remained by the side of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness, he would still have a chance to regain his current cultivation level.

The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin nodded, his gaze cold and indifferent.

His words carried weight; even if Ghost Chariot had refused, as the master of the Heavenly Seal Summoning Talisman, he could, with a single thought, strip his subordinate of everything—including his cultivation and very life.

Soon, the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin opened his mouth and drew in a breath, as if an endless abyss had gaped open, or a black hole capable of swallowing all worlds had descended.

Ripples spread out, like invisible circles of the Dao, enveloping the Ghost Chariot before him. Streams of golden, viscous energy, as dense and sticky as oil, flowed ceaselessly from the Ghost Chariot’s body.

The Ghost Chariot knelt there, trembling uncontrollably, like a vast, boundless vortex of air as countless threads of its essence were drawn out, and even its very form of life rapidly faded away.

Amidst this dense, viscous stream of air, the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin sensed a faint, familiar feeling.

He paused for a moment, then couldn’t help but think of the forbidden substances from the Vast Expanse.

Could there be some connection between the two?

But he quickly shook his head, hastily banishing the thought from his mind. This was a forbidden substance; even entertaining the idea in his mind could invite unwarranted calamity.

"This sensation is truly exquisite..."

"No wonder certain beings from the Nine Great True Clans accumulate magical power by bestowing blessings upon their followers."

The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin was enveloped in an indescribably warm and soothing aura, causing him to involuntarily recall the very beginning of his existence.

The magical power lost through the sacrifice had been replenished. Even the Heavenly Seal Talisman burst into brilliant radiance, reorganizing and refining the newly absorbed magical power to solidify his position in the celestial hierarchy.

“What a pity. After all, this isn’t magical power cultivated through my own arduous practice; it pales in comparison to the layer of power sacrificed…”

"It cannot restore me to the position of Heavenly Lord."

The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin sighed inwardly.

After all, Ghost Chariot had only just entered the Heavenly Seal Realm; he hadn’t even solidified his Heavenly Being status yet. How could the magical power he had accumulated through arduous cultivation over an entire Chaos Heaven and Earth Eon possibly compare to that of the Supreme Yin Heavenly Lord himself?

Even after absorbing this small amount, it was merely a drop in the bucket.

It felt as though Ghost Chariot had painstakingly gathered a single bowl of water drop by drop, while he had gulped it down in great mouthfuls—yet it still failed to quench his thirst.

Before long, Ghost Chariot’s life form withered completely, and even his aura plummeted to the lowest tier of the End of the Path in the Dao Realm.

The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin looked slightly regretful, but his complexion was indeed much better than it had been moments ago.

"Ancestor..."

At that moment, outside this domain, several other figures who had followed Ju Ling and Ghost Chariot arrived near the Absolute Yin Heavenly Lord.

These figures bore a striking resemblance to the human race—men and women, young and old alike. They wore exquisite, ornate robes; their hair was adorned with crowns as lustrous as jade; their figures were slender and tall; and their hair shimmered with a cold, silvery glow. Every gesture exuded the noble, dignified aura of a great clan of the Heavenly Seal.

As a titan who wields the Heavenly Seal Summoning Talismans, he stands among the very pinnacle of beings in the Land Of Truth.

The clan founded by the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin is also a truly deserving Heavenly Seal Great Clan, with power and heritage second only to the Nine True Clans.

According to the rules of the Council of Truth, each True Clan may possess only one Heavenly Seal.

Consequently, after the conclusion of the Ninth Era of the Chaotic Heavens and Earth, the Heavenly Seals bestowed by the Three True Ancestors fell into the hands of other clans.

Any clan possessing a Heavenly Seal may be called a Great Heavenly Seal Clan—these are the clans that can truly stand the test of time in the Chaos Cosmos Era, immortal and indestructible.

Of course, the Absolute Yin Clan, created by the Absolute Yin Heavenly Lord, is undoubtedly one of them.

"Over at the Council of Truth, the offspring of the King of the True Third Clan is seizing this opportunity to ally with the Council Elders of the True First and True Second Clans in an attempt to impeach the Ancestor. Although the Council Elders of the Xuan Third Clan stand by you, Ancestor, they cannot withstand the pressure from the True Third Clan..."

A massive figure—clad in a great cloak, a purple-gold hammer slung diagonally across his chest, with a leopard-like head, ringed eyes, a swallow-like chin, and tiger-like whiskers—reported respectfully before the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin.

The purple-gold hammer at his waist radiated wispy strands of supreme destructive energy, interwoven with layers of thunder and flashing lightning—as if a single strike could shatter the entire cosmos.

Naturally, the Nine Great True Clans could not have been kept in the dark about the mishap during the Eternal Grand Sacrifice, especially since even the Ancient Altar of Reckoning had revealed it.

The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness was not the least bit surprised by this.

The Nine True Clans respectively represent the three True Ancestors: "True," "Mysterious," and "Divine."

The original, first-generation clans were likewise named after these titles.

After numerous eras of chaotic worlds, the six additional True Clans that emerged were classified as “True Second,” “True Third,” “Mystic,” and “Mystic Third,” with a hierarchical relationship existing between them.

In the depths of the cosmos, the numbers one, two, and three also correspond to certain laws and principles.

Of course, even among tribes created by the same True Ancestor, relations between them are not always harmonious.

Now, in the thirteenth Chaos Heaven and Earth Era, the "True" clan serves as the overseer.

The early, middle, and late periods of the era are overseen by "Zhen Yi," "Zhen Er," and "Zhen San," respectively.

Coincidentally, we have now reached the final phase of this Era of Chaos and the Heavens, where the King of the "True Three Clan" oversees the entire Land Of Truth. His authority is so vast that no one dares defy his might.

The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin and this King’s offspring have had disputes, and the conflict is significant.

Therefore, he had long anticipated that upon returning to the Land Of Truth, he would face their accusations and suppression.

"What is the stance of Elder Xuan San Liu of the Xuan San Clan?" The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin, his expression somber, asked the clan member beside him.

This clan member, named Jueyin Kui, was not only a direct bloodline descendant but also a retainer who had attained the Heavenly Seal Realm.

Within a single Chaos Heaven and Earth Era, the Heavenly Seal could only bestow titles upon a maximum of nine followers, and Meng Yi’s conferment of titles was not without cost.

Even for the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin, the total number of his followers did not exceed twenty.

“Reporting to the Ancestor, Elder Xuan Sanliu is also waiting for you to go and provide an explanation to the Truth Council.”

“Opening the Ancient Reckoning Altar without cause, failing to obtain the True Clan’s permission, and causing the failure of the Eternal Grand Sacrifice as well as the collapse of the Ancient Hidden Civilization—each of these incidents has greatly displeased the elders of the Council.” Jueyin Kui’s face betrayed his concern.

The Heavenly Lord Jueyin had a still expression as he pondered a strategy in his mind.

“Father, what has become of my elder sister, the one left behind on that side?”

A soft voice rang out. A young man in white robes with black hair spoke. His features bore a striking resemblance to Lord Jueyin Heavenly Lord. His clothes and hair flowed freely, untied and unrestrained. Standing there quietly, his skin was as flawless as innate jade and glass, with a faint, ethereal glow radiating from it.
