"I have already seen it. The leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance possesses no authority over creation, yet he knows how to wield it. He harbors a great secret."

"Perhaps it truly is that third path you speak of."

"Within the destiny and faith of all beings lies a power we have long been unable to fathom—one that touches upon the most fundamental truths."

After the World Ruler appeared, the entire expanse of time and space seemed to come to a standstill; every moment and every breath appeared to be completely frozen at a certain point.

This was a stage that no one from the past, future, or even the present had ever set foot in; no one could eavesdrop on their conversation, nor would any karmic consequences be revealed.

Both the Lord of Calamity and the Lord of Destiny were secretly startled. They knew very little about the Realm of the True Path, but they could indeed sense the vast disparity between themselves and the beings of this realm.

Though they too styled themselves “Lords,” in the presence of the World Lord, they possessed absolutely no power to stand against him.

The Lord of Fate’s gaze flickered with a murky unease. He had believed himself to be very close to that realm; he was determined to attain the Dao Fruit in this lifetime. Yet now it seemed he had overestimated himself.

No wonder the Ancestor Xu had always held a dismissive and casual attitude toward his plans.

"The concept of destiny is ethereal and elusive. I once used the art of destiny to lay out the Yao Guang Ancestral Court, attempting to use destiny to sustain my own path, but in the end, I failed. The origin of destiny is an even more profound and mysterious concept—whether it stems from individual sentient beings or is bestowed by Heaven, no one can say for certain..."The Master of Destiny’s gaze was profound, as countless thoughts flashed through his mind.

He was called the Master of Destiny for a reason. The Dao he embodied was the very Dao of Destiny itself; otherwise, he would not have schemed to seize the destiny of the Yao Guang Ancestral Court. He had spent years laying out his plans, leaving behind strategic reserves in the form of civilizations such as the Vast Lands of Xianchu, the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, and the Ancient Kingdom of Nanzhao. Yet, despite all this... he still could not take a single step forward.

Only now did he believe he had reached the pinnacle of his path and felt confident enough to vie for power.

Yet, when facing Shizu Xu, he still sensed an unfathomable depth in his opponent. He was certain that Shizu Xu had never set foot in the realm of Supreme Shattered, nor had he ever grasped the essence of power.

Could it be that even within the Realm of the End of the Path, there are still deeper levels to be explored?

"The Lord of Reincarnation has long been waiting for the Lord of the World in the depths of reincarnation."

At that moment, the Lord of Calamity spoke. He held great respect for the Ruler of the World standing before him, who, like the Lord of Reincarnation, wielded authority.

In the vast and limitless Vast Expanse, those who wield authority are, in the truest sense, unrivaled beings possessing limitless and terrifying capabilities. They are figures who overlook the countless living beings from the heights of the True Path Realm; throughout history, those who could stand shoulder-to-shoulder with them are few and far between.

Any figure in the Realm of the True Path could effortlessly obliterate an entire True Realm with a single palm strike. In their eyes, there was little distinction between newly formed True Realms, ancient True Realms, or the most powerful True Realms.

They stand at the pinnacle of the Dao Realm; a single thought of theirs can traverse countless dimensions of time and space. They can freely draw upon the energy of the heavens and earth, and no living being in the Vast Expanse—regardless of their realm—holds any secrets from their gaze.

At the very heart of the Ancient Hidden Civilization lies a vast, boundless moon—its radiance both intense and gentle, as if nurturing infinite life.

This is the sole true form of the moon across the Vast Expanse; the moons of every world and timeline are but reflections cast forth from it.

Beneath this moon, one can see a towering divine tree trembling; its branches and leaves are massive, and from every leaf hang world particles and chaotic matter.

As the wind whistles, a rustling sound of worlds colliding echoes. Those world particles, like dandelion seeds scattered by the wind, continuously drift and fall into the Vast Expanse of the cosmos, taking root amidst the chaotic currents, and over the course of eons, begin to give birth to worlds.

This is the Ancient Tree of the Eon. Deep within its canopy dwells the Ji Chan clan.

Surrounding this moon are countless ancient stars, each as vast as a realm of its own. Even the surrounding bands of stars alone are home to billions of living beings. Small worlds connect to the greater world, overlapping like one bubble after another, a sight of incomparable wonder.

Surrounding the Ancient Tree of the Eon lies a boundless silver ocean, rippling ceaselessly. One after another, silver specks drift down from every dimension and time, for this is the final resting place of the living, the dwelling of the departed, and the realm where the true souls of all beings slumber and undergo reincarnation after death.

The Sea of Reincarnation is infinite and boundless; even the slightest ripple it creates is vaster than an ordinary universe. This is one of the most mysterious places in the Vast Expanse.

It also involves a certain supreme, esoteric law; even beings at the Dao Realm would be bound and suppressed here, unable even to take flight.

Surrounding the Sea of Reincarnation lies a land stretching to the horizon, vast and boundless. Yet every day, countless beings, driven by instinct, guide the drifting soul-lights into the sea, in an endless cycle, awaiting the day when someone will take their place.

At this moment, above the Sea of Reincarnation, space and time blurred as a fissure appeared, and the World Ruler, the Fate Ruler, and others materialized there.

The once-serene Sea of Reincarnation began to ripple incessantly, and a terrifying storm erupted. Countless soul-lights, driven by primal instinct, swayed violently under the storm’s pull, as if on the verge of shattering.

However, as the Ruler of the World raised his scepter and tapped it lightly, the storm subsided. Then, beneath the surface of the sea, a pitch-black, abyssal passage appeared, resembling a vortex leading to the endless depths.

The group stepped forward and soon vanished into it.

Meanwhile, on the shores of the Sea of Reincarnation, atop a barren, icy reef, a pale-faced woman in a green robe was calming the soul lights that had grown chaotic and terrified by the recent storm. Only then did she gaze into the depths of the Sea of Reincarnation.

She was beautiful, ethereal and gentle, her eyes calm and serene, like smooth, clear jade, or a flawless, radiant moon.

At her side stood a silver-haired girl with delicate features and eyes like lapis lazuli. However, both she and the silver-haired girl had their ankles bound by shackles of a peculiar nature; the edges were raw and bleeding, the wounds unable to heal.

"Mistress, how much longer must we suffer this punishment..." The silver-haired girl looked at the woman in green beside her with a pitiful expression.

“I don’t know…” The woman in green shook her head and reached out to gently stroke the girl’s head. “It’s my fault you’re suffering.”

In the depths of the Sea of Reincarnation, another strange sight unfolded. The crystal-clear, silvery sea seemed to hang motionless in the heavens, while a palace of clouds stood upon the seabed—grand and solemn, exuding a stern, ancient majesty, its existence lost in the mists of time.

With the arrival of the World Ruler and his companions, this silent, profound seabed suddenly seemed to come alive. Numerous powerful and terrifying auras swept through the area ceaselessly. This was the deepest recesses of the Ancient Hidden Civilization, harboring many of the world’s deepest secrets—a place even ordinary Ancestral Dao Realm cultivators were unworthy to set foot in.

However, as these auras swept past the Lord of Calamity, they all abruptly receded.

The Ancient Hidden Civilization is ruled by five great royal clans. The Lord of the Calamity Clan is the Calamity Lord standing before them, a figure who dwells within the Realm of the End of the Path and is also one of the five Reincarnated Ancient Emperors of the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

As for the World Ruler, his expression remained indifferent and serene from start to finish. Though he stood there, no one could sense his presence.

Unless one was of the same tier, or he chose to reveal his presence, not even those at the End of the Path could detect a single wisp of his aura.

At this moment, at the far end of the underground palace, a pair of ancient eyes opened. It was as if an ancient sun had risen from the depths of endless darkness—terrifying, vast, and overwhelming. Time, space, the laws of the universe—all were shattered within that single eye.

“Come in.” His voice was calm and ancient, as if he were speaking for the first time in endless ages.

However, aside from the World Ruler and his companions, no one else could hear it.

The space warped, and a great Dao emerged from the dark void, descending to the feet of the group and swiftly transporting them into the underground palace.

Inside the hall, it was pitch-black and profound, but as the group arrived, light began to appear, illuminating the magnificent, golden-hued divine palace—resembling the residence of an ancient emperor—shrouded in dense Daoist essence and chaotic mist.

Standing in the center of the hall was a figure wearing a crown and clad in a black-and-red cloak.

He appeared ancient one moment, yet youthful and innocent the next; the features of every age constantly shifted across his face, as if he were caught in some cycle of reincarnation. Merely standing there, he cast such an oppressive aura that the expressions of the Fate Masters and their companions darkened.

"The Lord of Reincarnation..."

Only the World Ruler’s expression remained utterly unchanged. As two figures who both wielded supreme authority, they stood as equals within the same realm.

Generally speaking, even Road’s End-level beings would not readily reveal themselves or meet face-to-face. Yet this era was exceptionally unique; Road’s End-level beings—rarely seen even over hundreds of eras—were now emerging one after another.

Now, even they—beings who held such authority—were compelled to make an appearance.

The World Ruler and the Lord of Reincarnation had known each other for many eons. Among the renowned figures of the True Path Realm in the Vast Expanse of the present age, the two of them were undoubtedly akin to allies.

“This era’s reckoning will begin sooner than expected. I have already sensed the omens. Next, the Five Great Royal Clans will assemble their armies and, in coordination with the descending emissaries, carry out the great purge,” the Lord of Reincarnation spoke.

The World Ruler replied, “I am already aware of this. The Great Calamity has arrived much earlier than in previous eras. Were it not for the existence of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, the Great Calamity would have been delayed for a long time. This must be the objective of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s leader.”

The Lord of Reincarnation asked calmly, “Have you met him?”

The World Ruler shook his head. “I have not met him in person, but I have caught a few glimpses of him outside the Heaven Slaying Alliance.”

The Lord of Reincarnation seemed to fall into deep thought before asking, “What is your opinion of the plan he is implementing?”

“He must possess a method unknown to us—perhaps a third path,” the World Ruler said.

“A method of creating something from nothing?” The Lord of Reincarnation nodded.

At that moment, the Ancestor Xu suddenly stepped forward a few paces and interjected calmly, “Rather than a method of creating something from nothing, it would be more accurate to say that he can distill a substance closer to the primordial source from the very essence of faith and destiny. That substance is something none of us have ever been able to master, nor do we know how to obtain it…”

The Lord of Reincarnation looked at him and said, “You have served the Lord of Heaven for quite some time; surely you have made some discoveries through your observations of him.”

The Ancestor Xu turned to the World Ruler standing beside him and spoke frankly, “World Ruler, having wielded the authority of creation for countless ages, you must surely know that creation itself is a process of transformation—absorbing energy and essence through a certain substance and transforming it into another. Worlds, life, opportunities, and the workings of fate are all like this.”

The World Ruler’s expression remained unchanged; this was no secret. Even he could not create something from nothing. Whether creating a vast world or a multiverse, one must draw upon the energy of heaven and earth, then fundamentally transform and organize matter.

This is a natural law; energy and matter certainly do not arise out of thin air.

Therefore, after witnessing the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s recent surge of extraordinary opportunities and transformations, he became even more convinced that the Alliance Leader held a path unknown to them. The transformation from nothingness to existence he had achieved relied on the power of faith and destiny.

Within faith and destiny, there undoubtedly lie secrets that they are currently unable to fathom.

This was precisely why he had come to seek out the Lord of Reincarnation—to discuss and gain insight into these matters.

“Could it be that the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance possesses some kind of treasure unknown to us, one that grants a power akin to divine authority?” the Lord of Reincarnation asked.

The two paths that currently stretch out before them in the Vast Expanse have been discovered step by step over countless ages by innumerable sages of the past. To suddenly be told that there is actually a third path—accepting that would likely be quite difficult.

Therefore, he would rather believe that Gu Changge possessed a treasure unknown to them.

After all, even before the birth of the Vast Expanse and the separation of heaven and earth, this world harbored secrets that remain unclear to them to this day.

There were certain figures who, even without wielding authority, still possessed the power to rival their own.

They referred to these treasures as “treasures of authority,” but even they did not know how they came to be. The Lord of Reincarnation was merely aware of their existence, yet even he had never encountered one.

"Of this I am certain: he does not possess such a treasure. He has always relied solely on his own cunning and strength—it has never changed."

"If you two do not believe me, take a look at this."

The Ancestor Xu seemed to have anticipated this; with a faint smile, a dense, pitch-black light materialized in his hand, from which terrifying particles of matter billowed. Even within this space suppressed by the few present, they showed a faint tendency to break free.

“Flesh and blood?” The Lord of Reincarnation’s gaze fell upon it, his expression seeming to grow slightly stern.

Meanwhile, within the Ancient Hidden Civilization, deep within the canopy of the Eon Tree, a grand and ancient palace stood tall.

A woman in colorful robes sat cross-legged there, her eyes closed, her body shrouded in a rainbow-hued mist.

One could see wisps of chaotic mist spreading out, flowing in and out of her nostrils and mouth, while the phantoms of countless small worlds flickered and faded—exquisitely beautiful and extraordinarily vast.

"Second Sister..."

Before her, a woman in black stood quietly in the grand hall, her cold, striking face bearing a hint of complexity.
