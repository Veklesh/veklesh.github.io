The grand hall was filled with a sense of grandeur and solemnity.

The numerous ministers and distinguished guests who had gathered there stood respectfully in the hall, holding their breath and focusing their attention.

Yue Mingkong, wearing a phoenix crown and a golden-red imperial robe, sat quietly in the seat of honor.

Her expression was serene, her face as beautiful as a painting; time had left not a single trace upon her, yet within her deep, piercing eyes, emotions stirred.

Those who once knew her now felt a sense of strangeness; the moment her beautiful eyes swept over them, it seemed as though the very fabric of time and space before them might collapse. This was an immense majesty, as if she were a true Empress who had traversed countless worlds and gazed down upon the entire realm.

Whether it was the Gu family’s ancestors, the Wine-Sword Immortal, or Ming, they all sensed an oppressive presence emanating from Yue Mingkong.

It must be noted that before Yue Mingkong went into seclusion, she wasn’t even an Immortal Emperor. Yet upon emerging this time, she had already attained the Dao Realm—her Dao Fruit solidified, her magical power boundless and endless, capable of draining an entire spacetime universe at any moment.

This was simply not the appearance of someone who had just broken through to the Dao Realm.

In fact, even beings at the Void Dao Realm who had survived several Celestial Waning Tribulations did not possess magical power as vast as Yue Mingkong’s at this moment.

"Moreover, this aura feels strangely familiar—I’ve seen it in ancient texts…" Gu’s ancestral forebear’s expression grew grave.

He did not know whether this transformation was ultimately a blessing or a curse for Yue Mingkong.

The other distinguished guests were equally shaken, and the few Dao Realm cultivators present found it particularly hard to believe.

In terms of age, Yue Mingkong and Gu Changge were actually quite similar. Many of the figures from their generation were still merely in the Immortal Realm, having only just broken through to the True Immortal realm.

Jiang Chuchu, the former Holy Maiden of the Ancestral Hall, was currently the fastest cultivator in the Daochang True Realm. Though aided by divine fortune, she had only just entered the Immortal Emperor realm; it was unclear how long it would take her to reach the Dao Realm.

As for Gu Changge’s other female companions, their cultivation levels lag slightly behind Jiang Chuchu’s.

In the years since Gu Changge left the Daochang True Realm, almost no new Dao Realm beings have emerged.

Of course, it is also possible that those who have reached the Dao Realm are no longer in the Daochang True Realm and remain unknown to the world.

Over the years, many cultivators have secretly left the Daochang True Realm and ventured into the vast expanse beyond.

As the Daochang True Realm gradually borders the outside world, the existence of the Vast Wilderness is no longer a secret, and many ancient clans and lineages have begun to plan their migration.

Back when the Immortal-Spirit Civilization invaded, many clans that sensed the danger had already left the Daochang True Realm ahead of time.

The vastness of the Vast Expanse is beyond the imagination of ordinary beings. Within it, the entire Daochang True Realm appears as insignificant as a single speck of light in the boundless starry sky.

“The Daochang True Realm has truly changed so much over the years…”

Suddenly, Yue Mingkong spoke. Her voice, like heavenly music, was calm yet tinged with a touch of emotion.

Compared to before she entered seclusion, the current Daochang True Realm could be described as having undergone a complete transformation—and, of course, her own transformation could be described in the same terms.

Before her seclusion, she had never imagined that her own strength would undergo such a transformation.

Is this a dream, or is it not?

"All of this is thanks to the Alliance Leader. If not for the Alliance Leader coordinating everything and seeking blessings for the Daochang True Realm, how could we have achieved what we have today?"

In the grand hall, upon hearing these words, the many distinguished guests and dignitaries spoke one after another with reverence and admiration.

The Divine Realm was the faction Gu Changge had established during his time in the Upper Realm, while the Heaven Slaying Alliance was the faction he founded after renaming it the Daochang True Realm. Cultivators from the Upper Realm were accustomed to addressing him as the Divine Lord or the Demon Lord, while those of the Heaven Slaying Alliance typically referred to him as the Alliance Master.

“Is this all thanks to Changge’s efforts?” Yue Mingkong paused slightly, then a faint smile touched his lips, though a tinge of longing stirred within him.

Although cultivators at their level were no longer bound by romantic entanglements—spending every day in tender, inseparable closeness—the time spent in seclusion without seeing Gu Changge had left her

unaccustomed to it.

Especially after emerging from seclusion, that longing deep within her heart had begun to spread and grow wildly, like weeds.

Moreover, an incident she had encountered during her seclusion left her with a lingering sense of unease, and she longed to tell Gu Changge about it.

Only with Gu Changge by her side could she feel at peace.

As her thoughts returned, a helpless smile flitted across Yue Mingkong’s flawless, ethereal face. They had been married for years now—an old married couple—yet she was still as clingy as she had been back then.

"He must be beyond the Vast Wastes right now, plotting his plans..."

“Back in the Upper Realm, I couldn’t offer him much help, but now I should be able to assist him a little,” Yue Mingkong murmured to herself.

Having attained the Dao Realm, she no longer needed others to report to her; with a single thought, she knew everything that had transpired in the Daochang True Realm over the years, including the series of actions Gu Changge had taken after his departure.

The Daochang True Realm was brimming with fortune, flourishing in peace. Myriad races thrived there, and the Divine Kingdom, situated deep within the Heaven Slaying Alliance, ruled over the heavens, receiving worship from all corners of the world.

She had absolutely nothing to worry about.

The legacy left by Gu Changge was as solid as a rock, enduring for all eternity.

"Today, I invite the entire world to a feast."

After leaving these words, Yue Mingkong’s figure vanished. She offered no explanations to the crowd and had no interest in formalities.

For her, breaking through to the Dao Realm was simply a natural progression, for before Gu Changge departed the Daochang True Realm, he had left seeds of the Dao Realm within each of his beloved wives.

A significant portion of Yue Mingkong’s rapid breakthrough was attributable to that Dao Realm seed.

Of course, another major factor was the self-created cultivation technique she had been practicing.

"Great Dream Returns to Immortality."

Even now, Yue Mingkong remains somewhat bewildered. Who exactly is she? In the Dream of Returning to the Primordial, the Primordial

—who had imparted the Dao to her, and who was she herself?

She once believed she had been reborn from the future, but with her current cultivation level, Yue Mingkong actually realized that it might have been nothing more than a single timeline related to her future self that she had suddenly grasped in an instant.

As for whether the events on that timeline would actually happen, no one could say for sure.

In every single moment, there are countless possibilities, and every decision she makes causes events on the future timeline to shift.

However, in every future timeline she observed during her journey back to the ancient past, she would marry Gu Changge and become his wife.

This outcome was already predestined and would not change based on her choices.

This filled Yue Mingkong’s heart with joy and sweetness, for no matter what the future held, she would become Gu Changge’s wife.

Unfortunately, Gu Changge was not currently in the Daochang True Realm; otherwise, she would have wanted to share this joy and sweetness with him.

"Perhaps only when the Demon Lord returns will he know what has happened..."

Watching Yue Mingkong vanish from the grand hall, the Gu family ancestor shook his head; he was still accustomed to referring to Gu Changge as the Demon Lord.

The profound transformation in Yue Mingkong reminded him of a certain era.

That era was incredibly ancient, far older than any known to living beings; indeed, were it not for the scattered references in ancient texts, even he might never have known of it.

After Yue Mingkong departed, the numerous distinguished guests in the hall also took their leave one after another.

The news that the Emperor and Empress had emerged from seclusion and broken through to the Dao Realm quickly swept through the entire Daochang True Realm like a hurricane, causing a tremendous sensation.

To celebrate this occasion, the Heaven Slaying Alliance hosted a grand banquet, extending invitations to all realms, races, and lineages across the heavens.

Inside the Temple of Destiny, upon hearing the news, Xiao Ruoyin remained silent for a long time. Had it merely been Moonlight Empty emerging from seclusion, she had no intention of going to offer her congratulations. But she never expected that she had actually broken through to the Dao Realm.

Among Gu Changge’s many wives, she was the first to break through to this realm.

“Wu Qi, come with me for a walk,” Xiao Ruoyin said. Draped in a light, mist-like veil and an outer robe of pure, snow-white silk, she slowly stepped out of the hall.

Deep within the Hall of the Ancestor of Mankind, radiant hues of dawn and dusk overflowed as the celestial energies of all realms converged, resembling a sky filled with mist and clouds. A silvery radiance enveloped the void, radiating boundless sanctity.

On the white jade corridor outside the hall, dozens of women stood quietly, holding lanterns. A woman approached—cool as the moon, ethereally beautiful, and of unparalleled grace. Upon seeing her, all the women bowed respectfully.

Jiang Chuchu gave a slight wave of her hand. A faint smile touched her flawless, ethereal features, and she then stepped lightly into the hall.

Wearing a pale green flowing robe, Wang Zijing sat dozing over a book on her desk. The jade hairpin slanted through her bun occasionally brushed against the purple-gold inkstone on the desk, leaving a smudge of black ink.

Because she was tall and slender, if viewed from the side, one would see her legs curled up on the cushion, with a portion of her lower legs hanging over the edge, giving her an incredibly lazy appearance.

"I knew you’d be slacking off."

Jiang Chuchu watched her with half-open starry eyes and red lips exhaling softly, looking just like a lazy kitten. She couldn’t help but smile, walked over, and pinched her slender, straight nose with her delicate hand.

Wang Zijing, her nap interrupted, was instantly displeased. Her beautiful eyes widened as she glared at her.

"You were so peaceful when you were in seclusion. Now that you’ve come out, all you do is disturb my sweet dreams every day," she muttered, stretching her soft, slender waist and yawning.

“If you don’t train harder, your cultivation will fall behind. I came here today because I want you to come with me.” A rare smile appeared on Jiang Chuchu’s usually cool and composed face.

Wang Zijing glanced at her. “Where to?”

The two had both been Holy Maidens of the Ancestral Hall, and their relationship had always been close. Every time Jiang Chuchu emerged from seclusion after finishing her cultivation, she would seek her out for a drink or two.

Ever since her drinking capacity was exposed in front of Gu Changge, Jiang Chuchu had stopped hiding it. Whenever she wanted to drink to relieve her boredom, she would come looking for Wang Zijing.

Wang Zijing herself had little interest in cultivation and rarely went into seclusion. Now, the many trivial matters and important duties of the Hall of the Ancestral Human had all fallen on her shoulders.

However, she liked to slack off and shirk her duties; whenever she couldn’t resolve an issue, she simply passed it on to the elders of the Ancestral Hall.

Despite this, her cultivation progressed rapidly. Even Jiang Chuchu, who spent most of her time in seclusion and was aided by divine fortune, wasn’t necessarily much more advanced than her.

“You really do have your mind set solely on taking it easy, turning a deaf ear to the affairs of the world,” Jiang Chuchu said with a touch of exasperation. “Sister Mingkong has emerged from seclusion and even broken through to the Dao Realm.”

"Ah."

Wang Zijing was taken aback; her beautiful eyes widened instantly, and though she’d been dozing off just moments ago, she was now wide awake.

Her dark brows furrowed as she began pacing back and forth in the hall. “This is bad, very bad. Something’s terribly wrong. How could she have broken through so quickly? Could it be that Gu Changge is playing favorites, deliberately saving the best for her?”

Jiang Chuchu was speechless. She extended her jade-like slender hand and gently patted her.

“What are you thinking? How could Changge possibly be biased? He set aside a Dao Realm seed for each of us.”

Her clear eyes shone with a gentle radiance. As she spoke, she shot Wang Zijing a sidelong glance. “Even you got one.”

Although Wang Zijing had never said so, she could tell there was definitely a connection between her and Gu Changge.

Last time, she’d been tricked by Wang Zijing and plied with alcohol in front of Gu Changge; she could well guess what had transpired between the two afterward.

The next day, after she sobered up, she’d seen Wang Zijing walking unsteadily and acting unnaturally—who knew just how wild things had gotten between them that day.

Wang Zijing felt a bit guilty and guilty-conscience, but she still smiled sweetly, “Aren’t I just worried about you? Thinking of your best interests? Don’t forget—even if you don’t compete now, you’ll have to eventually. Your cultivation is already falling behind; if you don’t make up for it in other areas, you’ll just keep falling further and further behind.”

She was also curious: Jiang Chuchu clearly possessed such outstanding talent, and with the aid of the Ancestral Hall’s fortune and faith energies, plus her relentless diligence, why was it Yue Mingkong who broke through to the Dao Realm first?

Could it be that, as the Empress, she truly holds a steady advantage over them?

"Now that Sister Mingkong has broken through, Changge should be coming back."

Jiang Chuchu shot her a sharp glance, warning her not to speak recklessly. “The last time he returned, I was in seclusion, and he didn’t wake me.”

Wang Zijing’s full, lustrous lips curved into a smile. “I thought you were truly empty-eyed, indifferent to fame and fortune, and uninterested in competition. I never expected you had your own little schemes.”

As if a thought had occurred to her, Jiang Chuchu’s flawless, porcelain complexion suddenly flushed slightly.

“Let’s go. Don’t delay any longer,” Wang Zijing said with a smile, no longer teasing her.

Among Gu Changge’s many wives, Empress Yue Mingkong and Divine Consort Yin Mei were particularly close. Although Jiang Chuchu was also a Divine Consort, she wasn’t very familiar with Yue Mingkong.

This was partly due to their respective statuses: one was a princess of the Peerless Immortal Dynasty and Gu Changge’s fiancée.

Jiang Chuchu, on the other hand, was the Holy Maiden of the Transcendent Ancestral Hall—a figure revered by the world as the embodiment of purity and otherworldliness—yet behind closed doors, she was involved with Gu Changge.

This puts Jiang Chuchu in a very awkward position, and she feels too embarrassed to meet Yue Mingkong face-to-face.

Although she was Gu Changge’s legally wedded wife, welcomed into his home with great pomp and ceremony, Jiang Chuchu still felt a sense of guilt toward Yue Mingkong.

Meanwhile, amidst the vast expanse, in a void where spacetime rippled chaotically and order was in disarray...

Jing Tianyuan, the ancestor of the Jing clan, stood before Gu Changge with reverence and awe, carefully accepting the misty orb of light he handed over.

This was his seclusion site. Because his current form was that of an immortal spirit corroded by dark matter, he could not seclude himself within the Jing clan’s territory; otherwise, the disturbance would be too great, attracting unnecessary attention and trouble.

“My Lord, rest assured. I will certainly carry out the task you have entrusted to me,” Jing Tianyuan pledged solemnly. The glowing orb, the moment Gu Changge handed it over, instantly transformed into a streak of light and vanished into his mind.

Gu Changge nodded slightly. He had left the Heaven Slaying Alliance specifically to condense this orb of light, so that Jing Tianyuan could carry it into the Nightmare Demon Realm.

Jing Tianyuan’s physical form had long been corrupted by dark matter, making him indistinguishable from a dark entity. Even if he entered the Nightmare Demon Realm openly, there would be no issue.
