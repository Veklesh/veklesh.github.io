The entire Nightmare Realm erupted in chaos, and a vast black mist spread across the sky.

The earth split open, and vast stretches of the black sea dried up, revealing a tangled mass of bones piled deep beneath the seabed—some snow-white, some golden, but most a deep, ink-black, as if thoroughly eroded and stained by dark matter.

This serves as a testament to just how powerful and extraordinary those beings must have been in life—yet they still met their untimely deaths here, their remains lying silent in the depths of the earth.

"Attack."

The faces of the eight powerful attackers all twisted in shock. Without a moment’s hesitation, they chose to strike.

One even transformed into a terrifying demonic shadow, charging toward the gentle figure holding the oil-paper umbrella, as if intent on tearing her apart.

"Countless years ago, we all shared the same origin. Why must you turn against one another?"

High Priestess Li Shuang sighed softly, her pale jade-like hand reaching out gently.

Out of the void, a terrifying, blood-red giant hand seemed to materialize, enveloping everything. It seized the charging figure in a single grasp, then squeezed it shut. With a splatter, a torrent of black blood exploded into the sky.

This scene sent shivers down the spines of the powerful figures among the Eight Clans, yet none were cowed; instead, more figures surged forward, engulfing the area.

“It seems we can’t sit down and talk this out after all,” High Priestess Li Shuang sighed softly. She made no further move, instead turning and walking slowly back the way she had come, vanishing from sight in the blink of an eye.

Behind her, numerous dark beings materialized, their auras equally overwhelming, and they quickly engaged their opponents in fierce combat.

The Ancestor Xu, Yan Zu, Yun Zhu, and the others did not intervene, merely watching the scene unfold with detached calm.

The vast black expanse stretched endlessly, with no end in sight. At that moment, both heaven and earth were filled with figures locked in fierce combat.

The Heavenly Host had initially sought diplomacy before resorting to force, demonstrating their reluctance to engage in combat. However, the Eightfold Host had come this time for the Coffin of the Underworld and had long resolved to fight to the bitter end; otherwise, they would not have brought so many powerful beings.

Since the Heavenly Host refuses to hand it over, there is no choice but to fight to the death to seize it.

If the Heavenly Host were to gather enough offerings for the Great Sacrifice and bring back the Ruler of Heaven, the Eight Clans would all face retribution—without exception; no one could escape.

Ever since the Heavenly Host fell into disarray, with the Eight Clans each seizing a faction and plundering the Heavenly Host’s foundations, it was destined that they would face retribution in the future.

Not to mention that many years ago, the Eight Divisions were complicit in the Heavenly Host’s upheaval; they opened the Coffin of the World’s Demise and desecrated the physical form of the Ruler of Heaven.

Once the Ruler of Heaven awakens, will he spare them?

"It is time to act."

At that moment, the Ancestor Xu seemed to sense something, speaking in a calm voice.

The powerful figures of the Heaven’s Decline Clan behind him all shuddered, their eyes burning with intense resolve.

Yan Zu nodded and said, “Indeed, we cannot delay any longer. High Priestess Wuyue’s strength alone is insufficient to hold us back.”

"You hold off the other High Priests. Once we strike, the hidden pawns lurking among the Heavenly Host will also make their move."

That last remark was directed at the many powerful figures of the Yama Clan behind him.

“Rest assured, Ancestor. We shall not fail you.”

The many powerful figures of the Yama Clan responded in solemn unison. They were already fully prepared; this battle was destined to succeed—failure was not an option. Otherwise, from this day forward, the Yama Clan would cease to exist in both heaven and earth.

The Master of Destiny said nothing, but his actions spoke volumes. He had come at the invitation of the Ancestor Xu, and out of a sense of obligation, he had no choice but to assist, stepping forward to confront the High Priest of the Moonless.

Three beings who had survived more than nine Celestial Waning Tribulations appeared and struck simultaneously. Such might was beyond imagination—utterly terrifying.

For countless ages, the Vast Expanse had not reappeared, yet today, within this boundless realm of darkness, it emerged once more.

That aura shook the heavens and the earth, overshadowed the cosmos, swept through all of history, and sent a chill through the ages.

Even in the depths of chaos, in many realms far beyond the Vast Expanse, countless ancient beings sensed it, trembling with shock and disbelief.

"Who dares to be so audacious!"

Before the Hall of the Celestial Mandate, the woman bearing a long sword on her back let out a cold snort. Her voice, filled with fury and murderous intent, was immense beyond measure, causing the entire dark land to tremble.

The long sword at her back drew from its sheath. Its blade shone like an endless river of stars—blindingly brilliant and boundlessly radiant—as it roared forward to strike. All matter was obliterated and shattered; even the Dao Laws grew blurred, on the verge of disintegration.

Had such a strike landed in the outer world, who knows how many universes would have collapsed and vanished.

Yet facing her was merely a giant hand. Yan Zu snorted coldly as well, and his slightly withered palm struck out—slowly and heavily, carrying an indescribable, terrifying might.

The other two High Priests standing beside the Moonless High Priest also moved at this moment. One of them raised her jade-like hand, and the jade flask flew out, expanding to an unimaginable size in an instant as endless Dao symbols surged forth from within.

Accompanied by overwhelming dark energy, it surged toward Yan Zu like a terrifying black hole that kept expanding, devouring the heavens.

The other High Priest summoned an ancient seal. Symbols of the Dao swirled around it as it crashed down. In its wake, the sun and moon revolved, countless stars shone, the cosmos unfolded, chaos swirled, and a path of reincarnation flickered into view.

Though this ancient seal appeared small and of ordinary size, it seemed capable of subduing all things in the world, splitting heaven and earth, and creating the cycle of reincarnation!

"An artifact from the Primordial Era..."

As a Primordial Being, the Master of Destiny recognized the weapons held by the three High Priests at a glance. Whether it was the long sword, the jade flask, or the ancient seal, they were all Primordial artifacts—born before the Vast Expanse of the cosmos, imbued with Primordial Divine Marks, capable of manifesting the Great Dao and the myriad heavens, their power terrifying.

The Ancestor Xu also moved at this moment. A blur surrounded him, as if the river of time itself were growing hazy. With a sweep of his wide sleeves, boundless energy surged forth and crashed toward his opponents.

Standing alone against three High Priests, a world-shaking collision erupted. The entire dark land seemed on the verge of being overturned; the heavens split, the earth collapsed, and an unimaginably terrifying storm arose.

Standing at the center of it all, he unleashed palm strikes and fist seals, intercepting the descending ancient seal and knocking away the approaching jade flask. Furthermore, he wielded his sleeves like blades, swinging them forth to unleash a blade of light just as terrifying—one imbued with all the mysteries of the multiverse—to counter the descending sword.

Witnessing this scene, both Yan Zu and Yun Zhu felt a slight tremor in their hearts; the power of the Ancestor Xu far exceeded their expectations.

However, since the Primordial Ancestor Xu had managed to hold off the three High Priests, the two of them joining forces would surely be enough to deal with the Grand High Priest.

The mighty warriors of the Eight Divisions, their morale soaring, followed in hot pursuit, determined to storm the Hall of the Celestial Mandate and seize the Coffin of the World’s Demise.

The Dark Realm, which had remained intact for billions of years, crumbled completely and shattered into pieces as this great battle erupted.

Above the Hall of the Celestial Substitute, the phantom of the Coffin of the World’s Demise appeared, floating there.

Countless members of the Heavenly Host knelt down, bowing their heads ceaselessly—fanatical, frenzied, and utterly devout. One could see countless dark substances swirling inward, constantly sinking into it, as if transforming into nourishment for the ritual.

Thick plumes of black smoke rose one after another across the land, ceaselessly pouring into the Coffin of the World’s Demise.

Across the heavens and earth, the figures locked in combat rapidly vanished, revealing patches of stark white bones.

Even so, many beings did not vanish or perish; flesh and blood still clung to their bones, and driven by a single will, they continued to fight fiercely against their enemies.

Such a sight was spine-chilling and hair-raising. Never before in history had there been such a bizarre scene; it did not resemble living beings clashing in battle, but rather countless spirits slaughtering one another in the Underworld.

When Yan Zu and Yun Zhu struck, no one could stand in their way; beings of a different tier did not even have the right to interfere.

Once one reaches a certain realm, merely standing there is enough to keep all beings of lower tiers at bay. They cannot approach; they would be crushed by the aura, their bodies and spirits utterly annihilated. Clearly, to the other dark beings, the two stood within that very realm.

At this point, unless a being who had survived nine Celestial Waning Tribulations intervened, no one even had the capacity to harm them.

The most terrifying scenario would be if a being of the Ancestor Xu’s caliber were to act. No means could withstand his grasp; all matter would rapidly vanish and dissipate.

Not even the three High Priests wielding Primordial Artifacts could stand against him. Even when they joined forces, they were no match for him, constantly being hurled aside. His power seemed to transcend the physical realm, having severed ties with the Dao Realm itself, causing every powerful being among the Heavenly Host to tremble.

In the presence of a being like the Primordial Void, even the priests of the Heavenly Host appeared utterly powerless. One priest attempted to intervene, but was struck by the aftershocks alone, coughing up blood as he was hurled backward, crashing into the ground.

This was even more true for beings like the Guardian Deities; they could not even approach the Primordial Void. He alone suppressed countless dark beings, leaving them gasping for breath.

Even the legions of Yama and Netherlight were filled with waves of dread, clearly sensing the Primordial Void’s overwhelming power and invincibility.

High Priestess Li Shuang held an oil-paper umbrella as a hazy darkness swirled around her like a torrential downpour. She stood between Yan Zu and Yun Zhu, but she didn’t hold out for long before coughing up blood and sustaining injuries.

Fortunately, dark matter surged in from behind her, instantly restoring her to her peak condition.

The Coffin of the World’s Demise hung high before the Hall of the Celestial Substitute, its radiance illuminating every area in front of it. Stream after stream of dark light surged from the bodies of the dark beings outside, followed by stream after stream of dark matter that gushed forth, showering the multitude of dark beings ahead.

Take one, give one.

This was the Heavenly Host’s greatest reliance. As long as the Coffin of the Underworld hung here, they would be invincible within this realm of darkness—unassailable and indestructible.

Conversely, the power of the dark entities from the outside world is drawn back, becoming nourishment for the dark entities of this place.

Yan Zu had clearly foreseen this outcome long ago. That was why, after severely wounding the Frost High Priestess, he did not continue his attack. Instead, he had the Fate Master hold her off while he himself headed toward the Hall of the Celestial Regent to vie for the Coffin of the World’s Demise.

"The Ancestor Xu has indeed reached that point..."

Before the Hall of Substitute Heaven, the High Priestess Wuyue watched this scene. On her face, shrouded in mist, a flicker of emotion finally seemed to appear.

From a great distance, she raised a palm and struck toward the Primordial Ancestor Xu. In that instant, it was as if boundless waves of fury were crashing down; any Daoist arts, rules, order, or the Great Dao lost all meaning under that single strike.

The Ancestor Xu seemed to sense this strike as well. He met it with calm composure, returning a palm strike across the void. In that instant, nothing appeared to change.

But in reality, within the area where their energies clashed, countless cycles of collapse and reformation had already occurred; the annihilation and creation of matter were all completed in the blink of an eye.

It was not until a moment later that a ripple began to spread from that spot—a single ripple, yet as devastating as an apocalyptic storm capable of reaping all life.

The High Priestess of the Moonless Rite rarely intervened, but whenever she did, she effortlessly crushed her opponents. Yet now, she had been blocked by the Ancestor—a sight that caused many of the Heavenly Host to shift their expressions, their faces clouded with subtle concern.

The Fourth Priestess, Qiluo, watched as her expression shifted between light and shadow.

According to her prior agreement with the Ancestor Xu, she was supposed to use the flesh of the Ruler of Heaven to stall the High Priest.

Yet, up to this moment, she had received no telepathic message or signal from the Ancestor Xu. Did that mean she wasn’t needed to intervene?

Yet she couldn’t shake the feeling that this great battle had erupted too abruptly. Moreover, Grand High Priest Wu Yue showed no signs of surprise or concern; from start to finish, he appeared utterly composed.

She even began to wonder: Could this be a scheme orchestrated by the Ancestor Xu?

Or was someone else setting a trap?

As various thoughts churned in Qiluo’s mind, she saw High Priestess Wuyue act once more. This time, it was not as casual as before. She stepped forward, leaving the Hall of Divine Representation and appearing high in the sky. Shrouded in thick mist, no one could make out her movements.

Yet everyone could sense that the Coffin of the World’s Demise seemed to respond to her at that moment. A rumbling echoed from within, accompanied by the sound of worlds colliding. Amidst the dark matter cascading like a waterfall, chaotic energy erupted.

Vaguely, it seemed as though some weapon was about to materialize; a hazy aura of murderous intent hung in the air. Even a mere wisp of that presence was enough to crush everything beneath it.

"This is bad..."

"She is about to condense the divine form of the Heaven-Reigning Lord’s fearsome weapon..."

Yan Zu seemed to sense something; he suddenly bellowed, his surging aura shaking back all the powerful figures around him. He thrust his massive hand forward, attempting to stop the High Priestess Wuyue from afar.

Behind him, a terrifying demonic shadow materialized, as if standing before the creation of heaven and earth, boundless in size.

Wherever that massive hand reached, the river of time surged violently. Simultaneously, a long, piercing howl erupted from his mouth, and one terrifying black hole after another appeared behind him.

Around each black hole, one could see the endless debris of worlds floating and sinking; it was as if three thousand dark realms were encircling him, bestowing boundless might upon him.

Though the Master of Destiny did not understand the meaning of this, he could sense a terrifying, icy killing intent reawakening—as if it were about to slaughter the Vast Expanse and the endless cosmos.

A vague, ethereal image of a weapon materialized above the High Priestess of Wuyue’s head. Her snow-white, nearly translucent arm rose high, as if to cradle that ethereal form. Everyone could see that it resembled a great halberd—its blade cold, heavy, and ancient, as if it could shatter all things with the slightest effort.

Even as a mere phantom, it exerted an overwhelming pressure, as if one were facing not a fearsome weapon, but an ancient, malevolent demon that looked down upon the world with contempt.

And now, that ancient, malevolent demon was about to awaken!

"Fellow Daoist, stop her."

“She is summoning the phantom of the Demon Spear—the once-fearsome weapon of the Lord of Heaven’s Successor…”

Yan Zu barked out the command, his urgency palpable in that moment. He roared once more, and the terrifying demonic shadow behind him fused with his own form. One black hole after another rapidly coalesced, forming a boundless demonic abyss that stretched out behind him.

A sense of heaviness weighed on Yun Zhu’s heart. He had not expected such terrifying might to be merely the phantom of the Lord of Heaven’s former weapon. No wonder the Ancestor Xu had been so cautious and had invited him here for this very reason.

"Kill!"

Without a moment’s hesitation, he unleashed his most formidable techniques. Around him, terrifying scenes from throughout history materialized—from the Vast Expanse at the dawn of creation, to the Cataclysm of the Great Opening, to the blood-soaked eras of later ages. He had penetrated the secrets of the universe, and now they all manifested within his Daoist arts, as boundless power surged through his being.
