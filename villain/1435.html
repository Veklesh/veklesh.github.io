Gu Changge’s expression remained unchanged; how could he fail to see through Emperor Weiyang’s scheme? No matter how one looked at it, if Emperor Weiyang wished to break free from the Master of Destiny’s control, he would inevitably have to seek outside assistance.

And the first option the Emperor of Weiyang considered was the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Not to mention, the moment the Emperor of Weiyang began plotting against the Heaven Slaying Alliance, Gu Changge sensed it immediately. With just a slight mental scan, he knew exactly what the Emperor of Weiyang intended.

Next, Jiang Weiyang began recounting the various matters her father, the Emperor of Weiyang, had entrusted to her during their time in the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty.

Since Gu Changge was even aware of the silk pouch her father had entrusted to her, there was naturally no need to keep these matters a secret.

Ever since she returned to the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty from the Yao Guang Ancient Realm, Emperor Weiyang’s condition had seemed somewhat off.

It was around that time that she began to suspect he might be unwell, that something was wrong with his health.

As she spoke, Jiang Weiyang produced the silk pouch. A streak of light shot out from within, materializing into a jade slip in the palm of her hand.

"This is a jade slip my father specifically left for me, but I have no idea what’s written on it."

“With my current abilities, I’m unable to open this jade slip. I’ve been wondering and speculating about what Father meant by leaving this to me.”

“But now it seems this must be the proof you mentioned, Alliance Leader,” Jiang Weiyang explained.

Gu Changge glanced at it and nodded. “It contains an imperial edict. The surface is sealed with Emperor Weiyang’s restrictions; only those whose cultivation exceeds the Ancestral Dao Realm can open it. It’s only natural that you can’t open it at the moment.”

He had actually guessed that Emperor Wei Yang would certainly leave behind some form of proof, such as an imperial edict or decree.

Otherwise, allowing Jiang Weiyang to leave the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty alone would serve little purpose.

And this edict was Emperor Wei Yang’s most crucial move—at a critical moment, it would allow the Wei Yang Immortal Dynasty to break free from the Master of Destiny’s control.

However, judging by the current circumstances, the Emperor of Weiyang had unexpectedly come into contact with dark matter.

With his current cultivation level, he can temporarily suppress it, but over time, it will inevitably be exposed.

At the founding ceremony of the Immortal Dao Alliance, Gu Changge had Ji Hanjing deliver a massive surprise to the Righteous Unity Alliance—and this was, in fact, related to that very matter.

Gu Changge also needs Emperor Weiyang as a pawn to completely throw the Righteous Unity Alliance into chaos.

If he no longer needs Emperor Weiyang as a pawn in the future, he might as well help him neutralize the dark substance in the process. After all, in his eyes, the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty is far more valuable than the Righteous Unity Alliance.

"I never imagined Father had so many tricks up his sleeve."

Jiang Weiyang’s expression grew increasingly worried; if even her father had been this cautious, it was clear just how dire her current situation was.

"This jade slip is likely what Father intended to pass on to you, the Alliance Master, through me." She then turned to Gu Changge, intending to hand him the jade slip.

After all, she lacked the power to open it and had no idea what its contents might be.

Gu Changge glanced at her and shook his head slightly. “You need not give it to me. Since Emperor Wei Yang left this jade slip with you, he must have intended for it to be made public through your hands.”

As soon as he finished speaking, he snapped his fingers. A streak of light struck the jade slip, and the numerous restrictions binding it instantly vanished.

Jiang Weiyang was stunned. She could sense a familiar aura within it—one that originated from her father.

It seemed that, in addition to the imperial decree, her father had deliberately left a fragment of his true soul within it.

The next moment, as if sensing something, Gu Changge turned his gaze in that direction.

“Jiang Yun, greets the Alliance Leader.”

Light swirled within the jade tablet, and an indescribable aura permeated the air. Then, a refined and handsome middle-aged man materialized. He wore a blue silk robe, had a medium build, and a gentle countenance; behind him, it seemed as though the vast power of the world was taking shape.

He bowed his hands slightly and spoke respectfully to Gu Changge.

It was none other than Emperor Jiang Yun of Weiyang.

To be precise, this was a strand of his true soul.

It was hard to imagine that the Emperor of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty—a figure who towered over the Vast Expanse of the world—would display such a submissive demeanor.

If any of the ministers of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty were present, they would undoubtedly be so astonished they would be speechless.

“Father…” Jiang Weiyang exclaimed in delight.

Gu Changge, however, was not the least bit surprised. His expression remained calm as he said, “Emperor of Weiyang, there is no need for such formalities.”

Upon learning that Jiang Weiyang carried the Imperial Edict of the Emperor of Weiyang, he knew the Emperor must have other means at his disposal.

This was a figure who appeared simple on the surface, yet possessed extraordinary tactics and astonishing resolve.

Jiang Yun, unwilling to become the Puppet Master’s puppet or to see his wife and daughter coerced by him, would certainly do everything in his power to oppose and resist the Puppet Master.

"The Alliance Master is all-knowing and all-powerful; I’m sure he’s already aware of Jiang Yun’s intentions."

Emperor Wei Yang glanced at his daughter with a gentle expression. Upon learning that the restrictions on the jade tablet had been broken, he knew his daughter had successfully arrived at the Heaven Slaying Alliance and met Gu Changge.

Just as he had anticipated, Jiang Weiyang did not disappoint him, even though he had not informed her of any of the relevant details.

Fearing that the Lord of Destiny might sense his presence from afar, Emperor Wei Yang had deliberately avoided many matters. After all, even he did not know what other means the Lord of Destiny might still have in store for him.

That was why he had so subtly arranged for Jiang Weiyang to flee the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty and distance herself from the Righteous Unity Alliance.

Gu Changge gently set down his teacup and said calmly, “I can guess why you’ve come, but now that you can barely save yourself, how can you expect me to cooperate with you?”

The Emperor of Weiyang had gone to great lengths and endured many twists and turns just to bring his daughter to his side, and then used this opportunity to manifest a wisp of his true soul—it certainly wasn’t simply to meet him face-to-face.

Given his character, he likely knows some of the Fate Master’s secrets. Combined with his knowledge of my plans to confront the Righteous Unity Alliance, he likely hopes to use this to form an alliance with me.

But Gu Changge has never given the Master of Destiny or the Righteous Unity Alliance a second thought.

The Emperor of Weiyang is not yet worthy of partnering with him.

Hearing this, the Emperor of Weiyang gave a wry smile. “The Alliance Master worries too much. Jiang Yun knows his own strength is meager and his position weak; he is not qualified to cooperate with the Alliance Master. The reason I have appeared today is actually to humbly request one thing of the Alliance Master.”

“Jiang Yun knows that Your Excellency likely does not regard the Master of Yinxu Cave as a threat, but there are others like him—those who hide their true nature and have vanished from the public eye. I believe Your Excellency would find them of interest.”

His tone was sincere, his demeanor genuine, and there was no pretense whatsoever.

As a legendary figure who founded the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, Jiang Yun was naturally no ordinary man. Even as the Master of Destiny was laying out his schemes, Jiang Yun had already begun plotting how to counter and resist him, how to break free from his control, and how to kill him.

Over the years, he had endured hardships and tried every possible method, even venturing into the depths of spacetime to seek the help of the Dark Clans.

But unfortunately, on that occasion, he unexpectedly encountered the High Priest of the Remnants of the Heavenly Dynasty and was severely wounded by him. It was also during that encounter that he became tainted by dark matter.

He has suppressed it all these years, yet the situation has not only failed to improve but is gradually becoming impossible to contain.
