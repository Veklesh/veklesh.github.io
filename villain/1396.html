Countless primordial beings were either captured or slain by those three inconceivable, indescribable entities of the Source World.

The demise of the Yao Guang Ancestral Court, the collapse of her father’s Dao, and the subsequent disintegration of the Yao Guang Immortal Dynasty—all are intricately linked to the Master of Destiny.

Consequently, she paid little attention to the life her younger sister shared with her husband, so much so that she only learned later that her sister’s husband had passed away in meditation years ago, leaving only her sister and their child behind in the world.

Who was he, really?

If the master of the Yinxu Cave is not merely a puppet put forward, then his ambitions are certainly no small matter.

If one were to liken the vast expanse of the cosmos to a vegetable garden or an orchard, what the master of the Yinxu Cave seeks to do is simply to pick the fruits that have already ripened.

Perhaps, in his view, every era’s reckoning occurs precisely because the fruit has ripened, which is why it is plucked away by the Realm of Truth.

If he were to consume these fruits, he could bypass the path of transcendence and metamorphosis in one fell swoop, leaping to another level and becoming a being of a different life tier.

However, he may have misunderstood this. Can the insects on the fruit tree, simply by eating the fruit, suddenly transform into humans?

Spring comes and autumn goes; when the fruit ripens, it falls from the vine—this is the unchanging law of the world. When the fruit ripens, people come to pick it.

Seeing all this, the insect on the tree naturally assumes that it is because humans eat the fruit that they become powerful.

Therefore, if it were to eat this fruit, it too could grow stronger—or even transform into a human.

In the past, Gu Changge would likely have found such a notion hard to grasp, but after living for a time with human thoughts and perspectives, he had come to understand the meaning of this so-called “struggle for survival.”

Although he was already aware of the Yinxu Cave Master’s schemes and plans, he had no intention of intervening.

As things stood, everything the Master of Yinxu Cave was doing held no secrets whatsoever in the eyes of a truly powerful being.

In other words, all his schemes and calculations were as simple and laughable as a blank sheet of paper.

What Gu Changge must do now is allow the Heaven Slaying Alliance to develop and expand naturally.

Then, he would gradually swallow up and erode the so-called Righteous Unity Alliance, before rightfully ruling over the entire Cangmang, forcing those lurking in the shadows to lose their patience and reveal their true intentions.

As for the Heavenly Host and the Eight Subordinate Hosts, no matter how much they squabble and scheme among themselves, in Gu Changge’s eyes, it’s nothing more than petty squabbling.

After all, given his current level of existence, there are virtually no secrets—whatever he wishes to know, he can uncover.

Any schemes or plots directed against him would be utterly ineffective unless they involved beings of his own level.

Past, future, and even the present—any timeline that affects him can be extinguished with a single thought.

Even if an unexpected loophole were to appear, Gu Changge could simply restart that timeline, personally eliminating or patching the flaw.

Therefore, Gu Changge now has little interest in any so-called schemes.

The only thing he can do now is truly wait—and during that wait, find a bit of amusement that piques his interest. That is all.
