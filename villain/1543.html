Clang!!!

He had never imagined this fearsome weapon to be so terrifying—it possessed a divine might capable of shattering the cosmos and annihilating all life.

Even though Yue Mingkong had not yet reached the realm of Supreme Shattering, he easily suppressed him with this fearsome weapon.

As for the Phoenix-feathered being named Jingni, after being pierced by the Eight Desolate Demon Halberd, she attempted to dispel the demonic flames consuming her.

This was an existence that transcended the True Path; even if figures like Li Zhu and others had come to confront her, they would have found it a grueling and daunting task. Yet now, she had vanished into eternal silence.

This scene stirred the hearts of the many experts from the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Palace of Defiance, who had retreated to a great distance, suddenly giving them hope.

With the divine being who serves as the Lord of Heaven using his malevolent will to counter the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness, and the wife of the Heaven Slaying Alliance dealing with his retinue, this battle might not be so bleak after all.

There is still hope!

The supreme figures from various super-multiverses were also stirred and encouraged, their eyes once again burning with hope.

"Kill..."

Existences such as the Lord of Calamity and the Lord of Longevity also sensed something amiss; the tide of battle seemed to be turning. To their surprise, the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness had not immediately suppressed the malevolent thoughts of the Lord of Heaven.

"It’s useless. Go ahead and kill Jingni now—as long as the Master remains, he can cast his light to resurrect her once more."

"You cannot kill us completely."

The other followers of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness, upon realizing the situation, moved in perfect unison. Boundless chaos surged forth, instantly sealing off the surrounding spacetime, severing all causal and fateful connections, and blocking the spread of any energy—all to eliminate Yue Mingkong.

Although Jingni’s death took them by surprise, they were well aware of the power and mystery of the Heavenly Seal Talisman, which could resurrect a fallen follower.

Yue Mingkong remained cold and silent, her gaze fixed on the being riding the skeletal steed, her killing intent surging, utterly icy.

Endless, hazy mists of light surged, as if carving out an unknown realm of dreams. She walked through it, like an unparalleled Empress, looking down upon all realms.

"Do not underestimate her or let your guard down..."

"There’s something wrong with the weapon in her hand; I can no longer sense the aura of Jingni."

"Strike with all your might and crush her."

That place was a chaotic maelstrom, where light and darkness intertwined, and all laws and cosmic principles were warped. No one could clearly discern what was happening.

Yet to the many beings who heard these words, there was a distinct undertone. The Absolute Yin Heavenly Lord no longer carried his previous air of condescension and aloofness; it seemed… he had encountered trouble.

Suddenly, it was as if the heavens were collapsing and the earth splitting apart; light and darkness were torn asunder.

In that region beyond the heavens, pitch-black mist engulfed the sky, accompanied by beams of light as blinding as a blood-red sun, as waves of blood rain poured down.

That vast, boundless dark figure extended a massive hand, seemingly tearing at something. The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness kept dodging, refusing to block the attack head-on. It appeared that one of his arms had already been severed, and the blood rain was pouring down from that very spot—a sight of utter horror.

"It’s not enough..."

"It’s still not enough…"

From that endless darkness, that deep, savage voice—resembling that of a primordial demon—echoed once more across the heavens, sending chills down the spine and freezing the blood in one’s veins.

"You are not enough."

However, unlike before, this voice now carried an air of domineering arrogance, an air of absolute supremacy—looking down upon the world from on high, holding sway over life and death, with any being that dared to defy it doomed to perish.

"What...?"

"Kill!!!"

The being riding the bone steed growled, its body surging with blinding light—an aura of patterns transcending the True Path. The shattered bone blade was reassembling as the bone steed beneath its feet, now clad in scaled armor, snorted flames from its nostrils and charged toward Yue Mingkong with a roar.

The other five followers did not hesitate; they acted in unison, joining the assault to seal off all space and time.

Yue Mingkong showed no fear; she knew this might be her only chance.

Relying on the Eight Desolate Demon Halberd and the Six Harmonies Heavenly Abyss, she was confident she could fight them.

"Kill!!!"

Unable to fathom the battle between the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin and the Lord of Heaven—a clash of malevolent wills—the great war waged by Yue Mingkong and her retinue drew the attention of all beings across the living realms.

The many powerful figures of the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Palace of Defiance, though seeing Yue Mingkong—this Matriarch—for the first time, could not help but feel a surge of admiration and be deeply moved; Li Zhu and the others were no exception.

True Path-level figures from other super-multiverses also joined the fray, entering the battlefield one after another.
