It has been several decades since the Realm of the Nine Heavens came into being and revealed itself to the world, causing the Dark Clans to lay low.

Many scenes have flashed by.

Yet the Vast Expanse of uncertainty has remained unchanged, and the momentum of the Heaven Slaying Alliance continues to surge like a rolling tide.

Over the past decade, many brilliant and extraordinary figures have once again emerged. Within the universes under its jurisdiction, numerous individuals blessed with great destiny have grown rapidly, their strength undergoing earth-shattering changes day by day.

During this process, many ancient beings returning from the boundless void have also arrived at the Heaven Slaying Alliance, seeking an audience with its leader to join its ranks.

Those ancient beings who had been hiding in the Boundless Void to evade the scrutiny of the Heavenly Will had all sensed that the Great Calamity was about to descend once more. The Vast Expanse now resembled a pool of thoroughly churned-up murky water; whether large fish or small shrimp, all were forced to surface, and even the great crocodiles could not escape.

Even those who had reached the end of the path have emerged one after another.

Among these beings are the Tathagata Buddha, who established the Buddhist system, abolished all other teachings, and held Buddhist arts in supreme esteem; the Three Purities Daoist Sovereign, who rode a blue ox, spread purple qi throughout the celestial realms, founded the Ten Great Daoist Realms, and left behind a legacy of Daoist teachings; the Great Ro Daoist Sovereign, who created the Wuji Immortal Realm, expounded the Great Ro Dharma, and had disciples throughout all ages; as well as the pioneers of the Primordial Dao Civilization, the Shamanic Dao system,Barbarian Dao systems—the list goes on and on…

Such a spectacle is exceedingly rare. Every single one of these beings is a figure who left a indelible mark on the history of past eras, having reached the very end of their paths.

Of course, not everyone wished to join the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

With the emergence of the Nine Heavens, the Fate-Defying Palace also revealed itself amidst the Vast Expanse, its name and origins becoming known to countless beings.

Among those ancient beings who have returned, there are many members of the Fate-Defying Palace; they had simply remained hidden in the depths of ancient time for various reasons.

Compared to the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s ruthless expansion, the Fate-Defying Palace has, at this juncture, become the better choice.

As for the Dark Clans, with the Lord of Heaven presiding over them, they were not even on the radar of those returning figures.

"The emergence of the Fate-Defying Palace has shattered the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s former monopoly. As the situation has grown turbulent in recent years, the number of returned ‘Path’s End’ beings has increased, and the true depth of the Fate-Defying Palace has gradually come to light."

"Apart from the two 'Path’s End' figures who appeared in the Nine Heavens back then, two more have been active within the Fate-Defying Palace recently. One is known as the Primordial Lord, while the other remains highly mysterious, with his origins unknown."

Inside the central palace of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, many high-ranking members were discussing this matter, fearing that if things continued this way, the existence of the Fate-Defying Palace might hinder the Alliance’s future development.

Of course, this was merely the opinion of some. With the Vast Expanse currently free of turmoil and enjoying a rare period of calm—a state many had long hoped for—

As for the Heaven Slaying Alliance ceasing its expansion and merely maintaining the current status quo, in their view, that was sufficient. Moreover, aside from the Alliance Leader, the Heaven Slaying Alliance truly had no other "Path’s End" beings to offer.

On the other hand, there were quite a few beings at the Ancestral Dao Realm.

However, the gap between the Ancestral Dao Realm and the Path’s End Realm is vast. To put it bluntly, a Path’s End cultivator could kill an Ancestral Dao cultivator without even lifting a finger—simply by sensing their location and sending a single thought. The two realms are fundamentally on different levels.

Under these circumstances, it was completely unrealistic for the Heaven Slaying Alliance to attempt to annex the Fate-Defying Palace.

Recently, Gu Changge, the Alliance Master, has rarely made public appearances. Only Deputy Alliance Master Ling Yuling has been handling the various affairs and leading the development of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

The senior leadership is deeply concerned about this situation.

What the Heaven Slaying Alliance truly lacks is combat power at the very pinnacle.

Any single "Path’s End" being could easily turn the tide of battle. No matter how vast the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s territory, how abundant its fortune, or how flourishing its development, the only "Path’s End" figures within the Heaven Slaying Alliance are the Eternal Primordial God and Lord Luo, both of whom remain by the Heaven Slaying Alliance Leader’s side.

This is a glaring weakness.

Even the Deputy Alliance Master, Ling Yuling, is merely at the Ancestral Dao Realm level, and as for the Ethereal Holy Ancestor, they are still far from the realm of the "Road’s End" beings.

"As those 'Road’s End' beings return, they will undoubtedly seek to secure advantageous territories and carve up the surging fortune currently flourishing in the Vast Expanse..."

"In the Xiao Xi Sacred Realm, a being claiming to be the Tathagata Buddha has recently emerged..."

"The Buddhist sect also seeks to spread its doctrines and faith, which conflicts with our Heaven Slaying Alliance. I believe the sudden appearance of that Tathagata Buddha is undoubtedly driven by envy of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s current vast fortune and power of faith."

The Xiao Xi Sacred Realm is a vast domain within the Vast Expanse, composed of numerous Great Thousand Worlds, encompassing countless Small Thousand Worlds and Middle Thousand Worlds, as well as diverse multiverses containing forces of all Dao Realm levels.

However, within the Xiao Xi Sacred Realm, Buddhism reigns supreme. The most powerful sectarian force is known as Xiao Xi Heaven, which encompasses all Buddhist sects and traditions. Its foundations rival even the mightiest civilizations.

But recently, the Tathagata Buddha appeared, displaying supreme Buddhist teachings and commanding all Buddhist sects to establish a pure land known as the Tathagata Buddha Realm deep within the Xiao Xi Heaven.

His followers are scattered across the vast star systems and universes spanning billions of light-years. In just a few days, the brilliant light of the Buddha illuminated countless worlds, sweeping forth to purify the souls of all believers.

Many high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance clearly noticed that the Tathagata Buddha was attempting to fathom the mysteries of the power of faith. In recent days, similar sects have emerged in some civilizations of the True Realm, all employing various methods to recruit followers and gather the power of faith.

The Heaven Slaying Alliance’s use of the power of faith to nourish all sentient beings has already drawn the attention of many ancient entities in the Vast Expanse.

However, since the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s high-ranking members were, after all, only at the Ancestral Dao Realm, they found it difficult to comprehend the reason behind this. Yet they could clearly sense that, in recent times, there had been a significant increase in sects and factions centered on faith.

Similarly, there are remnants of the Divine Path civilizations that have survived. After the great divine nations of the past were gradually devoured and annexed by the Dark Tribes, the number of beings belonging to the Divine Path civilization system in the Vast Expanse has dwindled even further, to the point of near extinction.

Absorbing and refining the power of faith has always been a hallmark of the Divine Path Civilization.

"The power of faith actually originates from the mind. Does this imply that when the power of the mind reaches its pinnacle, it can break certain rules?"

Figures from the Immortal Civilization, such as Zhuo Fengwei and Hun Yuanjun, had also been studying and contemplating these very questions.

However, no matter how deeply they contemplated these matters, they always felt as though a veil of incomprehension lay between them and the truth, preventing them from truly grasping its essence.

"Recently, the winds of change have been shifting within the Vast Expanse. The return of figures from the End of the Path has caused the forces of destiny within the Vast Expanse to break through to a certain limit. In fact, even I—a being who has survived nine Celestial Waning Tribulations—have sensed a will of the celestial phenomena in the depths of the unknown..."

"The Heaven Slaying Alliance is rejected by the Heavenly Dao. This may give rise to a grand trend of opposition against Heaven and Earth, bringing down unknown calamities and disasters." The Ethereal Holy Ancestor frowned inwardly. Surrounding him were numerous ancient beings who had once belonged to the Ethereal Holy Realm, including many at the Ancestral Dao Realm.

As he spoke these words, these Ancestral Dao Realm figures all nodded in agreement.

Even they sensed a vast, unseen force at work. The existence of the Heaven Slaying Alliance had likely been concealed by its leader, Gu Changge, through supreme means, causing the Heavenly Dao, which spans the Vast Expanse and is boundless, to remain entirely unaware.

After all, an act like “Heaven Slaying” is even more outrageous than “Rebelling Against Heaven” and is bound to be rejected by the heavens and earth.

As for those who have reached the realm of the "End of the Path," generally speaking, aside from evading the Great Calamity, they also go to great lengths to hide from the Heavenly Dao’s perception, concealing all their karma and aura.

For at this stage, they have reached the so-called “end of the Dao.” Having become indistinguishable from the Dao itself, they are drawn to the Heavenly Dao, treated as a part of it, and integrated into its perfecting rules…

This process is also known as “merging with the Dao.” Of course, by that point, they are no longer themselves; they have become a part of the Dao, completely dissipating into the heavens and earth.

And the return of those who have reached the end of the path at this moment is likely due to some kind of call from the depths of the unknown…

This has caused the Ethereal Holy Ancestor and others some concern; perhaps the Heaven Slaying Alliance will soon become the target of universal hostility. Even if it is merely a “pseudo-Heaven,” it still represents the Heavenly Dao.

In the central region of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, the World Sphere hung high in the sky. Endless faith energy converged and surged into it, then, under the refinement of the countless supreme laws inscribed within, transformed into pure, profound Creation Substance and Immortal Substance.

As for Gu Changge, he had been sitting in meditation here for the past few days. The vast, billowing masses of these two substances surged like a tidal wave, completely enveloping and submerging him.

At that moment, as if sensing something, his eyes suddenly opened, and he gazed toward the courtyard where he had been resting.

In the mist-shrouded pond, spiritual energies intertwined, as if an astonishing essence of creation were emerging. Among them, a single, vibrantly colored,crystalline fish suddenly leapt into the air. Thunder roared in the void, and a Dragon Gate appeared. In the blink of an eye, the fish leaped through the gate, its form enveloped by a cloud of rosy mist.

Gu Changge watched this scene unfold. With a wave of his hand, a dense mass of Creation Substance descended, completely enveloping the fish.

Half a day later, all the scenes within the mist dissipated, revealing a slender, delicate jade-like form. She was clad in a golden feathered robe, her eyes like golden clouds—dazzling and breathtakingly beautiful.

Her expression still seemed somewhat dazed and bewildered, until a moment later, a mark of reincarnation glowed between her brows. With a crackling sound, it shattered, transforming into a sky full of golden radiance that merged into her divine soul, and only then did she suddenly come to her senses.

A flood of memories surged through her mind, finally settling on the scene from the Demon Realm—that dreamlike vision from just before she had passed away in meditation.

She had believed that upon her death and passing, her soul would return to nothingness, but Gu Changge had used a defiant act against the heavens to grant her another life.

In this life, she had transformed from a fish into a dragon, yet she had remained by his side for a long time.

“Xiyao greets the Alliance Leader.” As her thoughts returned, the woman in the golden feathered robe, her expression complex, curtsied gracefully.

Gu Changge appeared in the courtyard. Hearing her address, he smiled with a carefree air.

“I did not follow your wishes that day. You might blame me for being selfish, but that is simply who I am—domineering, overbearing, and unreasonable…”

“Whether you resent me or are angry with me, the matter is now settled. You are no longer the Empress of the Demon Realm. No one will stand in your way now. I cannot make you a truly free fish, but I will do my best to accommodate you,” he said.

"I... actually knew you’d do this. You’ve never truly cared about others’ feelings or concerns, so I couldn’t resist you either."

Xiyao gazed at him wistfully, recalling how she’d told him she wanted to be a free-roaming fish in her next life. He’d actually turned her into a fish—and that was exactly the kind of thing only he could do.

“Since you said that back then, of course I’d find a way to grant your wish,” Gu Changge said with a smile.

“You… you’re someone I both love and hate, and I’m at a loss for words.”

Xiyao bit her lip, staring intently at him, then suddenly threw herself into his arms, clinging tightly to him as she murmured, “Is all of this real now?”

“It couldn’t be more real.” Gu Changge gently stroked her black hair.

“Then why can’t you hold me?” Xiyao lifted her face.

Gu Changge smiled, reaching out to pull her into his arms. Regardless of what she had been thinking in her final moments, women were known to say one thing and mean another. Her emotions at that moment might indeed have been genuine, but no one could predict what would happen in the future.

He could have foreseen the future when making that choice and decided accordingly, but if everything in the future were predictable and clear, life would become far too dull.

“I never imagined a future like this, but it seems pretty good now…” Xiyao murmured.

“No one can truly determine the future. Even the future we see in this moment, once it arrives, is no longer the future of the present. Therefore, the future is infinite, disordered, and chaotic—yet it holds endless possibilities…” Gu Changge said.

"You’re rambling," Xiyao said. She didn’t understand what he was talking about. She didn’t care what the future would be like; she just felt that this very moment was beautiful.

Gu Changge smiled and said no more.

Xiyao had reshaped her body and regained many of her past memories, yet at this very moment, there were so many things she wanted to talk about and share with Gu Changge.

In the courtyard, peach blossoms bloomed in brilliant splendor, their petals shimmering and the air filled with fragrance. At this moment, Xiyao was no longer the Empress who once ruled the Demon Realm; she was like an innocent young girl who had cast aside all her worries.

Gu Changge patiently stayed by her side, sharing stories of his travels and interesting anecdotes. Xiyao was genuinely fascinated by the Vast Expanse of the world today; it was as if a fish had left the bottom of a well and entered a vast river—everything around her was a novelty.

"Vast Expanse, the True Realm, the Realm of Truth, and the Great Calamity..."

“So, the reason the Prosperous Dao True Realm once faced a great catastrophe was also due to the Great Calamity.” This was her first time encountering such concepts.

News that was common knowledge throughout the Vast Expanse was, to her, a source of wonder in every detail.

After all, no matter how you looked at it, the Demon Realm—even within the Prosperous Dao True Realm—could only be considered a lower realm. Whether in scale or the level of the world, it could not compare to a true True Realm.

The difference between the two was like that between a speck of dust and a star.

Yet now, the Heaven Slaying Alliance founded by Gu Changge possessed the scale and momentum to unify the Vast Expanse of the cosmos.

Xiyao was indeed utterly astonished and shaken, but soon, her eyes began to sparkle with a remarkable radiance, filled with longing for such a vast and boundless world.

During her time as Empress of the Demon Clan, many matters were beyond her control; she could not leave the Demon Realm, and had never even set foot in the Prosperous Dao True Realm herself.
