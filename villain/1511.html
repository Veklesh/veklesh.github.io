"Lord Alliance Leader, spare... spare my life..."

Upon seeing Gu Changge, the High Priest of the Temple of Sacrifice immediately bowed his head and knelt, pleading for mercy incessantly. Gone was the sacred demeanor he had once possessed in the Ancient Hidden Civilization, when he ruled with an iron fist and was revered by countless living beings.

The man in blue robes standing in the center of the hall could not help but feel a complex mix of emotions upon seeing the High Priest in such a state.

"Now you know to beg for mercy from me?"

With a casual wave of his hand, Gu Changge sent the High Priest of the Temple of Sacrifice tumbling out of that miniature world, crashing to the ground.

Having endured untold torments for countless eons within that realm, her mind was on the verge of collapse. Though she was a being at the End of the Path, in Gu Changge’s presence, she was no different from an ordinary mortal.

"I know, I know. I beg you, Alliance Leader, spare my life."

"Qingyi, please spare me! I was blind back then; I didn’t know when to stop…"

The High Priestess, her face etched with terror, turned to Qingyi standing nearby, bowing her head repeatedly in supplication.

She would rather die immediately than endure that torment, which was even more dreadful than death itself; at this moment, she was willing to set aside all dignity.

"The Ancient Hidden Civilization is determined to seek its own destruction. The Heaven Slaying Alliance will use the Ancient Hidden Civilization as a sacrificial offering. You have lived within the Ancient Hidden Civilization for countless years, and your understanding of it should be no less than that of the Ancient Hidden Emperor. Therefore, I intend to appoint you as commander-in-chief to lead the troops in attacking the Ancient Hidden Civilization and capturing the Ancient Hidden Emperor. What do you say?"

Gu Changge said this with a casual air, his hands clasped behind his back.

The High Priestess froze upon hearing this, her bowing motion halting abruptly as she looked up. Send her to attack the Ancient Hidden Civilization?

"Alliance Leader, I am merely at the End of the Path cultivation level—a rank my father once helped me attain through other means. Even the five royal clan leaders of Zai Guo, Shou Jin, and Kong, whose cultivation and strength surpass mine, are on the verge of entering the End of the Path Ascension Realm, while I can only be considered at the Shedding Impurity tier…"

"The Emperor of Ancient Zang is an existence of the Supreme Shattering Realm, not to mention the Lord of the Twentieth Cycle deep within the Sea of Reincarnation. The World Ruler also maintains close ties with the Lord of Reincarnation."

"Even at the height of the Nine Heavens’ power, they dared not lay a finger on the Ancient Hidden Civilization."

The High Priest hurried to explain; in front of Gu Changge, he dared not conceal anything.

Her father was a Settlement Envoy; before the Land Of Truth had been subjugated, he had once remained here to safeguard the bloodline.

Thanks to this connection, she had successfully become the High Priestess of the Temple of Sacrifice in the Ancient Hidden Civilization, serving as the liaison to the other side during the Epoch Reckoning.

“So many powerful figures…”

Apart from the leader, the other high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance present in the hall were learning for the first time of the Ancient Hidden Civilization’s profound depth of resources. They couldn’t help but gasp in astonishment, deeply shaken.

"In other words, the Ancient Hidden Civilization possesses another True Path-level figure who has mastered the authority of the Heavenly Dao, as well as a Supreme Breaker-level entity. They may even have covert connections with other super-multiverses, or even some hidden True Path figures..."

Many felt a chill run down their spines.

“The Black-White Lord is a cunning and fickle individual who plays both sides. At a critical moment, he might side with the Ancient Hidden Civilization.” Li Shou spoke up at this moment.

The crowd was startled once more. This meant there could potentially be four combatants whose power rivaled that of True Path-level beings.

What Supreme True Realm or Civilization could possibly withstand such terrifying power?

Never mind the Supreme True Realms—even a super-multiverse guarded by figures at the End of the Path wouldn’t dare provoke it.

As for figures at the End of the Path level, their numbers would certainly be substantial.

As for the Heaven Slaying Alliance, aside from Alliance Master Gu Changge, there was currently only Li Zhu as a True Path-level figure.

"Never mind. The Heaven Slaying Alliance will soon gain another ally. With their assistance, attacking the Tongzang Civilization will be no difficult task." Gu Changge rubbed his hands together.

"Has the Alliance Leader already learned of the intentions of the Nine Heavens Realm?"

Hearing this, everyone in the hall froze for a moment, then simultaneously thought of the Nine Heavens.

Clearly, in the Vast Expanse of the current multiverse, aside from the Nine Heavens, no other realm possessed the strength to stand up to the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

Of course, the Nightmare Demon Realm was a wild card, as the identity of the Ruler of Heaven remained a mystery. But the only certainty was that the Dark Clans seemed to have only one figure at the End of the Path level—namely, the Grand High Priest.

None of the other four High Priests have yet reached that level.

"Alliance Leader, the Ancient Hidden Civilization has suddenly abandoned its previous nature and become defiant. It is likely that they have gained the support of an unknown power."

"In recent days, the environment of heaven and earth seems to have undergone some change. The suppression of the Heavenly Will upon the multiverse has weakened. I sense that, from the depths of the unknown, five streams of seemingly inconceivable power have emerged…"

"When I tried to divine and capture them, those five forces vanished without a trace. I suspect that some terrifying entity, never before seen, is about to emerge among the realms."

Li Zhu suddenly seemed to recall something; he stepped forward and spoke, a look of gravity crossing his face.

Even he found it hard to believe—proof enough of just how terrifying those five forces were.

"I am already aware of this. You need not worry. Proceed with the plan as scheduled."

Gu Changge waved his hand dismissively, then turned to look at Zhuo Fengxie and the others in the hall and asked, “How is the construction of the Heaven Slaying Platform coming along?”

“Reporting to the Alliance Leader, the tomb in the Ninth Heaven of the Nine Heavens has cracked, and the construction of the Heaven Slaying Platform has become much smoother. The ominous omens and curses drawn from the void have diminished.”

Zhe Fengxie replied.

In truth, they all had their suspicions: Gu Changge had vanished just as the Ninth Heaven Tomb in the Nine Heavens Realm shattered.

Super-multiverses across all realms could clearly sense a vast, furious will awakening at the edge of the unknown spacetime plane.

As for what exactly was going on, one didn’t need to be a genius to figure it out.

"Alliance Leader, since the Eternal Primordial God departed for the Ancient Hidden Civilization, we have received no word from him. We are concerned that he may have met with an accident. Someone dared to ask."

“Indeed, some unexpected events have occurred, but there is no danger to his life.”

Gu Changge said.

The Eternal Primordial God had been sent there on his orders, so he had naturally left a contingency plan in place. Without absolute certainty of victory, the Ancient Treasure Emperor dared not rashly take action against the Eternal Primordial God.

As decree after decree was issued, a mighty commotion arose across the True Realm civilizations and universes under the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s jurisdiction. One ancient warship after another began to assemble and converge; dazzling portals opened wide across the vast cosmos; and ancient trumpets sounded— these were the harbingers of war.

In many universes, sentient beings who had long witnessed the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s campaigns were all stunned, and soon began to speculate and discuss the matter fervently.

Which faction does the Heaven Slaying Alliance intend to attack this time?

At the same time, within the Eternal Darkness—the vast, boundless Nightmare Realm—dark matter swirled like a thick fog, and the world was forever shrouded in a monotonous, lifeless hue, so dim that it seemed sunlight would never reach it.

Woo-woo-woo...

Here, too, the sound of an ancient horn echoed. Forged from the horn of a True Dragon, it was blown, its sound sweeping across all realms. A massive, dark horde emerged and began to gather before the Hall of the Celestial Regent.

Before the Hall of the Celestial Mandate, the High Priestess Wuyue remained shrouded in a faint mist; her features were indistinct, save for a pair of cold, peered out, resembling a vast, starry cosmos—a sight that inspired awe. At her side stood four High Priests, attending her like servants. Streams of intense, surging bloodlight shot skyward from the Coffin of the World’s Demise, as the will of the coffin’s master stirred and revived.

Within the endless cascade of dark matter that obscured the sky and blocked out the sun, a vague figure could be faintly seen sitting cross-legged, as if seated at the very edge of all worlds. A single thread of its aura was enough to silence the four seas and the eight corners of the world.

All the dark clans prostrated themselves in worship before this figure, filled with fervor. Shouts rose and fell like the roar of mountains and the crash of waves; from afar, it resembled nothing less than an endless, boundless ocean of darkness.

"By the Lord’s command, the Ancient Hidden Civilization has defied the Heavenly Stems and rebelled against the great cosmic order..."

"Today, the great army has gathered to punish the Ancient Hidden Civilization."

The four High Priests spoke. Their voices were not loud, yet they echoed throughout the entire Nightmare Demon Realm, like a howling gale sweeping across all realms, as if every dark tribe were echoing the heavens’ call to battle.

High Priest Wuyue said nothing, merely standing there in silence. Moments later, he vanished from the spot without a trace.

At the border where the Nightmare Demon Realm met the Vast Expanse, the Great Cosmos split open. Terrifying dark fissures appeared, and from the many True Realm civilizations that had existed before, black mist once again spread and expanded. Within the black mist, dark beings descended...

Many beings and cultivators were filled with dread, believing the Dark Tribes were about to wreak havoc upon the world once more. However, they soon realized something was amiss—the realms where these Dark Tribes had descended appeared to border the Ancient Hidden Civilization.

"The dark tribes deliberately sent the Mirror of Reincarnation to the Heaven Slaying Alliance..."

"Could this be some kind of signal, indicating an agreement has been reached?"

"The Heaven Slaying Alliance is also gearing up for war; it seems they intend to take action against the Ancient Hidden Civilization as well?"

"This is not a good sign. Once the Ancient Hidden Civilization goes to war with the Heaven Slaying Alliance, who knows how many realms and universes will be caught in the crossfire."

Many felt a chill run down their spines.

Amidst the boundless Vast Expanse, one after another, spacetime corridors were opened. Huge numbers of troops were rushing through them—an army of billions upon billions, a terrifying sight, like locusts darkening the sky. Wherever they passed, the Great Cosmos trembled; no one could stand in their way.

Super-multiverses from all directions were also closely watching these events, equally shaken.

At this moment, the E-Region appeared to be deep within the Wulang Spacetime.

The surrounding void matter intertwined, forming a 757-meter-wide, amber-like barrier, within which stood a towering divine tree.

However, unlike the Mother Tree of the Eon, this towering divine tree was imbued with a mysterious life force—like a green mist—nourishing this expanse of spacetime. At the various nodes where the spacetime currents diverged, one could see vast worlds existing one after another.

Beneath the divine tree, a figure resembling an elf sat cross-legged, her hair tied in a green spiral bun. Endless life energy surged around her, as if even the dead could be revitalized and gradually revived by the nourishment of her life force.

"Master..."

Three figures stood silently before this figure, awaiting her instructions.

"Let us first observe how things unfold. The Realm of Life must not align itself with any faction at this time. The Vast Expanse of circumstances far exceeds anything we have seen before, and is more complex than ever. Even I dare not take sides lightly."

"Qingyi, you may go to the Heaven Slaying Alliance."

"Qingshuang, you may go to the Ancient Hidden Civilization."

"Qingming, take my Staff of Life and journey to the mortal realm to seek the Seed of Life. The Seed of Life has already descended into the world, but a strange veil of celestial mystery shrouds it; even I cannot divine its whereabouts." The Lord of Life sat there in stillness, issuing his commands in a calm tone.

Every True Path-level figure, in fact, has created a world of their own. The World Sovereign once created the World Clans, while the Fate Sovereign established the Realm of Life.

However, these powers have always remained hidden in the deepest recesses of spacetime, unknown to the ordinary civilizations of the True Realm.

A faction presided over by a True Path-level figure is far more formidable than even a super-multiverse.

"Yes, Master."

Upon receiving the order, the three figures shrouded in a hazy green light quickly vanished from the scene.

Shortly after their departure, a white-haired elder with the dignified bearing of an immortal appeared here—none other than the Immortal Elder.

"You sensed it too, didn’t you?"

"The suppression of the Heavenly Will has weakened; even the power of Heavenly Dao’s authority seems to have diminished considerably..." The Immortal Elder spoke simply and directly.

Although he had reached the realm of the Supreme Shattering, he seemed to possess a deep understanding of the True Path and was well aware of the current state of the Heavenly Will.

The Master of Life took the form of a green-clad maiden with a green spiral hairpin; she was not surprised by the arrival of the Elder of Forgetful Immortality.
