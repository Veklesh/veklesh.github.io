A realm apart from the world, a boundless expanse of time and space drifts like a vast ocean, its waves rising and falling.

Surrounding mountain ranges stretch out, seemingly rootless and boundless, as if they had taken root right here in this expanse of time and space.

Yet atop the highest and most majestic of these mountain ranges, there is a cave entrance.

Not far from the entrance stands a square blue stone stele, nearly ten meters tall and over two meters wide.

The three ancient characters "Yinxu Cave" were carved with silver hooks and iron strokes, their brushwork as fluid as dancing dragons and serpents, exuding a natural, mystical aura as if interpreting the myriad paths of the heavens, shrouded in a hazy glow.

"Judging by the current situation, the Ancestor Xu did not deceive me."

"Yet why, deep down, do I still sense that the timing is off? What exactly has gone wrong?"

"How on earth did he know that the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance would appear at the Immortal Dao Alliance’s grand assembly? What else is he hiding from me?"

"He must know the true origins of the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance..."

At the cave’s entrance, the light of the sun and moon shimmered as the dust settled. A wisp of pure energy drifted out, coalescing into a middle-aged man with a Daoist topknot. His features were square and unremarkable, yet his gaze was exceptionally gentle and profound.

It was none other than the master of the Yinxu Cave, the Lord of Destiny.

He wore a Taoist robe; divine radiance shone within his sleeves, the constellations of the heavens undulating, and orderly divine chains cascading down. A primal and vast aura surged around him, as if he existed outside of any known time or space.

In his palm, he held a feather duster. The duster was light and airy, seemingly crafted from the fur of some unknown beast, and it exuded the boundless aura of the Dao.

"Master, as per your plan, the Righteous Unity Alliance has already engaged in a decisive battle with the Heaven Slaying Alliance."

“The Second Master is personally leading the defense; there should be no issues.”

Two young attendants followed behind the Master of Destiny, and one of them spoke respectfully.

"I naturally have no concerns regarding the Righteous Unity Alliance."

"My only concern at the moment remains the true nature of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s leader; I simply cannot fathom him."

The Master of Destiny shook his head, his deep gaze fixed on the void before him.

As he waved his flywhisk, a wisp of pure energy drifted out, instantly transforming into a round mirror that reflected the scene from the Piao Miao Sacred Realm.

Alas, the boundless black mist, rising from the borders of the Ethereal Sanctuary, obscured the view. No matter how he tried to discern it, he could not make out even a single detail.

"This Black Lotus Sect must be inextricably linked to the Ancestor Xu. Is he planning to use the Black Lotus Sect to test the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance?"

"But if he doesn’t act himself, how can he truly test the waters? There’s no guarantee he isn’t using the Black Lotus Sect as a pretext to scheme against me."

The Ruler of Destiny furrowed his brow, inwardly torn over whether to accept the agreement with the Ancestor Xu.

Ever since the destruction of the vast lands of Xianchu, he had resolved to make a final stand, burning his boats and leaving no room for retreat.

For he sensed that certain "powers" of this age were beginning to surface. It might also be because the Great Cataclysm of the Eon was about to descend, causing the situation in the Vast Expanse to shift—many previously indistinct visions, shrouded in mist, were now clearing up and becoming clear.

Consequently, he felt he had an opportunity.

That elusive “authority” was the only hope they could pursue and pin their hopes on amidst the Vast Expanse.

Unfortunately, such “authority” is rare, and it is only when the Great Cataclysm of the Eon descends that fragments of it might emerge.

And every time the "Authority" appears, it causes the Vast Expanse to be turned upside down; even the Great Calamity of the Epoch struggles to erase its terrifying impact.

The Lord of Destiny harbors great ambition and does not wish to see his path crumble and his future cut off, which is why he intends to fight for it.

However, he sensed that if he were to take the plunge, he would face an unimaginable catastrophe and encounter a terrifyingly formidable foe.

That premonition had lingered in his heart since ancient times.

For one like him—who had already weathered nine Celestial Waning Tribulations and was groping his way along the path to the End of the Realm—the concept of a timeline had long ceased to exist; he could glimpse countless scenes of the past and future at a single glance.

To survive this fated calamity,

he had been laying the groundwork since ancient times, all for this sliver of a chance, and for this, he had paid an immense and terrible price.

And the greatest threat to him in this era was none other than Gu Changge, the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

For Gu Changge has been relentlessly expanding his influence; the Heaven Slaying Alliance has grown rapidly, devouring the realms and universes of all quarters, swelling in power at a terrifying pace, like fire consuming willow catkins.

Throughout this time, he never ceased his campaigns against the civilizations of the various True Realms, ceaselessly harvesting the fortune of the Vast Expanse.

The Master of Destiny did not want all his painstaking plans to go down the drain, so even though the time was not yet ripe, he had no choice but to act. Otherwise, if he delayed even a moment, the fortune of the Vast Expanse would be entirely reaped and claimed by Gu Changge.

It was now nearly impossible for him to act and seize the initiative.

To this end, he utilized the pawns he had left behind to establish the Righteous Unity Alliance, using it to counter the Heaven Slaying Alliance founded by Gu Changge and slow the pace at which Gu Changge was devouring the Vast Expanse’s fortune.

At the same time, he negotiated with some ancient beings he had known in the past, paid a certain price, and began laying the groundwork to counter the Heaven Slaying Alliance and confront Gu Changge.

He was waiting for a suitable, foolproof opportunity to crush and absorb both the Heaven Slaying Alliance and Gu Changge.

During this period, the Ancestor Xu approached him. This terrifying entity, who had once served the Lord of Heaven, possessed power just as unfathomable as his own—not in the least inferior to him.

The two tested each other’s limits and eventually struck a deal.

Shizu Xu openly stated that he coveted the physical body of the former Lord of Heaven’s Steed, intending to absorb the remnants of the Heavenly Host’s old faction and seize the power the Lord of Heaven’s Steed once held.

To achieve this, he needed the Master of Destiny to assist him in dealing with the current High Priestess of the Heavenly Host. When the Primordial Void launched his attack on the Heavenly Host, the Master of Destiny would need to intervene to stall the High Priestess, preventing her from diverting her attention.

The Master of Destiny readily agreed to this deal.

Of course, he also put forth his own demands, asking the Primordial Void to help him deal with the Heaven Slaying Alliance and Gu Changge. However, the Primordial Void flatly refused, stating that the terms were insufficient and that the Master of Destiny would need to offer more substantial incentives.

The Master of Destiny then settled for a compromise, asking the Ancestral Void to help him probe the true intentions of the Heaven Slaying Alliance and Gu Changge.

The Ancestor Xu did not refuse and agreed to intervene.

Both were calculating their own strategies. Although they had sworn an oath based on their respective paths and principles, their promises were not without hidden schemes and ulterior motives.

Just as Master Yun did not believe that Shizu Xu simply intended to deal with the Heavenly Host—why would the other party have sought him out for a deal?—and especially since this occurred shortly after the Dark Clans reemerged into the world, choosing a time that seemed perfectly reasonable yet was fraught with coincidences, no matter how one analyzed it, one could sense something amiss.

When the Ancestor Xu agreed to test the true strength of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, he was remarkably confident and resolute.

This has led the Master of Destiny to suspect, even now, that the Ancestor Xu might have known Gu Changge’s origins and background all along, which is why he approached him to make a deal.

There was an obvious trap waiting for him to fall into.

Yet the Ancestor Xu had read his impatience and restlessness perfectly, certain that even knowing the trap was there, he would still walk right into it.

The Master of Destiny had no choice but to grit his teeth and swallow this small loss.

"Just what is the Black Lotus Sect’s background that it could test the true strength of the Heaven Slaying Alliance?”

“Ancestor Xu wouldn’t dare deceive me. I suspect the mastermind behind the Black Lotus Sect is also someone he’s manipulated and exploited to test the Heaven Slaying Alliance Leader’s strength?”

The Master of Destiny’s gaze grew deep. With a sweep of his dust-whip, the scene before him vanished. When it reappeared, it was the battlefield where the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance were locked in combat.

The cosmos lay in ruins, Civilizations crumbled, and wherever the war swept through, nothing but horrifying, unspeakable scenes remained.

The flames of war spread; ancient warships rumbled as they soared across the sky, one after another, seemingly endless, obscuring everything in their path, their momentum terrifying and awe-inspiring.

Throughout the cosmos, chaos and slaughter were everywhere. Whether Immortal Realm beings or Dao Realm beings, all were engaged in fierce combat.

One after another, Dao Realm beings roared as they charged into battle, clashing with their opponents. As the shockwaves spread, countless worlds collapsed and shattered, and untold numbers of living beings perished within the chaos.

The Master of Destiny watched these scenes unfold, his expression devoid of sorrow or joy.

Such is the cycle of all worlds: no matter how many sentient beings perish or how many civilizations and universes crumble, before long, new life will emerge, and new universes will be born.

Like endless crops of chives, cut time and again yet never exhausted, they await each new spring’s rebirth.

If he cannot escape that fate, he too will eventually meet the same end as the countless beings now vanishing and crumbling before his eyes.

Under the Way of Heaven, all beings are equal.

"Power... only by seizing or abolishing a portion of that power can I have a chance to escape my fate..." The Master of Fate’s gaze was somber, yet even now, he could not hide that hint of determination.

Buzz!!!

With a sweep of his wide sleeves, a pitch-black fissure instantly tore open in the space before him. The Master of Destiny, accompanied by two young attendants, stepped into it and vanished in an instant.

The time agreed upon between him and the Primordial Void was fast approaching. The Primordial Void intended to strike against the remnants of the Heavenly Host’s old faction, and he, of course, would have to assist according to their pact.

But before that, he still needed to wait for one result.

He wanted to know whether this so-called Black Lotus Sect could indeed uncover the true nature of the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

And now, with the Alliance Leader absent, the Heaven Slaying Alliance posed little threat. To be on the safe side, he would still need to employ some additional measures.

In a universe where spiritual energy was nearly depleted, the laws of the cosmos were on the verge of erasure, and living beings were scarce.

On an ancient star shrouded in swirling yellow sand, a collapsed temple stood, buried beneath endless dunes. It was in a state of utter ruin; even the temple plaque was only half intact, the other half eroded by time until its shape was barely discernible.

But at that moment, a pitch-black fissure suddenly materialized out of thin air.

The Master of Destiny, accompanied by two young attendants, stepped out of it. He looked at the temple before him, pausing briefly at the two remaining characters—"Killing" and "Life"—before striding inside.

“Master, could there be trouble?” One of the two attendants, who clearly recognized the place, looked at him with a hint of fear in his eyes.

“Much time has passed; there is no need to worry,” the Master of Destiny said calmly.

Before long, the ground suddenly shook violently, and the entire ancient planet trembled.

Endless yellow sand surged upward, soaring into the sky like column after column of smoke formed by true dragons, swirling through the heavens.

The earth split open, spewing forth black lava. Endless black mist billowed upward, obscuring the sky and the sun, shrouding the entire star in an instant.

That black mist seemed tangible, eventually coalescing into an overwhelming aura of slaughter. Rivers of blood flowed ceaselessly, and all manner of corpses, bleached bones, shattered weapons, and fallen immortals and demons emerged simultaneously, as if plummeting from the heavens—a sight of unimaginable horror.

"Kill, kill, kill..."

A savage voice rang out, imbued with an endless aura of slaughter, as if intent on utterly annihilating every living being in all worlds.

The clanging of chains sounded as if it were bursting forth from the depths of the Underworld.

The temple collapsed in an instant, revealing a terrifying chasm. A horrifying figure, shrouded in black mist and bound by chains, broke free from the ruins.

Its emaciated form was draped in a tattered black robe; its face was indistinct, save for a pair of eyes—pitch-black and filled with hatred, as if a demon had been reborn.

"My Lord, why have you summoned me? Do you intend to release me?"

"Heh heh..."

The figure looked at the Master of Destiny who had appeared before him, a cruel smile curving his lips.

The moment one of the young attendants met its gaze, fear gripped his features. Instantly, his forehead split open, and with a puff, he exploded into a mist of blood that filled the air.

Another young attendant, his face equally filled with terror, began to tremble as if recalling some memory.

The Master of Destiny merely shook his head lightly. With a flick of his flywhisk, the dead child came back to life.

“Death alone is the ultimate destination for all beings. I have bestowed upon you the glory of death—why then do you still fear it?”

"Death has always been my deepest desire."

“Why do you, the Master, stand in the way of my granting them death?”

This figure gazed coldly at the Master of Destiny, a terrifying malevolence flickering in his eyes.

The Master of Destiny seemed somewhat exasperated by this figure, but he still managed to maintain his composure as he said, “Death is indeed the best destination. All living beings in this world are destined to die. Now, there are beings in a certain realm who foolishly seek to transcend death. I need you to send those beings to where they belong.”

“Where?”

Upon hearing this, the figure shrouded in black mist grew excited. A terrifying aura surged forth, overwhelming and intimidating, as if it were about to tear this entire universe apart.

“I will send you there. You will find out when the time comes,” the Master of Destiny said calmly.

Then, with a sweep of his flywhisk, a rift in space-time appeared before them. From the pitch-black depths on the other side, faint sounds of slaughter and chaos seemed to drift out.

"Very well, I shall go now and bestow upon them the supreme honor."

The figure let out a loud laugh; as he roared, the stars beneath his feet shattered.

Every chain binding him snapped and broke, and then, like a demon god, he charged headlong into the rift.

"Master, if you release him, won’t that place take notice?"

The resurrected young boy’s eyes were still filled with fear and trembling.

"It matters not. I have already refined him into one of my Five Essences. Aside from me, no one knows his true identity."

"Perfect. I’d like to see what happens to that place," the Master of Destiny said indifferently.

Throughout history, those realms that have wielded partial “authority” have always remained hidden from the world, revealing themselves only occasionally when the cataclysm of an era approaches.

But now, the waters of the Vast Expanse have grown so murky that even the Master of Destiny cannot control his own future fate.

He naturally feared not the consequences that might follow, nor did he have any reason to worry or care.
