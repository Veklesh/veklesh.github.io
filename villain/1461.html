The Land of the Source of Darkness was shrouded in deathly silence and icy cold, as if it had always been this way, as if it had been forgotten by all ages.

A World Tree, long since withered and dead, stood tall within it, its branches and leaves devoid of any sign of life—cold, lifeless, dark, and profound…

Whoosh, whoosh, whoosh...

But at this very moment, a terrifying roar like the roar of mountains and the roar of the sea echoed through the air, and all the dark mountains began to crumble.

The earth split apart, the sky cracked open, resembling shards of glass. At the edges, terrifying black flames erupted, burning and enveloping the shards of the shattered sky as they fell ceaselessly, crashing down onto the earth.

In the four corners of the dim sky, six hazy beams of light appeared, already faint and ethereal.

One after another, great dao runes materialized and surged forth from them, transforming into a torrent of power, as if to suppress the World Tree, which was violently shaking and trembling, preventing the existence sealed beneath it from breaking free.

Those six hazy beams of light were shrouded in a chaotic aura; faintly discernible were the ethereal forms of six distinct weapons: an ancient mirror, a portal, a pool, a coffin, a wheel, and a Daoist text.

However, it was clear that these six weapons were merely illusions, not tangible objects. As they continued to fade and dim, the residual power within them was gradually being eroded.

The withered World Tree was cracking continuously; fissures spread across every branch and leaf, making it resemble porcelain—as if the slightest pressure would shatter it into a skyful of shards and ashes.

Following the blood-red passage that pierced through heaven and earth, it plunged into this Land of the Dark Source, as if it had found the pivotal link connecting the two great realms.

The Source of Darkness had originally been falling toward the Vast Expanse at a steady, slow pace.

But at this moment, its course suddenly shifted. A world equally eerie and suffused with the aura of darkness appeared beyond the Realms. That vast, boundless realm of darkness seemed… like the lair of dark beings.

The Land of the Dark Source altered the pace of its descent and began to draw closer to that equally dark world.

The two vast realms were infinitely distant, utterly incapable of converging, yet the emergence of that crimson passage was as though a blood-red bonfire had been lit within the darkness, guiding its course.

"The Great Sacrifice is about to begin."

In the Vast Expanse, through endless time and space, and across countless universes, countless beings now hear that icy voice ringing in their ears.

The barriers of heaven and earth grew transparent, and within that vast, boundless dark world, a colossal, limitless altar appeared.

Within it stood a vague figure, shrouded in thick mist; yet its cold, merciless voice echoed throughout all realms.

Everyone trembled with terror; at this moment, their souls seemed on the verge of annihilation, dissolving into nothingness and flying toward that vast, boundless altar.

Regardless of which realm’s Civilization or which lineage they belonged to, at this moment, all could feel only boundless terror and trembling.

Around the four sides of that vast, boundless altar, four blurry figures appeared, seemingly clad in ancient ceremonial robes. These figures knelt there, as if reciting some sort of ritual text. Their obscure, incomprehensible tones and voices seemed to have traveled through the river of time, resounding and reverberating there.

Symbols of the Great Dao emerged there, transforming into a terrifying, colossal power—torrential, ancient, and unfathomable… until finally, they coalesced into a storm, sweeping out and spreading toward that vast, dark realm.

All living beings witnessed it: behind that vast realm of darkness lay an endless sea of dark creatures, solemn and silent, all prostrating themselves there. As wave after wave of terrifying crimson lightning struck down, slicing through the heavens and earth, that realm of darkness was illuminated with crystal clarity.

Like a surging tide, the endless, pitch-black hordes of dark beings knelt there, cold and lifeless, silently awaiting the commencement of this grand ritual.

This scene filled all beings in the Vast Expanse with a sense of cruel despair and dread.

Everyone felt as though a chill had swept over their entire bodies; they stared blankly at the scene, unable to move.

The grand ritual of the dark beings had finally arrived, far sooner than anyone could have imagined—right in the midst of the fierce battle between the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance, while the Vast Expanse was in constant upheaval.

The Dark Beings seized the opportunity to strike.

And what filled the hearts of countless beings with terror was that, at that very moment, they all heard a terrifying breathing sound ringing in their ears, as if it were right before their eyes. That sound could be heard in every universe.

Regardless of their cultivation level, every cultivator felt their scalps prickle, their skulls on the verge of exploding, and a chill that froze their entire bodies solid.

That sound was like a breath, yet it also resembled an indescribable entity awakening from slumber. The overwhelming dread and boundless terror it evoked left one feeling utterly hopeless and horrified.

At the source of the darkness, as the six blurred weapon imprints faded, the withered World Tree instantly turned to ash and vanished, leaving nothing behind. Endless dark matter surged skyward from that spot, like a waterfall flowing backward, swirling upward toward the vast heavens.

Vaguely, it seemed as though a terrifying will was reawakening from that place.

Many powerful beings seemed to catch sight of a pair of cold, indifferent eyes.

Those eyes were terrifyingly chilling, as if they had split the heavens and split the earth, causing space to collapse, time to warp, and all the heavens to fall into deathly silence.

Yet in that darkness, nothing could be seen.

Yet those terrifying eyes—boundless in horror, cold and merciless—hung high above, gazing down upon the heavens and watching them.

Though it lasted only an instant and vanished quickly, like a phantom, they were utterly certain they had not mistaken what they had just seen.

The Dark Beings have commenced the Great Ritual, preparing to open the source of darkness and awaken the will of the Lord of the Heavens, long sealed within.

And within the vast Dark Realm where the Dark Clans dwell, that grand altar grew ever more immense and solid, poised to emerge from the Vast Expanse.

A terrifying black fissure appeared beyond the realms—a gateway through which a supremely powerful Dark Spirit, shrouded in dark mist, stepped forth.

Across the Vast Expanse of the cosmos—a realm long since overrun by the Dark Clans—dark entities let out a deafening roar.

Those worlds were reaped in an instant. The mighty dark beings struck, their massive hands piercing through, causing entire worlds to collapse. Lands cracked, mountains and rivers shattered, and oceans dried up and evaporated. Endless beings perished, transforming into a dense torrent of life that surged toward the grand altar high in the sky.

In some worlds, powerful beings, unable to bear the sight, roared and charged forward, but they never managed to get close; they disintegrated mid-air, leaving nothing behind.

Everywhere was a scene of wailing and despair.

In the civilizations of the various True Realms, the beings who witnessed this scene trembled with fury, their eyes bulging with rage. If they allowed the Dark Beings’ grand sacrifice to continue, their turn would eventually come.

Amidst the Vast Expanse of the Void, the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance continued to clash relentlessly. The Immortal Dao Alliance had effectively collapsed midway through the conflict. Now, the Ethereal Sanctuary was shrouded in endless dark mist, and even Gu Changge, the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, was trapped within it. Looking across the current state of the Void, who could stop this catastrophic disaster?

"My main body is dead..."

"But I have no idea how exactly he died. Only a vague memory tells me that the Master was tricked by the Ancestor Xu."

"But it doesn’t matter. The Master and I are one and the same. From now on, I am the Master of Destiny."

In a mundane realm, within a village filled with birdsong and the fragrance of flowers.

A middle-aged man with a scholarly demeanor was teaching children to read in a private school when suddenly his expression froze. A vast, boundless current of cause and effect surged toward him, instantly granting him clarity regarding the events’ origins, consequences, and details.

Although the details of his journey to the Dark Clan’s lair had been completely erased, leaving him unable to know what had actually happened there, he still understood that he had been outmaneuvered by the Ancestor Xu.

Even the two young attendants who had followed him for so long had met a tragic end because of this.

A look of grief flashed across the Master of Destiny’s face. Those two young disciples had followed him since the Primordial Era; they had undergone many reincarnations, but he had always found them again.

Now that his physical form had met a tragic end, the cultivation memories of that body had been utterly obliterated and wiped clean. All traces of the two young disciples had vanished from the world, never to be found again.

Soon, he suppressed the sorrow on his face, his expression becoming as still as a deep well. He set down the book in his hand, gave a light wave of his hand, and the private school before him vanished. All the sounds of children reading aloud faded away, and even the children themselves disappeared...

As he took a step forward, there was no village left in this place; nothing remained, as if a great wave had crashed in, sweeping away everything on the sandy shore.

He had left behind his Five Aggregates Body as a precaution, specifically to handle a situation like this.

Although he had managed to escape in the end, the losses were still devastating. Everything on his original physical body had been obliterated, and even the Void Markings had dissipated—among them were several innate artifacts he had collected.

But now, dwelling on these things was pointless. Just as the Ancestor Xu had schemed against him, hadn’t he, too, schemed against the Ancestor Xu?

He had long suspected the true identity of Gu Changge, the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance. Now, seeing how cautious and circumspect the Ancestor Xu was acting, he was even more certain of his earlier suspicions…

"The leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance is none other than the Lord of Heaven. Though I do not know when he reappeared in the world, he must have broken free from his confinement long ago. All that is happening now is merely part of his grand scheme."

"Ancestor Xu used me to uncover the true nature of the Heaven Slaying Alliance Leader. Unfortunately, even he fell into the trap. I suppose this is the sequence of events that unfolded within the lair of the Dark Beings..."

"The Ancestor Xu knew exactly what was going on. I should have examined that physical body more thoroughly back then; otherwise, I wouldn’t have suffered such a heavy loss."

The Master of Destiny sighed softly. With his Five Elements body incomplete, his plan to condense the Five Elements Dao Fruit would have to be put on hold for now.

For now, the immediate priority was to retreat. The Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance was far too formidable; he had no confidence in his ability to confront him. The prerequisite for a desperate, all-or-nothing gamble was the strength to stand against him.

Now that he knew Gu Changge’s true identity, he had no intention of resisting; it was simply impossible to stand against him.

Since the dawn of the Vast Expanse, only one Ruler of Heaven had ever reached that level without seizing or usurping power—and he was the only one.

He even suspected that even if those who had seized power were to intervene, they might not be able to stand up to Gu Changge.

This was simply an unimaginable, anomalous monster—its origins were a complete mystery.

The Ancestor Xu’s ambitions were vast indeed; knowing all this, he still dared to act. Such a course of action could lead to only two outcomes: either death, or the complete mastery of its secrets, achieving enlightenment in a single stroke and embarking on an unprecedented path.

"Come to think of it, perhaps I still have a sliver of a chance."

"Since the Ancestor Xu dared to do this, he must be certain of his success..."

The Master of Destiny fell into a long silence. He had indeed prepared backup plans, and there were still many pawns and tactics he had not yet deployed. Yet in the face of absolute power, all those calculations and schemes seemed feeble and powerless.

Now, his only chance was to cooperate with the Ancestral Void, truly setting aside past grievances. He would help the Ancestral Void plot to seize the flesh and blood of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s leader, while the Ancestral Void would help him seize control of this world.

As for the Righteous Unity Alliance, it had been created specifically to counter the Heaven Slaying Alliance. Now that even he, the one wielding the pieces, had lost the courage to continue the game, what use was this pawn anymore?

After the Dark Grand Ritual began, the entire Vast Expanse plunged into unprecedented turmoil and chaos; order ceased to exist, and rules were shattered.

Amidst the battlefield where the armies of the Righteous Unity Alliance and the Heaven Slaying Alliance were locked in a fierce battle, a figure clad in white robes seemed to sense something. His expression shifted repeatedly, then he let out a deep sigh.

He swiftly appeared on the battlefield, grabbing the black figure who was engaged in a fierce battle with a group of Forbidden Zone beings. Chains materialized, instantly binding the figure. Without offering any explanation, he turned and walked away without pausing.

Mu Fuguang, Tong Xian, and the other Forbidden Realm beings of the Heaven Slaying Alliance were all deeply perplexed, yet none of them moved to stop him; they simply watched as he departed.

Even Ling Yuling struggled against that mysterious figure in white robes; she had once been trapped by the Fourth Killing Array and banished to the endless void of spacetime.

Naturally, they would not be so tactless as to intervene, especially since everyone was injured. It had already taken all their strength and every trick in their arsenal just to barely hold back that being, whose very presence reeked of slaughter.

"Did something happen?"

“Is it because the Dark Ritual has begun, so they’ve decided to retreat?”

They were still puzzled, unable to fathom why the figure in white would retreat—could it be that they feared something?

A ripple spread out, appearing beyond the realms. Ling Yuling materialized there. She had also witnessed the scene just now and, like the others, had not intervened—for she, too, could not have stopped it.

That figure in white was a descendant of the Central Lord. Though it was unclear why they had become a servant of the Master of Fate, the power of the Fourth Ancient Killing Array allowed them to come and go as they pleased, unhindered by anyone.

"It seems there are no surprises. The great final battle has barely begun, yet it is already coming to an end."

“The high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance have lost all will to fight and are already planning to flee; their backbone has crumbled.”

She gazed upon the various cosmic battlefields where fierce combat raged and already understood the outcome.

As the figure in white retreated, other beings with overwhelmingly powerful auras across the battlefields followed suit, swiftly departing.

The strands of willpower hovering above the Righteous Unity Alliance’s army rapidly collapsed and disintegrated, and the phantom battle spirits formed from their convergence could not endure.

Not long after, the situation—which had previously been in a state of mutual restraint—shifted abruptly. As the powerful figures withdrew, the Heaven Slaying Alliance instantly launched a crushing assault, sweeping through the enemy ranks with overwhelming force.

The Righteous Unity Alliance’s army had no choice but to retreat. Ordinary soldiers did not understand what had happened or why the situation had changed so drastically in an instant. Soon, morale began to crumble. The news that the Righteous Unity Alliance’s high command had abandoned them spread like wildfire, racing at a terrifying speed across the various cosmic battlefields…

Many members of the Righteous Unity Alliance, completely unaware of the truth, turned pale and then sank into despair.

There was still hope of defeating the Heaven Slaying Alliance—why retreat and flee? They had intended to fight to the death, yet now they seemed like clowns and a laughingstock.

"Something must have happened on the Lord of Destiny’s side."

Ling Yuling did not seek to exterminate them completely. Especially now that the Dark Clans had begun their grand ritual, it was a time when unity was crucial. The departure of the Righteous Unity Alliance’s high command provided her with the perfect opportunity to consolidate these forces.

She quickly issued an order: those who surrendered and submitted would not be killed. At the same time, she had word spread that the high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance had long been corrupted by darkness and had become part of the Dark Clan. The provocation of a major war between the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance had been orchestrated solely to initiate the Great Dark Ritual.

Now that the Great Dark Ritual had begun, the high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance naturally departed one after another, unconcerned with the fate of the remaining members and their forces.

Under her forceful consolidation, only a handful of die-hard resisters fought to the death, refusing to surrender. Ling Yuling was no one to show mercy; she immediately ordered their execution.

With Gu Changge absent from the Heaven Slaying Alliance, these matters have fallen to her, and she has finally asserted the authority befitting her position as Deputy Alliance Leader.

Although the decisive battle between the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance did not last long, the battlefields were already in ruins, a scene of utter devastation. Fragments of weapons and corpses littered the ground; countless lives were lost, and countless cosmic realms were caught in the crossfire, destroyed in the chaos.

Just as Ling Yuling was focusing all her attention on how to consolidate and absorb the remaining forces of the Righteous Unity Alliance,

A figure shrouded in black mist appeared as if out of thin air at the site of the previous fierce battle between the two armies, reaching out into the void as if tracing something.

"It is indeed the aura of my Wusheng Hall. Which one is it?"

The figure seemed slightly puzzled. The fact that someone from the Hall of No-Life had reappeared in the Vast Expanse, yet none of its members were aware of it, was problematic in itself.

Moreover, it was not yet time for the Hall of No-Life to complete its mission; its members were not permitted to roam the mortal realm lightly. If the Hall Master were to find out, they would inevitably be cast into the Abyss of No-Life to be devoured and tortured for eternity.
