Zhu Xuan was merely a pawn he had casually left behind; even if Zhu Xuan failed to bring back the Pool of Creation, Gu Changge had other ways to retrieve it.

Gu Changge placed the Pool of Creation at the center of the Heaven Slaying Alliance and refined it with his spiritual power. All the high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance clearly sensed the Alliance’s destiny surging toward it like a soaring dragon.

This power expanded rapidly, instantly sweeping through one true-realm universe after another, covering every territory of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Gu Changge remarked casually. During the refinement process, he had slightly altered the internal laws of the Pool of Creation, causing some of the originally crude markings within it to become more complete.

To be precise, this was not a sacrifice, but a ritual. Such rituals could take many forms—much like how descendants offer incense to their ancestors, praying for protection for future generations. This form of ritual tested, above all, the sincerity of the worshipper.

Soon, the Dark Clans responded, expressing their willingness to accept the terms.

The Heaven Slaying Alliance would not interfere in the Dark Clan’s Grand Ritual, and the Dark Clan would not set foot within the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s territory.

the remaining True Realm civilizations in the Vast Expanse were plunged into genuine despair and panic.

In the Land of the Dark Source, a magnificent altar stood, bathed in a blood-red glow that obscured the heavens and the earth.

Within the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Beings who believe they have broken free from the river of fate, once they fall into this sea, become but tiny creatures within it.

Yet it is precisely because of this that those massive fish and colossal beings, hiding in the deepest depths, have been able to evade detection until now.

However, this method is far too cumbersome and requires an immense amount of time.

Unless one possesses extraordinary patience, this process—which often requires observation and deduction spanning hundreds, thousands, or even more years—is utterly meaningless.

A true dragon in hiding possesses a body of such immense proportions that even a single opening of its mouth could stir up waves that shake the heavens.

Gu Changge now intends to cast some lively catfish into this pond that has lain dormant for so long. Those catfish will devour the small fish and shrimp within, growing ever larger, until the day comes when they stir the true dragon, forcing it to stir and move.
