Once one’s cultivation reaches a certain level, it becomes extremely difficult to produce offspring—and he was no exception.

In such a state, bearing offspring was even more difficult, especially in the Land Of Truth, where the total number of living beings remained constant.

"Your sister is dead. Both her body and spirit have perished," he said calmly.

“Even though I left her there back then, I granted her immeasurable wealth and glory through the other Settlement Envoys, elevating her to the highest realms. I owe her nothing.”

"Let’s not speak of her anymore."

The Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness wore an expression of indifference; in truth, he could not even recall the name or face of that offspring.

Even beings who have reached the End of the Path in the Dao Realm would find it difficult to survive this ordeal.
