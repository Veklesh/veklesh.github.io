The Black-White Lord’s eyes flickered. Although he coveted this power, he knew full well that it would be difficult for him to seize it on his own.

And the Heavenly Dao Realm mentioned by Yi Tian set his heart ablaze.

Wasn’t the very realm of the Heavenly Dao precisely what he was pursuing now?

"You were born because of the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance. "When you have grown to this stage, that will be the moment the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance reaps you."

The Three-Headed Master’s voice was calm, yet his eyes flickered with terrifying visions: moons shattering, stars sinking, galaxies fading into darkness. Threads— strands of blood seeped ceaselessly from his eyes, a horrifying sight that made it seem as though they might explode at any moment.

The Black-White Master was also shaken and startled by this scene, staring intently at the Three-Headed Master.

“What do you know?”

He knew the Three-Headed Master was currently enduring a massive backlash, for the words he had spoken touched upon terrifying karmic consequences and the very nature of existence.

In the next instant, the phoenix head among the Three-Headed Master’s three heads suddenly exploded with a bang, filling the air with a mist of blood. His other head opened wide, swallowing the blood mist whole to prevent a depletion of his primordial essence, though the aura emanating from his body had inexplicably waned considerably.

The Black-White Lord’s eyes flickered. He knew the Three-Headed Lord had touched upon a subject he should not have, thereby inviting this backlash.

“How could I not be aware of the Heaven Slaying Alliance Master’s open scheme? He has plotted against us in this manner; he will naturally reap what he has sown in due time.”

Yi Tian spoke coldly, though his tone had softened considerably compared to the beginning; clearly, the Three-Headed Master’s words had served as a reminder to him.

Amidst the Vast Expanse of the multiverse, there remained one unpredictable variable: Gu Changge, the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

No one knows what the Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance intends to do next.

"Then may we sit down and discuss our plans and cooperation? With your current strength, even if you were to encounter the other Heavenly Will factions—"

Yi Tian said coldly, “What would a partnership with you bring me?”

Otherwise, it wouldn’t have forced the Heavenly Will into hiding out of fear. Yi Tian gazed coldly at the two of them, then suddenly let out a cold laugh and said, "You actually presume to speculate on that fellow’s strength? Let me tell you, even the complete Heavenly Will fears him. What he is likely considering now is the trouble the Land Of Truth will bring..."

"However, if he insists on defying Heaven Slaying, the Heavenly Will will certainly entangle itself with him at all costs and will not let him succeed. I can tell you with certainty that he may vanish at any moment."

"What he calls 'seclusion' is actually an attempt to supplant the Heavenly Will and rule over the Vast Expanse. His ambitions are beyond imagination. At this point, even Yan Tian seemed overcome by fear, clearly harboring a deep dread of the Realm of Truth.

The Three-Headed Lord and the Black-and-White Lord both fell silent for a moment, then exchanged a glance.

"We can make a pact to help you overthrow the Heavenly Will, but you must reveal to us the missing element within the authority of the Heavenly Dao, so that we may continue on our path."

The Three-Headed Master said in a deep voice.

The Master of Black and White shared this same line of thinking.

Upon hearing this, Yi Tian fell silent, deep in thought as he weighed the pros and cons.

In the vast Expanse of today, the Master of Black and White and the Three-Headed Lord were indeed beings standing at the very pinnacle. It could be said that in an era without the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, their every word and deed would influence the entire situation of the vast Expanse.

If the two were to cooperate with it, each getting what they needed, there should be no problem. From a certain perspective, if the two did not cooperate with it, but instead went to seek out the other “Five Heavens,” that would pose a tremendous threat to it.

The Three-Headed Lord and the Lord of Black and White were in no hurry; they too were waiting for Yitian’s decision.

"I agree to your terms."

Yitian finally made his decision. Relying solely on Su En, it would be difficult to restore it to its full power in a short time.

With the help of the Three-Headed Lord and the Black-and-White Lord, perhaps they could locate the remaining “Five Heavens” sooner and—devour them.

Seeing this, a smile finally appeared on the Three-Headed Lord’s face, and the Black-and-White Lord let out a long sigh of relief.

Both were ancient beings who had survived since the days of Chaos; had the Guixu of the Heaven and Earth Era not come to an end, their lifespans could truly be described as endless.

In that fleeting instant, they had already analyzed and discerned billions of possibilities, realizing that the most advantageous course of action for them at present was to cooperate with Yitian.

Subsequently, the Three-Headed Lord took the lead in swearing an oath using his own cultivation and spiritual resolve. At their level, ordinary oaths to the Heavenly Dao held little binding power; indeed, no oath could truly influence them.

Yitian, of course, understood that this so-called oath was merely a gesture; if their interests diverged, they could turn against each other at any moment, casually breaking their vows.

It didn’t care about that, either.
