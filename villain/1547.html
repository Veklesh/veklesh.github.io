"I never would have thought..."

A deep, furious voice echoed out, causing the entire battlefield to tremble. Countless living beings and cultivators shuddered violently, nearly losing their footing and falling to the ground.

Those with weaker cultivation were on the verge of collapse, exploding into a mist of blood.

Even the Lord of Power and his companions were affected.

The surrounding spacetime collapsed and twisted; the river of time itself grew blurred, constantly evaporating and vanishing—this was an unimaginable, colossal power.

In the Vast Expanse of the present multiverse, one could clearly see the Absolute Yin Grinding Sphere, its power so immense it seemed almost beyond comprehension.

"I never expected to suffer such a crushing defeat here."

"Truly worthy of the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance—even the evil intent of a single deity left behind is this formidable."

The colossal, boundless figure of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness suddenly loomed in the deepest recesses of the Vast Expanse, radiating waves of power filled with endless terror.

He growled, his expression long since stripped of its former detachment; his hair was disheveled, and his robes torn.

Even half of his arm had vanished, blood trickling down; the moment it fell, it vaporized the very Dao itself, a testament to its destructive power.

Opposite him, the malevolent intent of the Lord of Heaven was equally immense, stretching to the very edge of the murky heavens, emitting a chewing sound that made one’s teeth ache and body tremble.

A bloodstained arm, crystalline and flawless as the very foundation of the Dao, was being slowly chewed. The eerie shards of broken bone were occasionally illuminated by streaks of blood-red lightning, making the scene even more spine-chilling.

"Master..."

The four remaining followers of the Heavenly Lord of Utmost Darkness all trembled at this sight, their expressions filled with terror, their scalps nearly splitting open.

This divine being’s malevolent intent was actually devouring the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin’s physical form.

One must understand that this was a truly flawless body, untouched by any impurity; even a being born of primordial essence could not possess such a pure constitution.

In the spacetime of the old cosmic order, this would be a physical form of the Heavenly Dao Realm—immortal and indestructible, impervious to decay even as the universe collapsed and all worlds returned to the Void.

The image of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin—mighty and invincible in their hearts—was now crumbling and dissipating inch by inch.

"How could the malevolent intent of this deity be so terrifying that even the Master is no match for it?"

“Could the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, at the height of his power, have actually surpassed the Heavenly Lord tier?”

"Could it be a Heavenly King-level being who snuck in?"

"Impossible. Once one possesses the Heavenly Seal Summoning Talisman, it is impossible to betray the Nine True Clans, let alone the Three True Ancestors. Moreover, the current status of those possessing the Heavenly Seal Summoning Talisman in the Land Of Truth is crystal clear—not a single one has departed."

"How on earth did the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance come to this..."

The followers of the Supreme Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin felt their scalps prickle and their bodies tremble; this defied all logic.

Could it be that after sneaking over to this side, he acquired a cultivation system independent of the Heavenly Seal—a path unknown to all worlds?

"Master the heavens, master the laws; master the myriad paths of all worlds..."

At that moment, high in the boundless, ethereal heavens, the deep, icy voice of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin suddenly rang out.

Vast and majestic, reaching to the farthest corners of the cosmos, it sounded like the murmurs of primordial ancestral immortals, or the prayers and prostrations of the ancient people offering sacrifices to the heavens.

A strange and mysterious fluctuation suddenly swept in, reflecting off the ancient altar of reckoning at the end of all worlds, causing it to hum.

That strange aura was like a gust of wind sweeping through the Ashen Realm.

All living beings trembled, their soul-fires seeming on the verge of extinguishing.

Endless supreme runes manifested there; past, future, and present—every river of time—rose and fell, taking shape.

Heaven and earth trembled in unison; the Vast Expanse, endless spacetime, and countless universes all seemed to be pierced by a single, indistinct line.

Yue Mingkong, the Lord of Power, the Lord of Command, the Lord of Restraint, and many other supreme beings all sensed a sense of unease at that moment. They furrowed their brows and looked in that direction, wondering what the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness intended to do.

"This..."

"This is..."

Only the retinue of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness suddenly grew agitated, their bodies trembling and faces flushed crimson—not from fear, but from overwhelming excitement, as if they were about to witness something extraordinary with their own eyes.

"Only those summoned by the Heavenly Seal Talisman can wield the Art of Commanding the Heavens—this is the boundless, vast power of commanding the Heavenly Dao..."

They were utterly thrilled.

In the Land Of Truth, such techniques are almost unheard of, for the Land Of Truth lacks the existence of the Heavenly Dao—the True Ancestor is the Heavenly Dao.

Who dares to command the Heavenly Dao?

This Art of Commanding the Heavens could be said to have been created specifically for the Land Of Truth, designed to harness the power of the Heavenly Will on this side.

That the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin had been driven to this point was sufficient proof of the terrifying malice harbored by the deities of the Lord of Heaven.

BOOM!!!

The multiverse trembled; even the phantom of the Ancient Altar of Reckoning twisted for an instant. Above the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness’s head, boundless, dazzling radiance swirled and converged, eventually coalescing into a hazy orb of light that resembled both a symbol of authority and a summoning talisman.

That hazy orb hung there, emitting no ripples or fluctuations, yet it seemed to embody the very essence of all matter across the multiverse, as well as the ultimate end of all nothingness.

No one could discern what it was, nor could anyone describe it.

It was an existence that transcended all concepts—one that would be mistaken the moment it was conceived, and fall into error the moment it was imagined—even even the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness could comprehend.

In the deepest recesses of that hazy orb, an unimaginable will and power seemed to slumber and surge.

Fate, causality, the passage of time, space and time—any concept or substance, in its presence, seemed as fleeting as bubbles in a stream, ready to vanish at the slightest touch.

It represents endless profundity, endless wonder, endless ineffability, and the endless source.

The moment the Lord of Power, Lord of Restraint, and the others cast their gaze upon it, their bodies shook violently. Terrifying cracks suddenly spread across their skin, and blood trickled from the corners of their eyes. In the end, they could only tear their eyes away with great difficulty.

Is this truly the Summoning of the Heavenly Seal Talisman?

They were utterly shocked and horrified. With their cultivation levels, they had nearly met their end just from a single glance?

"In my name, I command the Heavenly Dao. Avatar of Heaven, heed my call."

The voice of the Supreme Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin was deep and resonant, like the tolling of great bronze bells, reverberating throughout the Vast Expanse and echoing across countless realms of time and space.

The hazy light sphere suddenly erupted with dazzling radiance, brilliant and resplendent, its intensity as radiant as the sun itself, radiating out to countless regions.

Endless beams of light pierced through the void, penetrating into the deeper realms of spacetime far beyond. Each ray seemed capable of connecting to a separate super-multiverse.

BOOM!!!

Heaven and earth resonated; the Vast Expanse of all worlds roared in unison, as if the power of the Heavenly Dao were manifesting, and a supreme will began to radiate its influence throughout this realm.

"This is bad..."

The expressions of the Lord of Power and the others suddenly changed in unison.

This vast power surged forth like a erupting volcano, engulfing the entire battlefield.

As the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin finished speaking, a terrifying, massive fissure appeared on the front lines of the battlefield, stretching for who knows how many millions of miles, like a wound that had split the heavens and the earth.

Endless, hazy mist gushed forth from it, forming a vast, boundless expanse with no end in sight.

Vaguely and indistinctly, nine blurry figures slowly emerged from within that massive fissure. Their auras were profound and vast; each was but a step away from reaching the True Path realm, making them nine beings at the pinnacle of the End of the Path Ascension stage.

The Nine Minor Avatars of Heaven.

Apart from Chengtian, who had been born prematurely in the Nine Heavens, the other eight avatars have now appeared: Zhongtian, Xiantian, Congtian, Gengtian, Zuitian, Kuotian, Xiantian, and Shentian.

…Please support with flowers…

This terrifying, unparalleled power surged forth, causing Chen Po, who was observing the battlefield, to tremble with fear.

Any single Minor Avatar of Heaven could be considered an invincible being within the Realm of the End of the Path, and now there were a full nine of them.

With the exception of Shen Tian, the gazes of the remaining eight figures were all bone-chillingly cold and indifferent, devoid of any emotional fluctuation. Among them were former glorious emperors, undefeated kings, peerless empresses, unmatched sword immortals, as well as the Ancestor of Corpses and Fallen Immortals… Without a doubt, their ability to become Celestial Avatars meant they were all once the cream of the crop who had reached the End of the Path—the true protagonists of countless eras.

Each had left behind a legendary legacy etched in the annals of ancient history.

The nine Minor Avatars of Heaven now stood side by side high above the front lines of the Ancient Hidden Civilization, radiating an aura of boundless terror. They had locked onto the Eternal Ancestor, the Yellow Sovereign, the Arrow Sovereign, the Tathagata Buddha Sovereign, the Five Elements Dao Sovereign, the Light and Darkness Divine Sovereign, and the other End-of-the-Path-level beings, gazing at them with icy stares.

They await only a single command from the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness to unleash their fury.

“Do you fear me?”

Eternal Ancestor took a deep breath, showing no fear of that surging, peerless aura. Though he had not yet reached the realm of Ascension at the End of the Path, he was undaunted, having long since cast life and death aside.

………

"I am not afraid."

"If we are to stand, then we shall fight."

The Archer spoke in a deep voice, the first to respond. He drew his bow like a full moon, his arrow pointing straight ahead.

"Great Avatar of Heaven, where are you?"

At the farthest reaches of the vast, dark heavens, the voice of the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Yin had not yet faded; he continued to summon the descent of the Great Avatar of Heaven.

The power of the Minor Avatar of Heaven was already terrifying enough, capable of sweeping through countless super-multiverses across the Vast Expanse.

The power of the Great Avatar of Heaven is even more staggering.

That is the equivalent of no fewer than five beings on par with True Path-level realms. Because they are imbued with the Heavenly Will, their true strength is only slightly greater than that of Direct Path-level beings.

As the Heavenly Lord of Absolute Darkness issued his summons, the Heavenly Seal Talisman erupted with an even more dazzling and brilliant light.

"No..."

On this frontline battlefield, a voice filled with reluctance suddenly rang out.

The Lord of Power, Lord of Restraint, and the others all instinctively flinched as they looked over—the rumors had been confirmed.

The Great Avatar of Heaven was, indeed, a True Path-level figure endowed with the authority of the Heavenly Dao. This authority was not a source of power, but rather a set of shackles used to restrain and limit them; it had become the chains that bound them.

"Have I truly taken the wrong path?"

A faint, bitter self-mockery flickered across the flawless jade-like face of the Master of Fate, who held the authority over destiny.

At that moment, on another battlefield in a different realm, the Lord of the Void, locked in a fierce struggle with the Forgetful Immortal, was thrashing violently. Terrifying veins bulged beneath his skin, as if they were about to burst forth and seize control of his will.

He looked utterly ferocious, filled with disbelief and even greater resentment.

Of course, this Lord of the Void was not the ruler of the five great royal clans of the Ancient Hidden Civilization; he was a True Path-level being who had seized control of the Staff of Time and Space.

The authority of time and space manifested, causing a ten-thousand-foot-tall radiance to rise above his head—utterly sacred, suffused with an aura of supreme power.

Yet beneath that sacred, dazzling surface, root-like tendrils spread out from the authority, piercing through his skull, as if seeking to take root deep within his internal organs.

This scene was so jarringly incongruous and shocking that it sent shivers down the spines of countless beings, their eyes filled with terror.

The Authority of the Heavenly Dao, which had once sparked countless wars between civilizations and doctrinal traditions, had now become the Lord of the Void’s death warrant.

One can only imagine that, having had his will usurped by the authority of the Heavenly Dao, the Lord of the Void could no longer be himself; he would inevitably become nothing more than a puppet, a mere shadow, devoid of any will of his own.
