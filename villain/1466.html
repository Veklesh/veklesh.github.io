"It is the inevitable course of the universe—a sign that the Great Calamity is coming sooner than expected..."

"Indeed, it should be so."

Gu Changge gazed at the youth in the distant horizon, upon whom the nascent aura of a protagonist was beginning to coalesce.

If given time to grow, that youth would surely become a towering figure, stirring up countless storms amidst the Vast Expanse.

This is the natural order of the universe: after reaching its peak, decline inevitably follows.

When it reaches a certain point of decline, a period of renewal will follow, and during this time, many beings destined by the times will emerge.

This holds true for small worlds, vast realms, the multiverse, and even the True Realm.

The Dark Clans have held their Great Dark Ritual, and the number of worlds they have purged is vast; the civilizations they have swept away over the years are by no means few.

Under such circumstances, the fragmented, scattered, and drifting forces of the world and the cosmos, driven by the great cosmic currents, collide and undergo certain transformations.

Thus, they alight upon beings with particularly strong convictions, becoming their destiny—or, one might say, their "golden finger."

Immortal substances and the substances of destiny are, in themselves, the very source from which all matter in these worlds is constructed; they can be called the source of all things.

Back when he was in the Prosperous Dao True Realm, he personally extracted these substances. To ensure the Prosperous Dao True Realm’s fortune would flourish even more, he personally scattered those opportunities.

The vast and boundless Vast Expanse of the present era is precisely in such a period of emergence in response to the coming calamity.

Heaven and Earth have sensed it; a great calamity is approaching. Thus, during this period, numerous figures of great destiny have emerged like a gush of water to confront the great calamity.

Just from Gu Changge’s perspective, within those shattered and depleted universes, numerous beings of extraordinary destiny have emerged in the span of a few short years, each bearing their own unique opportunities.

However, while protagonists are born in every era, those who truly grow to the level of the Ancestral Dao Realm are invariably protagonists of hundreds of eras.

And those who reach the End of the Path Realm are protagonists of countless eras.

Such figures have, throughout the ages, left indelible marks across countless eras.

"What rises to its peak must eventually fall. Soon after the Great Dark Sacrifice, the Epoch Cataclysm will arrive to settle accounts, and I, too, must proceed with my next plan."

Gu Changge withdrew his gaze, no longer paying attention to the current changes in the Vast Expanse.

In the years that followed, while he had Ling Yuling and the others consolidate the Vast Expanse and resist the Dark Clans, he also began to take action.

While stirring the river of fate across the various cosmic realms, he continuously scattered opportunities and transformations into them through immortal and creative substances, deliberately manipulating the destinies of certain beings.

This included those close to him—such as Mo Tong, Chu Xiuyan, Ye Suyi, Ling Huang, the White Bone Ancestral King, Zhuo Fengwei, and others—as well as the many rising young and middle-aged powerhouses emerging within the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

Gu Changge presided over the Heaven Slaying Alliance, his presence commanding awe across the Vast Expanse.

Under his deliberate manipulation, these beings were forced to grow too quickly, developing rapidly within a single cosmic realm. They accumulated a certain measure of destiny, and the embryonic traits of era-defining protagonists began to emerge upon them.

Meanwhile, Ling Yuling, through a process of integration, absorbed the various civilizations—both large and small—from the surrounding cosmic space, causing the Heaven Slaying Alliance to grow ever stronger.

At this pace, they resembled a whale with its massive jaws wide open, constantly roaming the deep sea and swallowing up the various realms, large and small.

To confront this great calamity of the Black Disaster, the leaders of various True Realm civilizations—many of whom possessed cultivation levels sufficient to survive seven Celestial Waning Tribulations—held continuous discussions. Finally, they all gathered in the central hall of the Heaven Slaying Alliance to seek an audience with Gu Changge.

They had already noticed that the Dark Clans, in order to summon the source of darkness, had dispatched four High Priests, accompanied by a single, most magnificent and colossal altar.

Four smaller yet equally magnificent altars were arranged around the perimeter. Such a spectacle was unprecedented in history; it had never even been recorded in ancient texts.

Originally, it would have taken hundreds of years for the Source of Darkness to reach the Dark Clan’s stronghold.

But now, the Dark Clan has clearly grown impatient and refuses to wait any longer. They are employing every possible means to accelerate the descent of the Source of Darkness toward the Vast Expanse.

Many high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance were also gripped by a sense of gravity and unease. They did not know Gu Changge’s intentions, but it was clear that she did not seem to want to engage in a bloody battle with the Dark Clan, nor had she taken the initiative to intervene.

As Deputy Alliance Leader, Ling Yuling intended to issue an order for the civilizations of the True Realms to unite their forces and resist, but those Ancestral Dao Realm beings who had survived eight Celestial Waning Tribulations were unlikely to heed her commands.

Especially now that Gu Changge was stationed within the Heaven Slaying Alliance, it seemed to give those powerful beings a newfound sense of confidence.

The Dark Clans’ prolonged inaction against the Heaven Slaying Alliance, in their view, was precisely the source of their hesitation…

Perhaps this Cataclysm of Darkness will not be as devastating as the world imagines. The Vast Expanse is so vast—what harm is there in setting aside a territory for the Dark Clans?

As long as they do not interfere with one another—each minding their own business—it is not entirely unacceptable.

There were actually quite a few powerful figures who held this view, including those from the Forbidden Zone—Mu Fuguang, the Compassionate Monk, and Tong Xian.

As for what Gu Changge truly thinks, that is not something they can fathom.

But one might surmise that the reason he chose the name “Heaven Slaying” was hardly to establish the Heaven Slaying Alliance as a counterweight to the Dark Clans.

Within the grand hall, an overwhelming aura permeated the air as vague figures materialized and approached. Around them, time and space seemed to shift and transform.

Had Gu Changge not been present within the Heaven Slaying Alliance, suppressing all those auras,

Otherwise, with so many Ancestral Dao Realm beings gathered in one place, the mere chaos and collision of their auras would be enough to disrupt time and space, causing this place to collapse.

As the deputy leader, even Ling Yuling had no idea what Gu Changge had been doing during this time. The courtyard where he had been in seclusion remained utterly quiet, with not a single sound emanating from it.

However, she was busy handling the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s numerous affairs and rarely consulted Gu Changge on matters.

Now, Gu Changge had adopted the attitude of a hands-off leader, essentially leaving all matters of the Heaven Slaying Alliance entirely to her.

Left with no choice, Ling Yuling could only select suitable personnel—such as Ling Huang and others who had followed Gu Changge from the very beginning—to assist her in handling these matters.

Today, the vast Heaven Slaying Alliance could be called the most powerful force in the Vast Expanse—there was simply no other. The mountain of daily affairs was a constant headache for her.

“I’ve already gone to fetch the Alliance Master; he’ll be here shortly,” Ling Yuling said.

The grand hall was vast beyond measure, yet at this moment it was teeming with people—a truly astonishing sight. The gathering of so many Ancestral Dao Realm beings was an event of unprecedented rarity, one that would be almost impossible to witness again.

Among them were no shortage of beings whose strength had surpassed the seventh Celestial Waning Tribulation and had already survived the eighth.

Among them were figures such as Mu Fuguang, the Demon Ancestor Di Huang, and Ji Hanjing—the current ruler of the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty—as well as the most powerful beings from various reclusive clans who had emerged in recent times. Counting them all, there were over a dozen beings who had survived eight Celestial Waning Tribulations.

Furthermore, there are figures like the Ethereal Holy Ancestor, who has already survived nine Celestial Waning Tribulations…

Not to mention beings even more powerful than the Piao Miao Holy Ancestor, such as the Luo Ancestor and the Eternal Primordial God. Even though Ling Yuling has nearly recovered to her peak strength, she still feels considerable pressure when facing these two.

She surmised that these two must be true "End of the Path" level figures; while the Eternal Primordial God might be slightly weaker, his power still surpassed that of the Ethereal Holy Ancestor.

A single being at the Ancestral Dao Realm is more than qualified to preside over a realm of supreme power.

For the current Vast Expanse to have gathered such power was, in fact, not entirely unreasonable. Yet Ling Yuling could clearly see that the underlying strength of the Vast Expanse in this era was far inferior to what it had once been.

"To stop the Black Calamity, the best course of action now is to seek out the six treasures of the First Civilization..."

"Following the example of the sages of old, we must once again seal the source of the Black Calamity."

Gu Changge’s figure soon appeared in the grand hall. Facing the bows of the Heaven Slaying Alliance members, he merely waved his hand casually.

"If we cannot gather all six treasures of the First Civilization, then engaging in a bloody battle with the Dark Tribes is not a viable solution."

"I have no intention of wasting my strength here. The Epoch Cataclysm will soon descend once more; by comparison, the Black Calamity is hardly worth worrying about."

Gu Changge spoke succinctly, outlining his intentions. The momentum of the Vast Expanse of the cosmos was now unstoppable.

Why should he go through the trouble of staging a charade and needlessly squandering this power? Whether it was the Heaven Slaying Alliance or the Dark Clans, they were all merely his pawns and sources of strength.

Why should he engage in such a scheme—letting two pawns slaughter and exhaust each other? It offered him no benefit at this stage.

Since the conclusion of the Immortal Dao Alliance Assembly, he had remained in the Ethereal Sanctuary, joining the others in “fighting” the Black Lotus Sect. He had believed this approach would force the big fish that had been hiding all along to reveal their true colors.

But he had underestimated those bastards’ vigilance. In the end, only the Lord of Destiny fell into the net—and he naturally had no interest in keeping such a small fry.

As for the Ancestor Xu, Gu Changge had always been aware of that fellow’s ambition and schemes. Back when he was still the Ruler of Heaven, the Ancestor Xu had been determined to uncover his true origins.

The chaos among the Heavenly Host was also orchestrated by Shizu Xu...

Nevertheless, Gu Changge still had no intention of taking action against him. The current Shizu Xu was, in fact, unaware that he had already, unconsciously, become Gu Changge’s bait.

Moreover, this bait of his was still alive. As for whether it would reel in anything, Gu Changge could only wait and see.

Given the current state of affairs in the Vast Expanse, no faction or lineage remains capable of restraining or counterbalancing the Heaven Slaying Alliance. As for the existence of the Dark Clans, they can be completely disregarded—after all, they were his own creation.

The unification of the Vast Expanse was now a fait accompli, yet those who had been hiding showed no signs of emerging or making their move.

If it were just one person, it might be a coincidence, but with nearly all the major players refusing to show their faces, there is only one possible explanation.

In today’s Vast Expanse, they no longer see any reason to risk their own fates. Whatever happens to the Vast Expanse is of no concern to them.

Standing outside the Cataclysm, immune to its corruption, even if the Vast Expanse were to be purged, it would eventually enter a period of recovery. Those individuals have seen through the true nature of the Cataclysm; naturally, they are unwilling to be mere fodder, left to be slaughtered at will.

And as for the trivial Black Calamity—why would they even bother with such trifles?

Whether it be the Dark Clans or the Heaven Slaying Alliance, neither can harm their interests—so why should they even bother to step in?

That is the state of affairs today.

If Gu Changge weren’t concerned that revealing his true identity now would shatter the hard-won faith and unity of the Heaven Slaying Alliance—and even turn the many powerful figures before him, including Ling Yuling, against him—forcing him to spend time and energy cleaning up the mess all over again,

In fact, he could very well consider merging the Heaven Slaying Alliance with the Dark Clans.

"The current state of the Vast Expanse is far weaker than before. Even if we combined the civilizations of all the True Realms, we would still fall far short of the era when we fought against the Black Calamity. With our current strength, it will likely be very difficult to stop the Dark Clans."

"The Alliance Leader speaks the truth. Our current strategy must be to locate the six treasures of the First Civilization and once again seal the source of the Darkness. By maintaining this seal, we can gradually erode its power until the day comes when it is completely annihilated..."

The many powerful figures in the grand hall fell into deep contemplation and discussion.

Many were now waiting for Gu Changge to take a stance; after all, such matters required a guiding force, and at present, only Gu Changge could truly command the respect of everyone.

"As long as the scourge of the Dark Clans remains, the Vast Expanse will never know peace."

"So many civilizations have already been destroyed by the minions of the Dark Clan, and every day, countless cosmic realms are still being invaded and sacrificed. Even if we currently coexist peacefully with the Dark Clan, without interfering with one another, in the long run, once the Dark Clan regains its strength, they will undoubtedly target us first."

"Alliance Leader, we believe it is necessary to take action against the Dark Tribes. There is no need to delay any longer. None of us fear death."

Many were equally resolute in their resolve. The homelands of countless people behind them had already fallen, transformed into the Dark Realm, and they harbored a deep-seated hatred for the Dark Clan.

“Alliance Leader, the fact that the Dark Clans have yet to engage us in battle suggests they fear our strength.”

"Why not first feign peaceful coexistence with the Dark Clans? We can demarcate our territories and impose restrictions on their actions, preventing them from rampaging, slaughtering, and arbitrarily sacrificing the great realms. Meanwhile, we can secretly search for the treasures of the First Civilization to prepare for the opportunity to strike back..."

Others offered their views: regardless of the circumstances, there were only a few options when facing the Dark Clan. Either fight to the death, refusing to yield until one side is destroyed; or coexist peacefully; or feign coexistence while secretly devising a countermeasure.

Lord Luo and the Eternal Primordial God remained silent, listening to the voices of debate and deliberation echoing through the grand hall. Their hearts were filled with the most complex of emotions.

If these people knew that the Dark Clan they had long sought to vanquish was actually created by Gu Changge standing right before them, just how astonished would their expressions be?

How could they possibly discuss, right in front of Gu Changge, how to seal him away and deal with him in the future?

"Although I am the Alliance Leader, regarding this matter, I will follow your opinions."

"I give you three days. After three days, everyone shall come to this hall to vote, and the final decision will be made by the majority." With just a casual glance, Gu Changge knew the thoughts of the vast majority of those in the hall.

He also demonstrated his wise and democratic side, choosing not to make a decision on his own but instead allowing them to make their own choice.

In a situation like this, any clear-headed person would know how to choose the option that minimizes losses.

The Heaven Slaying Alliance was currently the most stable and peaceful place in the Vast Expanse. The various races and realms that had migrated here were all able to develop in peace. If a true, all-out war were to break out against the Dark Clans, who would bear the cost of the losses?

Human nature has always been self-serving; not everyone is capable of true selflessness and the selfless benefit of all living beings.

Three days later, the results of the vote were in. Nearly 90% of the Ancestral Dao Realm beings chose the third option: to feign coexistence with the Dark Clans while secretly searching for the treasures of the First Civilization.

Civilizations across the True Realms also began to focus all their efforts on gathering and investigating relevant clues, analyzing various causal relationships, and tracking down their whereabouts.

Of the six treasures of the First Civilization, the Gate of Eternal Life was in Gu Changge’s possession.

The Mirror of Reincarnation was in the hands of the Holy Maiden Xi Yuan; the Disk of Transcending Calamity was in the hands of the Master of Destiny; the Coffin of World’s Demise was with the Heavenly Host; the Book of the Great Dao was missing; and the Pool of Creation was on its way back to the Heaven Slaying Alliance…

Within Gu Changge’s perception, he could clearly discern the current whereabouts of the Pool of Creation.

The pawn he had casually left behind at the time—that youth from the Floating Continent named Zhu Xuan—carried the bloodline of the Lord of the Pan Clan.

He has now located the Pool of Creation, brought it back, and is currently on his way back to the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

As the Lord of the Pan Clan—once the Eastern Emperor who fought against the Black Calamity—his people were annihilated by the Heavenly Hosts, leaving only this single survivor. Indeed, he possessed clues leading to the Pool of Creation.

After all these years, Zhu Xuan has finally retrieved it. Although the journey was far from smooth—he encountered numerous obstacles—he was ultimately blessed with great fortune, allowing him to overcome every peril without serious harm.
