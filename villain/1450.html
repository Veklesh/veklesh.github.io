A brief, eerie silence fell over Piao Miao Peak.

All the high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance froze in place, as if frozen solid, not daring to move a muscle.

As the deputy leader, Zhao Tianqi took several deep breaths, forcing himself to calm down, but the hand trembling behind his back spoke volumes about the turmoil within him.

Gu Changge’s personal arrival was something no one had ever dared to imagine.

Not even the various civilizations of the True Realm had anticipated it; until now, they had only speculated about which high-ranking member the Heaven Slaying Alliance might send.

One could only imagine the terrifying ripples this news would send through the Vast Expanse once it spread.

As the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, every move Gu Changge made drew the attention of countless beings and lineages across the Vast Expanse.

Never mind his personal appearance at the founding ceremony of the Immortal Dao Alliance; even his mere appearance anywhere within the Heaven Slaying Alliance would spark endless speculation and wild imaginings.

For a single thought or action of his could lead to unforeseeable consequences.

Wasn’t the outbreak of this war between the Righteous Unity Alliance and the Heaven Slaying Alliance—a conflict that engulfed the entire Vast Expanse—simply the result of a single command from the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s leader?

"It is truly an honor for us that the Alliance Master has graced Piao Miao Peak with his presence."

Surrounded by the elders and ancient figures of the Piao Miao Holy Sect, the Piao Miao Holy Ancestor walked forward with a benevolent smile on his face and bowed his hands in greeting.

Though he was of ancient lineage and possessed unfathomable power, he dared not act presumptuously in the presence of Gu Changge.

The Sacred Ancestor of Piaomiao’s gaze swept over the Lord of Luo and the Eternal Primordial God standing behind Gu Changge, and his heart trembled slightly.

If Gu Changge standing before him was like a hazy void, its true nature and depth impossible to fathom, making one dare not even entertain the thought of probing it,

But that man in silver robes filled him with apprehension; this was a powerhouse whose strength was by no means inferior to his own.

“It is hardly an honor. The establishment of the Immortal Dao Alliance concerns the very survival of all beings in the Vast Expanse; how could I possibly refuse to attend?” Gu Changge smiled faintly, paying no heed to the members of the Righteous Unity Alliance, treating them as though they were mere air.

His gaze lingered briefly on a single figure among them, then, led by the Ethereal Sage Ancestor, he proceeded deeper into the hall.

As for the other distinguished guests present, he didn’t even bother to glance at them, as if he didn’t care about their existence at all.

Yet his attitude did not arouse the slightest hint of resentment among the crowd; in fact, everyone felt that this was only to be expected.

After all, this was the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance—a figure who, in the Vast Expanse of the present age, truly stood at the very pinnacle. At least for now, there was no one who could be considered his equal.

If he were to take notice of the people gathered here, they would be both flattered and astonished, finding it simply unbelievable.

The high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance now wore expressions that were both grim and humiliated.

Yet, as Gu Changge led the members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance past them, they could only bow their heads in humiliation, not even daring to breathe a single breath.

They had no doubt that if they dared to show even the slightest hint of discontent, the Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance would not hesitate to crush them right there, regardless of the occasion.

Zhao Tianqi stood there with a face as still as a deep well, appearing calm on the surface, yet every muscle in his body was taut. In the split second Gu Changge’s gaze swept over him, he felt as though every secret within him had been laid bare.

A chilling thought even crossed his mind: if Gu Changge wanted to kill him, would it truly take nothing more than a single thought?

He had once believed that having survived eight Celestial Waning Tribulations would grant him a view that towered above the Vast Expanse of the world. Yet the higher he climbed, the more he realized his former ignorance. Truly powerful beings simply dared not reveal themselves.

It was beings like Gu Changge, the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance—who scorned the Way of Heaven, trampled on the rules, and acted without restraint—who were the most terrifying.

"How could that be..."

Behind Zhao Tianqi, the Deputy Leader of the Righteous Unity Alliance, Zhao Xiuyan’s slender form trembled. Her eyes, usually cold and emotionless, now brimmed with a thousand surging emotions.

Her entire body trembled slightly, and her heart was stirred by boundless turmoil.

Her red lips parted, and she almost called out the name she had always kept in her heart, but at the last moment, she forced herself to hold back.

She knew that Gu Changge was him, and that the leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance was also him.

Although she had only caught that glance just now and hadn’t even been able to make out his true features, how could she possibly forget those gentle eyes that had accompanied her through her darkest, most desperate times…

"Master..." Zhao Xiuyan murmured to herself, then spotted the familiar silver-haired girl among the crowd accompanying the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

A faint smile touched her lips. So this was why she had sensed back then that the two of them would meet again sooner or later.

"Is it her?" Mo Tong also noticed Zhao Xiuyan’s gaze. His indifferent eyes flickered slightly, and across the distance, he gave her a faint nod.

Gu Changge’s arrival had made the situation at the Immortal Dao Alliance’s founding ceremony unpredictable and inscrutable. The various sects and factions, who had previously been chatting casually and speculating on the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s intentions, all fell silent, fearing that loose lips could lead to trouble.

Amidst the various peaks and pavilions, the clamor and heated discussions gradually subsided, and the atmosphere grew solemn.

Within the vast, boundless hall, ancient figures appeared one after another, taking their seats at the various tables.

The leaders from all factions, who had arrived long ago, were fully prepared to sign this Immortal Dao Alliance Pact.

Gu Changge, accompanied by the members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, took their seats in the viewing gallery. The Ethereal Holy Ancestor, as the driving force behind this Immortal Dao Alliance Pact, had originally intended not to make an appearance, but due to Gu Changge’s presence, he was compelled to step forward and preside over the assembly.

The high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance, however, remained notably silent; word of Gu Changge’s arrival had already been relayed back to the Righteous Unity Alliance.

The same was true for the other Daoist lineages; as soon as Gu Changge arrived at the Immortal Dao Alliance assembly, word had already been sent back.

Many sensed that something momentous was likely to unfold today. At such a critical juncture, Gu Changge had actually left the Heaven Slaying Alliance so easily.

Didn’t he fear that the Dark Clans might take advantage of the situation?

Yet no one dared to ask such questions outright; even the many Ancestral Dao Realm experts present remained extremely cautious.

“Why are you all so tense? I have never indiscriminately killed the innocent. Why do you all look at me with such apprehension? Or is it that you are dissatisfied with my Heaven Slaying Alliance?”

"Since its inception, the Heaven Slaying Alliance has made it our mission to uphold the world and aid all living beings. We have stood against the Dark Clans for many years, rescuing countless civilizations and realms from the brink of destruction—a feat far beyond the reach of the Righteous Unity Alliance, whose members seek only to deceive the world and steal glory."

"You all don’t seem concerned that the Righteous Unity Alliance might be scheming behind the scenes, yet you treat my Heaven Slaying Alliance with such wariness and caution. It’s a bit disheartening, to say the least."

Gu Changge’s soft chuckle rang out, and the previously solemn atmosphere fell into a sudden, profound silence.

The mightiest beings from civilizations across the True Realms dared not meet his gaze; as his eyes swept over them, they all instinctively lowered their heads.

Even more sentient beings and cultivators hurriedly lowered their heads, their hearts trembling.

The high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance watched this scene, their faces simultaneously breaking into strange smiles—some gloating, others brimming with scorn. When they turned their gaze toward the members of the Righteous Unity Alliance, their expressions grew even more pronounced.

Only the members of the Righteous Unity Alliance remained as stoic as stone, yet not a single one dared to speak up in rebuttal. Earlier, numerous leaders from the Daoist lineages and the True Realm had approached to greet them, but with the arrival of the Heaven Slaying Alliance—and especially after Gu Changge’s appearance—everyone had kept their distance.

Even the numerous elders of the Piao Miao Holy Sect were no exception; when speaking to the members of the Righteous Unity Alliance, they had to choose their words carefully, for fear of saying something wrong and causing a misunderstanding.

“The Alliance Master jests. The occasion is indeed solemn, and all the guests hold the Alliance Master in the highest esteem; naturally, they would not dare to act in any disrespectful manner.”

"It is common knowledge that the Alliance Master considers the greater good and cares for all under heaven; how could such a reputation be tarnished by mere rumors?"

The Holy Ancestor Piao Miao appeared as a refined middle-aged man, but even he felt a chill run down his spine at that moment. His tone was laced with bitter laughter as he forced a smile, steeled his resolve, and spoke against his better judgment.

Rumors from the outside world described the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance as ruthless, inscrutable, and unpredictable—a figure who was simply impossible to deal with.

Now he had truly experienced it firsthand. Who would expect a leader of such stature to personally step into the fray to intimidate the civilizations of the True Realm?

Take the Alliance Master of the Righteous Unity Alliance, Emperor Wei Yang—would he ever personally step into the fray?

This had shattered his long-held image of aloof detachment in a single day. Yet if he did not make an appearance, who knew what form the Immortal Dao Alliance Assembly might take.

“In that case, why are you all still hiding behind masks, afraid to reveal your true forms? Must I personally invite you?”

Gu Changge smiled faintly as his gaze swept across the crowd. The void ahead suddenly groaned with a muffled thud. A terrifying, torrential power flashed through it, followed by several blurred figures, blood trickling from the corners of their mouths, plummeting down from within.

Everyone present was filled with horror and trembled. Representatives from several major factions were so overcome with dread that they dared not step forward to assist their own ancestral elders.

"We implore the Alliance Leader to show mercy..."

Seeing this scene, the Ethereal Holy Ancestor felt a chill run down his spine.

Although he knew that many guests in the void had yet to reveal themselves, their auras faintly detectable, he paid it no mind.

After all, some figures simply preferred to remain hidden, and given the motley crowd gathered here—where even a sinister cult like the Black Lotus Sect had infiltrated—it was impossible to rule out the presence of old enemies.

Concealing one’s true form and identity was, in fact, understandable.

But…

“I have no fondness for those who hide their heads and reveal their tails,” Gu Changge said indifferently.

The Ethereal Holy Ancestor was momentarily at a loss for words, forced to offer nothing but a bitter smile. He dared not say anything further, though a wave of frustration welled up within him.

A figure of his stature, who had dominated the Vast Expanse for countless eons, had never found himself in a situation like this—forced to stand by and smile meekly.

The elders of the Piao Miao Holy Sect could do nothing but suppress the myriad feelings of frustration and helplessness welling up within them.

They were clearly on the territory of the Piao Miao Holy Realm; they were clearly the masters here. Yet with Gu Changge’s arrival, everything had changed…

Soon, the grand hall was shrouded in mist, with a chaotic light permeating the air. One meditation cushion after another appeared, each bearing a figure seated in lotus position—all of them ancient, weathered figures representing the true pillars of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm over the years.

"We pay our respects to the Alliance Leader."

In the remaining seats, the figures representing other lineages and civilizations—those who had been suppressing the realm’s foundations—were also compelled to reveal themselves. They stepped out of the void and took their seats there obediently. All were ancient beings of immense age, possessing terrifying cultivation and power.

Only now did many clans and factions realize that Piao Miao Peak had actually gathered so many supremely powerful beings all along; they had simply been hiding in the shadows. It seemed they were biding their time, waiting for the right moment. If the situation turned sour, they would likely be the first to evacuate with all their people.

Many cursed inwardly: this was supposed to be nothing more than a simple alliance, yet it was rife with so many schemes and calculations.

Yet Gu Changge’s arrival forced these old foxes to reveal themselves. They sat there like fish out of water, cold sweat beading on their foreheads.

Many disciples of the Piao Miao Holy Sect then brought in all manner of delicacies and fine foods to prepare for the commencement of the Immortal Dao Alliance.

Meanwhile, news from the Piao Miao Sacred Realm sent ripples of turmoil throughout the outside world.

Gu Changge’s arrival had truly taken all civilizations of the Vast Expanse by surprise. No one knew his true intentions—whether he genuinely intended to support the Immortal Dao Alliance in opposing the Dark Clans, or if he had some other agenda.

At the same time, some realms of civilization noticed that the massive armies originally stationed in the cosmos surrounding the Heaven Slaying Alliance had seemingly vanished, their whereabouts for the time being unknown.

Meanwhile, over at the Weiyang Immortal Dynasty, a cataclysmic upheaval has been unfolding in recent days.
