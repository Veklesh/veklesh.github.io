In the void, black flames coiled around the chains, like blooming flowers of death taking root and spreading from the darkness, relentlessly dragging Ling Yuling toward that blurred gateway.

After her initial shock, she gradually calmed down, ceasing her struggle. She allowed the black chains to bind her limbs and let the black flames engulf and swallow her whole.

Around the hazy portal, boundless killing intent surged forth; strands of destructive energy converged and collided, as if the heavens themselves were about to annihilate all living beings, reorganize earth, water, wind, and fire, and reshape the universe.

The Fourth Killing Array of Antiquity—a terrifying formation that, whenever it appeared throughout ancient history, brought boundless bloodshed and endless carnage.

It slays immortals and gods; those who hear of it turn pale, and those who speak of it tremble.

From the very beginning, Ling Yuling sensed that the aura of this mysterious figure in white robes felt somewhat familiar; it was likely someone she knew.

She tried to piece together connections in her mind, but to no avail.

But with the emergence of the Fourth Ancient Killing Formation, she was finally able to confirm the connection between this mysterious white-robed figure before her and someone she knew.

"I never would have expected this."

Ling Yuling let out a soft sigh, unable to hide her surprise. However, her sigh was quickly drowned out by the endless black flames, and her figure was gradually swallowed by the darkness, vanishing into that blurred portal.

The white-robed figure watched all this from afar, his expression unchanged.

"Even the Fourth Killing Array would struggle to kill her in the short term. At most, it would banish her into the void of endless spacetime, obscure her coordinates, and delay her return."

He shook his head slightly, paying it no further mind, and as he took a step, ripples of spacetime spread out, carrying him away from this secluded realm.

Meanwhile, on the boundless battlefield where the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance were engaged in the "030" battle, the scene fell suddenly silent as the white-robed figure reappeared.

Nearly every cultivator and living being turned their gaze toward him—whether Dao Realm beings or ordinary creatures, none were exempt.

The many high-ranking powerhouses of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, who had previously been brimming with high morale and a fervent fighting spirit, suddenly sensed that something was amiss.

Many of them paled slightly, then furrowed their brows—they could no longer sense the presence of Deputy Alliance Leader Ling Yuling.

“What’s going on?”

“Why has the Deputy Alliance Leader’s aura vanished?”

"Could it be..."

Even figures like Zhuo Fengwei and Hun Yuanjun began to look uneasy. They were well aware of Ling Yuling’s power; aside from Gu Changge, there were few within the Heaven Slaying Alliance today who could reliably defeat her.

Not even those beings from the Forbidden Zone were an exception.

In this vast expanse, how many others could pose a threat to her life?

"Something’s wrong. The Sage’s aura has vanished. Given her strength, could it be that even she is no match for this mysterious man in white?"

Mu Fuguang furrowed his brow; his gaze, which had previously been laced with disdain and casualness, grew grave in that instant.

The other beings from the Forbidden Zone beside her were no different; their gazes were fixed intently on the white-robed figure, trying to discern what means he had used to make Ling Yuling vanish.

"Senior Yu Ling is immensely powerful. Even if she were outmatched, she wouldn’t have perished so easily. I suspect she was ambushed and trapped in a void beyond this world," Chu Xiao said, his eyes blazing with sword-like intensity and surging with terrifying killing intent.

His words sent a chill through the hearts of Zhuo Fengwei and the others.

It now seemed highly likely that Ling Yuling had indeed met with misfortune.

At the critical moment when the two armies were locked in fierce combat, Ling Yuling—the linchpin of their command—had suddenly vanished without a trace during her clash with that mysterious figure in white.

This was undoubtedly a severe blow to the troops’ morale and resolve.

At that moment, it wasn’t just the high-ranking officials and powerful figures of the Heaven Slaying Alliance who noticed something amiss; many ordinary cultivators and sentient beings took notice as well.

After clashing with that mysterious figure in white, the venerable Ling Yuling had actually vanished and never reappeared.

What did this mean?

Many within the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s army were startled, and panic soon set in. Could it be that the Deputy Alliance Leader had met with an accident? Had she perished at the hands of that mysterious figure in white?

In other cosmic realms, the many Dao Realm beings currently engaged in battle with the Strong Alliance’s powerhouses also felt their hearts grow uneasy.

"Hahaha, as expected of the Elder. Once he strikes, there can be no surprises. Even the Deputy Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance—a former Sage—is no exception. He is still no match for the Elder and will likely not return."

Over at the Righteous Unity Alliance, many powerful figures had also noticed that Ling Yuling’s aura had vanished; she had not returned from the realm beyond the world alongside the white-robed figure.

Many of the powerful figures burst into laughter, feeling an immense sense of relief. Their voices echoed throughout the cosmos, sweeping away all the pent-up frustration they had felt before.

In their battle against the Heaven Slaying Alliance, the Righteous Unity Alliance had met with failure at every turn, suffering defeat after defeat until they were utterly routed.

This had left them seething with pent-up frustration and a deep sense of resentment.

In their eyes, Ling Yuling’s disappearance was the pivotal turning point that would reverse the tide of this battle.

"What a surprise. Who exactly is this mysterious figure in white? Even the venerable elders suffered a major setback at his hands—it was truly unexpected."

"Could it be that this battle between the Heaven Slaying Alliance and the Righteous Unity Alliance will take an unexpected turn and see a reversal?"

Across the vast expanse, many ancient beings watching this battle were equally startled and shaken.

Though they could not witness the fierce battle between Ling Yuling and that mysterious figure in white, they could surmise what had ultimately transpired.

If Ling Yuling had perished, there would have been far more commotion than this. Therefore, it was highly likely that the mysterious figure in white had employed some means to trap her within a void of time and space in a realm beyond the mortal world.

Such a tactic is the most troublesome and intractable, for in that void beyond the world—where there is no law, no order, and no

restrictions. Once one loses their coordinates, it means cultivators find it nearly impossible to return through normal means.

Even if one were determined to guide them back, they would first need to know exactly which void realm they were in.

But the cosmos is vast and boundless, with endless realms and countless universes. Nameless, lawless void realms are innumerable, making any attempt to guide her back akin to searching for a needle in a haystack.

"The Deputy Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance has already fallen at the hands of our senior. Everyone, follow me and charge! The Heaven Slaying Alliance is no longer a threat."

"Kill!"

The endless roar of battle once again echoed across the cosmos. The morale of the Righteous Unity Alliance soared; they were brimming with killing intent, no longer timid and hesitant as before, but rather as if pumped up on adrenaline, fearless.

The many powerful figures of the Righteous Unity Alliance were also greatly inspired. Leading the charge, they rushed forward, and a battle even more intense than before erupted once more.

"Senior Yu Ling has merely run into some trouble; she will break free soon. We need not worry."

"A mere bunch of vermin dare to act so arrogantly. Even without Senior Yu Ling here to hold the line, we can still sweep them all aside."

Chu Xiao’s eyes were icy as he bellowed, reigniting the fighting spirit of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s army, whose morale had begun to wane.

He charged forward first, his battle armor gleaming as his blood-red longsword slashed down. A streak of blade light, as vast as a starry river, swept through the cosmos, crisscrossing time and space with unparalleled might.

The remaining powerhouses of the Heaven Slaying Alliance also sprang into action without hesitation. In an instant, millions of chaotic sword beams collided across the cosmos, sending blood spattering and bones flying.

"Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, are you still unwilling to intervene? Do you intend to let all these powerful warriors perish here?

"However, I’ll push you one more time to see if you’ll step in. I refuse to believe you haven’t been watching this battle."

The mysterious figure in white robes stood calmly amidst the cosmos, silently observing the carnage.

As he spoke softly, a dazzling streak of light shot out from beneath his wide robes—it was an array diagram. An ancient, vast aura surged from it, unfolding like a scroll of the heavens and earth, as if to seal and subdue the cosmos.

The formation diagram shot rapidly toward the heights of the cosmos, expanding boundlessly in an instant. There, it rotated slowly, shrouding everything.

A haze of chaos shrouded the scene in utter obscurity, as if veiling all of spacetime. Within it, the laws of the Dao permeated the air, and a terrifying aura of murderous intent coalesced.

Every cultivator and living being engaged in the battle couldn’t help but shudder, their bodies growing cold as terror and horror filled their eyes.

"Could this be the legendary Killing Array Diagram?"

The Dao Realm beings currently locked in combat all felt a chill run down their spines, as if enveloped by a terrifying aura of murderous intent. Some glanced in that direction, their expressions contorting in disbelief.

In the vast cosmos, the figure in white robes did not stop after summoning the formation diagram. Instead, with a flick of his finger, blinding sword light emerged from the void. Simultaneously, shadowy forms coalesced behind him—one divine sword after another, all incredibly hazy. There were four in total, embedded in a mountain of rock where rays of light intertwined.

"Emerge!"

The white-robed figure barked the command, his voice cold and his eyes devoid of mercy.

The four divine swords clanged as they burst forth from the rock. The moment they appeared, the entire cosmos seemed to be filled with the sound of countless swords singing.

The four swords cut through the air, their sword intent piercing to the bone. All living beings and cultivators felt as though their bodies were about to explode, their very souls on the verge of annihilation under this overwhelming sword intent.

For a moment, countless phantom images of divine swords roared and crisscrossed the heavens and the cosmos—dense, endless, as if cleaving through time and space, or emerging from the void. This was the true intent of world-destruction, capable of slaying all immortals and gods.

It was only the Fourth Killing Array, aided by the Killing Array Diagram and the Four Swords of Immortal Slaughter, that possessed such divine might feared by both gods and demons. When he confronted Ling Yuling, he had merely drawn upon a sliver of that power.

"Just who is this fellow? How did this formation diagram even end up in his hands?" Among the vast army of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, Mu Fuguang and the other beings from the Forbidden Zone all saw their expressions shift slightly.

Many among them possessed ancient lineages and extraordinary insight; they immediately recognized the mysterious man in white’s techniques.

This was none other than the legendary Fourth Killing Array of Ancient Times, said to possess the power to slay immortals and annihilate gods.

"Moreover, it is the complete Fourth Killing Array, supported by the Four Slaying Swords and the array diagram."

"This won’t be easy. It seems only the Alliance Master can personally intervene to deal with this fellow." Tong Xian and the others also furrowed their brows; they did not wish to prolong the battle, for even they would be suppressed under the coverage of the Fourth Killing Array.

It is said that the origins of the Ancient Fourth Killing Formation can be traced back to the Primordial Era before the birth of the Vast Expanse, and it has appeared in numerous eras since the Vast Expanse came into being.

Each time, it unleashed endless bloodshed and carnage; countless powerful figures have perished within the Fourth Killing Array.

The last time the Fourth Killing Formation appeared was during the Black Calamity, when it was controlled by a certain sage of the past.

They were certain that once this killing formation took shape, not only the vast army before them, but even those at the Ancestral Dao Realm would perish, pierced and slaughtered right here.

"Let’s retreat. This mysterious man in white has a very strange background. Even if we were to intervene, we might not be able to break this formation—there’s a high chance of unforeseen complications. Moreover, within the Righteous Unity Alliance, there are many hidden forces that haven’t yet made their move, watching us with bated breath…"

"Unless the Alliance Master intervenes personally—though who knows if he’s even paying attention to this battle right now." Tong Xian sighed, a touch of helplessness in his voice.

When had they, beings from the Forbidden Zone, ever felt so stifled? Yet ever since emerging into the world, they had suffered one blow after another.

This vast expanse of water is truly terrifying.

Upon hearing the telepathic messages from Tong Xian and the others, Zhuo Fengwei, Hun Yuanjun, Chu Xiao, and the others grew solemn, taking the matter extremely seriously.

With Ling Yuling’s disappearance, these few beings from the Forbidden Zone were their only hope. Yet, the fact that even they spoke in such terms was enough to illustrate just how terrifying and dreadful this killing formation was.

For now, it seemed their only option was to retreat first and decide on a strategy later.

"Retreat."

Though Chu Xiao felt a pang of reluctance, he had no choice but to sound the horn, signaling the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s army to retreat. The vast fleet of ancient warships began to turn back along the route they had come.

At the same time, he grimaced as he crushed an ancient talisman in his hand. The talisman was simple in design, but imprinted in the center was a casual character for “soldier,” as if scribbled by hand.

In the 0.8 split-second it took to crush the ancient talisman, a terrifying, sky-piercing light shot upward.

The character "兵" seemed to come to life, evolving and unfolding to open a vast treasury of weapons within the void. A boundless aura permeated the space, filled with surging chaotic light, as one divine weapon after another emerged—a blue lamp, an ancient pagoda, a divine mirror, a ceremonial axe and shield, a bronze bell, a Bagua mirror… The embryonic forms of various Daoist artifacts coalesced, radiating a dazzling and awe-inspiring brilliance.

These weapons flew forth, radiating equally terrifying divine might. They charged toward the endless sword beams, colliding in the cosmos and unleashing boundless energy.

This was the military token bestowed upon him by Gu Changge; at a critical moment, it possessed unfathomable power and could save his life.

But at this moment, Chu Xiao had no time to spare. To ensure the army’s safe retreat, he activated the military token and stood his ground at the rear.

"Senior..."

“We cannot let them retreat like this.”

Watching that vast treasury of weapons—though it was constantly fading, blurring, and even cracking—it was actually holding back the Fourth Killing Formation as it slowly descended in a spiral. The expressions of the many powerful figures from the Righteous Unity Alliance changed, looking rather grim.

Some even approached the mysterious man in white, pleading with him to intervene.

"I cannot hold them back."

However, to their surprise, the white-robed elder—who had been so cold and aloof moments ago—now seemed to furrow his brow and shook his head.

“I must say, you truly live up to your reputation as the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance. Was this tactic intentional?” He gazed at the treasure trove of weapons and sighed.

The powerful figures of the Righteous Unity Alliance were all utterly astonished and shaken. What did this mean?

Could it be that the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance had already intervened? Even this elder, whose origins were mysterious and whose power was unfathomable, dared not act rashly?
