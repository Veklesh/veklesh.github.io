High above the Ethereal Sanctuary, the stars trembled as ancient warships rumbled past, racing swiftly toward the central region.

An ancient banner of the "Heaven Slaying Alliance" fluttered in the void, radiating a boundless, surging aura that echoed throughout the cosmos.

"The Heaven Slaying Alliance has arrived..."

Countless cultivators and sentient beings, witnessing this spectacle, could not help but gasp in astonishment.

"I wonder who the Heaven Slaying Alliance will send this time..."

"Still, the fact that the Heaven Slaying Alliance has arrived finally puts our minds at ease."

Many ancient figures rose from their pavilions and palaces, gazing out in awe as they began to speculate.

Across the Peak of Ethereal Mist, ancient beings who had received the telepathic message also looked on with astonishment.

"They’ve actually come."

The high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance were all taken by surprise, though many also looked on with a hint of curiosity. They wondered: with the Immortal Dao Alliance’s founding imminent, why had the Heaven Slaying Alliance suddenly arrived now? Was there some deeper meaning behind this?

Was this a show of force? Or did they simply not care?

The major clans of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm also received the news at this time. Amidst their shock, they were also overjoyed, letting out a long sigh of relief. As long as the Heaven Slaying Alliance did not intervene to block the formation of the Immortal Dao Alliance, that would be the best possible news for them.

Not to mention, the Heaven Slaying Alliance had actually sent representatives. In a sense, this was tantamount to acknowledging the formation of the Immortal Dao Alliance.

"Quickly, follow us to welcome the honored guests from the Heaven Slaying Alliance..."

The elders of the major clans did not in the least consider the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s hasty arrival to be a breach of etiquette. Overjoyed, they hurriedly rose to their feet, summoned a large number of clan members, and set off for the Ethereal Holy City to welcome them.

"Miss Luo, it’s been a long time..."

Meanwhile, aboard one of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s ancient warships, Gu Changge gazed at the coffin that lay shattered before him. He then withdrew his gaze and spoke in a measured tone.

Before him, Luo Xiangjun, the Lord of Luo, and the Eternal Primordial God stood frozen in place.

The latter two, in particular, were unable to move even an inch, as if bound to the spot, unable even to communicate telepathically, with only traces of terror and trembling remaining on their faces.

Luo Xiangjun was still in a daze, unable to recover from the shock of what had just unfolded.

It was only after a long while, upon hearing Gu Changge’s words, that a complex expression flashed across her eyes as she said, “Luo Xiangjun greets the Alliance Leader.”

She never would have imagined that Gu Changge would have left so-called “causal coordinates” on her back then—was it to ensure she would become entangled with certain beings?

How could she have imagined this scene back then? To her, it still felt like a dream or a myth—she couldn’t bring herself to believe it.

Gu Changge nodded, paid her no further attention, and turned his gaze toward Luo Zhu and the Eternal Primordial God.

The moment the Primordial God first met Luo Xiangjun, he had actually sensed the aura of her bloodline. If even the Eternal Primordial God could detect it, how could he possibly fail to notice?

It was precisely for this reason that he intended to use Luo Xiangjun as bait, to see if he could lure the Lord of Luo out from behind her.

Of the Eight Clans that once served Heaven, aside from the Heaven’s Decline Clans—Lesser Yin and Lesser Yang—the Sword Clan, Netherlight, Yama Soul, Formless, and the Luo Clan had yet to make an appearance.

“Luo, are you planning to betray me?”

He paid no heed to the Eternal Primordial God’s slightly pale, incredulous expression, turning to the Lord of Luo with a casual tone.

From the Luo Clan’s territory to the ethereal sacred realm before them lay an immeasurable span of time and space, yet in the blink of an eye, the two had been brought here.

Such a feat left the Eternal Primordial God both trembling with shock and filled with dread.

Just how powerful was this being? And to act so brazenly—did they truly not fear being detected?

It was only now, upon seeing Gu Changge in person, that he was truly convinced of his earlier suspicions.

The Ruler of Heaven and the Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance were one and the same.

"No... I dare not."

Lord Luo’s heart was even more unsettled than that of the Eternal Primordial God. He had never imagined that, years later, he would “reunite” with Gu Changge in this manner.

If not for the Eternal Primordial God bringing Luo Xiangjun to the Luo Clan’s territory, he might have remained hidden.

But who could have foreseen that Gu Changge had long since used this as bait, waiting for him to take the hook?

Lord Luo, who had lived a life of caution, never in a million years would have imagined that such a day would come.

Yet, facing Gu Changge, his agitated and trembling heart gradually calmed down.

“If you’re afraid, why didn’t you come to see me?” Gu Changge said indifferently.

Lord Luo’s heart trembled slightly. Forcing himself to ignore the tingling sensation on his scalp, he replied, “I did not know Your Majesty had already regained consciousness.”

Gu Changge smiled faintly and said, “Then you should have known by now.”

At a time when he needed capable men, he naturally had no intention of truly harming Lord Luo. He feared no betrayal, nor did he care about the petty schemes of Lord Luo and his followers.

Lord Luo’s heart trembled even more; even someone as powerful as he dared not meet Gu Changge’s gaze.

As for the Eternal Primordial God standing nearby, he had not yet fully recovered to his peak prime and was still far inferior in strength to Lord Luo.

What filled him with both humiliation and helplessness at that moment was that, despite both being beings of the Primordial Era, Gu Changge didn’t even acknowledge his existence—she didn’t even bother to speak to him, choosing instead to ignore him completely.

Yet the Eternal Primordial God had no choice but to accept this reality; Gu Changge was indeed far, far stronger than he.

At least, judging by the current situation, he was likely the only being in the present age to have reached the End of the Path.

As for whether she had transcended the Path’s End realm and advanced further to the realm of Supreme Shattered, that remained unknown.

The only thing the Eternal Primordial God could be certain of was that, barring the emergence of other "Path’s End" beings, there was simply no existence in the current Vast Expanse capable of matching Gu Changge.

Gu Changge’s power lay in the fact that he completely disregarded the existence of the Realm of Truth, acting without the slightest hesitation.

The arrival of the Heaven Slaying Alliance caused a massive upheaval in the Piao Miao Holy Realm. Outside the Holy City of Piao Miao, the Four Great Clans, having received the news, dispatched representatives to rush to the scene and wait there.

However, one ancient warship after another descended directly, not choosing to land within the city, but instead heading straight toward the area of the Piao Miao Peak, their presence immense and imposing.

The high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance who had accompanied them this time were all waiting outside the courtyard where Gu Changge resided, along with the White Bone Ancestral King, Yao Yao, Jiang Weiyang, and others.

A moment later, Gu Changge stepped out, but behind him were three additional figures.

"Alliance Leader..."

The high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance were all taken aback, looking at the three with confusion.

Apart from Luo Xiangjun, they could not gauge the cultivation levels of the other two, especially the man clad in a silver robe, who gave off an aura of being detached from time and space, as if he did not belong to this realm.

"Let’s go."

Gu Changge said, offering no explanation regarding the origins of Lord Luo, the Eternal Primordial God, or the others.

The high-ranking members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance, sensing the mood, wisely refrained from asking further questions.

On the vast plaza of Piaomiao Peak, ancient warships descended from the sky. The void trembled as a mighty aura radiated out, causing all living beings and cultivators in the vicinity to look on in shock.

Though they had long been aware of the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s might, they never imagined they would so blatantly disregard the rules of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm and descend directly upon this place.

Among the divine islands and mountain peaks, the distinguished guests who had already arrived were all shaken to the core. Yet at this moment, the atmosphere had grown eerily silent, the earlier bustle now completely gone.

"The Heaven Slaying Alliance has arrived. Out of courtesy and propriety, we should go pay them a visit."

"Otherwise, the outside world might say the Righteous Unity Alliance lacks manners. Hmph."

On the other side, the high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance, led by Deputy Alliance Master Zhao Tianqi, were also rushing toward the direction where the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s ancient warship was descending.

Many still harbored hostility and resentment toward the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

This marked the first time the high-ranking members of both sides had gathered since the ceasefire.

Numerous representatives from the various Daoist lineages and realms watched the scene with mixed emotions and looks of curiosity.

For a moment, the crowd surged; across mountain peaks and divine islands, streaks of light and divine rainbows flashed as figures rushed toward the location where the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s ancient warship had come to rest.

Meanwhile, atop the Piao Miao Peak, within a simple thatched hut, the Holy Ancestor of the Piao Miao Holy Realm sat cross-legged. At that moment, as if sensing something, astonishment flashed in his eyes, and his gaze suddenly turned toward the high-ranking officials of the Heaven Slaying Alliance.

"How could this be..."

In the next instant, his mind reeling, he hesitated not a moment. With a flick of his figure, he vanished from where he stood.

"Greetings, Holy Ancestor..."

In the reception hall established by the Immortal Dao Alliance, the many elders of the Piao Miao Sect responsible for hosting the guests were all startled by the sudden appearance of the refined middle-aged man. They then bowed respectfully in unison.

The guests present were initially bewildered, but some quickly caught on and looked at the middle-aged man in shock.

The Holy Ancestor of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm has appeared in person?

A considerable commotion immediately erupted within the reception hall.

The elderly woman leaning on her cane was also present in the hall; she, too, looked at the Patriarch of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm with a mixture of shock and confusion.

"Holy Ancestor, why have you personally appeared as well?"

"This is truly unexpected."

"I had thought the Heaven Slaying Alliance wouldn’t take this matter too seriously; the fact that they didn’t intervene was already a tremendous blessing..."

The Ethereal Holy Ancestor stood there, his features handsome, with a white beard framing his face and deep, piercing eyes. He wore a long robe of cloud-patterned brocade, with a red jade pendant hanging from his waist, exuding an ethereal aura.

But at this moment, his expression was grave, even hesitant.

“Is the Heaven Slaying Alliance led by someone of such great stature that it requires Your Holiness to receive them personally?” the old woman asked, shaken.

“I fear something momentous is about to happen today…”

The Ethereal Holy Ancestor furrowed his brow. Without lingering in hesitation, he strode out of the hall and headed toward the square outside.

Deep down, he had a sense that something was amiss; for a being of his stature, his perceptions rarely erred.

Why hadn’t he sensed it before? Why did he only perceive it now that the members of the Heaven Slaying Alliance had arrived? Could it be that some entity was blocking his perception?

Moreover, regarding those auras he had sensed just now—if he was not mistaken—one of them must have been the Heaven Slaying Alliance Master.

When did he leave the Heaven Slaying Alliance to appear here in person?

This news was truly shocking.

Misty, ethereal clouds shrouded the summit of Piao Miao Peak, permeated with an aura of ancient desolation.

In the vast void of the heavens, one could even see occasional rifts in space-time slicing through the sky, with streams of light falling from within them.

The Piao Miao Holy Ancestor set out personally, accompanied by numerous elders of the Piao Miao Sect, and made his way immediately to the landing site of the Heaven Slaying Alliance members.

"Heaven Slaying Alliance brothers, thank you for traveling such a long distance. We apologize for not greeting you sooner..."

Members of the four major clans of the Piao Miao Sacred Realm all came forward to greet them, their faces beaming with smiles.

Many leaders from the True Realm civilizations also stepped forward, not wishing to antagonize the Heaven Slaying Alliance, especially at this critical juncture.

However, the sudden appearance of the Piao Miao Holy Ancestor left everyone stunned, followed by disbelief—though this news had, of course, already reached them from the Guest Hall.

This was a legendary figure of his generation; it was said that the Piao Miao Sacred Realm had been founded by his own hand, with origins stretching back to ancient times—no one knew exactly from which era he hailed.

Even the elders and disciples of the Piao Miao Sect had only recently learned of the Piao Miao Holy Ancestor’s reappearance in the world.

"The Piao Miao Holy Ancestor has actually appeared?"

The high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance could no longer maintain their smiles. When they had arrived earlier, they had been received only by a few elders of the Piaomiao Sect.

Not even Li Hanying—who is hailed as the greatest prodigy the Piao Miao Holy City has seen in countless ages and has now become the Patriarch of the Piao Miao Sect—had come out to greet them.

Zhao Tianqi, the Deputy Alliance Master, though clearly embarrassed, maintained a composed demeanor, as if he didn’t care much.

"It seems the Piao Miao Holy Realm places great importance on the Heaven Slaying Alliance. Even though the founding ceremony of the Immortal Dao Alliance is fast approaching, and the Heaven Slaying Alliance has arrived so belatedly, the Piao Miao Holy Ancestor has personally come out to welcome them..."

The crowd was astonished, their minds churning with shock.

Many of the guests and dignitaries were also curious; numerous eyes turned toward the direction from which the elders of the Piao Miao Sect had arrived. As an ancient figure like the Piao Miao Holy Ancestor, one could only imagine how unfathomably profound his power must be.

The establishment of the Immortal Dao Alliance was made possible precisely because of his backing.

Soon, the faces of those present were filled with even greater astonishment and disbelief; they could scarcely believe their ears.

"The Alliance Master’s personal arrival truly graces the Piao Miao Sacred Realm with his presence."

"The Piao Miao Sect failed to greet you from afar; we hope the Alliance Leader will forgive our negligence."

A refined and cheerful voice rang out as the Piao Miao Holy Ancestor walked forward, flanked by a group of Piao Miao Sect elders.

His words struck like a bolt of lightning, instantly plunging the vast, expansive plaza into dead silence.

The cultivators and sentient beings on the surrounding mountain peaks and divine islands stood frozen in disbelief.

The leaders of every sect and faction of civilization now stared intently in his direction, their very breaths seeming to freeze.

The Alliance Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance? Has he personally descended?

The high-ranking members of the Righteous Unity Alliance, including Deputy Leader Zhao Tianqi, were all taken aback, freezing in place.

Then, many began to tremble, their bodies quivering slightly as fear washed over their faces. Their complexions turned pale, and cold sweat beaded on their foreheads.

How could this be? Isn’t the Master of the Heaven Slaying Alliance always stationed at the Alliance headquarters? How could he have appeared here?

Weren’t they afraid that the Dark Clans would take advantage of his absence to launch an attack?

However, these were the very words spoken by the Holy Ancestor of the Piaomiao Sect—how could they possibly be false? In an instant, the area fell into an even deeper silence; the once bustling Divine Mountain, teeming with people, grew deathly still.

In the Vast Expanse of the present age, those who have seen the true face of the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance can be counted on one hand; even among the Alliance’s high-ranking officials, few have had the opportunity to meet him in person.

And today, it seemed everyone had the chance to witness the true appearance of that mysterious Alliance Master with their own eyes?

"We’ve all been speculating about which powerful figures the Heaven Slaying Alliance would send, but who could have imagined that the Heaven Slaying Alliance Leader himself would make an appearance?"

"This news is truly staggering. If it reaches the Righteous Unity Alliance, I wonder what kind of earth-shattering upheaval it will cause."

"The Righteous Unity Alliance probably never imagined the Alliance Leader of the Heaven Slaying Alliance would come in person. This truly shows great respect to the Immortal Dao Alliance."

Countless cultivators and sentient beings felt their hearts stirred by a massive upheaval, unable to calm down for a long time.

Gaze after gaze converged from all directions, all turning toward the figures shrouded in a divine glow of rosy mist aboard the Heaven Slaying Alliance’s ancient warship.

The figure at the forefront appeared young and slender, clad in snow-white robes that exuded an otherworldly aura. Unfortunately, many could not make out his features clearly; they could only see a pair of eyes that seemed gentle yet immeasurably profound, as if they encompassed all worlds and the boundless expanse of time and space.

Beside him, the forces of causality, time, reincarnation, and all laws… with every passing moment, it seemed as though countless laws were surging and fading, like a layer of chaotic mist enveloping and shrouding him. With each step he took, it was as if he were walking through another dimension of time and space.

"With the establishment of the Immortal Dao Alliance being such a momentous occasion, how could I possibly fail to come and offer my congratulations?"

Gu Changge’s voice was calm, as if he were responding with a smile, yet everyone present felt an immense, overwhelming sense of dread wash over them from the depths of their souls.

It was as if they could not even retain the memory of that voice; it seemed destined to vanish from their minds the very next moment.
