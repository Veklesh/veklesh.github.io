The giant Ambris had inflicted massive damage using the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, Death Scythe, and Twilight Spear, but as black mist seeped up from the surrounding ground and merged with it, its form returned to normal once more.

Seeing this, Rei, still deploying the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, muttered with displeasure.

"This is never-ending. ...Well, come to think of it, since it's just black mist over there, all it has to do is restore the severed duplicates back to normal."

Facing the giant Ambris fixed in Rei's gaze, Rei remained motionless.

He hesitated, unsure how the other would react if he made a move.

But just as Rei remained still, the giant Ambris showed no sign of moving either.

After restoring the black mist that Rei had burned away, significantly reducing its volume, it simply drifted motionless in place once more.

If this were an ordinary monster, it would likely react in some way—either counterattacking Rei or fleeing the scene.

But... the natural phenomenon known as the Ambris showed absolutely no sign of moving from its current state.

Above, Set flew around dangling Vihera, yet it showed no reaction to that either.

Rei was wearing the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, and above all, the flames generated by the Deep Flame were causing the temperature around them to rise rapidly. Yet, it too showed no sign of being bothered by it, as if it were only natural.

Rei, still deploying the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, and the giant Ambris.

Both remained silent, simply existing in that space.

...Though, setting Rei aside, he suspected the giant Ambris had no self-awareness.

For that very reason, facing each other like this held almost no meaning... In fact, one could say that as time passed, Rei was at a disadvantage due to the magic power being consumed by using the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour.

While the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour consumes less magic power, that's only in comparison to the Overlord’s Armour.

Only someone with Rei's immense magical power can wield it effectively; ordinarily, a mage couldn't endure the consumption it demands.

"Anyway... I guess I have no choice but to try again."

He knew wasting time like this would only make his situation worse.

Rei channeled magic into the Twilight Spear clutched in his left hand... After a few paces of running start, he twisted his body and hurled it with all his might.

The Twilight Spear sliced through the air, easily piercing through the high magical defenses of Ambris's body and flying right through him. It then returned swiftly to Rei's hand.

"How was that?"

Rei muttered, but the part of Ambris that had been pierced and vanished by the Twilight Spear was immediately repaired as black mist gathered around it once more.

Seeing this, Rei sighed in exasperation.

The Twilight Spear wasn't that powerful, but it possessed a recovery-inhibiting effect.

In other words, wounds inflicted by the Twilight Spear wouldn't heal unless powerful potions or recovery magic were used... or so it should have been...

"No good either?"

Seeing the part obliterated by the Twilight Spear recover effortlessly, Rei sighed.

Was it because Ambris was a being born from magical aberration that the Twilight Spear's healing suppression effect didn't work? Or was it simply that Ambris's healing speed surpassed the Twilight Spear's suppression effect?

(No, it's also possible that the Twilight Spear's healing suppression effect isn't working because Ambris is a monster... no, not even a living creature.)

Naturally, even if the Twilight Spear destroyed something rock-like, the recovery-inhibiting effect wouldn't take effect on it.

It's only natural, since the target is a natural object, not a living creature.

And if Ambris is also recognized as a natural object rather than a monster, then this result wasn't strange at all.

"This is troublesome. ...Should I just incinerate it with magic? But to defeat an Ambris this size... Or perhaps just keep attacking relentlessly until the black mist composing its body dissipates? ...Worst case, that might be the only option."

With the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour activated to its fullest, Rei charged toward the Ambris.

Just as he was about to swing the Death Scythe... at that moment... the Ambris displayed a reaction unlike any before.

Up until now, it had simply been attacked one-sidedly by Rei, its body-forming black mist being eliminated.

But this time, the black mist moved as if repelled by Rei's approach.

No, it wasn't as if it seemed reluctant—it was clearly acting out of aversion.

Twitch.

Seeing this unprecedented reaction from the giant Ambris, Rei, closing the distance, raised his eyebrows slightly in surprise.

But by the time the black mist moved, Rei was already inside the giant Ambris.

"Burn from within and turn to charcoal!"

With those words, the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour enveloping Rei rapidly increased in temperature.

Not only that, the crimson magical energy of flame enveloping Rei's body rapidly intensified, moving freely within the black mist like whips.

The giant Ambris, upon contact, was instantly incinerated, vanishing... consumed by flames.

Though the giant Ambris was being consumed in an instant, its size remained unchanged because it was continuously absorbing Ambris rising from the surrounding ground and Ambris flying through the air to approach it.

Though the giant Ambris remained unchanged in size despite being continuously consumed by flames, after several minutes in this state, it finally began to show movements never seen before before Rei.

Until now, Rei's figure had been at the center of the black mist.

From there, he had been burning the center of the giant ambris... but suddenly, Rei realized the black mist no longer existed around him.

Had it been burned away in an instant? Wondering, he looked around, but the black mist was still there.

...Yes, the black mist had created a gaping void centered around Rei.

"...It shouldn't have a self-awareness, so did it respond to my movements? Or does 'no self-awareness' only apply to the split fragments, and this main body actually has one?"

Though puzzled, his objective remained unchanged.

He unleashed a flying slash toward the giant Ambros, hurled the Twilight Spear, whipped the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour like a whip, and unleashed Deep Flames to create explosive blazes.

As he performed these actions, the giant Ambris likely assessed the danger Rei posed even further. It retreated the black mist more widely, centering it around Rei... From the outside, it looked like Rei was at the center of a dome of black mist, with a space devoid of the black mist surrounding him.

Though the black mist was densely packed, it was still possible to make out Rei's figure from outside. This was likely because it maintained its form as mist.

Even densely packed, one could discern the situation within to some extent.

"Is it okay? I thought Ambris had no self-awareness... Or is that different because that Ambris is the main body?"

"Guruu."

While hanging onto Set's leg, Vihera muttered. Set, flying through the air with his wings flapping, managed the dexterous feat of tilting his head in response.

Vihera, too, was prepared to head straight for the ground if anything happened, but judging from Rei's initial fight, it seemed like she was overwhelming the giant Ambris one-sidedly.

No, rather than pushing it, it was more accurate to say the giant Ambris showed no reaction whatsoever.

No matter what Rei did, the giant Ambris seemed utterly indifferent.

However, once the majority of the black mist composing its body was destroyed, its behavior changed.

Despite having completely ignored Rei's existence, it began to respond.

"Set, what do you think we should do? Should we go help after all?"

"GRRRR!"

Set shook his head at Vihera's words.

Currently, Rei was using the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour to burn away the surrounding black mist.

Given that, entering the battlefield between Rei and the giant Ambris would inevitably injure Vihera as well.

Moreover, even up here in the sky, where they were keeping their distance from the combat zone, the heat was becoming unbearable.

He couldn't just charge into the battlefield under these circumstances.

Vihera understood that too. Yet, seeing Rei inside the giant Ambris, he felt compelled to do something.

"...He'll be fine, right? It's Rei."

Those words, which could be interpreted as timid, came from the usually strong-willed Vihera.

They'd known each other for quite some time, yet she still worried about Rei.

But all Vihera could do now was believe in Rei's safety.

Rei, who is worried about by Vihera, is currently attacking the surrounding black mist, as if being swallowed by the giant Ambris.

"Flying Slash!"

With that cry, a slashing strike flew forth, simultaneously piercing the black mist with the Twilight Spear that Rei had hurled.

In terms of offense, Rei held an overwhelming advantage; it could be said he was attacking one-sidedly.

However, though not exactly as compensation, the giant Ambris continuously replenished the black mist it had expelled.

Furthermore, it gradually expanded the empty space around itself, avoiding contact with Rei's Death Scythe and Twilight Spear.

By now, the black mist no longer existed within a radius of about five meters centered on Rei.

"Damn it, cut it out already. You don't seriously have an infinite supply of that black mist to replenish yourself, do you? If you do, that's going to be a real pain in the ass."

Laying into his two-spear stance with Death Scythe and Twilight Spear, Rei let out a grumble.

No matter how much he attacked, the enemy showed no sign of being shaken. Even to Rei, who was attacking one-sidedly, it wasn't exactly thrilling.

Even if it was just pain or something, any kind of reaction would let him know his attacks were having an effect. But there was absolutely no reaction of that sort. It just mechanically absorbed the damage and healed itself with the black mist.

(The fact that the black mist around me has disappeared probably means it genuinely dislikes my movements, though.)

Watching Ambris move like this, Rei steeled himself.

Ideally, since Ambris was created by magical aberration, he didn't want to use magic against it.

But... this endless game of cat and mouse was clearly going nowhere.

(Well, what can you do? If this situation drags on indefinitely, I might eventually run out of magic power.)

Considering raw magical power alone, it was inconceivable that any being could surpass Rei.

That held true not just for humans, but for every living creature.

But even so, it wasn't infinite. It was certain my magic would eventually run out.

Or, if the Magic-Sucking Bracelet were working—absorbing magic from enemies I damaged—perhaps my magic could last forever.

But... unfortunately, either Ambris wasn't being recognized as an enemy, or attacking the black mist alone was pointless.

Rei couldn't discern the reason, but regardless, no matter how many times he struck Ambrys, his magic power did not recover.

Quickly gathering his thoughts, he chose the spell most appropriate for the situation—a spell to incinerate the giant Ambryse with scorching inferno—and spoke.

'Flames, manifest one of your true forms—destruction. You are that which consumes all, extinguishes all, annihilates all. Bring forth great destruction, and thereby become the sacrifice for new rebirth.'

A fireball was born from Death Scythe.

But there is only one. ...Even so, this single fireball, infused with a massive amount of Rei's magical power, is truly worthy of the words "scorching inferno" spoken by Rei.

Swinging the Death Scythe, he unleashes the fireball.

'Scorching Inferno!'

The fireball created by Rei flew straight into the black mist... specifically toward the densest, darkest concentration of it.

And in the next instant... a hell of flames was born.

While the majority of the black mist was consumed by the flames, the black mist surrounding the area where Rei had been—except for the spot where the fireball had been released—remained untouched.

Nevertheless, the black mist flowed into the vanished areas one after another, only to be consumed by the flames.

How long did this continue...? When the surrounding flames finally died down, only an exceptionally dense black mist remained.

Rei whipped the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour like a whip toward the last remnant of the black mist.

With this, the turmoil in Ambris would finally end.

The instant he thought that, suddenly, before Rei's eyes... the black mist that should have been where the flame magic of the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour had been whipped down vanished.

"...Huh?"

"Kyaaaah!"

A scream pierced the air.

Rei instantly turned toward the source of the scream. What he saw was Vihera, the black mist sinking into her body.
