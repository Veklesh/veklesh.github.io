The Kingdom of Reburina.

While it shares the monarchy system with the Kingdom of Mireana, its scale is vastly different.

It is one of several small nations surrounding the Kingdom of Mireana, treated as a vassal state.

That said, despite its vassal status, it isn't treated particularly harshly.

It must pay an annual sum to the Kingdom of Mireana under the name of a security guarantee, and several individuals from Mireana are sent to participate in Reburina's national governance. Even so, a certain degree of diplomatic freedom is guaranteed.

Furthermore, a military force from the Kingdom of Mireana is stationed there as a deterrent. While the funds for this presence are provided by the Kingdom of Reburina, the mere presence of Mireana's troops prevents attacks from neighboring countries.

It was not a country with any particularly striking features; it was truly just an ordinary small nation.

It was simply inconceivable to those present that it would ever do anything hostile toward its suzerain, the Kingdom of Mireana.

"Calm down. Like I said before, it wasn't some big shot from the Kingdom of Reburina who ordered us to bring the woman. It was solely the organization I belong to."

"...Even so, if someone from Reburina causes trouble in this country, what do you think will happen? I doubt they're unaware of it."

Elena responded with a troubled expression at Ajax's words.

If the Kingdom of Reburina were to harm the Kingdom of Mireana this time, regardless of whether it was done by Reburina's will or not, it was certain that the Kingdom of Mireana would impose some form of penalty on Reburina.

And this was in Gilm, ruled by Daskar, the central figure of the Neutral Faction, where such a commotion had been stirred up.

Naturally, the penalty would be severe.

The Noble Faction and the Neutral Faction have recently developed friendly relations, while the Royal Faction and the Neutral Faction remain hostile. However, this is strictly an internal matter within the Kingdom of Mireana.

If this incident serves the interests of the Kingdom of Mireana, the Neutral Faction and the Noble Faction will show no leniency toward the Kingdom of Reburina, even if the Neutralists suffer damage.

...Though, to be honest, the Royal Faction might actually prefer the Neutral Faction to suffer more damage.

Normally, it would be unthinkable for a vassal state to attempt anything resembling an attack against its suzerain.
