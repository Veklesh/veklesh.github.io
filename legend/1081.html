His father's hobby and side business: cultivating mushrooms on logs.

Most mushrooms sold in regular supermarkets are produced using a method called sawdust cultivation.

Bed cultivation involves planting mushroom spawn into a block of sawdust or rice bran mixed and compressed into a block about the size of one or two fists. Log cultivation, on the other hand, involves drilling holes into logs and planting the spawn there.

When doing log cultivation, the logs are stacked in multiple layers, staggered to allow for growth. The scene unfolding before Rei's eyes now was strikingly similar to that.
