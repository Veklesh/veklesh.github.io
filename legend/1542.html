"Come on in, come on in! We've got some delicious bread today! How about some freshly baked bread for lunch?"

"We've got special oak-fed beef soup today! Once the pot's empty, it's sold out, so grab some before it's gone!"

"Cheese! Cheese! You'll regret it if you don't try this cheese!"

Such voices echoed around them.

It was right around noon, so the street echoed with the voices of many people seeking their lunch or those selling food.

But... those loud voices gradually grew quieter.

That was likely because of the presence of Rei... or more precisely, the presence of Set, whom Rei was leading.

The residents of Rossi, still unaccustomed to Set, were overwhelmed by his sheer strangeness when seeing him up close, despite the fact he wore a familiar's necklace.

If they spent more time around Set, they might eventually come to understand his endearing qualities, even if they were scared now.

But there wasn't enough time for that yet, and as a result, right now, seeing Set just made everyone scared... that was the situation.

"Grrr..."

Set whimpered sadly.

Stroking Set like that, Rei walked down the main street.

"Kyuu!"

On Set's back, Yellow let out a cheerful cry, trying to encourage him.

In response to Yellow's concern, Set purred softly.

Some passersby, seeing Set and Yellow like this, instinctively took a step back. But Rei and the others paid no attention whatsoever, continuing on their way.

(They're backing away from us... Considering what we're about to do, I guess that reaction isn't entirely wrong.)

The impending event... namely, the assault on Mejogo.

Even the residents of Rossi knew, as an open secret, that the organization called Jaya effectively controlled Mejogo.

But Mejogo, as an entertainment district, naturally brought blessings to the surrounding villages and towns, and even to the capital city of Rossi.

Specifically, it was the money spent by customers heading to Mejogo.

It wasn't that they couldn't stay in Mejogo itself, but using the villages and towns along the way as bases was cheaper.

Moreover, given that these were men who made the trip specifically to buy women, their spending habits were notoriously extravagant.

Because of this, the villages and towns through which carriages bound for Mejogo passed had been experiencing what could almost be called a special boom for some time now.

Rei and the others were well aware that destroying this boom would almost certainly make them the targets of resentment.

Of course, since the source of that boom was women abducted from other countries and forced into prostitution under slave collars, they couldn't possibly condone it.

(The real question is how much those profiting from this boom actually know, right?)

With these thoughts running through their minds, they made their way forward and soon completed the procedures at the main gate.

It being daytime, there was no need to wait for the formalities.

If this were Gilm, even on a normal day, they'd likely still have to queue at this hour. More importantly, with the expansion work underway, there should have been a large crowd gathered.

Considering that, leaving Rossi was incredibly easy... Rei and the others simply exited Rossi and moved to a spot a little further away.

Next, Rei took the Set Cage from the Misty Ring, looked at everyone present, and spoke.

"Alright, the assault is about to begin. Basically, we'll push through forcefully, just as we discussed before leaving the inn."

Normally, detailed role assignments would be necessary, but fortunately, Rei and his group possessed enough raw power to force their way through.

Furthermore, since they didn't know the exact layout of the underground facility, they couldn't plan ahead by deciding which areas to investigate first.

As a result, the only things decided beforehand were: Byune and Yellow would scout; Set would act as a barrier from above to prevent enemy reinforcements; Marina would provide various support using spirit magic. And Rei, Elena, and Vihera would unleash their full combat power to wreak havoc... that was all.

"Well, that's probably the quickest way to go about it. Giants will be coming out too,"

Vihera said cheerfully, clearly looking forward to the battle with the giants.

Of course, she couldn't condone what Jaya was doing, but that was that, and this was this.

Especially lately... since absorbing Ambris, there hadn't been anyone besides Rei and the others she could properly fight, leaving her combat urges unsatisfied.

For that very reason, despite having various thoughts, he had no objection to this surprise attack.

In fact, she'd been looking forward to it ever since she heard about the giants.

"I agree. For someone who creates such foolish creatures as giants, a bit of rough treatment is just what they deserve."

Marina nodded with a smile, agreeing with Rei's words.

Though, while her lips curved in a smile, her eyes were completely devoid of laughter.

If Rei could see spirits, he would have seen them cowering in fear from the rage emanating from Marina.

"...This is unforgivable."

The one who murmured this was Elena.

Elena had seen her share of depraved individuals through her experiences.

Even so, this particular incident was unforgivable.

At least, within the scope of what Elena had seen and heard, there was no place she could consider forgivable.

Though Elena had come with Rei and the others under the pretext of assisting with the retaliation against Jaya, her desire to actively destroy Jaya now far outweighed any notion of mere assistance.

"Hmm."

To the three of them, Byune only murmured briefly as usual.

Of course, Byune's expression never changed, but that didn't mean he lacked emotion.

The fact that Jaya's existence was unpleasant remained unchanged, but she didn't make any move to show that irritation on her face.

"Alright... Let's go then."

After hearing everyone's words, Rei murmured.

The others nodded in agreement and boarded the Set Cage.

Yellow, who had been playing with Set, hurried back over upon seeing Elena and the others enter the Set Cage.

"Grrr..."

Seeing Yellow like that, Set let out a slightly lonely-sounding growl.

Having been feared by Rossi during the move, that feeling was likely all the stronger.

Rei approached Set and gently stroked his head.

"Well then, I guess we should get going. Let's wrap up our business in the Kingdom of Reburina quickly and head back to Gilm."

"Guru? ...Guru!"

At Rei's words, Set let out a happy cry.

He probably wanted to return quickly because there were many people in Gilm who doted on him.

Still, Rei had no illusions about returning immediately after finishing his revenge against Jaya... not even that same day.

He could predict that... no, he was certain that finishing today's raid would lead to trouble.

Of course, he could ignore it and return to Gilm anyway, but unfortunately, from what Rei could tell, the others wouldn't tolerate it.

There was the desire to destroy the organization Jaya, responsible for such atrocities, and the need for retribution against the upper echelons of the Reburina Kingdom who had likely aided them.

Furthermore, with Rei and the others causing such chaos, it was impossible to guarantee that opportunists wouldn't take advantage of the situation to loot or commit other crimes.

And if such things were happening in Mejogo, the ones suffering would be the women forcibly brought from other countries and forced into prostitution.

Rei had asked the Resistance to handle that aspect, but whether they'd actually heed their request... he couldn't be certain.

Still, considering Sura's character, he felt it would probably be okay. It would be... but the problem was whether the Resistance alone could completely prevent looting and protect the prostitutes from injury.

No, completely... Rei didn't believe it was possible to ensure not a single one of them would get hurt.

But as long as the question of how many resistance members there actually were remained, he couldn't place complete trust in them.

"Well, there's no point in overthinking it now. All we can do is act. ...Set, let's go."

Set let out a short cry at Rei's words as he leapt onto his back.

After a few paces of running, he flapped his wings and shot upward toward the sky. Once he gained a certain height, he began his descent back toward the ground.

Grabbing the Set Cage without any apparent danger, he headed back up into the sky.

A guard who had been watching from a distance, wondering what Rei and the others were doing, simply stood there, mouth agape, observing the exchange.

When Rei and the others first arrived at Rossi, they had landed the Set Cage a short distance away from the city. However, the guard on duty today was not the same guard from that time.

Of course, since it was the kind of event that became a topic of conversation, he had heard about it from colleagues who witnessed it... but seeing it with his own eyes was vastly different from what he had imagined.

"Impressive... huh."

"Yeah. So that's the famed adventurer and the high-rank monster..."

His colleague murmured in agreement with the words he had spoken.

Hearing those voices, the man who had spoken first watched as Set's form rapidly shrank away.

A sudden thought crossed his mind: where could so many people be going? But he dismissed it, reasoning that since they were adventurers, they were probably off gathering something.

He probably made that assumption because gathering was the most common job undertaken by Rossi adventurers.

Unlike the frontier town of Gilm, monsters weren't particularly common around Rossi.

In fact, bandits appeared more often than monsters, but even those bandits rarely showed themselves around the capital city of Rossi... Above all, committing banditry around Mejogo, right under Jaya's nose, could only be described as having suicidal tendencies.

Of course, there were some naive bandits who came in without knowing any better.

Just like the ones Rei had defeated before.

The guard wondered if such a thief might have appeared... but if he knew where Rei and the others were headed and what they intended to do, his face would have turned ashen.

The very act of laying a hand on Jaya was something unimaginable for a citizen of the Kingdom of Reburina, and considering the massive uproar it would surely cause, that was only natural.

"It's a nice, summery day... Huh? It hasn't rained lately. Is there going to be a water shortage or something?"

The endless blue sky, cotton-candy-like white clouds, and the sun pouring dazzling light down upon the earth.

It was a scene truly worthy of being called a summer sky.

That said, since summer was already past its peak, the heat should gradually subside.

...For Rei, wearing a Dragon Robe equipped with what could be called a makeshift air conditioner, neither heat nor cold posed any particular problem. To him, it was simply the impression of summer scenery passing by.

As he enjoyed that summer sky, a town gradually came into view ahead... his destination this time, Mejogo.

"There it is."

"Guruu!"

At Rei's words, Set let out a joyful cry.

Though, in Set's case, it wasn't really that he was happy to see Mejogo; it was more likely that he was happy to be flying through the sky with Rei again after so long.

There, Elena and the others were relaxing in the Set Cage... yet simultaneously preparing for battle.

Having witnessed it firsthand during the war against the Bestir Empire, Elena felt this keenly.

Vihera, too, understood the sheer power of Rei and Set's partnership from witnessing it up close during the civil war.
