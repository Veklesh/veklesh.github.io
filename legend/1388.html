Time rewound, and Vihera entered the Treant Forest with Set, moving straight through the night forest.

Riding on Set's back, Vihera used claws extended from her gauntlets to slash through vines reaching toward her.

The vines stretched out, trying to knock Vihera off Set's back, but they couldn't withstand the claws made of magic power and were sliced apart one after another.

"Still, I'm fine, but what about Set... Ah, he seems okay."

"GRRRR!"

Though Vihera couldn't fully understand Set's will like Rei could, seeing Set's current state made it immediately clear what he wanted to say.

Hearing that proud growl, anyone could tell, not just Vihera.

...After all, Set was already fast to begin with, and powerful on top of that.

It was difficult for vines to wrap around Set's limbs, and even if they accidentally did, Set would keep running as if unaware, easily tearing them off.

"If it were just vines, it might be okay... but!"

The magic claws swung out, slicing through the entity that had approached him just like the vines before.

It could be called a vine too, but more accurately, it was a thorn bush covered in countless sharp spikes.

If it were just vines, tearing them apart wouldn't be a problem.

But a thorny bush with sharp thorns was a different story.

It wrapped around Set's body, its thorns trying to pierce his flesh.

Perhaps finding this unpleasant, Set dodged the brambles' attack by moving his body in quick, jerky motions.

Though Set was now nearly three meters long, his griffin nature granted him exceptional burst speed and reflexes.

Swiftly dodging the successive barbs, he pressed onward into the depths of the forest.

Set's target was the enormous flower growing deep within the forest.

It towered even higher than the trees of Treant Forest.

Even while running through the forest, he could see through gaps in the branches that seeds were occasionally being released from the flower, heading toward the front lines of the Treant Forest, which had become a battlefield.

What effect those seeds might have was a concern for Vihera too... but still, no, precisely because of that, she judged they needed to deal with that flower as quickly as possible.

"Set, I'm sorry, but could you hurry a bit more? We don't know what effect those seeds might have on Rei and the others."

"GRRRR!"

At Vihera's words, Set roared, "Leave it to me!"

Heeding that resolve, Set's legs kicked the ground with even greater force.

...Then, as if waiting for the exact moment Set increased his speed, a plant with sharp points sprouted from the ground.

It happened at precisely the moment when, had Set been moving any slower, those plants might have pierced the soles of his feet or his torso.

(Coincidence? Or was this inevitable?)

Seeing the new plant appear as if it had been waiting for Set to accelerate, Vihera, perched on Set's back, felt doubt.

Though he hesitated for a moment, wondering what the mastermind behind this was planning... Vihera decided that getting under that enormous flower was the absolute priority right now.

Above all, there was also the pure desire to enjoy the fight against an unknown monster.

Vines and thorns reached out to halt Set and Vihera's progress, but they sliced through them as they advanced deeper into the forest.

How much time had passed since they entered the forest...? It felt like barely ten minutes, yet it also felt like they'd been running through the woods for an hour already.

Just as that thought crossed his mind... Set suddenly stopped in his tracks.

Stopping so abruptly after running at such speed naturally placed a heavy burden on Vihera, who was riding on Set's back. However, Vihera possessed physical abilities sufficient to easily absorb that kind of momentum.

The abrupt halt left four deep gouges in the ground where Set's hooves had struck.

Though Vihera wasn't thrown off, she didn't understand why Set had stopped so abruptly. Looking slightly confused, she spoke up.

"What's wrong, Set?"

"GRRRRRRRR!"

But Set didn't respond to Vihera's words, instead letting out a high-pitched growl.

Then, he unleashed his Crystal Breath.

The breath released from Set's mouth transformed everything it touched into crystal.

...To be precise, it wasn't transforming them into crystal; rather, everything the breath touched became covered in a thin layer of crystal.

Vihera wondered why he'd suddenly done such a thing, but he surely didn't think Set would perform such a stunt without meaning.

He scanned his surroundings sharply, ready to react to anything that might happen.

But... even in that state, there were no monsters in particular, and certainly no attacks.

Still unsure why Set was using the Crystal Bracelet, she spoke up.

"Hey, wait a minute. Set. ...What happened?"

"GRRRRRR!"

But Set showed no sign of responding to Vihera's words, continuing to spew the Crystal Breath toward the direction they had been heading.

Though confused, Vihera decided it wasn't wise to interfere with Set in the current situation and let him do as he pleased.

Several more minutes passed.

Finally, Set stopped spewing the crystal breath.

"Um, Set?"

"Grr."

What? Set tilted his head slightly, but Vihera didn't know how to ask him.

In the end, she decided not to waste any more time asking questions, figuring she could ask Rei about it later. Mounting Set once more, Vihera continued through the forest.

As they ran over the area where the Crystal Breath had been used, the sound of Set's hooves crushing crystals echoed around them, and it felt slightly pleasant... but that was beside the point.

However, if Vihera had picked up one of the crystals fallen to the ground and examined it closely, she would have noticed yellow powder trapped inside.

And though Vihera didn't know it, that powder was pollen used to strip monsters of their thinking ability and lure them into the forest.

Set didn't know the exact details of its effects either, but the moment he saw the pollen, he got a bad feeling and dealt with it using his Crystal Bracelet.

...The moon was already partially hidden behind clouds, and on top of that, Set and Vihera were deep within the forest.

The moonlight was heavily obscured by branches... Even with night vision, spotting something like pollen in those conditions showed off Set's... no, the griffin's keen senses.

Furthermore, something Set hadn't known: pollen that had become an attractant substance was ineffective against those possessing a certain level of magical defense.

In other words, it should have had no effect on either Set or Vihera.

In fact, nearly all the adventurers participating in this mission could likely nullify it.

Even so, the fact that Set neutralized the pollen like this was undoubtedly fortunate for the entire expedition team, if not just for Set and Vihera themselves.

Though, to be fair, the pollen itself was originally intended for Set and Vihera.

Anyway, after the Crystal Bracelet incident ended, Set and Vihera kept running through the forest... until suddenly, they emerged into a bright area.

Yes. A bright spot.

The place they emerged into was devoid of trees, with one sole exception.

Grass grew across the ground like a meadow, but it was only grass.

At the center of this treeless area stood a massive... thick stem so substantial that at first glance, one might easily mistake it for a tree trunk.

That stem bore numerous thorns that looked sharp enough to pierce.

To an outsider, it wouldn't be an exaggeration to say it looked like dagger blades were growing straight out of it.

"...What do you think?"

Vihera asked Set, staring at the obviously suspicious thorns.

But Set had never seen this giant flower—no, this flower monster—before today either.

Given that, it was only natural he'd be unsure how to respond.

"Guruu... Grrrr!"

Still, the only thing he could do now was confirm what kind of existence that flower was.

As if to say "for now," ten arrows made of ice materialize around Set, who lets out a growl.

It was Set's skill, Ice Arrow.

The ice arrows materialize and fly straight toward the giant flower's stem.

Perhaps due to his low level, the Ice Arrow's attack power was by no means high.

However, the sharply pointed tip of the Ice Arrow would undoubtedly inflict some damage if it pierced its target.

Vihera didn't know much about Ice Arrow either, but she could still predict it would provoke some kind of reaction.

And then... just as the ice arrow seemed poised to pierce the stem, several thorns growing from the stem fired simultaneously, intercepting the Ice Arrow.

Thorns and ice arrows collided midair.

Ultimately, the ice arrow prevailed. Yet, even as it deflected or pierced the thorns, the sheer number of thorns launched from the stem vastly outnumbered the ice arrows.

And so, as if quantity trumps quality, countless thorns kept striking the ice arrow, eventually forcing it off course.

Seeing the ice arrow miss the giant flower's stem and fly off in an unexpected direction, Vihera smiled, understanding the enemy's attack method... but the next moment, a slight look of surprise flashed across his face.

From the stem that had fired the thorns, new thorns were sprouting.

(What a remarkably convenient body it has. But... at that speed, I should be able to handle it.)

Considering her own physical abilities, she was confident she could easily evade an attack moving at that speed.

"Set, I'll take the front line. Cover me, okay?"

"Gurur... Guruu?"

"Are you okay?" Set growled, and Vihera opened her mouth to speak...

"Huh!? Could you at least let me speak for a moment!"

Seeing tentacles—no, vines—sprouting from the stem and reaching toward her, Vihera spat out the words as she stepped forward.

...Though, contrary to her words, the smile on Vihera's face was one relishing the fight against a formidable foe.

Watching the vines reach for her, she extended claws made of magic from her gauntlets.

The tips of the vines bore thorns similar to those growing from the stems.

However, these thorns were larger and sharper than those growing from the stem.

They were more akin to needles than thorns.

Vihera charged straight toward the vine bearing those needles.

Just as the tips of the needles were about to touch her body, she forcibly changed her body's orientation while moving.

The vine pierced the space mere centimeters from Vihera.

Though it was clear that contact would undoubtedly tear skin and rip flesh, Vihera advanced without showing any fear.

Not only that, but while evading the vine's strike and advancing, he flashed his magic claws, even severing the vine in half.

Thorns shot out from the stem to intercept Vihera, but she evaded the barrage of countless thorns with minimal movement, thanks to her astonishing dynamic vision. She deflected only the thorns that were certain to hit her with her magic-formed claws.

It was movement that should be described as dance rather than battle.

If a man were here... no, even if it were a woman, they would be captivated by Vihera's movements right now.

"GRRRRRR!"

Of course, Set wasn't just standing idle.

He intercepted the thorns heading toward Vihera with Ice Arrows and Wind Arrows.

If they collided head-on, it was clear from the start which would be stronger: the thorns or the Ice Arrows and Wind Arrows created by Set's skills.

If the thorns were fired continuously, it would be a different story, but this time the goal was to deflect the thorns aimed at Vihera, so there was no problem.

And so, despite wearing only a thin robe and lacking any armor beyond his gauntlets and greaves, Vihera continued advancing, evading every attack with innate talent... until he reached the base of the plant's stem.

A vine lashed down from above Vihera's head, but despite the blindside strike, Vihera effortlessly severed it with the claws on his gauntlets.

The giant flower must have realized it was in danger. Judging that vines and thorns alone couldn't stop the attack, it dropped seeds directly beneath itself.

Normally, these seeds were a means to spread the Treant Forest, making dropping them toward its own location an unthinkable choice... but that was how dangerous it judged Vihera to be.

But Vihera effortlessly dodged the seeds falling toward her... and worse, she used that evasive movement as a prelude to her next strike, lightly touching the stem with her palm.

"Hah!"

With a cry of determination, magical power slammed into the stem's interior as a shockwave... The Infused Palm, its power amplified after the incident with Ambris, instantly shattered the stem supporting the giant flower from within, destroying it.

"...Phew, that was quite enjoyable."

Watching the stem-broken flower fall in the direction away from herself and Set, Vihera smiled and said this... but the very next moment, seeing several giant flowers identical to the one she had just destroyed sprouting from the forest, she finally stopped moving.
