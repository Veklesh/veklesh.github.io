After completing the sand ship's test run, Rei promptly stowed the vessel inside the Misty Ring without a hint of lingering attachment.

The exhilaration he felt when he first seized the sand ship from the sand pirates was already gone.

If anything, he now felt a desire to auction it off and get rid of it as soon as possible.

While storing it inside the Misty Ring solved the problem of it taking up space, he still felt like just possessing something like the sand ship was a nuisance.

"...Well, I suppose I'll have August handle the matter with the sand ship. More importantly, Set, let's scout the area a bit. We might discover some unknown monsters."

"Grrr?"

Set tilted his head slightly, asking, "Is that okay?"

With the Sand Serpent dismantled, it was already past mid-afternoon.

When Set questioned whether it was wise to search for monsters now, Rei nodded reassuringly.

"It's fine. If anything happens, you can get back to Gosh right away, right?"

"Grr."

Set growled in agreement at Rei's words.

It hadn't taken them much time to get this far.

As Rei said, if any danger arose and they needed to return to Gosh, they could undoubtedly get back immediately.

Plus, Set still wanted to stay with Rei. In the end, he was persuaded by Rei and ended up scanning the area from above to check for monsters.

Flying through the sky for over ten minutes. Soon, Set turned his gaze toward the desert below and let out a sharp cry.

Rei followed Set's gaze and saw a monster with a huge shell on its back.

"...A coconut crab?"

That was the only word that escaped Rei's lips upon seeing the monster.

Indeed, from the air, it did resemble the coconut crabs Rei had seen on TV back in Japan.

Of course, being a monster, there were differences.

The most striking difference was the slime-like substance covering its shell.

The translucent liquid covered the coconut crab's shell while writhing.

It was as if the slime itself possessed a consciousness separate from the coconut crab.

"Still, it's definitely a coconut crab-type monster... But coconut crabs in the desert? What the hell?"

In Rei's understanding, coconut crabs were creatures that lived in places like tropical rainforests.

Or hot places like Okinawa.

This desert was a place that should be described as scorching rather than just hot, but moisture was critically scarce.

"Well, monsters don't follow common sense anyway. That's precisely why they're called monsters."

They differ greatly from ordinary animals, many possessing abilities beyond common sense.

...Though, monsters probably wouldn't want to hear that from Rei, who's even more unconventional than they are.

"Grrr?"

"Kill it?" Set turned his gaze to Rei, who was riding on his back. Rei nodded back as if it were obvious.

Having come seeking the magic stone of an unknown monster, it was only natural that upon finding this coconut crab-type monster, they'd decide to defeat it.

"Of course we kill it. The problem is how to attack it. A regular coconut crab would be manageable, but that one has its shell covered in something slime-like, and above all, it's enormous."

Without a point of comparison, it was hard to be precise, but based on past experience, the height from its legs to the top of its shell was estimated to be around three meters.

Being a coconut crab-type monster, its total length wasn't particularly surprising.

"Besides, I'm pretty sure coconut crabs are edible. ...Huh? Right? I'm sure I saw on TV that they're edible... Or did they have poison?"

With a hazy memory, Rei tried to recall the exact details of the TV show he'd seen before, but all he could remember was the scene of people eating coconut crabs.

To be precise, the coconut crab itself doesn't have a toxin like pufferfish, but the omnivorous coconut crab can become poisonous depending on what it eats.

Therefore, while palm crabs raised specifically for consumption are safe to eat, it's best not to eat wild ones.

Unaware of this, Rei tried recalling knowledge from the Monster Compendium, but unfortunately, this species wasn't listed.

"Guess we'll just have to try defeating it. We can ask the guild if it's edible or not. Set, who's gonna take it down?"

Both Rei and Set possessed the strength to easily defeat a monster like a coconut crab.

If there were multiple, it might be a different story, but with only one present like this, it posed no problem at all.

At Rei's question, Set growled, "I'll do it!"

Rei had intended to use the Twilight Spear if he took it on, but since Set wanted to do it, he figured he could let him handle it. He gave Set a light pat on the back.

"Got it. Well, I'd probably just get in the way if I stayed on your back, so I'll hop off for now. I'll distract that coconut crab while I'm at it."

Saying only that, Rei leapt off Set's back.

...Yes. Mid-air.

Leaping from Set's back at a height of about 100 meters above the ground, Rei naturally began falling toward the earth.

Hearing the sound of his own body slicing through the air, Rei suddenly recalled the time he fought that giant cicada.

(Come to think of it, I forgot to try that. If I did it now, the coconut crab would be scared into combat incapacitation... Even if I try it, it'll have to be later.)

Falling straight toward the ground, Rei contemplated this fact without the slightest hint of fear.

Even if the landing spot was desert, falling from a hundred meters up would obviously not end well.

Yet Rei felt no panic, because deep down, he knew he'd be fine.

His body, crafted by a being known as the demonic Zepair, was part of it. But above all...

(I have this, after all.)

mid-fall, he activated the Sleipnir Boots.

Instantly, Rei's descent halted, a slight jolt running through his body.

He kicked off from where he stopped, using the Sleipnir Boots repeatedly to descend onto the desert surface.

By the time he landed on the desert, most of Rei's falling speed had already been neutralized.

He could have landed on the desert completely silently, but he chose not to, aiming to draw the coconut crab's attention to himself.

Rei's plan worked perfectly. The coconut crab noticed Rei, who had landed a short distance away.

"Gyaaaaaaaaaaaah!"

"...Huh?"

That roar from the coconut crab caught Rei slightly off guard.

The sound, likely meant as a threatening roar, sounded undeniably like a human scream.

Still, just in case something happened, he drew the Death Scythe from the Misty Ring and readied it.

"Gyaaaaaaa!"

The cry, sounding like a scream, came again from the coconut crab.

(Seems that scream really was a roar after all. ...Honestly, it's a bit weird though.)

Holding the Death Scythe, I waited for the coconut crab to approach.

"GAAAAAAAAAAAAA!"

Along with another scream, the slime-like substance covering the coconut crab's shell transformed into whip-like tendrils and lashed out at Rei.

Having fully expected an attack using the giant claws the coconut crab possessed, a hint of surprise flickered across Rei's face.

Still, he had no intention of silently taking the direct hit from the multiple slime whips flying straight at him.

While the Dragon Robe's defense should have easily blocked them, Rei had no intention of relying on that.

"Flying Slash!"

The Flying Slash, now at Level 5 and possessing power incomparable to before, flew straight toward the slime's whip.

The slash easily sliced through the slime whip, sending it flying off in another direction.

"Gyaaah!?"

Even for the coconut crab, the result seemed unexpected. It let out a cry thick with surprise...

"GRRRRRRRR!"

With that roar, Set plummeted from above... no, descended.

The swing easily passed through the slime, now reduced in volume after the whip was sliced away, and came crashing down toward the shell beneath it.

Not a Power Crush... just a single strike.

Even so, the blow delivered by the Power Bracelet, Set's own physical prowess, and the momentum from a fall of roughly 100 meters from above easily shattered the coconut crab's shell.

The sound of destruction echoed around them.

To Rei, it was an irritating sound, but for the coconut crab, it was far from a mere annoyance.

After all, the shell that served as its last refuge, where it could hide, had been shattered.

"GAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA

Its original cry resembled a human scream.

But now that its shell had been destroyed, that scream could truly be called a scream in the literal sense.

Being a shell, it probably didn't feel physical pain, but having its last bastion destroyed must have caused immense psychological damage.

"GAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA!"

And naturally, the coconut crab, its shell destroyed, could not help but feel rage. It swung its massive claws in a backfist-like motion toward Set, who had just landed in the desert from the blow.

It understood that it was Set... a Griffin... standing there. Yet, consumed by rage, it had lost itself.

Under normal circumstances, knowing the opponent was a griffin would be reason enough to flee... but the coconut crab's rage was that intense.

"GRRRR!"

However, Set had anticipated the coconut crab launching such an attack and kicked off the sand to retreat backward.

"GRRRRRR!"

While still in the air, he swiftly activated Wind Arrow, releasing all fifteen arrows the instant he landed.

Fifteen wind arrows, true to their name, attacked the coconut crab at the speed of wind.

However, even without its shell, the coconut crab's claws and body were encased in sturdy carapace. Set's Wind Arrows, among his skills, were too low-powered to inflict a fatal wound.

Yet, even seeing the coconut crab like this, Set showed no sign of panic.

Wind Arrow was merely a diversionary strike; it was sufficient if it could halt the coconut crab's movement even slightly.

As the coconut crab's movement halted for an instant, Set opened his beak wide...

"GRRRR!"

With a roar, a fire breath erupted from his beak, striking the coconut crab head-on.

A savory aroma spread across the desert.

(Roasted crab, huh. ...I'm more of a shrimp guy though.)

Rei muttered, his stomach growling at the appetizing scent drifting around them, but of course, the battle continued unabated.

"Gyaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaah!"

A bloodcurdling scream erupted from the coconut crab as the ground simultaneously surged upward, blocking Set's fire breath.

"Grrrr..."

Set, observing this, growled as if amused.

How effective would his skills be against an opponent living in a real desert, not the dungeon in Exil?

Watching Set attack as if to confirm this, Rei looked around without seeming particularly moved.

Having witnessed the battle thus far, he judged Set's victory to be unshakable.

All around them stretched an endless sea of sand.

Though it was afternoon and the sun had dipped considerably, its direct rays still relentlessly baked the desert sand.

(I've seen people use a car hood heated by the summer sun to fry eggs, but stuff like that would probably be totally doable here.)

When I looked away from the battle, the distant landscape shimmered... a heat haze.

I didn't see any mirages, but just seeing the heat haze made it easy to understand just how hot it was.

"GRRRRRRRR!"

Rei turned his gaze toward Set at that cry and saw the sight of countless bubbles being released from its beak.

Bubble Breath—bubbles that burst into a sticky liquid.

Rei was slightly surprised that this skill appeared here.

But it wasn't a bad choice.

Having its shell shattered and then scorched by flames, the coconut crab was visibly enraged and incapable of rational thought.

Despite using the sand wall to block Set's line of sight, the fact that it hadn't moved at all from its spot was proof of that.

Watching from a distance, Rei could see the coconut crab's body tense with effort.

Just as Rei predicted, it was trying to destroy the sand wall to charge. As soon as the fire breath ended, the coconut crab shattered the wall, attempting to close the distance to Set. But the instant it destroyed the wall, a bubble breath struck it squarely in the face.

"GAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH

A battle cry? A scream? Whatever it was, the last thing the coconut crab saw was Set closing the distance and bringing his front legs down.

And then, the single blow of Power Crush effortlessly shattered the coconut crab's very life.
