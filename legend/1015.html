The sound of Zubo's right elbow snapping echoed around them.

The raw, visceral sound made the pain all too easy to imagine, causing several people to grimace.

But the monstrous form Zuboz had become now wouldn't be fazed by a broken right elbow.

Even using leverage, breaking the elbow of an arm as thick as Vihera's torso would normally warrant caution.

Yet, the moment Vihera broke the elbow bone, he immediately put distance between himself and Zuboz.

In fact, that action was by no means wrong. No, it could even be called the correct choice.

Because Zuboz, leaving his broken right arm as it was, forcibly swung his free left arm.

The left fist passed right where Vihera's face had been a moment before.

Though he seemed dissatisfied at the lack of impact, Zuboz regained his freedom of movement once Vihera vanished from atop him. He immediately stood up.

Though Vihera had moved away, Zuboz still aimed his right fist at the figure now nearby... but his elbow was broken, making it impossible to clench his fist.

"Gaaaah?"

Zuboz let out a puzzled cry, as if asking "Why?" but soon realized his right arm was barely moving.

He pressed his left hand against his right elbow... and in that instant, leapt high into the air.

The tip of Mirage's sword, arriving with a sound that sliced through the air, pierced the spot where Zuboz had been standing.

"Hey, Elena!"

Vihera, recognizing the weapon just by seeing the Mirage, shouted with obvious displeasure.

"This battle isn't being fought solely for Vihera's satisfaction. Besides, Vihera has already finished fighting that man over there. Considering that, if you get too greedy, you'll destroy yourself."

"...If you're going to say that, Elena, then you're the same... right..."

Vihera's words trailed off mid-sentence.

It was because she saw Elena using Sleipnir's boots to pin Adria's back down, rendering her unable to move.

Given that she possessed the attack method known as the Mirage of the Linked Sword, it wasn't unreasonable for Elena to attack from a distance.

But if she carelessly focused on attacking, she wouldn't be able to handle Adria if she tried to escape.

The solution Elena chose, based on that reasoning, was to simply make it impossible for Adria to escape in the first place.

(I get that, but...)

While shifting her gaze back to Zubo, who was clutching his right elbow, Vihera felt her fighting spirit drain away completely at the sight she'd just witnessed.

It felt like witnessing a woman's intimate affair.

Of course, Vihera understood the reality was vastly different.

After all, even now, Adria was struggling to escape from beneath Elena's feet, desperately trying to flee the scene.

Even so, it felt like being forced to witness sadistic pleasure, and Vihera's will to fight plummeted rapidly.

Seeing this, Rei shouted while keeping the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour active.

"Vihera, stand down! I'm better suited to deal with that one!"

"Got it!"

Feeling somewhat unsatisfied, Vihera leaps away from the spot, obeying Rei's command.

Not wanting to let Vihera escape, Zubo, whose right arm had already finished regenerating and was now fully functional, tried to strike her with it...

"Do you really think I'd let you get away with that?!"

Still standing on Adria, he swung Mirage.

The whip-like Mirage used its blades to restrain Zuboz, who was attempting to pursue Vihera.

The Mirage's blades sliced through Zubo's body one after another.

Because the longsword's blade had fragmented into multiple pieces, each individual blade was small.

But wielding them is Elena, the Princess General who inherited the Ancient Dragon's magic stone.

Rather than slicing with the blades, she delivers a single strike to Zuboz, smashing the whip-like blades against him.

"GAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA

Even when beaten, kicked, slashed by claws and blades, and subjected to the Immersion Palm, Zuboz had never let out such an irritated cry. This strike, however, seemed to have hit him hard.

Well, the damage inflicted on Zuboz wasn't that great, but it was a blow that purely and sharply stimulated his pain receptors.

The thought of pursuing Vihera had already vanished from Zuboz's mind. He fixed a gaze of pure hatred on Elena, the one who had inflicted this pain.

"Hey, hey, you hate me, don't you? So why go after Elena?"

"Gah!?"

Rei, still clad in the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, had somehow... truly somehow... appeared right beside Zubo.

The moment he noticed Rei's presence, perhaps due to the heightened instincts of his rampage, Zubo swung his fist at Rei.

Being so close to the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, Zubozu’s skin suffered severe burns visible to the naked eye.

Even so, his innate high regenerative ability began repairing the burned areas, but its speed gradually fell behind the heat radiating from the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour.

He caught the swinging fist with his left hand, the one not holding the Death Scythe.

Zuboz's swollen fist would normally have easily crushed Rei's body.

However, Rei currently had the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour deployed, greatly enhancing his physical capabilities.

Catching Zubo's fist with that hand posed absolutely no problem.

And Zuboz, whose fist was caught by Rei, let out a scream from the piercing pain that assaulted his fist.

"GAAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH

A burn that scorched his skin, seared his flesh, and turned the bones of his fist to charcoal.

Though it would normally regenerate, the heat was too intense for regeneration to keep up. Zubo's right arm turned to charcoal, fell to the ground, and shattered into powder that drifted into the sunset sky.

"You're beyond saving now. ...You're unconscious, aren't you? Then it's better for you to die than to live like this."

"Gaaaaaahhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhh

Sensing something dangerous in Rei's words, Zubo, driven by his runaway instincts, leapt backward with a great leap.

It was an attempt to gain distance from Rei, but it was far too reckless.

Rei, having activated the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, had indeed seen his physical abilities significantly enhanced. But he also possessed the long-range attack skill known as Deep Flame.

The crimson magic of the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour was hurled after Zubozo, who had gained distance.

The flame flew swiftly through the air, heading straight for Zubozu...

"Gwaaahhhhh!"

Instinctively sensing the danger of that crimson magic, Zuboz leapt again to create distance.

The Deep Flame that struck where Zubozu had been standing created a pillar of flame several meters tall at the impact site, rapidly raising the surrounding temperature.

Had Zubozu remained there, the heat would have undoubtedly incinerated his entire body.

Seeing it up close, even in his rampage... no, precisely because he was rampaging, Zubo instinctively felt the terror of death.

Judging that continuing to fight Rei would surely mean death, he decided to leave this spot for now...

"Not happening."

In that instant, Elena's Mirage transformed into a whip-like form, coiling around Zubo's leg.

It wasn't a pure whip, but a Mirage of a linked sword. Naturally, the section wrapped around Zubo's leg contained blades, slicing through the leg it touched.

"GAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA

Zuboz roared in irritation—was it from the pain in his legs, or because his escape attempt had been thwarted?

Regardless, Zoboz prioritized putting distance between himself and Rei over the pain in his legs and attempted to leap away.

At the end of the tentacles wrapped around its legs was a single woman.

Even in his rampage, Zuboz understood that much.

And that with his current power, it would be futile for a single woman to try to stop him.

...At this moment, a new intelligence was clearly beginning to form within Zuboz.

Not the intelligence Zoboz originally possessed, but a unique intelligence belonging to this entity—the former Zoboz, now rampaging and gigantic.

Of course, that intelligence wasn't entirely free from Zubo's influence.

No, since it was based on Zoboz's body, that influence was undoubtedly present.

But...even so, the newly born intelligence failed to notice the strangeness of the one at the tip of the weapon wrapped around its own leg... Elena.

"Gwooooooooooooooah!"

Zuboz kicked off the ground, trying to forcibly drag Elena along with the Mirage wrapped around his legs. But the next instant, he felt a weight as if he were chained to the earth, and was dragged down to the ground.

"Gurraa? Gurrooah!"

He couldn't comprehend what had just happened.

Zuboz let out a confused cry and looked around.

Then, seeing Elena holding whatever was tangled around his legs, completely unfazed, he was struck by even greater confusion.

Why was his power being defeated by a single woman who was vastly smaller than him?

Zuboz looked utterly baffled, but Elena pulled the Mirage along as if it were perfectly natural.

Then, Zuboz's body was dragged along the ground, forcibly moved toward Elena.

"Gaaaaaahhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhh

But... Elena had inherited the Ancient Dragon's magic stone, and her physical capabilities were now so powerful that they were no match for Zubo's current state.

Unaware of this, Zuboz desperately stomped the ground, clung on, and tried to escape Elena...

"Haven't you forgotten about me?"

Had he been in a complete frenzy, he might have instinctively sensed Rei's presence, but his lingering intelligence likely played a part.

The combat reflexes stirring sluggishly within its body could do nothing but endure the blow of the Death Scythe.

"Gah... GAAAAAH?! Gah!"

Zuboz let out a momentary sound of bewilderment at the strike's sharpness, but quickly gave way to a scream.

The sensation of its torso shifting must have been unknown even to Zuboz.

It wasn't pain, but it felt hot.

Perhaps it stemmed from the fact that Zuboz had admired Kabajido, a person of the same origin as himself.

The upper half of its body collapsed to the ground, still trying to move even as its entrails spilled out.

Being near the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, the blood evaporated and the organs burned away.
