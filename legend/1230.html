Opening her eyes, the first thing that came into Vihera's field of vision was a skeleton.

Not just any skeleton, but one that instantly conveyed the presence of a powerful monster.

"What!?"

Vihera, who had assumed it was Rei who had saved her, instantly leapt to her feet and tried to put distance between them...

「Vihera！」

The sudden voice made her freeze.

If asked why, it was because the voice sounded familiar.

The voice of the one who reminded her she was a woman, back when she cared only for battle.

There was no way she could forget that man's voice.

"Rei, what the hell is going on...?"

Vihera tried to ask Rei while sharply watching the skeleton ahead, but seeing that the undead monster didn't seem to care about her at all, she couldn't help but ask suspiciously.

The undead creature seemed to be performing some task. Something was floating in the air near where she had been sleeping moments ago, and from it poured down what could only be called a rain of light.

And the one being showered by this rain of light was also someone familiar to Vihera.

"...Um, who is that again?"

She recognized the face, but unfortunately, Rhodes seemed to have left no trace in Vihera's memory.

Seeing Vihera tilt her head and mutter, those who knew Rhodes' feelings felt pity.

"It's Rhodes. Rhodes."

"Ah. The Axe of the Thunder God."

That alone was enough to show just what place Rhodes held in Vihera's mind.

Anyway... Vihera averted her gaze from Rhodes, where a rain of light was falling, and looked around once more.

There, besides Rei, were many other familiar faces to Vihera.

Elena, Marina, Ara, Elk, Min, Johanna... and Johanna's companions as well.

Also visible, though not human, were figures like Set and Yellow.

"So, could you tell me what the hell just happened?"

In Vihera's mind, his memories ended at the battle with Ambris.

It was no wonder he was puzzled, given he'd woken up to find himself in this situation.

But... when she was met with that gaze, Rei found herself at a loss for words.

He was glad Vihera's consciousness had returned... but that wasn't the only reason he couldn't speak.

"Vihera, your eye color...?"

At Rei's muttered words, Elena and the others also turned their gaze to Vihera once more, immediately understanding.

Even those who were fairly close to Vihera—like Rei, Elena, Marina—noticed the difference from the eye color they had seen before.

"Huh? Eye color?"

But, naturally, Vihera herself didn't notice that her own eye color had changed.

Still, if asked why her eyes had changed color, she could vaguely understand.

"It's Ambris, isn't it?"

「……Ambris？」

It was Marina who asked back at Vihera's words.

"You used the Silver Lion's heart to deal with Ambris, right? If it's affecting you, then I think it's the Silver Lion's heart. After all, your eyes are silver now."

"Silver Lion?"

Next to tilt her head in confusion was, unsurprisingly, Vihera.

But she had heard the name Silver Lion before from Rei. Panicking, she scanned her surroundings. Her gaze landed on a massive corpse... far too large to be described as a lion.

Its fur was silver, and Vihera could easily imagine that this was the monster called the Silver Lion.

"So, this is the dungeon near Gilm?"

"Correct. You should be thankful, you know? I defeated an S-rank monster to save Vihera."

"...I suppose so."

We defeated an S-rank monster.

Hearing that, Vihera's first thought was why she hadn't been there herself.

Vihera was someone who loved combat. An S-rank monster was nothing short of enviable.

As if understanding Vihera's thoughts, Elena murmured with a hint of exasperation.

"The fight with the Silver Lion was brutal in every sense, you know? Even Rei got injured. Why don't you at least say thanks?"

"...You're right. Thank you, everyone. For taking care of things for me."

Though Vihera said this, she couldn't hide the lingering envy she felt.

"If Vihera had been there, we could have fought the Silver Lion more advantageously."

Marina, who understood Vihera's quirks, murmured with a hint of exasperation.

"True. We actually had a surprisingly tough time with the Silver Lion's fur. Honestly, I can't count how many times I wished Vihera was here."

Rei murmured in agreement with Marina, and Elena and the others nodded.

Vihera's Immersion Palm, which delivers shock directly into the body, was arguably the worst possible matchup for the Silver Lion, whose fur provided high physical and magical defense.

But at the same time, a single strike from the Silver Lion's forepaws or tail would undoubtedly be a fatal blow to Vihera as well.

They were each other's natural enemies. That's what Rei thought, but recalling the battle with the Silver Lion, he doubted Vihera's Immersion Palm could inflict damage that easily.

(Vihera's attacks would probably inflict damage, but defensively, we'd be at a huge disadvantage.)

A short distance away from Rei and the others lost in such thoughts, Elena and Marina were explaining to Vihera the current situation.

Meanwhile, within the magic circle, Rodos lay still as the light rain generated by the Silver Lion's heart continued to pour down upon him.

Still feeling a dull ache throughout his body, Rei, who didn't want to move much, lightly stroked Set, who had come near him alongside Yellow.

"Grrr."

"Don't worry. It's not that serious. The potion healed the wound on my face, and the others should heal soon enough."

He said it as if trying to convince himself, but upon hearing this, Set tilted his head slightly, as if asking, "Really?"

For Set, the blood-soaked sight he'd seen after the battle with the Silver Lion was far from something he could calmly look at.

It was something he'd rather never have seen at all.

"Forget about me. How are you, Set? You took that Silver Lion's tail attack twice in a row at the end, right? ...Nah, guess I don't need to ask."

Running a hand over Set's body, he could immediately tell there were no scars.

In fact, not even a trace of blood remained on his fur.

Who had wiped it away was obvious without needing to think about it.

Because there was only one person present who could be called addicted to Set.

In fact, Rei had seen him head straight for Set the moment he entered the room.

While waiting for Rhodes to regain consciousness, playing with Set, they must have finished explaining the situation. Elena, Vihera, and Marina, followed shortly by Ara, approached Rei.

"I heard about the situation. ...I never imagined Rei would have an undead acquaintance. Well, considering Rei's personality, I suppose it's not surprising at all."

Vihera asked with a smile, and Rei returned a wry smile.

Rei understood all too well that he was, in many ways, special.

"More importantly, Vihera, is there something wrong with your body? Your... your eye color seems different."

"Seems so. ...But other than that, nothing else. No physical changes or anything like that."

Vihera demonstrated by moving her body lightly, and as she said, there were no apparent problems.

"In fact, I feel better than ever! Could this be... the influence of Ambris? Or perhaps the Silver Lion?"

「Ambris？」

Hearing Vihera's words, Rei tilted his head.

He couldn't understand why that name had come up now.

Of course, he knew Ambris was the reason Vihera hadn't regained consciousness this time. More than anything, Rei had seen Ambris enter Vihera's body with his own eyes.

But considering Vihera's current words, it almost felt like she was saying her physical condition was good thanks to Ambris.

That impression wasn't just Rei's; it seemed the others, including Elena and Marina, felt the same way.

Meeting the questioning gazes directed at her, Vihera flashed a small smile before speaking.

"It's true that I kept fighting Ambris inside me. In fact, I had cornered Ambris, but from there, there was nothing I could do. She was locked away within her own core, and I simply didn't have enough offensive power to defeat her."

Of course, since it was within the spiritual realm, it wasn't actual physical attack power, Vihera clarified.

They probably hadn't experienced it themselves. Elena and Marina nodded, seemingly unable to grasp the essence of Vihera's words.

In contrast, Rei, who had enjoyed reading manga and novels while in Japan, could vaguely imagine what Vihera meant.

"Honestly, I have absolutely no sense that nearly two months have passed since I was knocked unconscious by Ambris. Time felt terribly vague in the spiritual world. If someone told me only a single day had passed since I lost consciousness, I'd believe them."

Vihera murmured in wonder, though this was likely also connected to the fact that her body hadn't deteriorated at all.

Under normal circumstances, two months of unconsciousness would cause muscle atrophy.

At the very least, one couldn't possibly claim to be walking freely like this immediately after regaining consciousness... or even express regret at not being able to participate in the battle against the Silver Lion.

Well, he might be able to say it, but maintaining the physical condition to actually fight the Silver Lion right away would be nearly impossible.

But what made it possible was Vihera's state while unconscious.

Vihera's condition suggested that Ambris's magic was at work.

"Ah, I digressed. Anyway, I was fighting Ambris in the spirit world, but I couldn't break through his final defense. Then, suddenly, power surged forth... As a result, Ambris must have judged that he would vanish if things continued like this."

Vihera shrugged her shoulders slightly, a smile playing on her lips.

A combative smile, typical of Vihera.

And yet, somehow, the impression she gave was different from before.

"With his annihilation certain, Ambris chose to be absorbed by me—to leave behind some proof he had ever existed. The irony that Ambris, who tried to absorb me, ended up being absorbed by me... well, that's rather amusing, isn't it?"

"...So, Vihera absorbed Ambris's magic? Are you okay? Ambris was born from abnormal magic, right? Normally, that just sounds like it would be bad for your body."

Vihera nodded reassuringly at Rei, who was looking at her with concern.

"There's no need to worry about that. Like I said, Ambris wanted to leave behind proof of his existence. Do you think he'd do anything to me now that I've obtained that proof? ...Well, it was only possible because of the sudden surge of power."

Without using the Silver Lion's heart, Vihera couldn't have dealt with Ambris's core.

In other words, the current Vihera had effectively inherited not only Ambris's power, but even that of the Silver Lion.

Though perhaps it could be called a different kind of inheritance compared to Elena inheriting the Ancient Dragon's magic stone.

To begin with, the Silver Lion's magic stone was within Rei's Misty Ring. What Vihera inherited was the Silver Lion's heart... its fresh, living blood and magic power.

"Considering that, the only thing that changed was the color of her eyes, huh?"

"That's right. I thought there'd be more obvious changes. Like, wings sprouting, horns or fangs growing, or a third eye appearing on my forehead."

"...What exactly do you intend to become?"

It was only natural that Rei muttered this in response to Vihera's words.

"What? Well, what exactly? I suppose it's certain that I can now live alongside Rei."

As Vihera said this and smiled happily, the rain of light showering Rodos vanished... and her eyes opened, almost simultaneously.
