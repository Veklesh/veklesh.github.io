Riding on Set's back as he charged skyward, Rei saw an enemy far larger than he had anticipated.

He had mentally dubbed it a giant cicada, so naturally he expected its size to be extraordinary.

Moreover, unlike Set, who was two meters tall, Rei was only about 160 centimeters tall.

In contrast, the giant cicada measured about twenty meters in total length.

The difference in size was starkly obvious.

(I saw it up close when it was about to emerge from the World Tree, and I attacked it. I even saw it emerge and fly away from the World Tree. Even so... seeing it like this, it really is huge.)

On Set's back, he gripped the hilt of the Death Scythe tightly.

The giant cicada had already confirmed Set's presence, directing its emotionless eyes toward Set... and Rei riding on his back.

"GIGIGIGIGI!"

The giant cicada moved first.

It unleashed a sonic blade of ultrasonic waves at Set and Rei.

"Gururururu!"

But for Set, who had seen this ultrasonic attack many times before, evading it was no difficult task.

He flapped his wings wide, creating distance from the directional ultrasonic range.

However, the giant cicada seemed to understand from past experience that Set could evade its ultrasonic attacks. It fired a spear of earth toward the spot where Set had dodged.

The spear, launched after reading his evasion path, aimed straight for Set's body, but...

"Flying Slash!"

The Flying Slash unleashed from atop Set's back sliced through the earthen spear cleanly.

"I'd hate for you to forget I'm here. Besides, the reason Set sacrificed even a little mobility to carry me on his back... I'll show you why!"

Another Flying Slash is unleashed.

The giant cicada countered by emitting ultrasonic waves, but even those sonic blades couldn't completely nullify the Flying Slash's strike.

Rei thought it would hit the giant cicada directly, but in reality, a barrier was created just before impact, blocking the Flying Slash.

"Damn. So it can still be used in this state... Yeah, I figured."

Since it was merely a test strike, the fact that the Flying Slash was blocked didn't particularly shock him.

Still, it was undeniably frustrating, and he immediately moved to his next action.

"Set, get on top of that giant cicada and drop me off. Then, while diving down separately from me, attack the giant cicada. I'll attack while moving through the air using Sleipnir's boots."

"GRRRR!?"

Dangerous, Set growled frantically.

But Rei stroked Set's neck as he circled, watching for the giant cicada's opening, and spoke.

"Don't worry. When we were in the World Tree, the forest was too close to use it, but here, I can use the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour. ...Well, I'll have to choose where to drop him carefully, though."

Since the forest lay below, he couldn't carelessly use the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour to drop it anywhere.

Considering the danger of the forest being burned by a single swing, they needed to bring the giant cicada down in an area with as few flammable objects as possible.

While dodging the giant cicada's ultrasonic waves and earth spears, and watching Set counterattack with water balls, wind arrows, and ice arrows, I turned my gaze toward the ground.

Somewhere to drop the giant cicada... a giant cicada twenty meters long, engulfed in flames... somewhere it could be safely slammed onto the ground...

But such a vast, open space simply doesn't exist...

(What should I do? What should I do?)

While Rei scanned the ground searching for a suitable location for the battle against the giant cicada, the fight between Set and the giant cicada continued.

He had to find a place where he could knock the giant cicada down as quickly as possible... Just as he was thinking this, the orb of light that had remained by Rei's side throughout the battle suddenly began to flicker violently.

Though Rei and Set were busy moving about in the air, the orb flew toward Rei as if to say it didn't care about that.

"What is it?"

As Rei turned his gaze toward the sphere in response to the flashing... suddenly, a scene came into view where part of the ground seemed to be pulsating.

The answer to his question became clear almost immediately.

The countless trees that had stood in the forest on the ground moved as if forcibly relocated, creating a bare clearing where no trees or anything else remained.

Not far from the Dark Elf settlement... a bare patch roughly the same size as the settlement itself had appeared, about a twenty-minute walk away as the crow flies.

"...Is this your doing?"

As Set tilted his body to dodge the earth spear, Rei also shifted sideways, still keeping the light sphere close as he asked.

In response to Rei's words, the light orb flickered.

(Even if it is the World Tree... does it possess power this vast? Moving just me would be one thing, but moving other trees growing in the forest too? No, the cleared area is barren land with nothing growing in the soil, no animals either. That means this orb moved not just trees, but grass, animals, everything together.)

Witnessing this act, Rei understood why the World Tree was almost worshipped by the Dark Elves.

The power the World Tree displayed now, fully restored and not weakened... it left Rei with nothing but astonishment.

But feeling Set, the beast he rode, shift its body swiftly, Rei snapped back to reality.

(Right. Now is not the time to be stunned in shock, but to act.)

The current threat wasn't the World Tree's abilities, but the giant cicada rampaging in the sky.

That giant cicada must have noticed the disturbance on the ground as well. The attacks directed at Rei and Set were clearly slower compared to before.

The attacks firing earth spears were slightly slower, and their accuracy was off.

While the area-of-effect sonic attacks suffered no major issues even when slightly off-target, the fact remained that the attacks had slowed.

However, the cleared field incident wasn't entirely advantageous for Rei and the others.

Watching the giant cicada's movements while clinging to Set, Rei caught sight of several Dark Elves on the ground heading towards the cleared area at the edge of his vision.

It was only natural. For the Dark Elves, experiencing the ground suddenly shift while fighting monsters was terrifying.

Having experienced the World Tree nearly withering and the barrier's protective field being destroyed, even temporarily, in such a short time, they naturally worried this incident might also work against them.

He couldn't overlook such a factor.

But even if Rei shouted from here, the dark elves wouldn't hear him. And he certainly couldn't tell them to descend to the ground and move away while the giant cicada and Set were locked in combat.

Rei could do nothing but pray he wouldn't get caught in the crossfire when the giant cicada was knocked down, and actually call out to them if the fight ended up on level ground.

(The Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour might be immobilizing them... but that's a gamble.)

Steeling his resolve, Rei tapped the back of Set's neck and spoke.

"Listen, Set. Like I said before, you fly up to him and knock me off. I'll use Sleipnir's boots to launch a full-on attack. Given his size, I doubt one hit will finish him, but I'll at least slice off his wings. You handle his counterattack from below."

"GRRRR!"

Set growled fiercely at Rei's words.

Though he still worried for Rei, he must have decided that the giant cicada, now a powerful monster after absorbing Rei's magic, required some recklessness to defeat.

Confirming this, Rei swung the Death Scythe to draw the giant cicada's attention even slightly.

"Flying Slash!"

The flying slash streaked straight toward the giant cicada.

Though Flying Slash had dramatically increased in power upon reaching Level 5, its main drawback was its trajectory—it could only fly in a straight line.

On the ground, evasion options like trees or rocks were limited, but in the aerial battlefield, there was no shortage of places to dodge.

The giant cicada used its four wings to slightly alter its flight path, evading the Flying Slash that came straight at it.

Rei, whose strike had been evaded, showed no particular sign of frustration.

After all, this strike was merely a feint; if the giant cicada's evasion slowed its movement even for an instant, that was sufficient.

"GRRRRRR!"

The instant he saw the giant cicada change course, Set charged upward into the sky, using the King's Intimidation.

The King's Intimidation slightly slowed the giant cicada's movements.

Set didn't expect the King's Intimidation to stop it completely, given how powerful it had become after fully absorbing Rei's magic.

But even so, it was possible to slow it down... just a little. And that little bit was all Set wanted.

Flailing his wings, Set arrived directly above the giant cicada. From his back, Rei leapt down.

"Set, I'll handle the rest."

"Guruu!"

Falling through the air, Rei called out to Set. In response, Set flapped his wings and began moving toward the ground.

The speed at which he passed the free-falling Rei made her briefly fear he might crash straight into the ground.

But in the next instant, he shook that thought from his mind and activated his skill... not Death Scythe, but his own unique skill.

The magical energy around Rei thickened, condensed, and intensified.

Soon, it thickened to the point of becoming visible... and due to Rei's attribute, the surrounding temperature rose rapidly.

The giant cicada, sensing this danger, shifted its focus away from Set, who was passing right beside it and heading downward, and turned toward Rei instead.

Given that the aerial battle had been focused solely on fighting Set until now, this decision must have been unexpected even for the giant cicada.

But even so... the threat it sensed from Rei was absolutely not something it could overlook.

Even before the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, it didn't cower and freeze—perhaps because it harbored Rei's magical power within itself.

"Giiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiii

Ultrasonic waves fired toward Rei.

Having gained Rei's magical power, these ultrasonic waves possessed several characteristics impossible for ordinary ones...

BOOM!

The moment it touched the crimson magic clinging to Rei's body, it was incinerated—even though it wasn't even a physical substance like sound waves.

"Gigi!?"

The giant cicada clearly hadn't anticipated its own ultrasonic waves being incinerated. It let out a cry of surprise... but in the next instant, multiple earth spears materialized around the giant cicada and were launched toward Rei, who was falling toward it.

Seeing the earth spears falling toward him mid-air, Rei swung down his left hand, the one not gripping the Death Scythe.

Simultaneously, fragments of the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour that had enveloped Rei tore away in multiple pieces, becoming fist-sized as they flew out to intercept the earth spears.

Deep Flame. One of the skills utilizing the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, a skill that manifests the flames Rei imagines.

The fist-sized Deep Flames flew ahead of Rei, while the earthen spear flew ahead of the giant cicada.

Each collided near the center of the space between Rei and the giant cicada... The instant it saw this, the giant cicada abruptly changed direction using its four wings.

Was it the giant cicada's instinct as a monster, or was it simply a meaningless turn of events? Rei couldn't tell, but it soon became clear that the action was the right one.

The fist-sized deep flame and the earth spear collided, but the earth spear was consumed by the flame in less than a second.

Worse still, even as it consumed the spear of earth, the deep flame continued its charge unabated... passing right through the spot where the giant cicada's body had been mere seconds before.

Had the giant cicada not changed course, the deep flame would undoubtedly have struck it.

Watching the scene while falling, Rei confirmed the giant cicada was not at the intended target location. Yet, showing no sign of panic, he activated Sleipnir's Boots.

Using the Sleipnir Boots to treat the air itself as a foothold, he leapt mid-fall toward a different direction.

The giant cicada was, of course, waiting there.

Apparently not expecting such a move as kicking off the air itself, the giant cicada froze for just an instant... That single instant was more than enough of an opening for Rei, who had deployed the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour.

Before he knew it, Rei, who should have still been a short distance away, was directly above the giant cicada.

Using his enhanced physical abilities from the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, he kicked off the air again with Sleipnir’s boots, moving directly above the giant cicada. He used that spot as a foothold too, moving with the technique of a triangle jump.

(This should work.)

His initial target had been the wings, but if he could finish it with this strike, that was preferable.

He shifted his target from the wings to the torso... and swung his magic-infused Death Scythe down, aiming for the giant cicada's body.

With the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour deployed, the giant cicada surely hadn't anticipated that movement. Yet... whether due to the magic nurtured by Rei or a wild instinct sensing mortal danger... it forced its body to move, successfully evading what should have been a fatal blow.

Yet it couldn't completely evade the Reaper's blade. The two wings on the giant cicada's right side were severed midway.
