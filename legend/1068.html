"GRRRRRRRRRR!"

The first thing Set did after chasing the giant cicada was to use the King's Intimidation.

The moment he unleashed the King's Intimidation, pain shot through his body.

If Set had any healing skills or magic items, he might have been able to manage, but unfortunately, he possessed none.

Still, being a high-rank monster like a griffin, his natural healing ability was indeed higher than other monsters. For now, he could only rely on that.

Lower-ranked monsters might even lose consciousness outright if they were unlucky.

Even if they didn't, they often became paralyzed with fear and unable to move.

But... that only applied to low-rank monsters.

The giant cicada was, of course, a monster Set had never seen before, but it was certainly not a monster that could be called low-rank.

Low-rank monsters simply couldn't inflict injuries on someone like Rei . The giant cicada was clearly a high-rank monster from the start.

Therefore, even upon hearing the King's intimidating roar unleashed from Set's mouth, it continued flying through the sky, feeling not the slightest bit of discomfort.

"GIGIGIGIGIGIGI!"

No, worse still, it immediately launched a counterattack.

"GRRRRRR!"

Grumbling, Set flapped his wings and instantly moved away from the spot.

The usual Set would have dodged the enemy's attack at the last moment. And he would have launched a counterattack.

But... that tactic wouldn't work against this giant cicada.

Given that the giant cicada's attack method was an invisible ultrasonic assault, one that covered a wide area, dodging it at the last second while landing a counterattack was no easy feat, even for Set.

"Giiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiii

"Gurrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrr

Set dodged the giant cicada's attack—a shockwave of ultrasonic waves released across the entire area—by angling his body, then fired Wind Arrows.

Multiple wind arrows flew straight toward the giant cicada, but it used its four wings to forcibly change their direction and evade them.

Perhaps due to its four wings, in terms of aerial maneuverability and agility, the giant cicada and Set were nearly evenly matched.

"Grrr..."

While dodging the repeated ultrasonic attacks, Set gradually gained composure.

Having seen the attacks many times, he had come to understand the range and power of the giant cicada's ultrasonic assaults.

Normally, it would have taken more time to read an enemy's attack... especially one combining invisible blades and shockwaves.

Yet, whether driven by his griffin's fighting instincts, Set had already begun to read these attacks.

Considering the giant cicada's abilities alone, it was strange that the gap between it and Set had grown this wide.

The giant cicada, driven by something akin to instinct, voiced its frustration at this discrepancy in a chirping cry.

"Giiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiii

"GRRRRRRRRRR!"

They moved violently around each other in the air, unleashing their respective attacks.

The giant cicada unleashed ultrasonic shockwaves, while Set fired attacks of water, wind, ice, and flame.

To an uninformed observer, the sight of these forces colliding mid-air might have seemed almost beautiful.

But what was actually happening was an exchange of devastatingly powerful attacks. Anyone who dared enter that space risked losing their life.

Proof of this came when Set dodged an attack the giant cicada unleashed in the air, only for dozens of goblins on the ground to be sliced apart simultaneously by ultrasonic blades, their screams of agony piercing the air.

"Gluu!?"

Seeing this, Set realized that if things continued like this, the Dark Elves fighting the monsters—and above all, the Dark Elf settlement itself—might suffer damage. He spread his wings wide.

He had to get away from this settlement.

And the giant cicada, unable to leave behind a being powerful enough to defeat it, gave chase.

Twenty meters long versus two meters.

The exact measurements might vary slightly, but even so, the giant cicada was nearly ten times Set's size.

Two creatures of that size were seriously playing a game of tag.

Naturally, those on the ground couldn't miss it.

"Hey! Isn't that Set?!"

"Yeah, I guess..."

In one corner of the battlefield, Earth shouted while firing arrows at a monster that had strayed from the fight between monsters and was heading toward the settlement.

The one who answered Earth was Marina, her complexion pale despite her brown skin.

Normally, she excelled at rapid-fire, releasing arrows one after another without pause, but there was no trace of that skill in Marina now.

Her arrows were piercing monsters like goblins, but her movements were clearly sluggish, hardly resembling the once-famed, formidable adventurer she was known to be.

"Haa, haa, haa..."

Gasping for breath after each shot, Marina still kept firing her bow.

For Marina, whose bloodline held a strong connection to the World Tree, the current state of the World Tree itself, teetering between life and death, was taking a significant toll on her.

Moving a single finger consumed the kind of stamina that would normally be required to run at full speed.

No joke, that was Marina's current state.

Even so, she had to keep killing the monsters to prevent them from reaching the settlement.

To that end, she was desperately mustering her strength and willpower to shoot arrows, but even that was nearing its limit.

If the monsters gathering around here had all charged toward the settlement now that its barrier had vanished, she wouldn't have been able to stop them.

"Marina, shouldn't you go back inside the settlement for now?"

The dark elf woman drawing her bow beside Marina said this with concern.

Her casual tone likely stemmed from having looked after Marina when she was young.

Being that kind of person, she naturally understood Marina's deep connection to the World Tree and knew the current state of the World Tree was far from secure.

"It's okay, it's okay... I'll manage somehow. Right now, just focus on keeping the monsters out of the settlement."

Saying this, Marina, whose body was already nearly drained of strength, abandoned her bow attacks and switched to spirit magic.

This drained her magic power, but given that she still had more magic left than physical stamina, she judged it to be the more efficient option.

'Wind spirit, hear my wish...'

Marina spoke aloud to the spirit, but suddenly froze.

"Marina!?"

The dark elf woman, who had been watching Marina, cried out in panic while desperately firing arrows.

Her panic stemmed from understanding Marina's true power as a spirit magic user.

Under normal circumstances, Marina almost never stopped casting spirit magic midway.

Which meant something significant had happened...

"...Huh?"

That something was revealed.

Not as despair, but as hope.

The barrier that had vanished just moments ago, like shattering glass, was rebuilt.

Suddenly... as if its earlier disappearance had been a lie.

"What... on earth...?"

It wasn't just the Dark Elves who were shocked by the sight. Earth, who had been desperately drawing his bow and firing arrows, was equally stunned.

"Polo-ro-ro-ro!"

Each of the nine tails unleashed purple lightning, simultaneously defeating nine monsters. Poro shook his head, seemingly unable to comprehend what had just happened.

Probably... no, undoubtedly, Marina was the only one who understood what was happening in the present situation.

It was something only Marina, deeply connected to the World Tree, could comprehend.

And Marina understood who had caused it, through the magic flowing from the World Tree through the earth.

「Rei……」

Without even realizing it, Marina found her cheeks wet with tears.

Why the tears flowed, Marina herself did not know. Yet, it was a fact that they flowed unceasingly.

Within the magic flowing from the World Tree, Marina sensed Rei. Her being filled with Rei's magic.

It brought Marina an absolute sense of security, as if she were being embraced by Rei himself...

"Heh heh, I never imagined... I was drawn to him, but for him to enter me this completely all at once... I can't let go now."

The magic power she sensed emanating from the barrier's protective field reflected in Marina's gaze was the same magic power now filling her body.

In other words, Marina now felt enveloped by Rei from both within and without.

And a smile appeared on Marina's lips.

Marina had smiled as a woman before, but the smile now on her face was more distinctly feminine, a lustrous smile.

With a smile that seemed similar to her previous ones yet was fundamentally different, Marina drew an arrow from her quiver.

Just minutes ago, even the slightest movement had drained Marina of immense physical strength. Yet now, the way she held her bow made it impossible to believe that was the case.

Rather, she seemed brimming with vitality.

She released the arrow from her hand... The arrow flew straight, piercing the orc's head. The orc, who had been fighting several kobolds, collapsed to the ground.

"Heh heh, heh heh heh... Power... power is overflowing!"

Right now, she felt an omnipotence, as if she could take on every monster before her and handle them all by herself.

Earth, who had been desperately attacking monsters nearby, watched Marina's state with a look of astonishment.

To Earth, Marina embodied the very essence of a mature woman.

That mature woman was now radiating an even more intense aura of sensuality than usual. It was only natural that a man of Earth's age would be captivated.

"Wind Spirit, hear my words and cleave the enemy with your blade!"

The wind spirit immediately granted her wish at the clear voice sung aloud.

The magic power filling Marina's entire body was of the highest grade, even for the spirits.

Obeying that magic, the wind spirit unleashed countless blades to fulfill Marina's wish.

Before Marina, Earth, Polo, and the many other Dark Elves watching, the blades sliced through countless monsters.

While not as wide-ranging as Rei's fire whirl, the monsters surrounding Marina and the others were nonetheless wounded to varying degrees by this single strike.

"I-It's incredible..."

For Earth, who couldn't use magic, the sheer effectiveness of a single strike could only be described as astonishing.

Earth's partner, Polo, also specialized in wide-area attacks using lightning, but even so, the lightning Polo unleashed came from each of his nine tails, and nine was his limit.

But... Marina's wind blades, due to their sheer power, dealt only moderate damage to many monsters. As a result, they ended up drawing the attention of the surrounding monsters.

"Hey, Marina-chan!? What are you doing!?"

The dark elf woman exclaimed, realizing the monsters' attention had shifted to them. Marina, however, smiled and spoke.

"It's fine. If anything, having them focus their attention here makes the fight easier."

Marina's radiant smile might have resembled Vihera's .

But unlike Vihera, who smiled because she enjoyed fighting, Marina was enveloped in Rei's presence both inside and out... no, she felt Rei's existence so close, as if they were one and the same, that she smiled.

It resembled Vihera's smile, yet was fundamentally different.

With that smile still on her face, Marina, still connected to Rei through the World Tree and the ground, glanced at the approaching monsters and spoke to the spirit.

'Water spirit, hear my wish. Bring water that washes away all things here.'

As soon as she spoke, water materialized from nowhere.

Normally, water spirit magic achieves its greatest effect only in places where water exists.

But Marina now held Rei's magical power within her body. Even in a desert, she could likely make rain fall.

Compared to that, using water spirit magic in a forest abundant with water was hardly difficult.

The volume of water that appeared was truly enough to cover the heavens.

This was not only due to harboring Rei's magic power, but also purely because of Marina's high skill as an elemental mage.

The other Dark Elves present, being able to use spirit magic themselves, understood all too well just how unbelievable what Marina was doing was.

Even the Earth elves, who couldn't wield spirit magic, could grasp that what Marina was doing now defied common sense.

Under the gaze of those around her, Marina showed no sign of tension whatsoever... Yet, smiling happily at her current state, sharing Rei's magic power, she swung her hand downward.

In that instant, every monster present was swept away by the water. In the torrent, monsters collided with each other and crashed into trees, nearly all losing their lives. The few monsters that survived were left with injuries that could be called severe, only to be hunted down by the Dark Elves and Earth who had regained their senses.
