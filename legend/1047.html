After searching the forest for several hours, Rei , Set, and the two who joined them along the way—Earth and Polo—returned to the settlement without finding anything particularly unusual.

The only real "harvest" was a handful of edible nuts and berries they gathered.

"Hmm, this berry's sour, but it's got an addictive taste."

Rei murmured happily, nibbling on a purple and black spotted fruit about the size of a fist.

At first glance, its appearance hadn't suggested it was edible, but after seeing Earth confidently declare it safe to eat and actually demonstrate by eating one in front of Rei, Rei couldn't doubt his word.

When he actually popped one into his mouth, a refreshingly sweet-and-sour taste—though the sourness was stronger—spread across his palate.

Pleased with the fruit, Rei picked about half of what was there.

This too was only possible because of the Misty Ring, and Earth looked at Rei with envy.

"Gururuu"

Set, who was also eating fruit beside Rei, purred happily.

Despite the fruit's strong sourness, it seemed to suit Set's taste as well.

Set had been aiming for Aerowings, but unfortunately, he hadn't been able to find any. Still, his good mood was likely due to the fruit he was eating now.

Anyway, as they continued eating fruit, they eventually arrived before the barrier's protective field.

"Hm!"

Already waiting there, eagerly anticipating Rei and the others' arrival, were Elena , Vihera , Marina, Ara , Byune , and Yellow.

Seeing Rei, who arrived last, leisurely eating fruit, naturally didn't sit well with those who had been left behind.

Earth gave Marina a small bow, likely related to having split off earlier to pursue Polo.

Among them, Byune—the most particular about food—immediately protested the moment she saw Rei.

"Here, eat this instead."

Rei tossed the same fruit he'd taken from the Misty Ring toward Byune.

Upon catching it, Byune paused for a moment at the fruit's appearance, but the next instant, he bit into it.

"Mmm!"

A sudden wave of sourness flooded her mouth. And faintly... yet undeniably, a sweetness existed.

The taste of the fruit, unimaginable from its appearance, made even the usually expressionless Byune give off a slightly pleased vibe.

"...So, you said Earth parted ways with Marina partway through, but he ended up meeting up with Rei. Seems he didn't find any clues after all."

Elena took the fruit from Rei and asked.

"Yeah. I was sure we'd run into some ferocious monsters, but we didn't encounter a single one. The only thing we got was that fruit."

Laying out the fruit for Marina, Vihera, Ara, and Yellow, Rei informed them. The others nodded in agreement.

"We were talking about this before you arrived. None of us encountered any monsters either. ...Could this be related to Rei treating the World Tree?"

"I wonder. It's true the World Tree suddenly became active, but I don't think that would have such an immediate effect."

Marina tilted her head at Elena's question.

"The World Tree... I've seen a lot of things, but that was the first time I'd ever seen a tree that big. There was a forest near the village where I was born, but..."

He trailed off, silently shaking his head.

Earth had been born and raised in the village of Roof, a place that could unquestionably be called the countryside even within the Kingdom of Mireana. Precisely because of that, the nearby forest didn't harbor monsters of such formidable power.

Earth himself had rarely ventured into that forest, but if there had been a giant tree like the one visible upon entering the settlement, it would have been visible from the village. ...That is, if the World Tree hadn't erected a barrier around the settlement like this one.

"Hehe, I'm glad to hear you say that."

Nearly a century had passed since leaving the settlement, yet Marina hadn't abandoned it.

That much was clear, considering how the settlement acted as an intermediary in deals with Gilm, and how Audban had come seeking help all the way to Gilm.

That's precisely why she couldn't possibly feel bad about her homeland being praised.

Of course, her good mood was likely influenced by the World Tree's healing as well.

"Anyway, let's call it a day here and head back to the settlement."

"...Is that okay? The sun is still high in the sky."

Vihera's gaze turned skyward.

True to her words, the sun was still high, and it seemed there were still about two hours until dusk.

But Marina nodded back at Vihera.

"Yes. There's plenty to do back at the settlement too. We need to check if Grandfather heard the full report from Ragdo."

"Ah... yeah. That's true."

Recalling Optis's eager desire to join the feast, Vihera couldn't help but agree with Marina's concern.

Still, Marina understood that her grandfather, as the village elder, was doing what he needed to do, no matter what.

Even if he was letting loose a bit, she felt she shouldn't blame him too much.

After all, the World Tree had regained its power, so it wasn't unreasonable for him to be a little giddy.

(Though getting too carried away could be a problem, you know.)

As if agreeing with Marina's thoughts, Earth spoke up.

"Ah, I agree with Marina-san. Besides, Rei said he'd serve Gamelion meat for dinner tonight."

The moment the words "Gamelion meat" were uttered, everyone's gaze turned to Rei.

When they'd been discussing the fruit they found on the way back, Rei had mentioned offering Gamelion meat in the flow of conversation, and Earth had clearly remembered it.

It was proof that Gamelion's name was renowned for producing such delicious meat.

Under everyone's focused gaze, Rei finally spoke, sounding resigned.

"Alright. Then today, let's celebrate the World Tree's recovery with Gamelion meat."

And so, the Gamelion Party was set to begin.

"Now then, to celebrate the World Tree's recovery... Cheers!"

"Cheers!"

Everyone echoed Marina's voice in unison, raising the cups they held.

Everyone except Rei, Byune, and Ara had alcoholic beverages like ale or wine in their cups, but despite it being daytime, no one questioned it.

It was true that it was only about two hours until evening, but more importantly, the entire settlement was in a festive mood.

Feasts were happening everywhere in the settlement, not just in the garden of the house Elena and the others were renting.

In fact, having spent time searching for anomalies outside the settlement, one could say Rei and the others were late to the party.

Seeing several groups holding feasts on their way to this house had also played a big part.

On the garden table lay numerous dishes made with Gamelion meat provided by Rei.

Stir-fries, skewers, stews, soups, steamed dishes—anyone familiar with Gamelion meat would undoubtedly envy these offerings.

While Gamelion dishes were the main attraction, it wasn't just about the meat.

There were also salads packed with vegetables, dishes of chopped nuts dressed with seasonings, and leafy greens used to wrap Gamelion stir-fries.

At the edge of the table lay the purple and black spotted fruits Rei had gathered... but for now, everyone was savoring the Gamelion dishes, the main event of the day, paying them no mind.

"Pluuuh, plororororor!"

Polo, his mouth full of Gamelion meat, swallowed and let out a cry of delight.

That sound could truly be described as one of pure delight, and it was by no means an exaggeration.

After all, even Set and Yellow were happily chirping away as they ate their Gamelion dishes.

Meanwhile, Set was eating a dish prepared by rubbing salt and herbs into a chunk of Gamelion meat and grilling it, seated beside the table.

Set's appetite was incomparable to that of ordinary humans, hence this specially prepared dish.

It was a very simple dish, but the quantity exceeded five kilograms, and Set ate it with obvious satisfaction.

The exterior was thoroughly roasted, while the center remained nearly raw.

Set seemed to relish this contrast in textures, purring with delight.

Because it had been stored in the Misty Ring, the Gamelion meat was fresh, perfectly safe to eat even raw.

"Ah, delicious. Never thought I'd get to eat Gamelion meat at this time of year."

Earth murmured deeply as he brought a skewer to his mouth.

The others agreed with his sentiment.

"That's right. Being able to eat Gamelion meat outside of winter is quite the luxury. Nobles... well, those of a certain rank could probably use magic items to eat Gamelion meat even in other seasons."

Marina murmured, recalling a nobleman who had once made advances toward her.

His line back then had been, "How about we eat Gamelion meat together this summer?"

...I heard later that the magic item he had was defective, and he ended up with an upset stomach.

"It's delicious, Elena-sama."

"Yes. I find this stew particularly to my taste. The texture that melts in your mouth is unlike any ordinary meat."

"Indeed. It pairs well with the vegetables too."

Elena and Ara each enjoyed their Gamelion.

At the same table, Byune was desperately stuffing small bites of Gamelion into her mouth.

Beside her, Vihera was wiping sauce from Byune's cheek.

"Polo, porururu!"

Polo demanded more food from Earth.

Earth scooped up some food and handed it to Polo.

Polo then happily took a bite, wagging all nine tails.

"Rei, try this dish too. I'm kinda proud of it."

Marina offers Rei a dish of stir-fried Gamelion meat topped with a refreshing green sauce.

"Wow, this looks delicious. Is it okay for me to eat this?"

"Sure. Rei helped us out a lot with the World Tree business, so consider this my thanks."

"You're giving me materials from the World Tree anyway, so I don't think you need to go out of your way like this."

Even as he said this, Rei's eyes were firmly fixed on the meat.

Indeed, the aroma wafting from the plate was tantalizing.

Amidst the rich aroma of the sauce, there was a hint of something refreshing.

Alongside the meat, several leafy greens were stir-fried together, their green, red, and yellow hues making the dish look even more delicious.

"This much should be fine, right? Here."

"...Well, if you insist, I won't refuse."

Using a fork, he brought the dish to his mouth.

Of course, Rei and Marina's actions were being observed by the others sharing the table.

『……』

Elena and Vihera both wore slightly displeased expressions.

Still, the main reason they didn't interfere with Marina here was likely because it was the day the World Tree had recovered.

They figured it was fine to let Marina have Rei all to herself for today.

Even understanding that, it was only natural to still feel a bit annoyed.

"Ah, ahahaha. I kinda feel like the atmosphere just got a little awkward."

Earth, who was eating Gamelion's stew, muttered while looking around.

He didn't know why the atmosphere had turned so sour, but he could still sense that it had.

He understood it wasn't a pleasant situation for Earth, but he didn't voice it.

He had a feeling that if he said anything here, it probably wouldn't end well.

"Pororo? Porururu!"

As if cheering Earth on, Polo waved its nine tails.

Fortunately, they were outside, so it wasn't a problem, but if they'd been inside, it would have kicked up quite a bit of dust.

Watching Polo, he drifted into a bit of escapism, then focused solely on eating the Gamelion cuisine.

"Rei, how about this dish? Elena and Vihera worked together to make it."

As if sensing the gaze directed at him, they offered the dish Elena and Vihera had made together toward Rei.

The plate held thinly sliced Gamelion meat, blanched in hot water, topped with what appeared to be a vegetable-based sauce.

(Shabu-shabu, huh?)

The dish Rei knew: thinly sliced meat dipped in hot water to cook, then eaten with a sauce. The sauces included ponzu, sesame sauce, and sometimes something a bit unusual like grated red pepper. There were many different ways to eat it.

Technically, it wasn't just hot water since the broth was made with kombu and other ingredients, but the cooking method was similar to the dish right in front of him.

He picked up a piece of meat with his fork and brought it to his mouth.

Elena and Vihera watched him intently, looking worried.

"Mmm, delicious."

Hearing Rei's words, Elena and Vihera smiled happily.

...The jealousy they had felt toward Marina just moments before was nowhere to be seen.
