"Giiii!? Giiiiiiiiii!"

Struck by Rei's Death Scythe, the giant cicada had two right wings severed midway. Unable to continue flying, it plummeted toward the ground.

Realizing it would surely die if it fell like this, the giant cicada desperately flapped its four remaining wings: the intact left pair and the two right wings severed halfway.

This prevented a straight plunge to the ground, but it also caused an unexpected situation for Rei.

Perhaps due to the loss of its right wing from the middle, it was drifting away from the cleared ground created by the World Tree.

If this continued, it would veer off the cleared ground the World Tree had painstakingly created.

With that thought, he tried to use Sleipnir's boots again to reach the giant cicada... but hesitated for an instant.

He was currently falling through the air, but if he used the Sleipnir's Shoes again, he might not have enough uses left if Set didn't pick him up.

Sleipnir's Boots allowed him to walk a few steps through the air, but he had already used them several times.

(No, with the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, I can't die... Huh? Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour? Deep Flame? Could it be...!)

Suddenly, he remembered Deep Flame's ability... specifically, its capacity to generate flames exactly as Rei envisioned them. While it might be necessary if there were no other options, there was no need to test it in his current predicament.

"GRRRRRRRR!"

As Rei fell, Set's roar echoed around them.

"Giiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiii

The giant cicada tried to counter Set with its own cry, but with its right wing barely functional, it couldn't land an accurate attack.

Set, rising sharply from below toward the sky, roared and swung a strike with his front legs.

"GRRRRRRRR!"

That strike was the most powerful attack Set could currently unleash.

Borrowing the power of the Power Bracelet, the blow headed straight for the giant cicada's body... but the giant cicada used its ten legs to block the strike.

Those legs, capable of withstanding even a Death Scythe strike, were a powerful trump card for the giant cicada.

If it could block a Death Scythe strike, stopping Set's Power Crush was hardly difficult.

However... While it managed to block what could have been a fatal blow from Set, it couldn't completely absorb the impact and was blown away.

Had its wings been intact, it might have been able to withstand Set's blow and maintain its position in the air. But with its right wing sliced off halfway, it couldn't even regain its balance mid-air.

"Giiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiii

The unpleasant sound emitted from the giant cicada's mouth—was it frustration at failing to withstand Set's blow?

Whatever the reason, the giant cicada, blown far away, plummeted toward the cleared ground created by the World Tree.

Perhaps even the giant cicada disliked the idea of crashing directly into the ground, desperately flapping its wings to lessen the impact of its fall.

Normally, launching a follow-up attack on the giant cicada here would have been the best move for Set.

But right now, Set had something more important to do than pursue the giant cicada.

"GRRRRRRRRRR!"

The target of its frantic wing flapping was Rei, plummeting straight toward the ground.

When it first leaped off Set's back, it was a jump from considerable height.

On top of that, he had used Sleipnir's boots mid-fall to halt his descent and cut the giant cicada's wings, gaining a little altitude in the process.

Still, the fact remained that Rei couldn't fly, and Set desperately flapped his wings, trying to catch her.

(He's got the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour deployed, strengthening his body. Even if we fall like this, I doubt he'd die, just get injured at worst.)

Gazing at the dense red magical energy surrounding his body, concentrated to the point of being visible, Rei murmured inwardly.

Still, he wasn't entirely unmoved by the fact that Set was struggling so desperately for his sake.

Set circles beneath the falling Rei, positioning himself to land safely with his back facing her.

If he fell like this, his full weight would land on Set. So, he used Sleipnir's boots to create a cushion, then leapt onto Set's back.

"Set, sorry about this."

"GRRRR!"

Feeling Rei's weight on his back, Set breathed a sigh of relief.

It was understandable that even Set felt considerable mental anxiety about dropping Rei, who was riding on his back mid-air... and from a considerable height at that.

With a growl that skillfully conveyed a hint of concern amidst his relief, Set, now carrying Rei on his back, flapped his wings and turned toward the giant cicada plummeting toward the ground.

Despite having half its right wing severed, the giant cicada desperately tried to slow its descent. Its attempt was only partially successful.

It had avoided a straight plunge, but it couldn't completely stop its descent and crashed down...

「Set！」

"GRRRRRRRR!"

Seeing this as the perfect opportunity, Rei called out to Set.

Set must have understood what Rei wanted just from that voice. Flapping its wings, Set closed the distance toward the giant cicada plummeting straight toward the ground.

With only Set carrying Rei and the giant cicada descending, Set entered the attack range just as the giant cicada was about to hit the ground.

「Set！」

Another brief call.

But once again, Set discerned what Rei wanted from the tone of his voice and let out a high-pitched cry.

"GRRRRRRRRRRRRRR!"

"Flying Slash!"

Fifteen wind arrows materialized around Set.

Excluding the close-range Power Crush, the most powerful skill was Fire Breath. Unfortunately, Fire Breath was a mid-range skill, not a long-range one.

Among the long-range skills Set could currently use, Wind Arrow was the most powerful and, above all, excelled in attack speed.

While Shock Eye was superior in terms of attack speed alone, its damage was fatally insufficient, making it unsuitable for use here.

The wind arrows fired struck the giant cicada one after another just before it crashed to the ground.

It was an extremely easy-to-use skill in terms of speed and number of hits, but its actual damage output wasn't particularly high.

Even so, the Wind Arrows striking its body one after another were a nuisance for the giant cicada focused on landing.

And then, blending in with the Wind Arrow impacts... Flying Slash sliced through the giant cicada's left wing at the root.

Normally, it should have deployed a barrier to block the Flying Slash. However, its right wing was severed halfway through, forcing it to focus on landing. Furthermore, its attention was being disrupted by Set's Wind Arrows, so it likely didn't have the presence of mind to react.

"Giiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiii

Just as it was about to land, its sole remaining left wing was suddenly severed at the base. Naturally, the giant cicada lost its balance.

Fortunately, it had already descended to about five meters above the ground, so the giant cicada managed to land without failing. But...

"GIGIGIGIGIGIGIGIGIGI!"

Frustrated at losing its last remaining left wing, the giant cicada turned its body skyward and emitted an ultrasonic blast.

For the giant cicada, this was not only a strike born of frustration but also served the purpose of buying time.

But... as the giant cicada emitted its ultrasonic waves while facing upward, it saw Rei, clad in the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, plummeting straight toward it.

What the giant cicada thought upon seeing Rei like that is unknown.

Still, being alone in the air, it must have reasoned that facing Rei alone was better than fighting alongside Set. The magical energy infused into the ultrasonic wave it fired at Rei increased further, greatly enhancing its power.

"Hah! I've seen that attack countless times already!"

Pouring magic into the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour to counter the sonic wave, Rei had no other countermeasure. He continued his straight descent toward the giant cicada.

Then, the giant cicada's ultrasonic blast struck Rei... yet showing no sign of damage whatsoever, Rei swung the Death Scythe upward mid-fall.

The Death Scythe came down as the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour burned the ultrasonic waves themselves.

The giant cicada, stunned that its own sonic attack had no effect, attempted to block the Death Scythe with one of its legs.

This action stemmed from its understanding that, during the battle near the World Tree, Rei had blocked a single strike of the Death Scythe imbued with his magic power...

Slash!

Rei's physical capabilities, enhanced beyond comparison to his normal state by the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, meant he wasn't merely blocking the attack—he couldn't even exchange blows for an instant before the tip of the leg was severed.

The sheer power and sharpness of Rei's strike... was evident in the speed at which the severed tip of the giant cicada's leg flew away.

And the fact that the giant cicada's front leg had managed to clash with the Death Scythe, however crookedly, meant it possessed that level of hardness.

Given that the tip of a leg possessing such hardness was severed by Rei's strike using the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, it was only natural that it would impale itself into the tree trunk ahead.

(Will I get scolded later?)

He thought that for just an instant, then suddenly realized the World Tree's light sphere was no longer near him. He immediately pushed the thought from his mind.

Simultaneously, he flipped the handle of the Death Scythe, still in its downward swing, upward with just a flick of his wrist, now slashing upward.

The giant cicada seemed to grasp the sheer terror of Rei's current attack. It tried desperately to evade, but with all four wings severed—the right wing cut halfway through, the left wing severed at the root—its balance was shattered. Its movements were inevitably slowed.

At this close range, only its leg attacks were truly dangerous. Rei intended to sever its torso while evading those leg strikes.

Even if the giant cicada measured twenty meters in length, Rei believed that with his Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, severing its torso in two wouldn't be difficult, even if it tried shielding itself with its legs...

"Dammit!"

He leapt backward in an instant.

The ground where Rei had stood just an instant before vanished.

Yes, it was so perfectly timed it felt like a pitfall had suddenly opened there.

(Magic!?)

Rei thought this as he leapt back, but then realized it made sense—if he could cast a spell to launch spears of earth, it wouldn't be surprising if he could also cast a spell to create a pitfall.

And to Rei's bitter annoyance, a pitfall trap was undoubtedly effective against her in her current state.

If it were a spell that grew earth spears, he could incinerate them like the shockwave earlier with the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour. But if there was nothing there, he couldn't burn anything.

All Rei could do was avoid falling into that pitfall.

(Wait a minute... A pitfall... A pitfall. And if that bastard got a taste for it just now...)

A sudden thought struck him, and though he smiled inwardly, he made sure not to show it on his face.

He didn't know if the giant cicada could read his facial expressions, but he decided it was best to hide them as much as possible anyway.

In fact, to prevent the giant cicada from discerning his thoughts, Rei even shouted.

「Set！」

"GRRRRRRRR!"

After setting Rei down from his back, Set, who had circled around behind the giant cicada, dove down with a loud cry.

The giant cicada likely judged that the combined force of Power Crush, the bracelet of brute strength, and the sheer speed of the fall would deliver a blow powerful enough to be fatal even to it if it connected. It tried desperately to evade, but without wings, it couldn't fly away on the spur of the moment and had no choice but to move its legs to dodge.

Even so, it had ten legs... no, nine, since one had been severed by Rei's Death Scythe. But the fact remained that with those nine legs, it could still move somewhat on the ground.

The giant cicada moved desperately, yet even as it struggled, it sensed Rei waiting for an opening.

"Gigi Gigi Gigi!"

It fired multiple earth spears toward Rei as a deterrent, while also interfering with the ground near Rei to cast magic, launching earth spears from beneath her feet.

Despite performing evasive maneuvers from Set's attack while casting, the earth spears approaching from both sky and ground did not veer off course, heading straight for Rei.

"You think something like that will work on me now?"

Seeing the earth spears heading for him, he didn't dodge. Instead, he stepped forward.

He could have intercepted them with Deep Flame, but that would only prolong the fight needlessly.

To finally end this prolonged battle, Rei deliberately stepped forward.

The earth spear charging straight at him was easily incinerated by the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour... and he immediately closed the distance to the giant cicada.

For the giant cicada, Rei's action presented a perfect opportunity.

To deal with them one at a time, rather than both simultaneously...

The instant Rei stepped forward, the ground vanished within a five-meter radius, with only the giant cicada's location spared.

A pitfall. Unlike the previous one, this pitfall was vast enough to leave no escape.

For the giant cicada, it was a golden opportunity...

"You fool!"

At the same time, it was the perfect opportunity Rei had been waiting for.

As Rei was about to fall into the pit, he activated Sleipnir's boots.

The giant cicada had witnessed this ability firsthand—the power to kick off and fly through the air—but it surely hadn't anticipated it being used to avoid falling into a pit.

Rei took one step, two steps, three steps through the air... walking where the ground should have been, closing the distance with the giant cicada.

Noticing Rei's movements, the giant cicada frantically swung one of its legs...

Slash!

The enhanced physical prowess granted by the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour severed the giant cicada's torso along with its leg. As a follow-up strike, Set's blow shattered and blew away the upper half of its body where it had been severed.
