It's not uncommon for guild requests to involve requesting a specific person who handled a previous job.

"Hey, wait a minute, Rei? Something feels off about the area... Are you sure this is okay?"

"Don't worry. It's fine. No problem. ...Probably."

There was no way money-loving Kyurotto wouldn't be thrilled by this.

"...Hey, Rei. Are you really okay with those two?"

"They're fine. They may look like that, but they're pretty skilled. You know that too, don't you, Marina?"

"The Power of Eternity, huh. I thought they were a fairly active party."

It was true that, to the extent Marina remembered, the Eternal Power party had been fairly active.

Considering the number of adventurers in Gilm and the number of parties, Marina couldn't possibly have kept track of them all.

The fact that this party stuck in Marina's memory meant they were considered competent at that time.

That said, Marina had been keeping an eye on Rei since he arrived in Gilm.

After all, she had ordered Lenora to be considerate toward Rei in various ways.

Given that she had become involved with Rei, it was undoubtedly true that she also kept an eye on the Eternal Power party, which included Alogan and Culotte.

And there, where they turned their gaze, stood Azimov's house.

"Yeah. Since Rei talks about him like that, I totally thought he'd be someone with a big, fancy lab or something."

"If Azimov had any desire for prestige or anything like that, he might have been in a higher position and been put in charge of a lab or something, but that's just not his personality."

Arogan and Kyurott nodded at Rei's words.

And then, upon arriving in front of Azimov's house...

"GRRRRRR!"

Before Rei could do anything, Set, who had been in the courtyard, leaped straight at him.

With a body length of three meters, catching Set was no easy feat.

Nevertheless, Rei firmly caught Set.

He took a few steps back, but he didn't stumble or fall.

"Seriously, Set's such a mama's boy."

"Grrr..."

Set turned his round eyes toward Rei, who murmured while stroking him.

The time they'd spent apart hadn't been that long, but Set probably just wanted to be with Rei.

They were often apart for much longer periods, but today's mood seemed to dictate this.

"Grrrr?"

Perhaps reassured by Rei's strokes, Set shifted his gaze to the person behind Rei.

Both Arogan and Culotte knew Set, of course, but compared to Rei, they weren't that close to him.

They had certainly met Set several times before, but seeing how overwhelmingly larger he had become compared to their previous encounter left them speechless.

...Originally, Set's body length was about two meters.

The Set they knew was from that time.

But now, Set was about three meters long.

Considering that difference, it's only natural to stare in amazement.

"Hey, Set. Don't you remember? It's Arogan and Culotte."

"Grr... Grrrr?"

Judging by how Set tilted his head, he probably didn't remember much.

Incidentally, Alogan, who had picked a fight with Rei upon their first meeting, was shocked to learn he too had been forgotten.

Though, that was probably fortunate for Alogan.

If Set really did remember him, he might have ended up disliked by Set... no, recognized as an enemy.

"Grrr!"

Satisfied he'd gotten to cuddle with Rei for now, Set rubbed his face against Rei's before heading toward the courtyard.

"Hehe, he still adores you, doesn't he?"

Marina, who had kept her distance from Rei to avoid getting caught in the crossfire when Set came running, murmured with a smile.

"Well, it is Set. ...Anyway, I think it's about time. I was going to say 'knock,' but guess that wasn't necessary."

"Judging by the situation, it seems there were no particular abnormalities."

"Mm."

As usual, Byune spoke softly before opening the door.

Her attitude clearly indicated she was inviting them inside.

Rei understood this too, so he obediently entered the house.

Arogan and Culotte, entering an alchemist's house for the first time, looked around curiously... then gradually wore disappointed expressions.

(I'd heard it was a top-notch alchemist's house, so I thought there'd be more stuff... but this place is just ordinary.)

(Judging by this, the house itself isn't all that special, huh?)

Had they imagined something more extraordinary... perhaps a place adorned with countless magic items? Alogan and Currot looked somewhat deflated.

...The room where they'd been attacked before. The spot where the window had been was supposed to be somewhat damaged from where Set had crashed through... but somehow, it was completely repaired.

As Rei nodded in understanding, looking at the spot where the window had been, Azimov, who had been fiddling with a dagger, spoke up.

His slightly irritated demeanor was likely due to having to hire someone he wasn't particularly close to as his escort.

"So, are those two the bodyguards you mentioned, Rei?"

They shot Rei a displeased look, but took no further action.

If they made a scene here, it would mean losing the prime opportunity to establish friendly relations with Azimov's bodyguard... a highly skilled alchemist.

Opportunities to become acquainted with an alchemist were rare enough as it was.

Setting aside what he meant by "unnecessary things," the vague warning about "strange things happening" filled them with a certain dread. Still, both Arogan and Culotte nodded.

Seeing this, Azimov suddenly looked at Arogan... or more precisely, at the scabbard at Arogan's waist.

"Is that a Magic Sword?"

"Ah, yeah. That's right."

Apparently not expecting his Magic Sword to be spotted so suddenly, Alogan answered with a slight surprise and reached for the scabbard.

The longsword he drew was his partner... the Magic Sword that had saved his life countless times.

Once, Alogan had relied entirely on the power of the Magic Sword. But after meeting Rei and being instantly defeated, then fighting thieves during his rank-up exam, forming a party with Culotte and Scola, and completing various quests...

Through that process, Alogan had learned not to rely on the Magic Sword's power, but to wield it firmly.

He had become capable of doing so.

Though he found Rei difficult to deal with, he still trusted and relied on him.

That's precisely why, upon seeing Rei's nod, he handed his partner over to Azimov.

Azimov took it, holding the Magic Sword... staring at it intently for several seconds.

Then, sheathing it, she finally spoke as if satisfied.

"I'm counting on you for protection."

The expression on Azimov's face as he said this was different from just seconds before, somehow resigned.
