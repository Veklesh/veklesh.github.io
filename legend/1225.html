Everyone... her comrades were fighting, yet Ara could only stand silently watching, her beloved weapon, the Power Axe, in hand.

Truth be told, she wanted to join the fight right now.

Her beloved Elena was battling a Rank S monster, the Silver Lion.

The fact that she, Elena's best friend, subordinate, and captain of the escort knights, wasn't participating in that battle was something Ara herself couldn't accept.

But... even though she understood this rationally, her body refused to move.

Ever since seeing the Silver Lion and hearing its roar that shook his very soul, his body had refused to move.

Challenge it, and death awaits.

It was as if she had come to understand that truth.

Her heart yearned to fight, yet her body remained utterly still, in direct opposition to Ara's will.

No, it is moving.

If trembling visibly can be called moving, then yes, it is moving.

(Why... Why won't my body move!?)

I felt pathetic, unable to do anything but watch the fight from outside the room.

But still, my body won't move.

Even as I felt this shame toward myself, the battle inside the room continued.

Suddenly, something erupted simultaneously from the Silver Lion's body, injuring those nearby like Rei, Set, and Elk.

It seemed to be a close-range attack, so it was a silver lining that Elena, Marina, and Min, who were providing support from a distance, weren't hit.

Elk, damaged by the close-range attack, swung the Axe of the Thunder God regardless.

The Silver Lion was blown away by that single blow.

Then, following Rei's orders, Elk exited the room.

"Haa, haa, haa... Dammit, what a troublesome attack... Hey, Ara. Sorry, but pull the hair out of my body!"

Leaning against the wall, Elk's voice finally snapped Ara back to her senses, and she moved her body without thinking.

Since the attack had come from the Silver Lion's front, it was only natural that no hair—or rather, hair needles—were stuck in his back. But for Elk, leaning against the wall to recover even a little... truly, even the slightest bit of stamina, it could be called fortunate.

"I'll pull them out."

He spoke briefly, then began pulling the hair stuck in Elk's body.

Hair... yes, but sharp, pointed, and long, these strands were truly worthy of being called metal needles.

If shown to someone unfamiliar with them, anyone without deep knowledge of monsters might well mistake them for metal needles.

"Guh!"

Elk let out a small groan of pain as the hair needles were pulled out.

Fortunately, these hair needles were made from the fur of the Silver Lion.

Had this needle-hair had a barb like a fishing hook, Elk would have suffered far greater pain when it was pulled out.

Just as when an arrow pierced him, in such cases, pulling it out in one swift motion caused the pain to be over in an instant, rather than pulling it out slowly.

From her experience charging across battlefields alongside Elena, Ara knew this. She swiftly and methodically pulled out the barbed needles one after another.

The Elk seemed to understand Ara's actions, not voicing any complaint as it reached for the potion Rei had left behind.

The barbed needles, while painful, didn't inflict particularly deep wounds.

Therefore, even the potions placed here, which were less potent than the World Tree potion, were sufficient.

Precisely because they were less potent, he applied the potions, which had been prepared for unlimited use, to his own body.

Immediately, the sharp pain felt at the site of the hair needle's entry began to fade.

"...How is it?"

Elk nodded back at Ara, who had pulled all the needles from his body.

Thanks to the potion, the pain was already gone.

The hair needles that had hindered his movement were gone, and he could return to the fight against the Silver Lion immediately.

Having made that judgment, Elk rose to his feet, Axe of the Thunder God in hand... He turned his gaze toward Ara and spoke.

"What about you? You're ready to join the fight now, aren't you?"

"Huh!?"

At that moment, Ara realized for the first time that her body was no longer trembling at all.

The trembling she had felt upon seeing the Silver Lion had subsided, and she had been treating Elk normally.

She couldn't understand why the trembling in her body had stopped like that.

But even so, she judged that now was the time to use her power, and nodded back at Elk.

This swiftness of judgment likely stemmed from her battlefield experience.

On the battlefield, where quick decisions were often required, Ara had always been by Elena's side.

"Alright. Your weapon is also effective against the Silver Lion. ...Let's go."

"Yes!"

At Elk's words, Ara answered with renewed vigor.

Seeing her energy, hard to believe she was the same person trembling at the sight of the Silver Lion, Elk nodded with a slight smile.

Then, turning his gaze back into the room... toward the Silver Lion, he smiled in the same way.

...Though, while the smile he'd shown Ara could be described as gentle, the one directed at the Silver Lion was savage, like that of a ferocious carnivore.

Elk advanced into the room, the Axe of the Thunder God in hand, and Ara followed behind, her Power Axe gripped tightly.

Inside the room, what Elk and Arah witnessed was the sight of Rei's Death Scythe blowing the Silver Lion away.

It was a blow similar to the desperate strike Elk had just unleashed... no, one wielded with even greater force. The Silver Lion, measuring about six meters in length including its tail, was sent flying.

Frankly, such a sight was beyond Ara's comprehension.

But... having resolved to join this battle, she couldn't afford to be distracted by such combat scenes.

Right now, she needed to support Elena.

Making that judgment, she headed for the front lines alongside Elk.

"Ara!? Are you okay?"

"Yes, I'm fine. Sorry for the trouble."

As she passed by, Elena noticed Ara and called out to her, but Ara only gave a brief reply.

Seeing Ara like that, Elena must have decided there was no need for concern. She said nothing more, merely glancing at Ara with a look of trust before once again whipping Mirage into shape and focusing solely on keeping the Silver Lion at bay.

Ideally, she'd want to wrap the whip-like blades around its legs and immobilize it... but facing a six-meter-long Silver Lion, such a feat was far from easy.

Moreover, the Silver Lion's speed was far too swift for a monster boasting a six-meter frame to possess, making it difficult to entangle its legs with Mirage blades.

Ultimately, all he could do was strike the silver lion's body with the whip-like mirage to keep it at bay.

Even so, the silver lion likely judged the mirage's strikes troublesome, as they landed precisely where they disrupted its initiative.

Moreover, it wasn't just Elena's Mirage; Marina's spirit magic and bow, along with Min's magic, were also irritating.

"GRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRR

Another roar was unleashed toward Elena and the others.

Compared to when the battle began, it was slightly weaker. Perhaps the Silver Lion had also been sufficiently worn down by the fighting so far.

"Alright, the Silver Lion is definitely weakened! Keep it up!"

Rei, continuing the fight while deploying the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, hurled the deep flames enveloping his body at the Silver Lion.

While the Silver Lion possessed strong resistance to magic, the Deep Flames were concentrated manifestations of Rei's own magic power and wouldn't be easily nullified.

In fact, the Deep Flames could be imbued with properties exactly as Rei envisioned. The Deep Flames that struck the Silver Lion's face proceeded to burn the face itself.

Of course, Rei didn't expect it to break through the Silver Lion's high magical defense.

But the Deep Flame wasn't released to damage the Silver Lion.

It adhered to the Silver Lion's face, burning the entire visage.

While there was no direct damage from the flames themselves, having its face burned was extremely disruptive to the Silver Lion.

Even without taking damage, the flames block its vision.

Naturally, it also made it difficult to sense the surroundings or detect movement.

That was precisely Rei's objective. Having successfully conjured the Deep Flame exactly as envisioned—a flame that adheres—Rei flashed a triumphant smile and shouted.

"Now! Attack all at once! Right now, the Silver Lion's evasion isn't that high!"

Set was the first to react to those words.

"GRRRRRRRR!"

Set, who had been circling overhead waiting for an opening, let out a deep growl and dove straight down toward the ground.

Elk, returning to the fight to follow Set, swung the Axe of the Thunder God with all his might toward the Silver Lion's torso.

Finally succeeding in banishing his own fear of the Silver Lion, Ara too unleashed his formidable strength, smashing his Power Axe with all his might into the massive form before him.

Rei, who had naturally immobilized the Silver Lion by making it shake its head in disgust with Deep Flame, also moved into action.

"Power Slash!"

There weren't many skills effective against the Silver Lion.

Among those few was the Power Slash Rei unleashed.

Having been used multiple times in this battle, the Silver Lion naturally understood Rei's attack pattern.

But... While Rei's attack could be handled in any number of ways under normal circumstances, Deep Flame still clung to his face, burning relentlessly.

His face was being scorched continuously, yet he felt no pain or damage.

However, the flames obstructed his vision, and the crackling sound made it difficult to hear surrounding noises.

For the Silver Lion in this state, dealing with all attacks, including Rei's, was extremely difficult.

"GRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRR

The roar rose again.

It was the same attack as before, sending needles of fur flying in all directions.

But... the next instant, water materialized, enveloping the Silver Lion.

The quills fired in all directions pierced through the water enveloping the Silver Lion's body. However, they didn't possess significant power to begin with, so by the time they emerged from the water, they no longer held enough force to pierce a person.

Most of the needles simply fell to the floor. Those with a bit of momentum left flew off in random directions or struck armor before falling to the ground. Even when they hit Elk or Ara's bodies, they couldn't even pierce their skin.

"An attack you've seen before? Did you really think I hadn't prepared for that?"

Marina, who had enveloped the Silver Lion in water with her spirit magic, declared with a radiant smile.

The attack's raw power wasn't particularly high, but it compensated by being capable of striking in all directions.

From the moment she saw it, Marina had sensed the danger and devised countermeasures.

Admittedly, she hadn't had much time, so she could only come up with a largely improvised countermeasure.

Even such an improvised countermeasure was sufficient to protect those capable of damaging the Silver Lion in close combat—Rei, Elk, Set, and Ara.

...However, the Silver Lion's face, enveloped in water, was burning... A somewhat contradictory sight—burning within water—was unfolding before her eyes.

「Rei！」

At Marina's shout, Rei responded by channeling magic into his Death Scythe instead of answering.

A strike powerful enough to be instantly fatal under normal circumstances.

"Gyaaah!"

The swing of the blow sent a metallic clang echoing loudly through the air, accompanied by the Silver Lion's cry of pain.

That it could take such a blow and still only manage a scream spoke to the Silver Lion's formidable abilities.

But it didn't end there.

Elk and Ara's strikes landed on the Silver Lion's body, just like Rei's had.

Then, the Power Crush unleashed by Set, who had swung down from above, slammed the Silver Lion into the floor.

The sight of the six-meter-long Silver Lion smashed into the floor was anything but ordinary.

"Haaaaaaaah! Power Slash!"

With the effects of the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour at full power, Rei swung his Death Scythe at the Silver Lion pinned to the floor.

Activating a skill he had already used multiple times in this battle, the Silver Lion took the blow while still pinned to the floor.

"GRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRR

Screaming like that, the Silver Lion still immediately rose, swinging its whip-like tail to strike at Rei...

"Like hell you will!"

Elena's whip-like Mirage seized the tail.

"Attack now!"

Elk shouted, Axe of the Thunder God in hand... and the combined blows of those present slammed into the Silver Lion.
