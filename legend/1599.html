Even without that, the women who were taken are just ordinary people—though they do share the common trait of being beautiful.

The food inside the Misty Ring is enough to get everyone here to Gilm.

Hearing Sura's words, Rei pulled the Death Scythe and Twilight Spear from the Misty Ring while saying it wouldn't be a problem.

For those living in the small nation of Reburina, they had heard tales of item boxes, but seeing one with their own eyes was a first. It was only natural.

Two weapons suddenly appeared in Rei's hands.

Both possessed an overwhelming presence.

Moreover, both the Death Scythe and the Twilight Spear were long-handled weapons that, at first glance, seemed far too large for Rei to wield.

Amidst this, only Sura and a few others didn't seem to show such obvious surprise at Rei's appearance.

Even as they neared the battlefield, Set didn't slow down at all.

He charged straight into the fray with that momentum... Naturally, no one would even consider confronting Set, who stood over three meters tall, head-on, and they quickly made way.

Even if intoxicated by the battlefield's atmosphere of blood and violence, anyone who saw Set charging straight at them would sober up instantly.

The very presence of Set... and the sight of Rei atop Set's back, wielding the Death Scythe and the Twilight Spear, had such a profoundly shocking impact on those around them.

Under these circumstances, it naturally becomes difficult to distinguish them.
