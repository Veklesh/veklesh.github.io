Riding Set, Rei and Set flew through the sky above the forest.

The forest was filled with countless large trees, creating a green carpet distinct from the grasslands.

Gazing at that green carpet while letting the birdsong drift in from somewhere, they searched for familiar faces like Rei and Johanna.

But the green carpet spread out below them covered most of the ground, making it impossible to spot their targets after just a quick glance.

"Set, looks like I'll have to rely on you after all. Can you handle it?"

"Guruu!"

Set growled in agreement, his sharp gaze immediately turning toward the ground.

Using not only his keen eyesight, but also his hearing, sense of smell, and even his ability to sense magic, he searched for Mireine, Johanna, and the many other familiar faces.

Surnin and Ekril, while not as close as Mireine, were always kind to him.

He remembered every face from the former guerrilla squad members he'd fought alongside last autumn.

With such close allies facing mortal danger, Set resolved to save them at all costs.

He gazed down at the ground for several seconds...

"GRRRR!"

Set let out a sharp growl as he spotted Mireine and Johanna through the gaps in the trees at the end of his gaze.

"Found them! ...Whoa, whoa, whoa, whoa!"

Following Set's gaze, Rei saw Mireine and the others fleeing through the forest.

Surnin was running at the rear, with a group of Cyclopes chasing them at a distance.

"They lost a fight and are running away now?"

Rei muttered this upon seeing the sight, for numerous arrows were embedded in the Cyclops' bodies.

But he quickly shook his head.

Arrows were indeed stuck in them, but there were no other wounds to speak of.

If Mireine and the others had fought the Cyclops, it would have been strange if they hadn't sustained many more wounds besides the arrows.

Rei understood perfectly well that Mireine and Johanna possessed that level of skill.

"Then..."

"Grrr!"

Before Rei could finish his thought, Set found the answer.

Following the direction of Set's gaze, which had drawn Rei's attention with a cry, they saw Ekril standing on a tree branch, desperately firing arrows at the Cyclops.

"Taking out the rear guard alone? ...Wait, she's providing covering fire from behind that tree branch. Maybe 'rear guard' doesn't apply here?"

The lack of anxiety in Rei's words stemmed from the fact that, as Dietz had said, all five Cyclopes were pursuing Mireine and the others, and Ekril's arrows were successfully hindering them.

He was certain that if things continued like this, Rei and the others would escape the Cyclops without him needing to do anything.

But as if mocking Rei's prediction, the situation changed abruptly.

"GRRRR!"

Set suddenly flapped his wings toward the ground, letting out a sharp cry.

「Set！？」

For a moment, Rei couldn't understand why he'd acted without warning. But then he saw something red right beside Ekril, who was shooting arrows from a tree branch. It looked like it was about to smash whatever huge thing it was holding down onto the tree Ekril stood on.

The instant he recognized that red something as a Cyclops, Rei drew the Death Scythe from the Misty Ring.

「Set！」

The same voice he had called out just a moment before.

But the emotion behind it was entirely different.

Set must have understood this too. He flapped his wings and headed straight for the ground... but his action was a few seconds too late.

The green of the trees rapidly approaching the ground grew closer, but before Rei and Set could reach where Ekril stood, the red cyclops slammed whatever it held into the tree directly before it.

The tree trunk was thick enough that several adults holding hands would barely reach around it, yet it snapped cleanly under the blow from whatever the red cyclops wielded—Rei finally realized it was a metal hammer.

Simultaneously, a blinding light erupted around them, accompanied by a deafening roar.

(What the hell!?)

Rei watched the scene on the ground rapidly approaching while readying his Death Scythe, filled with questions.

But before he could gather his thoughts, the red cyclops' next action captured his attention.

It was the sight of the hammer being brought down toward Ekril, who lay convulsing on the ground.

"Flying Slash!"

"GRRRRRRRR!"

When Ekril was perched on a tree branch, using ranged attacks carelessly risked disrupting its concentration and causing it to fall to the ground, making such attacks unusable.

Ironically, it was only because Ekril had fallen to the ground that this attack became possible. From Rei's Death Scythe came a flying slash, and from Set came multiple arrows made of wind.

While the Wind Arrows themselves weren't particularly powerful, their speed was incomparable to something like a water sphere.

Normally, the Impact Magic Eye would be the fastest attack, but its power is inversely proportional to its speed.

Set likely judged that the Impact Magic Eye would be nearly ineffective against this Cyclops... especially one with red skin, suggesting it was either a rare species or a higher-tier variant.

The Wind Arrows, launched ahead of Rei's Flying Slash, struck the red Cyclops' body one after another.

However, while the Wind Arrows did wound its body, the damage healed visibly and rapidly.

Even so, being wounded must be an annoyance for the red Cyclops. It halted the swing of its hammer aimed at Ekril and turned its single eye toward the direction the Wind Arrows were flying.

And at that precise moment, the Flying Slash Rei had reached the red cyclops. The flying slash severed the right arm holding the hammer from the shoulder.

Even from his distance, Rei thought he heard the sound of that right arm... or more precisely, the hammer it held, hitting the ground.

Even as he heard that sound, the distance between Set and the red cyclops rapidly closed...

"GRRRRRRRRRR!"

Set's front paw swung down with a roar. Empowered by the Power Crush skill and the effect of the Bracelet of Might, the blow sent the nearly five-meter-tall red cyclops flying.

"While I'm at it, take this too!"

Leaping from Set's back, Rei launched a follow-up strike at the red cyclops, which had been blown away, snapping several trees along the way.

That strike resembled the one Set had unleashed earlier, yet it was different.

"Power Slash!"

What erupted from the Death Scythe prioritized the sheer weight and impact of the blow over sharpness.

"GAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH

The two-meter-long scythe shattered the red cyclops' ribs as it sliced through its torso.

The blow from the Death Scythe, channeled through magic, sliced clean through the torso of the red cyclops, nearly five meters in size.

Even though it prioritized power over sharpness, the attack possessed sufficient force to sever the Cyclops' torso. Yet, just as it was about to cleave the body in two, Rei witnessed an unbelievable sight.

The red cyclops, which should have been split cleanly in two by the Death Scythe, was already beginning to heal at the cut surface, small bubbles forming as it closed.

"Blast!"

Seeing this, Rei realized the creature before him was no ordinary Cyclops.

No, normally green-skinned cyclopes had red skin, and this one was about a meter larger than usual.

Even that alone made it different from a normal cyclops, but the fact that it was regenerating at an abnormal speed despite having its torso nearly severed was completely unexpected, even to Rei's eyes.

He swung his Death Scythe, then kicked at the cyclops' body—which was visibly healing the severed torso that should have been severed—to create some distance...

"Ow!"

In that instant, a stabbing pain shot through his entire body.

Even so, kicking the red Cyclops' body, he twisted mid-air to check its condition. Its left hand was gripping its right hand.

More accurately, it was the tip severed from the right shoulder by Rei's flying slash moments ago that he was gripping with his left hand.

Somehow, when it was blown away, it had managed to clasp both its right hand and the hammer.

At the same time, I understood the source of that sudden, piercing pain.

Purple lightning was running through various parts of the red Cyclops' body.

The source, naturally, was the hammer gripped by the severed right arm.

"...Troublesome in more ways than one."

Rei muttered while readying his Death Scythe.

He'd seen his fair share of opponents who dodged, blocked, or deflected his attacks.

But this was the first time he'd seen such an absurd entity that regenerated the instant it was struck.

The healing ability... no, the regenerative ability of the red cyclops before him couldn't be explained simply by it being a cyclops.

(There's definitely something special about it.)

Rei's gaze shifted toward the hammer, which seemed to be the source of that seed.

There, a red cyclops was reattaching its severed right arm to its right shoulder.

"If it can regenerate even after its torso is severed, reattaching a severed right shoulder is probably a piece of cake. Troublesome."

The red cyclops casually swung its hammer with the newly reattached right shoulder, right before their eyes.

From its appearance, it was impossible to imagine that its right arm had been severed just seconds before.

Keeping a wary eye on the enemy, Rei briefly scanned the surroundings and frowned slightly.

He had expected the five Cyclopes to return since their boss was under attack, but there was no sign of them at all.

No, worse than that, the presence of the five Cyclopses continued moving further away.

Did they place absolute faith in this red cyclops' power? Or were their minds so consumed with pursuing Mireine and the others that the red cyclops had already slipped from their thoughts?

Recalling how the cyclops had been held off by Ecuril's arrows earlier, Rei judged it was probably the latter.

For a moment, he considered activating the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour, but decided it was too dangerous to use in this forest. He faced his opponent in his unarmored state.

"GAAAAAAAAAAAAAARGH!"

The red cyclops roared with fury, raising its hammer and charging toward Rei.

As Rei prepared to meet that blow with Death Scythe, a vision flashed through his mind: the scene from earlier when the hammer had swung toward the tree where Ecuril had been positioned.

Lightning had shot from the spot where the hammer struck.

He instantly realized that must have been the source of the sharp pain he'd felt earlier.

"Damn!"

Driven by a bad feeling, instead of blocking the hammer with Death Scythe, he instinctively tried to leap away... but then, out of the corner of his eye, he saw Ecuril lying unconscious on the ground.

If he evaded now and lightning flowed through the ground like before, Ekrill would take the direct hit.

Realizing this, he forcibly changed his jump's direction. Instead of evading, he charged straight toward the red cyclops.

He spun the Death Scythe halfway in his hand, pointing the stone tip toward his opponent...

"Penetrate!"

The moment the skill activated, wind coiled around the spike.

Using the momentum from kicking off the ground, the Death Scythe thrusts sharply toward the red cyclops's right arm, which is raised to swing its hammer.

The strike, its penetrating power enhanced by the wind, pierced straight through the red cyclops's right elbow.

"GAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA

Along with the red cyclops's scream, Rei felt the sensation of crushing its right elbow in his hand.

No matter how quickly its wounds regenerated, pain was something it couldn't ignore.

While it was likely accustomed to the pain of being cut, the experience of having its bones shattered was probably rare.

Generally, adventurers favored longswords, and among the enemies this red cyclops had fought before, none possessed an attack method that shattered bones rather than merely cutting through flesh.

Hence the scream.

And with his elbow shattered, he couldn't keep holding the hammer. He dropped it to the ground.

(That hammer is in the way!)

Since the hammer, capable of unleashing lightning, was amplifying the power of the red cyclops before him, he had to deal with that hammer first.

With that thought, Rei closed the distance to the hammer.

If Rei were an ordinary adventurer, such an attempt would have been pointless.

But Rei possessed an Item Box, capable of storing anything that wasn't a living creature inside the Misty Ring.

Just as Rei's hand reached for the hammer lying on the ground, he saw the red cyclops' single eye glow. Abandoning the hammer, he leapt backward.

Simultaneously, a heat ray was fired from the red cyclops's single eye.

The beam swept across the spot where Rei had been standing just an instant before, instantly scorching the ground it touched.

"Troublesome. ...Set, Eclir!"

"GRRRR!"

At Rei's command, Set, who had been waiting for an opportunity to attack, descended to the ground. He hooked his two front legs onto the leather armor covering Ekril's body, then flapped his wings to create distance.

And so, Rei found himself facing the red cyclops one-on-one.
