The sight before him left Rei utterly speechless.

He understood the sheer power behind Set's strike.

But even so... the fact that a single blow could shatter a boulder twice as large as Set himself was completely unexpected.

It wasn't so much shattered as utterly destroyed.

The power of Set's Power Crush far exceeded Rei's expectations.

"This... is incredible."

"GRRRRRRRR!"

Set responded to Rei's involuntary murmur with a delighted, almost boastful growl: "Pretty awesome, huh?!"

Rei snapped back to reality at Set's cry and hurriedly turned back to him.

"Se-Set! Are you okay!?"

His panic in checking on Set stemmed from remembering the time he himself had used Power Slash, a skill of the same type as Set's Power Crush.

It was undoubtedly a powerful strike, but its nature meant the recoil hitting the user's body was also immense.

If the recoil wasn't skillfully absorbed, it would inflict damage on the user just as much as it did on the enemy.

Mastering Power Slash required considerable practice for Rei, precisely because it was a skill where the line between success and self-inflicted damage was razor-thin.

But... even though Set's Power Crush was now two levels stronger, he unleashed it without the slightest hesitation.

That very fact made Rei deeply concerned about Set's condition.

Could that single strike have broken the bones in his front legs?

That was the level of worry he felt. But...

"Grr?"

Seeing Set tilt his head slightly as if to say, "What's wrong?", Rei breathed a sigh of relief.

Still, wondering if he might just be enduring the pain, she gently reached out to check Set's front leg for injury. Fortunately, there was no sign of any injury anywhere.

"...That's good..."

"Grrr?"

Set tilted his head, looking as if he didn't understand what Rei was worried about. Rei gave Set's head a light pat.

"Seriously, don't make me worry so much."

"Grrr."

Seeing Set growl, Rei let out a sigh mixed with relief, then shifted his gaze toward where the rock had been... yes, where it had been.

What remained there was no longer a rock, but mostly the shattered debris left by Set's strike.

"...Hey, Set. Just asking, but you didn't use the Power Bracelet this time, right?"

"Grr."

Set nodded in response to Rei's words.

Seeing this, Rei turned his gaze back toward where the rock had been.

(This isn't just a matter of Level Five, is it?)

The sheer difference in power was undeniable... no, it was too much of a difference. That attack possessed a destructive force that could easily be described as vicious.

Simply shattering the rock was impressive enough, but on top of that, the single blow had reduced it to what could barely be called large stones.

Of course, not all of the rock was destroyed; what could be called the base of the rock still remained on the ground.

But even so... the sheer power of that single strike was undeniable.

(And he says he's not even using the Bracelet of Might... Just how powerful would it be if he did? And then there's that attack Set excels at, unleashed while diving at full speed... What was I even thinking, worrying about Level Five and all that?)

Reaching Level Five caused the Flying Slash's power to skyrocket.

But right now, Rei was completely overwhelmed by the even more intense shock of Set's strike, completely overwriting the impact of the Flying Slash.

"Ah... yeah. At least I get that Set's strike got way stronger."

"GRRRR!"

"Pretty awesome, huh?" The way he proudly puffed out his chest was quintessentially Set, as always.

Seeing this, Rei finally understood that the unease he'd felt towards Set had vanished. He smiled softly and stroked Set's head.

The sensation must have felt good. Set purred, leaning his body into Rei's hand... After a few minutes of this, Set suddenly lifted his head from where it had been resting while being stroked.

The movement, reminiscent of a wild beast spotting prey, showed that Set was no mere pet. ...Though, to be honest, the idea of a pet capable of destroying a massive rock with a single blow was something Rei couldn't quite imagine, nor did he particularly want to.

「Set？」

"Grrr..."

Rei turned his gaze toward where Set was looking, but all he could see were the trees of the forest.

(What on earth is it?)

As this thought crossed Rei's mind, Rei suddenly thought he saw something between the branches.

"That's... an Aerowing!?"

Rei knew that bird-like monster with four wings approaching them in a straight line from the sky.

Or rather, it would be more accurate to say he had been searching for this monster.

And the Aerowings were heading straight for them.

(A moth flying into the flame... or rather, a firebird. ...Calling it a firebird makes it sound slightly stronger, doesn't it?)

Even as he thought this, the Aerowings continued charging straight toward them.

"Set, I'll launch a diversionary strike first. When an opening appears, hit it!"

"GRRRR!"

Gripping the Death Scythe, Rei swung it at the Aero Wing charging straight at them.

"Flying Slash!"

The skill he unleashed was the one he had just enhanced moments ago.

The slash flew toward Aero Wing, which was heading straight for Rei and the others.

"Shhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhh

Just like before, it let out a serpentine cry, flapping its wings to unleash multiple wind blades toward Rei.

Set was no longer beside Rei.

He had activated his optical camouflage and moved to prepare his next attack.

Under normal circumstances, the Aerowings would have noticed Set's disappearance.

But the Aero Wing was now extremely aggressive, incapable of rational judgment.

Therefore, it charged straight toward Rei, the first target it spotted.

Multiple wind blades collided in midair, clashing with a single, massive slashing blade.

The Flying Slash blade advanced, kicking away the wind blades approaching it one after another.

Even so, the impact of numerous wind blades likely succeeded in somewhat diminishing the Flying Slash's power. Aero Wing flapped its four wings to evade the Flying Slash blade.

"Shhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhh

Now it was his turn.

From the Aero Wing's cry, Rei could even sense that intent.

But what Rei directed at the Aero Wing was a smile... a smile of absolute confidence in his own victory.

Whether the Aero Wing noticed something about Rei's demeanor or not... it prepared to unleash another wind blade. The next instant, it heard a roar from above.

"GRRRRRRRR!"

Using optical camouflage, Rei moved away from the Aero Wing, soaring into the sky from a position invisible to it. He then delivered a strike while falling from a higher altitude.

Moreover, the effect of the Power Bracelet was active, delivering a blow using the Power Crush he had just acquired.

A blow powerful enough to shatter rock—there was no way the Aerowings could withstand it.

"Whoa!"

But Rei, who had seen the lethal strike Set had launched at the Aerowings below, let out a scream and fled the spot.

He headed for a tree a short distance away.

Why Rei screamed and fled became clear the very next instant.

...Yes, because a literal rain of blood began to fall.

More accurately, it was a rain of blood, flesh, bone, entrails, feathers, and various other things.

After all, it was a strike utilizing the Power Crush—a technique possessing enough power to shatter a five-meter boulder—along with the Strength Bracelet and the momentum from the high-altitude descent.

Taking such an attack, even Aero Wings couldn't block it... no, it was impossible for the corpse to retain any semblance of its original form.

"Ugh..."

The sound of Aerowings' blood and flesh raining down.

Rei managed to escape under a tree, so he was fine, but if he hadn't realized the full force of Set's strike, he would have been drenched in it.

It wouldn't have been strange if a scream had escaped his mouth.

(Wait, if the Aero Wing shattered this completely, what happened to the magic stones? Did I not get any magic stones either?)

Once the rain of Aerowings—a term that encompassed blood, flesh, bones, and entrails—had ceased, Rei cautiously emerged from the tree's shadow.

Looking again, the scene spread before him could only be described as a hellish tableau.

"Even the Aero Wing's flesh... once it's like this, we can't eat it, can we?"

"Grr..."

Set, who had landed on the ground using the momentum from his earlier Power Crush, let out a sorrowful growl at Rei's mutter.

Set had desperately wanted to absorb the Aero Wing's magic stone, and he remembered its flesh being exceptionally delicious.

Furthermore, though Set had no interest whatsoever, even the feathers—a highly valued material from the Aero Wing—were now covered in blood and flesh, rendering them completely unsellable. Gathering them would undoubtedly be a struggle.

Seeing Set like this, Rei, perhaps feeling sorry for him after all, gently stroked his dejected head.

"Come on, cheer up. That magic stone might not have completely vanished from that last strike, right? Look a little harder... Ah!"

Rei's words trailed off before he could finish.

Because his gaze fell upon something... a magic stone hidden among the Aerowings' flesh fragments.

"Set... Set! Set!"

"Guru?"

Set turned his gaze toward Rei, who had suddenly shouted, asking what was wrong.

But Rei ignored Set and started running toward the magic stone.

The magic stone he picked up was covered in blood and flesh, yet strangely, it wasn't broken and remained perfectly intact.

"Set! This!"

Set tilted his head slightly, wondering what on earth Rei was talking about, but upon recognizing the magic stone in Rei's hand, he approached with a delighted growl.

"Gurururu!"

His light-footed dash across the ground probably stemmed from his own fear that he might have shattered the magic stone.

No, it wouldn't have been strange at all if it had been broken.

Perhaps it was a miracle brought about by being encased within Aero Wing's body.

Wiping the blood and flesh fragments from the magic stone with a nearby leaf, Rei then turned to Set and opened his mouth.

"Here, Set. Let's go. This is the Aerowings' magic stone you wanted."

The magic stone, tossed along with those words, was caught in Set's beak and swallowed.

[Set has learned the skill 'Tornado Lv. 2']

A message echoed in his mind, and Set let out a triumphant roar.

"GRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRR!"

That joy was undoubtedly largely due to finally mastering Tornado.

Always by Rei's side, Set had naturally witnessed countless times how Rei unleashed fire whirlwinds to trample enemies.

Because of that, he knew Rei held a strong attachment to the fire whirlwind among his attack methods.

The fact that Tornado, which could make that fire whirl even more powerful, had become stronger was undoubtedly a joyous event for Set.

"Set, why don't you try using it right away?"

"Guruu!"

At Rei's words, Set, purring happily, fixed his gaze firmly on the space ahead and activated the skill.

"GRRRRRRRR!"

The tornado that appeared with that sound stood about three meters tall.

"At level one, it was about one meter tall, so roughly three times bigger now. ...Based on past patterns, I thought it'd be around two meters. This is a pleasant surprise."

With a pleased smile, Rei stroked Set's head as he stared intently at the tornado.

Whether pleased by the sensation or not, Set purred happily and unintentionally moved the tornado in an unintended direction... leading to tragedy.

The area was littered with large amounts of Aerowings' flesh, bones, organs, feathers, and such. The tornado moved right into that mess.

What happened next was obvious.

The remains of the Aerowings scattered on the ground were sucked into the tornado and absorbed, while at the same time, the tornado's centrifugal force hurled torn flesh, organs, and bone fragments outwards.

When Set defeated the Aerowings, it had been a vertical rain of blood and flesh. This time, it was a horizontal rain of flesh, bones, and organs.

...Set stood in front of Rei, so Rei remained unharmed, but as a result, Set ended up covered in flesh and bone stuck to his entire body.

"Guruu..."

Only Set's sorrowful cry echoed around them.
