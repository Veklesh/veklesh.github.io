Rei turned around at the sudden voice behind him.

He didn't instinctively brace himself, partly because he figured he wouldn't be attacked in the middle of town—especially in front of the guild—and mostly because the voice sounded familiar.

Turning around, Rei found himself face to face with a pair of thieves and warriors.

Recognizing the pair, Rei spoke up.

"Culotte and Arogan? What's up? That's an unusual pairing."

Standing there were indeed the two members of the Rank D party, Eternal Power, just as Rei had said.

Normally, the mage Scola would be here to complete the Eternal Power trio, but Scola was nowhere to be seen today.

They were Rei's peers from when he had challenged the rank-up exam before. Originally, Culotte and Scola were childhood friends, and after the rank-up exam ended, Alogan joined them, forming the Eternal Power.

Arogan had tangled with Rei even before the rank-up exam, learning the harsh reality of their power disparity... but things hadn't soured completely between them, likely because they had shared meals and lodging, however briefly.

"Scola's off that way to study magic a bit. Thanks to that, I'm stuck hanging out with this guy for about ten days."

Culotte shot Alogan a displeased look.

Being naturally short-tempered, Alogan wasn't about to stay quiet when Culotte pulled that stunt.

"Hmph! What are you talking about? Don't you know I'm the one putting up with this?"

"What?"

"What is it?"

Guh-guh-guh-guh, the two glare at each other.

But Rei showed no sign of stopping them.

Wondering about this, Marina tugged lightly on Rei's Dragon Robe and spoke up.

"Hey, wait. Is this okay?"

"Yeah, it's fine. It's just their usual roughhousing. You know what they say, right? Fight like cats and dogs, but they're actually best buds."

"Who says I'm like that!"

Hearing Rei's words, Alogan and Culotte stopped arguing and shouted at him.

...And seeing how perfectly timed their shouts were, it was undeniable they were in sync.

"...But seriously, Rei. Being with you might actually be..."

"Whoa... No way..."

Culotte, who had stopped arguing for the moment at Rei's words, looked at Rei and stopped speaking in shock.

Well, not exactly at Rei, but at the figure behind him... Marina.

Arogan, too, had known Marina, the former Guild Master, and had stopped moving. ...No, it was more accurate to say he was captivated by Marina's beauty.

Kyurot kicked Arogan's leg.

"Ow! Hey, what the hell do you think you're doing?!"

"Shut up! Seriously, drooling over the Guildmaster like that!"

"Hah. What's this? Jealous... Ow! Stop kicking me out of nowhere!"

"Shut up, you self-important jerk! Who says I'm jealous?!"

Seeing the two start arguing again, Marina seemed to understand this was normal for them.

She didn't try to stop them any further... and opened her mouth as if she'd come to terms with it.

"Ah, I remember now. If you took the rank-up exam with Rei, it must be that one, right? The one just before the Elena incident."

"Yeah, that's it. There were a few others too, but..."

An elf woman and a human man.

Recalling the archer and the warrior, Rei smiled.

They were quite a colorful bunch, but that rank-up exam now felt like a distant memory... almost like it happened ten years ago.

It was proof of how fulfilling, how rich, the days Rei had lived since then had been.

Ten years ago, Rei was probably only around ten years old.

After elementary school ended, he'd play with friends, running around in the mountains.

"Rei? What's wrong?"

"Nothing. It just feels like that rank-up exam was ages ago."

"Considering how easily you get dragged into trouble, that's hardly surprising."

Though Rei felt a slight irritation at Marina's words, he suddenly noticed something. Marina was giving him a meaningful look... and then directing that same look toward Arogan and Culotte.

Yes, the two people right in front of him were, to a certain extent, people he could call close. And with Schola off on her own, he couldn't properly take on a formal request.

They were precisely the people Rei needed to find right now.

In terms of strength, he didn't know how powerful the person who attacked Azimov was, so he couldn't be completely confident.

But Alogan wielded a Magic Sword, and his combat prowess would likely place him among the top-tier D-rank adventurers.

Culotte was a thief and not particularly strong in combat, but as befitted a thief, she had a keen ability to detect traps and ambushes.

...In terms of pure combat strength, Byune was probably superior to Cullot.

Her weapon, White Cloud, and the repeated combat training she underwent throughout the winter had granted Byune that level of combat strength.

However, in aspects beyond combat strength, Culotte is undoubtedly superior.

"...Well, now that we've met again after so long, I have a favor to ask of the Eternal Power... What do you say?"

At the word "request," both Kyurott and Arogan wore peculiar expressions.

Expressions that were both pleased and fearful... complex expressions.

Without Skola, they couldn't take on large-scale requests.

But they wanted to earn some money if possible.

In that sense, they were pleased to have a request... but the biggest problem was that it was Rei who brought it.

There was no way a request from Rei was just a simple request.

An almost strange level of trust in Rei.

Faced with that gaze, Rei flashed a look that was... just a tiny bit displeased.

"If you have a problem with it, you don't have to take it. The guild will look for someone else,"

That's what Rei said, but in reality, since he didn't have that many adventurer acquaintances, searching the guild for Azimov's escort wasn't guaranteed to turn up anything.

It was almost a bluff, but fortunately, it seemed to work perfectly well for now.

Culotte spoke up, sounding flustered.

"Hold on. I didn't say I wouldn't take the job. So? What kind of job is it? At least tell me. If it's something I can handle, I might take it."

"It's fine, right?" Cullot glanced at Arogan, who nodded without objection.

"Yeah. If it's a request we can handle... I mean, you're not asking us to go take down a Rank A monster or anything like that, are you?"

If it was, he'd back out... That was the unspoken message in his gaze. Rei nodded.

"I don't think it's that dangerous of a request. It's just escort duty."

"...Escort?"

Culotte and Arogan, clearly not expecting the word "escort" to come from Rei's mouth, turned to look at Rei and Marina with blank expressions.

"I'd like to discuss the details... Why don't we head to a nearby diner? It's probably better if we don't have people overhearing us, right?"

"Is it okay for the Guildmaster to take on a job that doesn't go through the Guild?"

To Kyurot, Marina was someone far above his station.

That was precisely why he used such polite language, unlike his usual tone.

But Marina, addressed in such a manner, wore a radiant smile.

It was the kind of smile that could make even a woman blush involuntarily.

"It's fine. I'm just an adventurer now. Even if I was once the guildmaster."

Led by Marina, Rei and the others entered a restaurant a short distance from the guild.

A few customers were inside, but it was still before noon, so many tables remained empty.

"Welcome. Are you here to eat?"

The shop attendant asked when they saw Rei and the others.

Seeing Marina, they looked slightly surprised, but the expression vanished quickly, replaced by a friendly smile as they asked.

"We'd like something light. Just prepare something suitable. Oh, and drinks would be nice too."

Marina said with a smile as she handed the staff member several silver coins.

The moment the staff member saw that smile, their face flushed bright red as they took the silver coins, then hurriedly opened their mouth.

"Please, feel free to sit anywhere you like. I'll bring it right over!"

With that, the male clerk headed straight for the kitchen.

Marina had given a few silver coins and a bit extra, but that amount was meant to cover the cost of using the seat.

...Even so, it was still a bit too much.

As soon as they sat down at the table, Arogan spoke up, seemingly unable to wait.

"So, Rei. What's this about a bodyguard? You're not my bodyguard, are you?"

It wasn't exactly a lead-in to the conversation, but Alogan said it as if it were a joke.

In truth, it was merely a conversation starter; Alogan didn't actually think Rei needed a bodyguard.

Culotte felt the same way, and she even shot a look of exasperation at Alogan for saying such a thing.

These two had participated in the rank-up exam alongside Rei and had seen his abilities demonstrated to the utmost degree.

For someone like Rei to need an escort was simply inconceivable.

...Well, maybe if he was sick or something and his body was weak, he might want an escort. But from what they could see, Rei didn't look weak at all.

Then who needs protection? The next time Alogan and Culotte looked, their gaze fell on Marina, who was with Rei.

But just by looking at her, they could tell she radiated an aura stronger than their own.

In fact, it felt more accurate to say that instead of them guarding her, she should be guarding them... something like that.

"Ah. Actually, I'm having a bit of a problem right now."

At those words, Alogan's expression soured.

Of course, since he had come to him with this matter, he understood there must be some reason behind it.

But hearing that there was an actual problem like this still made him think about various things.

And it wasn't just Alogan; Culotte felt the same way.

Showing a hint of caution, she opened her mouth.

"Just to be clear, is this something we can actually handle?"

"Ah... well, I don't think it involves the problem itself. What I need you to do is provide escort, but it's really more of a precaution."

"Just in case?"

"Yeah. The person we want you to escort was attacked recently. We warned whoever we think did it, so it should be fine now. But it's not guaranteed, and that person has actually been attacked before. That's why it's just a precaution."

"Ugh, just hearing about it sounds like a hassle."

"Is that so? Like I said, I don't think there'll be an attack, but it's just a precaution. I don't think it'll actually come to combat."

"That's just your perception, Rei, right? Applying that to us is a bit of a problem."

Culotte muttered, genuinely troubled, not playing games or anything.

Having completed numerous requests over the past few years, they had some confidence in their skills.

But even so, they knew the gap between them and Rei was something they could never bridge.

After all, rising to a Rank B adventurer in just a few years after joining the guild, and even earning a nickname, was something beyond what anyone could reasonably believe possible.

"Well, what do you think, Marina?"

"You're asking me here? Oh, thanks."

After thanking the waiter who had just brought over some easy-to-eat items like fruit and sausage, along with fruit juice, Marina thought for a moment before speaking.

"As Rei mentioned, this request is mostly just a precaution. So, I think a Rank D adventurer should be fine for this?"

"...I'll take it."

"Wait, Alogan!? What are you suddenly saying?!"

Arogan nodded abruptly at Marina's explanation.

Seeing his cheeks flush slightly, it was obvious why he'd accepted the request.

Glaring at him resentfully... yet realizing it would be unwise to leave him alone, Culotte sighed and accepted Rei's request.
