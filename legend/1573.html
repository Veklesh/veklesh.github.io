In terms of setting traps, this cave was undoubtedly easier to rig than the underground facility in Mejogo.

However, that also meant disarming them would be more troublesome.

Above all, unlike the Mejogo underground facility, which had a reasonably well-equipped living environment, this cave wasn't particularly spacious.

Given that, there was frequent traffic in and out of the cave.

Setting traps each time was deemed wasteful, so they stopped being set.

"Guess there aren't any bats or such around since people live here. Maybe the giant's presence has something to do with it."

Though, with the light present, she wouldn't be likely to trip over a step unless something unusual happened.
