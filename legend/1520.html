Waiting beside the guardhouse for the Yellow-clad man Rei had sent off, he watched as numerous others passed by in front of the guardhouse, following his gaze.

Some were just a prostitute and a client, while others were men with a prostitute clinging to each arm. There was even the rare pair of women walking together with a prostitute.

For a moment, Rei wondered if perhaps two prostitutes were walking together for some reason, but even he could see the sweet, intimate atmosphere flowing between the two women.

Given that, Rei concluded they must be lovers, and was forced to listen to the sweet exchange between them.

Because the woman with the prostitute was whispering words of love right next to the alley where Rei was hiding.

(I wish they'd do that somewhere else... No, wait, it's weird that I'm hiding in an alley in the first place. But then again, this is that kind of town.)

Watching the woman leave with the prostitute, Rei felt relieved that his hiding spot hadn't been discovered. He shifted his gaze toward the office... and soon noticed something small darting out the window.

Recognizing that something as a familiar presence, he felt a relief several times deeper than when the female customer and the prostitute had disappeared from near him.

"Phew... You made it back safely."

"Kyuu!"

Rei, flying through the air, chirped in response to Yellow's mutter.

Yellow landed safely on Rei's outstretched hand and immediately burrowed into Rei's Dragon Robe.

...The reason it seemed so pleased was partly because it had been useful to Rei and to its master, Elena, but also because it could enter the Dragon Robe.

The temperature inside the Dragon Robe was maintained for comfort.

Yellow was a black dragon, a species possessing some of the greatest power in this world, even as a child.

Even so, being still a child, the summer heat probably wasn't exactly pleasant for him.

Being unable to move was a slight annoyance, but at least inside Rei's Dragon Robe, he could spend his time comfortably.

"Ah... yeah. Yellow, just stay like that. Now, what else should I look at... I wonder."

Rei murmured as he surveyed the surrounding scenery visible from the alleyway.

If Mejougo were a bit smaller, he might have a better idea of where to look, but unfortunately, given its size, it wasn't so easy to figure out where to start searching.

"Well, let's walk a bit more for now. We still have time before the carriage back to Rossi leaves."

"Kyuu!"

Inside Rei's Dragon Robe, Yellow gave a short cry of agreement.

He'd rather not have her cry out where people could hear, but here and now it shouldn't be a problem. Rei gently stroked the spot where Yellow was from outside the Dragon Robe.

Whether he liked the sensation or was being considerate to avoid drawing attention... Yellow fell silent after that.

Rei emerged from the alley and began walking down the street.

He passed near the guard post, but the man standing in front of it showed no particular interest in Rei.

(Huh? I thought that orc-like woman mentioned me earlier... Why no reaction? Wait, maybe because I'm hooded? Well, there are plenty of short people around, so it's understandable if he didn't recognize me.)

For now, avoiding unnecessary trouble was fortunate for Rei too, so he didn't make any moves to provoke anything himself and walked away from the guard post.

(This town's this big, so there's probably more than one or two guard posts... but sending Yellow to all of them is a bit much. Besides, we probably don't have that kind of time anyway.)

As Rei walked down the street, unsure what to do next, it was only natural that many voices called out to him.

No one mistook Rei for a prostitute, and since that was the case, they assumed he was a customer.

But Rei didn't take the bait, of course. He kept walking down the street, observing his surroundings.

More precisely, he was observing the structure of this pleasure district called Mejougo.

(If I really wanted to investigate it properly, I could just ride Set and survey it from above. ...Maybe I'll ride Set and come during the day tomorrow? I don't know if there are any barriers or anything... Wait, are there?)

Setting up a barrier isn't free, of course.

It requires a starting point like a magic item or a magic circle. In rare cases, they might keep a monster alive and use its life force as the power source... In that way, setting up a barrier costs money in various ways.

Given that, it's impossible for a small country like Reburina to cast barriers over every town and village.

Considering that even the Kingdom of Mireana, the overlord of the Kingdom of Reburina, can't afford to set up barriers in every single village and town, you can understand just how much it costs to set up a barrier.

(Well, Mejougo is a town under Jaya's direct control. If that's the case, it's entirely possible they could afford a barrier. After all, Jaya seems to possess a large number of magic items, so it wouldn't be strange if they could afford one, right?)

As Rei walked down the street lost in such thoughts, he suddenly stopped and glanced toward an alleyway.

At the same time, Yellow inside the Dragon Robe lightly scratched him, trying to get Rei's attention.

Laying a light hand over the Dragon Robe to stroke Yellow, who understood, he casually scanned his surroundings. Confirming no one was watching him, he stepped into the alleyway.

The alley Rei entered didn't seem to hold anything special at first glance. ...Well, at first glance, that is.

But Rei sensed a human presence there.

A presence that was terribly frightened, its nerves frayed...

It gave the impression of a kitten, wet from the rain, feeling vulnerable yet putting on a brave front. As he advanced down the alley, the passage eventually came to a dead end.

It existed precisely between the gaps of shops... but on the wall at that dead end, there was a small hole.

And the presence he sensed was coming from beyond that hole.

"Is anyone there?"

Forgetting to put on Ren's mask, Rei asked. The person on the other side of the wall visibly tensed at his words.

"Come out. I mean you no harm. If you're willing, I'd like to hear your story."

Rei said this because he suspected the person on the other side might know some secret about this Mejougo.

He didn't expect things to go that smoothly, but given he had no leads at all right now, if there was anything that might be a clue, he had no choice but to take it.

Rei called out through the hole, but there was no reply.

But he could sense, without a doubt, that someone was definitely there.

(Now then, what to do. ...Hiding in a place like this means they definitely have some reason. ...Reason, reason, huh?)

If they were hiding here for some reason, it stood to reason that whoever was on the other side must be struggling to get food and water.

Of course, how badly they wanted food and water would depend on how long the person beyond the hole had been hiding.

In extreme cases, if they'd only been hiding there for a few hours, they probably weren't even hungry or thirsty yet.

But if they'd been hiding for a day, two days... or even longer, they could very well be nearing their limit.

(Well, what will they do?)

Thinking this, Rei took a loaf of bread from the Misty Ring.

Not a sandwich or anything with fillings, just plain white bread.

However, it was freshly baked from a bakery renowned for its delicious bread, so the aroma of freshly baked bread—unsuitable for an alleyway—wafted through the air.

That scent would likely stimulate the appetite of someone enduring hunger more than any mediocre dish.

In fact, Rei could sense movement on the other side of the hole.

Did it work?

Thinking that, Rei opened his mouth.

"Hey, aren't you hungry? Right now, besides the fresh bread, I could throw in some jerky or soup too. ...Ah, needless to say, I'm not exactly one of these Mejougo... Jayah people. No, if anything, I'm on the side opposing that Jayah."

Clearly stating that he was hostile to Jāya in this Mejōgo carried considerable risk.

But to gain the trust of the person beyond the wall, Rei needed to take at least some risk.

And then... silence filled the surroundings for several tens of seconds.

That silence was confined only to the spot where Rei stood. From the street came the sounds of women's cheers and cooing voices, along with what sounded like a man's roar.

Listening to the sounds while keeping his eyes on the hole in the wall, Rei... eventually sensed movement beyond the hole.

Soon, a woman cautiously peered out from the hole in the wall, as if surveying her surroundings.

She was clearly ready to flee at the first sign of danger, but upon realizing it was truly only Rei there, she let out a slight sigh of relief.

She had probably been hiding here for several days.

Her face was dirty, and the clothes she wore were revealing, like those of a prostitute, though they too were stained with mud and grime.

Her age was somewhere between late teens and early twenties.

She wasn't exactly a stunning beauty, but her features were attractive enough that she could probably build a steady clientele as a prostitute.

But even that well-featured woman was now overcome by intense suspicion, staring at Rei as if to keep him at bay... no, glaring at him.

To dispel her suspicion, Rei offered the bread he was holding.

The savory aroma wafting from the bread in Rei's hand made the woman's stomach growl insistently.

"Huh!?"

The silence surrounding them made it impossible to mask the sound.

Even while hiding in this spot, the woman still had enough presence of mind to be concerned about her surroundings. Her cheeks flushed with embarrassment as she glared at Rei.

To such a woman, Rei handed over the freshly baked bread without seeming particularly bothered.

The sound of her stomach growling didn't particularly bother Rei.

Of course, it depended on the situation, but at the very least, he understood that it was only natural for a woman hiding in a hole like this, who probably hadn't eaten properly, to have her stomach growl.

Perhaps seeing Rei's reaction eased her slightly. Taking the bread from his hand, she hurriedly brought it to her mouth.

Seeing the woman eat not just eat, but devour it, Rei handed her the soup.

She drank the vegetable soup from the bowl directly, without even using a spoon.

(They say eating suddenly after fasting is bad for you... but seeing how she's eating, is she really okay?)

Rei felt a slight concern for the woman, but seeing how vigorously she was eating, he decided it must be fine. He went to offer her another piece of bread... and noticed ears protruding from the woman's head.

They were quite small, dog ears, probably usually hidden by her hair.

(A dog beastman? Or maybe a wolf?)

Honestly, Rei couldn't really tell the difference between a dog beastman and a wolf beastman.

If he got it wrong, her attitude would probably harden.

Deciding that, he chose not to specify what kind of beastman she was for now, stating only the plain facts.

"A beastman?"

"Huh!?"

The woman must have heard Rei's mutter. She frantically tried to hide her ears, which were hidden in her hair.

But Rei paid her no mind, handing her another freshly baked loaf from the Misty Ring.

"There's no need to hide it, right? This country doesn't discriminate against beastmen, after all."

Rei knew there were countries that discriminated against beastmen and treated them as slaves, but at least in this country of Reburina, and in the vassal states of the Mireana Kingdom, it was prohibited.

Of course, given the variety of personalities, it wasn't as if there were absolutely no one who harbored aversion toward beastmen.

Seeing Rei's attitude, the woman seemed slightly relieved... Seizing that opening, Rei spoke up.

"Well then, could you tell me a bit more about everything?"
