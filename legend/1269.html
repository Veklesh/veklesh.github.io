"Yes. Rank B monsters are brought in fairly often, so even I can manage them. With Rank A monsters, failures would be more frequent, but if you're willing to sacrifice half... no, more than half the materials, I think I could manage. However..."

Making clothes from Rank S monster materials is something I simply cannot do.

Kornes stated.

As one of the finest artisans in Gilm, she must feel frustrated to have to refuse the request.

The fox ears sprouting from his head flattened against his scalp.

Marina glanced down at her own outfit.

Today, Marina wore a pure white party dress, as if tailored for winter.

Marina recognized Cornes' skill, and when Rei asked her to recommend a tailor who could make clothes using Silver Lion fur, Cornes was the first person who came to mind.

Claiming she was the finest in Gilm was neither conceit nor anything of the sort.

Cornes shook his head at Rei, who was asking on Marina's behalf.

"With my skill level, I'd undoubtedly ruin every single piece of material."

He explained that while he might manage materials from low-rank monsters, he couldn't bear the thought of ruining materials from an S-rank monster like the Silver Lion due to his own lack of skill.

He was confident in his skill, but he didn't let it go to his head.

Seeing Cornes like this, Rei felt a sense of admiration.

Of course, this was not romantic affection, but rather respect for Cornes as a master tailor.

"Then... hey, Cornes, right? Even if it's impossible to make clothes from silver lion fur now, someday... when your skills improve enough to make it possible, could I ask you? Assuming the silver lion fur is still around by then, of course."

Implied: If a more skilled tailor is found, I'll ask them instead.

Hearing Rei's words, Cornes stared at him with a look of surprise.

Cornes wasn't entirely unmoved by the idea of using materials from a rank S monster like the Silver Lion to make clothes.

In fact, he had even thought he absolutely wanted to handle this project himself.

But... even so, it was clear his current skill level made it impossible. He couldn't risk wasting the Silver Lion's materials by botching the job.

That's precisely why he couldn't help but feel happy at the thought of someday being able to...

"Yes. I don't know when it will be. But I will definitely... I want to become able to make clothes using Silver Lion materials as soon as possible."

Walker handled the most urgent matters, but he couldn't resolve everything.
