Five men, clearly drunk, still drinking around a table.

Fortunately, the drunks were already quite inebriated and didn't seem to have the presence of mind to notice their surroundings.

Even with his hood up, the shopkeeper must have sensed from Rei's demeanor that this was serious business.

For a moment, Rei wondered if it was okay to leave the drunks like that, but he decided they were probably fine, given how intoxicated they were.

The fact the shopkeeper didn't seem particularly concerned about them was also a big factor.

And so, just like before, they descended into the basement. Just like before, the door was knocked at a steady rhythm.

He probably figured he couldn't leave the drunks alone for too long, even if it was just for a little while.
