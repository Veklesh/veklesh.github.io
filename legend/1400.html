Rei and Vihera soared into the sky alongside Set, but Giganto Turtle would never allow an opponent capable of easily damaging it to act freely.

Timing his move for when Set took flight, one of the three faces—the one closest to Set—opened its mouth.

"GRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRR

Along with that roar, a fierce wind was unleashed from the Giganto Turtle's right neck.

It wasn't a direct attack like a wind blade; it was truly just wind... a breath worthy of being called a Wind Breath.

Not like a blade of wind, but more like a wind club... a powerful breath that fit that description perfectly.

"GRRRRRRRR!"

But naturally, Set wasn't about to meekly take such an attack.

Taking advantage of his small stature—small only when compared to the Giganto Turtle—he flapped his wings and swiftly flew away from the spot.

Unlike the various breath attacks Rei had seen in previous battles, which tended to be wide-ranging, the Wind Breath was fired in a straight line toward the direction Giganto Turtle's face was facing.

That's precisely why evading it wasn't difficult...

"This is troublesome!"

"Yeah!"

As Rei muttered atop Set, who moved swiftly through the sky, Vihera, clinging to Set's foreleg, shouted to resist the wind's flow.

In truth, the one finding the Wind Breath most troublesome in this situation—more so than Set flying through the air or Rei riding his back—was likely Vihera, clinging to Set's foreleg.

She wasn't wearing any kind of safety harness like she usually did.

In such a situation, if they were hit by a fierce wind attack like Windbreeze, they could easily lose their grip on Set's foreleg.

And if she let go, Vihera, having no means to fly, would naturally fall to the ground.

"Gurrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrr

"Don't fall, Vihera!"

A different head from the one that had just unleashed the Wind Breath opened its mouth, and in the next instant, a massive torrent of water erupted from it.

And it wasn't just water—it was water compressed by magic.

And though Rei didn't know it, countless grains of sand, coated in magic, were mixed within that water.

Set managed to dodge the Water Breath hurtling straight toward him. But where he dodged... the Water Breath struck a spot far from the battlefield. The next instant, it violently scattered the earth that had been there.

It was an attack so powerful it felt like an explosion had occurred.

"Set, the last head's coming! Circle around to the shell!"

"GRRRRRRRR!"

At Rei's words, Set flapped his wings wide once more.

The third head opened its mouth... and what it spat out was a rock.

Not just any rock, but a long, spear-like one with a sharp point.

It would be more accurate to call it a rock spear.

Countless such rock spears were spewed toward Set, who was flying through the air.

Following Rei's instructions, Set dodged the attack by moving toward the shell of the Giganto Turtle.

His plan was that the creatures couldn't possibly attack him from behind... and true to his plan, the wind, water, and rock breaths fired from the three mouths each halted their motion.

But instead, the trees growing on the shell hurled vines, thorns, and nuts at Set, who had entered their respective attack ranges.

"Treant?!"

Rei shouted in surprise at the unexpected turn of events.

But then again, this was a Giganto Turtle, practically the embodiment of the Treant forest itself. It was hardly surprising that Treants grew on its shell.

Rei's realization and Vihera letting go of Set's leg happened almost simultaneously.

Rei, momentarily thrown off by Set's movement, looked down and saw Vihera plummeting toward the Giganto Turtle's shell.

Since Set had descended to a distance where Treants and the others could attack, such a height posed no landing concern for Vihera.

"You'll just get in the way down there. I'll attack from over here!"

Vihera shouted as she fell toward the ground—or more accurately, the Giganto Turtle's shell.

That decision was absolutely correct.

Indeed, clinging to Set's front leg with nothing but her grip, no safety line, against a Giganto Turtle unleashing three types of breath attacks, was nothing short of a death-defying act.

One slight misstep, and she'd be blown off course, hurtling from the sky toward the ground.

Moreover, the thought of Rei and Set being unable to fight at full strength because they were protecting her was something Vihera could not bear.

So, following her instinct, Vihera let go of Set's leg and fell toward the Giganto Turtle's shell.

Perhaps understanding Vihera's resolve,

Rei glared once more at the Giganto Turtle, which was watching for an opening between him and Set, and spoke.

"Got it. Count on me!"

"Yes!"

Twisting his body to evade attacks launched from Treant, or intercepting them with his gauntlets and greaves, Vihera moved with agility unimaginable for someone in midair as he shouted.

Unable to see Vihera's state, Rei glared at the Giganto Turtle's face and addressed Set.

"First, we'll take down one neck at a time."

"GRRRR!"

Rei's first target was the mouth spewing the final spear—the rock spear—among the wind, water, and rock spears.

The wind spear restricts movement, but its actual destructive power isn't that high.

However, both the water and rock spears possessed power that could be described as vicious.

He targeted the mouth spewing rock spears over water because rock spears allow for wider-area attacks than water breath.

(Well, if it could sweep through while spewing Water Breath, that would be no laughing matter.)

Watching the three necks of the Giganto Turtle rapidly closing in, I found myself slightly amused by these thoughts.

A small smile twisted the corners of his mouth as he channeled magic into the Twilight Spear gripped in his left hand.

「Set」

With just that one word, Set must have understood exactly what Rei wanted.

He flapped his wings harder, increasing his flying speed.

From atop Set's back, Rei hurled the Twilight Spear clutched in his left hand.

Though it was a throw using only his upper body, the spear's speed was by no means slow.

Would it be affected by the magic barrier? He wondered, but this time there was no barrier. The spear flew straight toward the neck spewing the rock spear.

Naturally, the Giganto Turtle tried to dodge the spear hurled at it.

But, naturally, the speed of the thrown spear and the Giganto Turtle's mountain-like bulk meant that in terms of speed... especially agility, the spear was overwhelmingly superior.

The Giganto Turtle surely knew the power and speed of the Twilight Spear fired, but did it not expect Rei to fire it while riding Set?

(Didn't he know everything that happened in Treant Forest?)

When defeating the giant flower, Rei had thrown the Twilight Spear while riding Set.

That thought flashed briefly in the back of his mind, but focusing on the immediate situation took priority.

The Twilight Spear pierced the eyeball of the right eye on the neck that spewed rock spears.

Of course, this was a single strike from Rei's Twilight Spear, unleashed while he was in the state of activating the Flame Emperor’s Crimson Armour. It wouldn't end there.

It shattered the eyeball, pierced straight through the skull, crushed half the head, and emerged from the back of the skull, where the Twilight Spear appeared.

Against enemies like those encountered before... monsters fought in the Treant Forest, for instance... even after shattering and piercing the body, the Twilight Spear still possessed ample speed.

But this time, after shattering and piercing through half of the Giganto Turtle's skull, the Twilight Spear's speed was already nearly extinguished.

But... given one of the Twilight Spear's abilities—its capacity to freely return to its wielder—this posed no particular problem.

The next instant, the Twilight Spear that had shattered the Giganto Turtle's head vanished, reappearing in Rei's hand.

"GRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRR

The Giganto Turtle's roar echoed throughout the area.

It was filled with intense rage.

...Well, one of its heads had been destroyed, so that was only natural.

"But why... why does it still move without any trouble? Its body... Whoa, Set!"

"GRRRRRR!"

Set let out a sharp cry at Rei's words and flapped its wings.

The next instant, the tail of the Giganto Turtle swept past the spot where Set's body had been just moments before.

It was a blow truly worthy of being called a morning star.

Had Rei been a split second slower to react... or had Set been even a fraction of a second late obeying Rei's command, while it wouldn't have killed him outright, he would undoubtedly have been blown away.

But that was precisely because it was a semi-surprise attack.

Even though they were facing each other, the tail reaching all the way to where they stood now was completely unexpected for Rei too... but once seen, a surprise attack loses half its effect.

"GAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA

The Giganto Turtle's roar echoed once more.

However, the cry was different from before.

Along with the roar, both wind and water breath attacks were unleashed simultaneously.

But unlike the rock spear breath, the attack range of this breath wasn't that wide, making it not particularly difficult for Set to dodge.

Especially since Vihera wasn't clinging to his legs now, merely carrying Rei on his back.

The Giganto Turtle's two types of breath attacks couldn't keep up with Set's movements as he flapped his wings and maneuvered skillfully through the air.

Despite performing what could be called aerial acrobatics, Rei showed no signs of dizziness or motion sickness.

In fact, he even had the composure to glance down at the battle unfolding on the ground.

"Don't get trampled by its feet! Anyway, distract it so Rei can focus on attacking! Just buy us time! It's frustrating, but there's no way we can beat this thing right now!"

One of the adventurers shouted as he leapt backward.

When Rei attacked, it shifted its foot to regain balance. Even that movement alone possessed enough power to instantly kill any ordinary human who couldn't dodge it.

And that was completely unintentional... For the Giganto Turtle, it was just a normal movement, an attack performed simply by moving. For the adventurers fighting below, it must have been unbearably nerve-wracking.

Nearly all the adventurers present possessed a certain level of skill.

This meant they could somewhat anticipate the monster's next move based on its behavior.

However, that was only when facing the monster directly. Fighting an entity like the Giganto Turtle was simply not something they had anticipated.

Moreover, the Giganto Turtle wasn't attacking intentionally; it was simply moving its legs to shift position or support its body weight. This left the adventurers with little choice but to react on the fly.

Even in such a situation, the fact that the adventurers suffered no casualties was likely thanks to Marina.

Borrowing the power of the wind spirit, the water spirit, and the earth spirit... she was rescuing those about to be crushed by the Giganto Turtle.

"Whoa!"

Set flapped his wings in midair. Rei, who had briefly looked down at the ground, saw the Giganto Turtle reappear in his line of sight.

Having lost one head, the Giganto Turtle's remaining two heads were filled with eyes burning with hostility and hatred.

Apparently deciding that breath and tail attacks alone were insufficient against Rei and Set, the two heads chose a more direct assault next.

"GRRRR!"

"Whoa!"

The head that had just fired the compressed water breath... the Water Cutter... opened its enormous jaws. The very next instant... it lunged forward, attempting to crush Rei and Set in its bite.

The fangs protruding from its jaws were each as long as a longsword, if not longer.

It extended its neck to deliver this blade-like attack.

Normally, it couldn't possibly reach them, but that neck... stretched.

(A softshell turtle!?)

The sight of the softshell turtle extending its neck he had seen then closely resembled this attack.
