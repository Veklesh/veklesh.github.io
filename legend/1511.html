A back alley a short distance from the tavern.

There, Rei, Vihera, and Byune were with a man.

This man was the same one who had tried to hit on Vihera at the tavern. When Vihera refused him, he flew into a rage as expected, determined to make her his woman by force if necessary.

...Considering Vihera's beauty, it wasn't surprising, but given the difference in their abilities, it was nothing short of suicide.

As a result, the man, easily disarmed by Vihera, had ended up here in the alley with Rei and the others, being forced to assist with gathering information.

"Now then, brothels... specifically, where are the brothels with the largest number of prostitutes? Could you tell us?"

"Hii! Wh-why do you want to know that?!"

"Oh, didn't I tell you? He's just too strong at night."

As she spoke, Vihera's gaze fell upon Rei.

Receiving a seductive, feminine glance unlike his usual self, Rei quietly averted his eyes.

He already knew the story: the women forcibly taken away—even using slave collars—were working as prostitutes.

If that was the case, then the brothel where those prostitutes worked must naturally be Ajax's organization.

And the reason the woman Vihera chose to search for a brothel was because her lover was too vigorous for her body to handle, so she was taking him to a brothel...

That in itself didn't bother him, but Rei certainly hadn't expected to be cast as that lover.

Of course, Rei knew exactly how Vihera felt about him, so perhaps this was only the natural outcome.

"Liar! Then why are you forcing this on me?!"

"Because you look like you're about to lie, don't you? So I figured something like this was probably for the best... What do you think?"

Saying this, Vihera flashed a smile that would captivate anyone who saw it at first glance.

...That is, if she hadn't been clenching a stone she'd picked up from the ground in her right hand, crushing it to pieces.

No matter how captivating the smile of the beautiful woman beside him, seeing her do something like that made it impossible to be charmed.

"Alright, alright! I'll tell you anything I know! Please! Just don't do anything!"

Vihera smiled at the man's plea, looking satisfied.

The smile he'd worn just moments before was different from the one he wore now.

Whether the man wearing the smile understood this himself, he opened his mouth while still smiling.

"Then tell me. Where is this brothel with all the prostitutes?"

"A lot? You really want to know about a brothel? ...Well..."

The man was slightly surprised. Despite threatening him like this, did she genuinely want to know about prostitutes?

After all, since she'd been brought here by force, he'd assumed the talk of prostitutes was just a pretext for something else... something more sensitive, the kind of information one wouldn't normally ask for so easily.

After all, the man was just an adventurer... merely an adventurer from the small country of Reburina.

He did have a certain pride in his abilities, given he worked as an adventurer in the capital, Rossi. Still, he was only a Rank D adventurer.

"For now, let's talk about the prostitutes. So? Where exactly are the brothels with the most prostitutes?"

The question came from Rei, not Vihera, but the man's will to resist had already been completely crushed.

He would have answered Byune's questions just as readily. ...If he could have understood Byune's questions, that is.

"Ah, there is a district in Rossi with brothels, but I don't think any particular place has an especially high number of prostitutes. Of course, I haven't been to every brothel myself, so I can't say for sure. ...But..."

Vihera turned his gaze back to the man, who had stopped speaking with a meaningful pause.

In that instant, the man felt a chill run down his spine and hurriedly opened his mouth.

"Well, there's this town called Mejougo, about a day's carriage ride from here. It's basically just brothels and taverns. Maybe that's where you'd find a huge number of prostitutes, like you guys were saying?"

"...Huh."

Vihera voiced her interest in that decidedly shady-sounding town of Mejougo.

The man was fortunate that Vihera's tone held no hint of displeasure.

Vihera understood perfectly well that some women could only survive as prostitutes.

...Though, if it was like what Ajax did in Gilm—forcibly abducting women and forcing them into prostitution with slave collars—that was utterly unforgivable.

As a woman who loved Rei, she fully understood the agony of being taken by a man against her will.

She knew her own feelings were full of contradictions, but right now, she needed to learn more about the city of Mejougo.

"So, how do I get into this Mejougo? Can I just walk in normally?"

"Ah, apparently you can enter without any particular trouble. The tavern drinks are said to be expensive, but the brothel prices are quite cheap, they say. But..."

The man paused, looking at Vihera.

More precisely, at the thin garment covering Vihera's body.

"What is it?"

"Well, you aside, a girl like you would stand out like a sore thumb in Mejougo."

"...I see. You might be right."

Since Mejougo is fundamentally a town centered around brothels, while men gathering there is one thing, women gathering there is basically unheard of.

Of course, that's just generally speaking. It wouldn't be strange for women who like women to be there.

In any case, if Elena, Marina, or Byune were in such a place, they'd definitely stand out.

If it were Vihera, her attire would likely make her appear to be a prostitute.

And if she looked like a prostitute, well, that would just invite more trouble.

After all, a prostitute as beautiful as Vihera would likely be desired by most men.

But naturally, Vihera had no such intention.

Given that, a commotion between customers wanting to buy Vihera and Vihera herself would be inevitable. It would interfere with investigating Mejougo... and ultimately, with carrying out retaliation against the organization Ajax belonged to.

"But... sending Rei alone to that Mejougo... I'm a little uneasy about that."

The Vihera who said this was utterly unrecognizable as the same woman who had threatened the man.

Women are scary, the man couldn't help thinking.

"We can talk about that later. It's not something we can decide on our own anyway. ...More importantly, tell me the exact location of this Mejougo place. How do we get there specifically?"

"Ah... Well, how to get there... Basically, you won't be accepted there unless you take one of the carriages leaving from here."

"...Huh? So, even if I happen to pass through this Mejougo town, I won't be able to enter it?"

"Ah, yeah. That's right."

"I see. So they're keeping a tight watch around there. ...But doesn't that kinda get in the way of making money?"

Carriages obviously can only carry a set number of people, and above all, maintaining them requires a certain amount of money.

The animals pulling the carriages, starting with the horses, are expensive, and if they're monsters, the price goes up even more.

If running a brothel is for making money, then it's clearly misguided... at least, that's how it seemed to Rei.

(So, maybe they're not running the brothel to make money after all? Well, I heard there are a fair number of women who actually want to be prostitutes, so it probably isn't worth forcibly abducting people from other countries just for that. ...But then, what is it for?)

In the end, it always comes back to that.

"I agree with what you said about me holding you back if it's just about making money. But then, I hear some people get kicked off the carriage. Considering that, there must be something going on at Mejougo after all."

As I talked with Rei, the tension gradually eased.

Vihera's actions were terrifying in many ways, but Rei never threatened me.

Besides, Rei was a man.

He probably figured Ray was naturally interested in Mejougo, a place teeming with prostitutes.

...Vihera shot him a displeased look at his demeanor, but the man seemed oblivious.

"You've never been?"

"Ah... money's tight. Sure, I hear Mejougo has cheaper brothels too, but if I'm going all the way there, I'd rather go to a slightly pricier one... not the absolute best, but..."

The man faltered mid-sentence, likely noticing the cold stare Vihera was directing at him.

As the man froze, his words completely halted, Vihera spoke again.

"So, to go to Mejougo, is there anything you need? Like a letter of introduction from someone?"

"Huh? Ah, um... I heard you don't really need anything. Of course, they'll probably make things easier for you if you have a letter of introduction."

"Right. Thanks for the info. But... you might want to watch your words and actions from now on, you know? ...As you can see, I have enough grip strength to crush stones..."

As she spoke, Vihera once again picked up a nearby stone and crushed it.

The gaze Vihera directed was aimed at the man's... crotch.

The man understood perfectly... perhaps too perfectly, what Vihera meant.

He nodded repeatedly, so fast you might have seen afterimages.

Vihera stared intently at the man's reaction, then finally smiled and spoke.

"I'm glad you understand."

At Vihera's reply, the man instinctively crossed his legs, relieved... saved.

The man had planned to relax for a few days starting today after finishing work yesterday... but starting off like this on day one was nothing short of sheer bad luck.

Of course, since the man had gone out of his way to provoke Vihera, Rei could only think it was his own fault.

"Well then, thanks for all the info. Next time, you should be more careful about who you pick a fight with, okay?"

With that, Vihera flashed a dazzling smile and walked away.

Normally, it wouldn't be strange to be captivated by that smile. It wouldn't be strange... but what the man felt now wasn't something so sweet. It was fear.

At least it ended safely... He felt relief at that.

Byune, who had watched the entire exchange impassively, followed after Vihera. Rei also started to follow... but stopped.

Though the man had made advances toward Vihera, even from Rei's perspective, the outcome this time could only be described as tragic.

Thinking this, he felt it wouldn't be right to just leave things as they were. He turned on his heel and approached the man.

"Huh!?"

The sound of footsteps made the man tense, fearing Vihera had returned. But recognizing it was Rei approaching, he relaxed slightly.

Still, this was a man who had been with someone as dangerous as Vihera.

And the man who was the object of Vihera's intense affection—that much was clear from their earlier exchange.

Such a person couldn't possibly be what he seemed, meaning Rei was nothing but a source of terror to the man.

Fortunately, Rei hadn't directly harmed the man. And though he stood before him now, he showed no particular intent to cause harm.

To the man, relieved by this, Rei handed over several silver coins he'd taken from the Misty Ring.

"Fee for the information."

"...Yeah."

Taking the silver coins, all the man could manage was that response.

He had been entangled, had information wrung from him, had been mesmerized, had been terrified... He had experienced emotions in such a short time that he normally wouldn't encounter so easily. Combined with being spared by Vihera, he was mentally exhausted.

The man gazed at the silver coins he'd received from Rei... Yes, he thought, he'd go to the tavern.
